## YouTube Description
Slow down for a warm collection of sleepy, excited and quietly curious Pompom Hills moments.

In this gentle preschool compilation, Arda and Opa share a peaceful goodnight, Mimi notices a big yawn and explores an excited feeling, and the Pompom Hills friends appear in a calm character introduction collection.

These stories support emotional awareness, gentle observation and settling into calm moments without loud or hurried storytelling.
