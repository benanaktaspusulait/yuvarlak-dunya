# Pompom Hills World Bible
Version 2.1

---

# Overview

Pompom Hills is a peaceful preschool fantasy world where kindness, curiosity, imagination and friendship shape everyday life.

There are no cities.
No roads.
No cars.
No danger.

Every location can be reached on foot through soft winding paths.

Everything is round.
Everything feels safe.
Everything encourages discovery.

The Big Pompom Tree stands at the center of the world.

Every path eventually leads back to it.

# World Rules

Nobody owns the forest.

Nobody locks their doors.

Everyone helps everyone.

Children never travel alone after sunset.

Opa always knows where everyone is.

Every location has at least two walking paths.

No sharp edges exist anywhere.

Every building is rounded.

Every tree can be climbed safely.

Flowers bloom all year.

Rainbows appear often.

Magic is subtle.

Kindness is stronger than magic.

# Story Logic

## Morning

Everyone gathers near Central Square.

## Afternoon

Children split into adventures.

## Evening

Everyone returns to Story Circle.

## Night

Opa tells a story.

Children walk home under the stars.

This daily rhythm keeps every story connected naturally.

# Residents & Location Ownership

Pompom Hills has two layers of residents:

## Main Cameo Cast (13 named characters)

The named, designed characters live in `01-CHARACTERS/` and are introduced in
S01E01. They are placed across the world's locations in the scenes, for example:

| Character | Associated Location |
| --- | --- |
| Kiko | Kiko's Home (Flower Hill) |
| Mimi | Mimi's Burrow (Flower Hill) |
| Opa | Opa's Tree |
| Tillo | Tillo's Garden / Tillo's Treehouse |
| Sofia | Rosie's Rose Garden (art/colour scenes) |
| Aiko | Butterfly Meadow |
| Amara | Friendship Meadow |
| Freya | Lily's Lavender Farm |
| Arda | Friendship Meadow (football) |
| Mirnik | Little Forest |
| Mimo | Rainbow Creek |
| Noah | Central Square |
| Luca | Stone Hill |

## Ambient Owner-Names (not speaking cast)

Some locations carry an owner-style name for warmth and flavour — **Rosie's Rose Garden,
Milo's Pond, Lily's Lavender Farm, Poppy's Bakery, Benny's Playground, Lulu's Greenhouse**.
Except where an owner is also a member of the main cast (e.g., **Tillo**), these names are
**ambient/background residents only**: they are not part of the 13-character speaking cast and
do not require character bibles in Season 1. They may be developed into full characters in a
later season if needed.

# Master Visual Style

```text
MASTER VISUAL STYLE

Pixar-quality preschool animation.

Soft clay + plush hybrid materials.

Rounded geometry everywhere.

Oversized proportions.

No realistic textures.

No tiny surface details.

Everything looks handcrafted.

Large readable silhouettes.

Big expressive eyes.

Soft matte finish.

Very subtle fabric texture.

Very subtle clay fingerprints.

No glossy plastic.

No metal reflections.

No photorealism.

Warm cozy atmosphere.

Friendly toy-like world.

Designed for children aged 3-5.

Every object should look touchable.

Nothing sharp.

Nothing dangerous.

Everything slightly oversized.

Everything visually readable from distance.
```

# Environment Motion Rules

```text
ENVIRONMENT MOTION

Nature:
  Flowers gently sway (20 frame cycle)
  Grass moves in waves (15 frame cycle)
  Leaves wiggle (10 frame cycle)
  Clouds drift slowly (100+ frame cycle)
  Water ripples slowly (30 frame cycle)

Creatures:
  Butterflies float (soft curved paths)
  Birds glide slowly (gentle arcs)
  Fish swim lazily (slow curves)

Atmosphere:
  Smoke curls gently (40 frame cycle)
  Fire flickers softly (8 frame cycle)
  Dust motes float (60 frame cycle)

Structures:
  Tree crowns bounce slightly (25 frame cycle)
  Flags wave gently (15 frame cycle)
  Wind chimes sway (20 frame cycle)

Rules:
  Nothing moves fast
  Nothing jerks
  Everything has gentle easing
  All motion is loopable
```
