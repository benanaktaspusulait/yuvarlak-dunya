# Episode Audio Workflow — PomPom Hills

---

## Core Audio Principle

> **Visual continuity makes the world believable. Audio continuity makes the episode feel whole.**

---

## 1. Music is Episode-Level, Not Shot-Level

Each shot video should not rely on its own separate music.

All shot videos will be edited together first.

Then one continuous background music track will be added across the full episode.

```
Shot 01 (no music)
Shot 02 (no music)
Shot 03 (no music)
Shot 04 (no music)
    ↓
Birleştir
    ↓
Tek sürekli müzik parçası ekle
```

---

## 2. OpenArt Video Audio

OpenArt-generated music should not be used as final episode music.

If OpenArt creates music inside individual shots, mute or remove it during editing.

OpenArt may be used for visual generation and possibly ambient reference only.

---

## 3. Ambient Sound

Ambient sounds may be added separately in editing:

- Crickets
- Soft wind
- Leaf rustle
- Gentle night ambience
- Birds
- Soft footsteps

Ambient sound should support the world without distracting from the story.

---

## 4. Music Style

Pompom Hills music should be:

- Soft
- Acoustic
- Low-stimulation
- Gentle
- Warm
- Memorable
- Safe for bedtime viewing

### Preferred Instruments

| Enstrüman | Kullanım |
|-----------|----------|
| Piano | Ana melodi, yumuşak anlar |
| Ukulele | Neşeli anlar |
| Glockenspiel | Sihirli anlar, kelebek, yıldız |
| Marimba | Ana melodi, oyuncak hissi |
| Soft strings | Duygusal anlar |
| Light hand percussion | Ritim, hafif |

### Avoid

```
✗ Electronic beats
✗ Loud drums
✗ Harsh synths
✗ Sudden hits
✗ Fast rhythm
✗ Aggressive bass
✗ Overly dramatic orchestration
```

---

## 5. Editing Workflow

Episode audio workflow:

```
1. Import all shot videos
2. Place shots in order
3. Mute original shot music if needed
4. Add one continuous music track across the full episode
5. Add ambient sound layer
6. Add dialogue or voiceover
7. Add small SFX only where needed
8. Fade music in at the beginning
9. Fade music out at the end
```

---

## 6. Audio Continuity Rule

The audience should never feel that music restarts between shots.

Music must flow continuously across the whole episode.

Shot changes should not create audio jumps.

---

## 7. Voice Identity Rule

The audience should never feel that a character's voice changes between shots.

For every speaking character, maintain:

- same voice identity
- same age impression
- same pitch
- same timbre
- same speaking speed
- same warmth
- same preschool energy
- same speaking rhythm
- same pronunciation
- same accent
- same recording quality

If the production system supports Voice Reference or Voice ID, use the same Voice ID for the same character in every shot. If Voice ID is not available, reuse the closest approved character voice reference and flag any drift in QA.

When a previous shot exists, treat the previous shot audio as the voice reference.

All speaking shots in one episode must sound as if they were recorded during the same recording session.

Do not generate a different narrator, alternate performer, older/younger version, accent shift, pronunciation shift, recording-quality shift, or different emotional delivery unless the story explicitly requires it.

## 8. Pompom Hills Original Music Library

### Planned Music Themes

| Parça | Kullanım | Tempo |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Main Theme** | Giriş/kapanış | 90-100 BPM |
| **Night Theme** | Gece sahneleri | 60-70 BPM |
| **Morning Theme** | Sabah sahneleri | 80-90 BPM |
| **Wonder Theme** | Keşif anları | 90-110 BPM |
| **Comfort Theme** | Güven veren anlar | 70-80 BPM |
| **Playful Theme** | Oyun sahneleri | 100-120 BPM |
| **Bedtime Theme** | Uyku sahneleri | 60-70 BPM |

### Kullanım Kuralları

- Her tema birden fazla bölümde tekrar kullanılır
- Marka kimliği oluşturmak için tema tanıdıklığı önemlidir
- Temalar arasında yumuşak geçiş yapılır
- Bir bölümde en fazla 2-3 tema kullanılır

---

## 9. Ses Seviyesi Standartları

| Ses Tipi | Seviye | Not |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Diyalog | -6 dB | Ana ses |
| Müzik | -12 dB | Arka plan |
| SFX | -10 dB | Tamamlayıcı |
| Ambiyans | -18 dB | Çok sessiz |
| **Toplam** | **-3 dB** | **Asla 0 dB'yi geçme** |

### Ani Ses Değişim Kuralları

- Fark: Maks. 6 dB (2 saniye içinde)
- Yükseliş: Yavaş, kademeli
- Düşüş: Yavaş, kademeli
- Kesme: Asla ani kesme
