# Pompom Hills - Visual Style Guide v2.1

---

## 1. STYLE OVERVIEW

### What Is Pompom Hills Style?
Pompom Hills uses a soft, rounded 3D cartoon style that looks like a living toy set. Everything is made of simple, rounded shapes with pastel colours and warm lighting. It is richer than Pocoyo (which is very minimal) but simpler than Disney (which is very detailed). The sweet spot is "soft toy come to life."

### Style Keywords
- Soft
- Rounded
- Warm
- Pastel
- Toy-like
- Gentle
- Simple
- Cosy
- Safe
- Friendly

---

## 2. SHAPE LANGUAGE

### Primary Shapes
- **Circles**: The dominant shape everywhere
- **Ovals**: For slightly elongated forms
- **Curves**: For paths, hills, branches
- **Rounded rectangles**: For very rare rectangular needs

### What Shapes Are Used For

| Element | Shape | Example |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Heads | Circle | Kiko, Mimi, Opa |
| Bodies | Circle/Oval | All characters |
| Eyes | Circle | All characters |
| Doors | Circle/Arch | All houses |
| Windows | Circle/Oval | All houses |
| Trees | Circle (top) | Pompom trees |
| Flowers | Circle (petals) | All flowers |
| Clouds | Circle | Sky elements |
| Sun | Circle | Sky element |
| Moon | Circle | Night sky |
| Stars | Circle | Night sky |
| Stepping stones | Circle | Paths |
| Hills | Curved | Landscape |

### What Shapes Are NEVER Used
- Avoid sharp triangles and hard squares
- If shapes are used for learning, they must be rounded, soft, oversized, and non-threatening
- No straight lines (rigid)
- No sharp points (dangerous)
- No angles (harsh)

### Why This Matters
- Circles feel safe and friendly
- Rounded shapes are toddler-friendly
- No sharp shapes means no visual danger
- Consistent shape language creates brand identity

## 3. COLOUR PHILOSOPHY

### Colour Feelings
- **Pink**: Warm, loving, Kiko's colour
- **Blue**: Calm, friendly, Mimi's colour
- **Green**: Natural, wise, Opa's colour
- **Yellow**: Happy, sunny, accent colour
- **White**: Clean, pure, breathing space
- **Orange**: Warm, energetic, accent colour
- **Purple**: Soft, magical, accent colour

### Colour Rules

**DO:**
- Use soft, muted pastels
- Use warm tones
- Use 3 dominant colours per frame, with soft supporting accent colours
- Leave white space
- Make characters stand out from background

**DON'T:**
- Use neon colours
- Use dark, saturated colours
- Use black backgrounds
- Use too many colours at once
- Use complex patterns

## 4. LIGHTING STYLE

### Daytime Lighting
- Warm, soft daylight
- No harsh shadows
- No strong directional light
- Everything evenly lit
- Gentle, not dramatic

### Shadow Style
- Very soft, subtle shadows
- Under objects only
- No cast shadows on faces
- No dramatic lighting
- Never dark or scary

### Night Lighting
- Soft, gentle glow
- Moon provides soft light
- Stars twinkle gently
- Everything still visible
- Never pitch black

### Lighting Colours
- Day: Warm yellow-white
- Sunset: Soft orange-pink
- Night: Soft blue-white
- Indoor: Warm yellow

## 5. COMPOSITION RULES

### Frame Composition

**Rule of Simplicity:**
- One clear focal point per frame
- Maximum 3 characters per frame
- Simple backgrounds
- Lots of breathing room
- No clutter

**Rule of Space:**
- Leave space around characters
- Don't crowd the frame
- Let characters breathe
- Background should not compete

**Rule of Balance:**
- Characters centred or slightly off-centre
- Visual weight balanced
- No heavy elements on one side
- Symmetrical when possible

### Camera Angles

**Eye Level:**
- Camera at character height
- Most common angle
- Creates intimacy
- Toddler-friendly perspective

**Slightly Above:**
- Camera slightly above characters
- Shows environment
- Used for establishing shots
- Still gentle, not dramatic

**Close-Up:**
- Character face fills frame
- Shows expression clearly
- Emotional moments
- Simple background

### Camera Movement

**DO:**
- Slow, gentle pans
- Soft, slow zooms
- Stable, steady shots
- Smooth transitions

**DON'T:**
- Fast pans
- Quick zooms
- Shaky camera
- Jump cuts
- Rapid editing

## 6. TEXTURE STYLE

### Surface Textures
- Soft, matte surfaces
- No realistic textures
- No wood grain
- No fabric weave
- No fur detail

### Toy-Like Quality
- Surfaces look like plush toys
- Soft, fuzzy edges
- No hard, shiny surfaces
- No metallic looks
- No glass effects

### Why This Matters
- Toddlers relate to toy-like objects
- Soft textures feel safe
- Easy to recreate with AI
- Consistent across episodes
- Brand-defining look

---

## 7. CHARACTER RENDERING

### Body Rendering
- Smooth, soft surfaces
- No muscle definition
- No bone structure visible
- Simple, rounded forms
- Plush toy appearance

### Face Rendering
- Large, simple eyes
- Small, simple mouths
- Rosy cheeks (on humans)
- No wrinkles, no lines
- Clear, readable expressions

### Hair/Feather/Fur Rendering
- Simple, solid shapes
- No individual strands
- No complex texture
- Soft, rounded forms
- Toy-like appearance

### Clothing Rendering
- Simple, solid colours
- No fabric texture
- No wrinkles, no folds
- Smooth, rounded forms
- Easy to identify

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENT RENDERING

### Hills
- Smooth, green surfaces
- No grass detail
- Soft, rounded forms
- Gentle gradients
- Toy landscape feel

### Trees
- Round, fluffy tops
- Thick, smooth trunks
- No bark texture
- No leaf detail
- Pompom appearance

### Houses
- Smooth, solid colours
- No wall texture
- Simple, rounded forms
- Toy house appearance
- No complex architecture

### Paths
- Smooth, round stones
- No surface texture
- Clear, simple pattern
- Easy to follow
- Toy path appearance

### Sky
- Smooth gradients
- No cloud detail (just round shapes)
- Soft, gentle colours
- No dramatic clouds
- Peaceful, simple

---

## 9. WHAT TO AVOID

### Sharp/Dangerous
- No sharp corners
- No pointed edges
- No angular shapes
- No weapon-like objects
- No dangerous tools

### Dark/Scary
- No dark scenes
- No scary shadows
- No horror elements
- No villains
- No fear-inducing imagery

### Realistic
- No realistic textures
- No realistic proportions
- No realistic lighting
- No realistic movement
- No photographic elements

### Complex
- No complex backgrounds
- No many details
- No busy compositions
- No complex patterns
- No visual clutter

### Modern/Urban
- No cars
- No buildings (except simple houses)
- No technology
- No city elements
- No industrial objects

### Loud/Aggressive
- No bright neon colours
- No harsh lighting
- No fast movements
- No loud expressions
- No aggressive poses

## 10. AI GENERATION GUIDELINES

### Base Style Prompt
Always start with:
"Soft 3D cartoon animation style, rounded shapes, pastel colours, warm daylight, gentle shadows, toy-like appearance, Pompom Hills world"

### Quality Tags
Always include:
"High quality, detailed, professional animation, children's show style, rounded 3D, soft lighting, warm tones"

### Avoid Tags
Always include:
"no sharp edges, no dark shadows, no realistic textures, no complex backgrounds, no scary elements, no harsh lighting, no neon colours, no busy compositions"

### Character Consistency
Always include character-specific:
- Exact colours (with hex codes)
- Key features (glasses, pigtails, ears)
- Outfit details
- Expression style
- Body shape (round, soft)

### World Consistency
Always include world-specific:
- Rounded shapes
- Pastel colours
- Warm lighting
- Soft textures
- Simple backgrounds

## 11. STYLE EXAMPLES

### Good Example
"Three characters in a soft 3D cartoon world: a girl with coral pink outfit and brown pigtails, a rabbit with soft blue fur and yellow t-shirt, and an owl with green feathers and golden glasses, standing on a soft green hill with round pompom trees, pastel colours, warm daylight, toy-like appearance, Pompom Hills world"

### Bad Example
"Three realistic characters standing on a hill with detailed trees, dramatic lighting, sharp shadows, complex background, vibrant colours, modern animation style"

### Why Good Example Works
- Specifies "soft 3D cartoon"
- Mentions "rounded shapes"
- Uses "pastel colours"
- Includes "warm daylight"
- Describes "toy-like appearance"
- Names the world "Pompom Hills"

### Why Bad Example Fails
- Uses "realistic" (wrong style)
- Mentions "dramatic lighting" (too intense)
- Includes "sharp shadows" (too harsh)
- Describes "complex background" (too busy)
- Uses "vibrant colours" (too bright)
- Says "modern animation style" (too generic)

## 12. BRAND IDENTITY

### Visual Signatures
1. **Pompom trees**: Round, fluffy tops
2. **Round doors**: Circle or arch shapes
3. **Stepping stones**: Round, colourful
4. **Spiral paths**: Curved, gentle
5. **Soft hills**: Rounded, green

### Colour Signatures
1. **Coral Pink**: Kiko, warmth
2. **Soft Blue**: Mimi, calm
3. **Warm Green**: Opa, wisdom
4. **Soft Yellow**: Accents, happiness
5. **White**: Breathing space

### Shape Signatures
1. **Circles everywhere**: Safety, friendliness
2. **No sharp corners**: Gentleness
3. **Rounded forms**: Toy-like quality
4. **Soft edges**: Warmth

### Feeling Signatures
1. **Safe**: No danger, no fear
2. **Warm**: Always comfortably lit
3. **Gentle**: Everything moves softly
4. **Cosy**: Small, comfortable spaces
5. **Friendly**: Everyone is kind

## 16. CAMERA SCALE RULE

Kamera hareket edebilir.

Dünya değişmez.

Pull-back, yeni bir establish shot demek değildir.

Kamera yalnızca mevcut alanın içinde hareket eder.

Yeni manzara oluşturulmaz.

Yeni ağaç oluşturulmaz.

Mekan yeniden yorumlanmaz.

Dünya zaten kurulmuştur.

O dünyanın içinde devam edilir.
