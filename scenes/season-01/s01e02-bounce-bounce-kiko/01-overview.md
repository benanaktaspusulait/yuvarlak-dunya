## Story Summary

Kiko wakes up, finds a yellow ball on Flower Hill. She bounces it, chases it, and meets Mimi at his burrow. Together they play in Butterfly Meadow surrounded by butterflies. The ball rolls away and they chase it to Central Square. Opa watches from his tree and says "Well done!"

---

## Ending

Kiko learns that games are more fun when shared.
