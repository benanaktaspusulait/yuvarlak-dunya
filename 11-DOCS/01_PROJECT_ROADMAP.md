# 01 — Project Roadmap

---

## Vision

Pompom Hills is a gentle preschool animation universe designed for children aged 3–4.

The goal is not simply to publish videos.

The goal is to slowly build a believable, reusable world with memorable characters, locations, and stories.

Every episode should strengthen the universe.

Every new asset should become reusable.

Every production decision should prioritize long-term efficiency.

---

## Mission

To create a safe, warm, and memorable animated world that children want to visit again and again — a world that grows with its audience and sustains a production pipeline for years.

---

## Creative Philosophy

- **Calm over chaos:** Low stimulation, slow pacing, gentle emotional arcs
- **Simplicity over complexity:** One emotional idea per episode
- **Safety over excitement:** No conflict, no villains, no danger
- **Warmth over spectacle:** Soft colors, rounded shapes, acoustic music
- **Discovery over instruction:** Children learn through observation, not teaching

---

## Production Philosophy

- **Sustainability over speed:** Build for 10 years, not 10 months
- **Quality over quantity:** Every episode meets the same standard
- **Reusable assets:** Every character, world, and prop supports dozens of stories
- **Pipeline first:** The system is the product; episodes are built on top
- **Lesson-driven improvement:** Learn from production, not from theory

---

## Growth Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundation

Establish the core universe and production pipeline.

- 1-minute episodes
- 4 active production worlds
- 6 active production characters
- 26 episodes planned

### Phase 2 — Expansion

Grow the world naturally while maintaining quality.

- Introduce 2–3 new worlds
- Expand to 8–10 characters
- Same episode format
- Increased production speed

### Phase 3 — Storytelling

Tell richer stories within the established format.

- Multi-beat emotional arcs
- Character-driven episodes
- Deeper world continuity
- Recurring themes

### Phase 4 — Premium

Create special episodes that celebrate the world.

- 3–5 minute episodes
- Season specials
- Holiday episodes
- Large ensemble stories

---

## Asset Strategy

- Characters are permanent — never retired
- Worlds are permanent — never deleted
- Every new asset must support 5+ future episodes
- Priority: core characters → core worlds → supporting characters → secondary worlds

---

## Success Criteria

| Milestone | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| Pipeline Established | 10 episodes at consistent quality |
| World Recognized | Audience identifies Pompom Hills from a single frame |
| Character Attached | Children request characters by name |
| Season Complete | 26 episodes produced |
| Brand Established | Merchandise potential verified |
| Universe Mature | 50+ episodes, 6+ worlds, 8+ characters |
