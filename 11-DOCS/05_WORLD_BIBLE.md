# 05 — World Bible

---

## Overview

Pompom Hills is a gentle preschool world where everything is round, safe, and encourages discovery.

---

## World Rules

- No sharp edges anywhere
- Every building is rounded
- Everyone helps everyone
- Children never travel alone after sunset
- Flowers bloom all year
- Magic is subtle
- Kindness is stronger than magic
