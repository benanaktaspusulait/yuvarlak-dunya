## Recommendations Before Artwork Production

### Immediate (Must Fix)

1. **Add "Spatial Zones" section** to all world bibles — define walkable areas, staging areas, and resting areas
2. **Expand story opportunities** to 10+ for all worlds currently under 8
3. **Fix Major Revision worlds** — Tillo's Treehouse, Lulu's Greenhouse, Friendship Meadow, Milo's Pond, Rainbow Bridge, Wish Pond need full rebuilds
4. **Remove excess props** from Hobby Horse Trail (2-3 props)
5. **Add clearing zone** to Little Forest for character staging

### Short-Term (Should Fix)

6. **Add entrance camera view** to all worlds missing it
7. **Define staging areas** for dialogue scenes in all community areas
8. **Add medium shot angle** to Camping Grove and Tree Hill
9. **Create Hero View** for Pony Meadow

### Long-Term (Nice to Have)

10. **Standardize story opportunity count** — target 12-15 per world
11. **Add seasonal/time-of-day variations** to key worlds
12. **Create prop density guidelines** for all worlds
