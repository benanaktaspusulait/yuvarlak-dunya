# 09 — YouTube Strategy

---

## Publishing Philosophy

- Quality over quantity
- Consistency over speed
- Evergreen content over trending
- Audience trust over algorithm chasing

---

## Long-form Strategy

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Duration | 60 seconds |
| Shots | 4 per episode |
| Frequency | 2–3 per week |
| Target | 3–4 years old |

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Percentage |
|--------|------------|
| Discovery | 60% |
| Comfort | 20% |
| Play | 15% |
| Special | 5% |

---

## Shorts Strategy

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Duration | 15–30 seconds |
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 |
| Frequency | 3–5 per week |

### Content Types

- Character intros
- Key moments
- Gentle loops
- Discovery clips

## Playlist Strategy

| Playlist | Purpose |
|----------|---------|

---

## Metadata Philosophy

- Title: Character + Action + Object + Emoji + "PomPom Hills"
- Description: Summary + learning + CTA
- Tags: 15–20 terms

---

## Thumbnail Philosophy

- 1280×720
- One happy face
- Clear action
- Warm colours

---

## Evergreen Strategy

- Bedtime routines
- Nature discovery
- Friendship, sharing
- Colours, shapes, feelings
- Seasonal themes
