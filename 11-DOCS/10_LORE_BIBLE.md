# 10 — Lore Bible

---

## World Rules

### Physical Rules

- Everything is round
- No sharp edges exist anywhere
- Every building is rounded
- Every tree can be climbed safely
- Gravity is soft
- Objects bounce slightly
- Nothing breaks
- Nothing explodes

### Social Rules

- Nobody owns the forest
- Nobody locks their doors
- Everyone helps everyone
- Children never travel alone after sunset
- Opa always knows where everyone is
- Everyone is kind
- No one is excluded
- Sharing is natural

### Nature Rules

- Flowers bloom all year
- Rainbows appear often
- Magic is subtle
- Water is always clean
- Trees are always healthy
- Nothing is dirty
- Nothing is broken

---

## Timeline

Pompom Hills exists in a timeless present.

There is no calendar, no clocks, no deadlines.

Events happen when they feel right.

Seasons change gently, but time is not measured.

## Character Consistency Rules

- Characters never age
- Characters never change appearance
- Characters never learn negative behaviors
- Characters always remain kind
- Characters always remain gentle
- Characters always remain safe

- Character personalities

- The safety of the world
