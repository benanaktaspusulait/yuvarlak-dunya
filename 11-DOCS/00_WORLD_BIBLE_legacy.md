# Pompom Hills — World Bible

---

## Overview

Pompom Hills is a gentle preschool world where kindness, curiosity, and imagination shape everyday life.

Every location is reached on foot through soft winding paths.

Everything is round. Everything feels safe. Everything encourages discovery.

## World Rules

- Nobody owns the forest
- Nobody locks their doors
- Everyone helps everyone
- Children never travel alone after sunset
- Opa always knows where everyone is
- Every location has at least two walking paths
- No sharp edges exist anywhere
- Every building is rounded
- Every tree can be climbed safely
- Flowers bloom all year
- Rainbows appear often
- Magic is subtle
- Kindness is stronger than magic

---

## World Consistency Rules

- Worlds are permanent assets — never deleted or redesigned
- Each world maintains the same visual identity across all episodes
- New worlds are introduced only after the pipeline is proven stable
- Worlds support at least 5 future episodes before being created
