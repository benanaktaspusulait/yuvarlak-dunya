# Pompom Hills World Bible
Version 2.1

---

# Overview

Pompom Hills is a peaceful preschool fantasy world where kindness, curiosity, imagination and friendship shape everyday life.

There are no cities.
No roads.
No cars.
No danger.

Every location can be reached on foot through soft winding paths.

Everything is round.
Everything feels safe.
Everything encourages discovery.

The Big Pompom Tree stands at the center of the world.

Every path eventually leads back to it.

# Main Locations

## 1. Central Square

The heart of Pompom Hills.

The Big Pompom Tree grows here.

Everyone meets here every morning.

Events, celebrations, games and festivals happen here.

Connected to:

- Sun Hill
- Flower Hill
- Cloud Hill
- Tree Hill
- Stone Hill

Walking time:

1–3 minutes to any nearby location.

---

## 2. Cloud Hill

Highest hill.

Closest place to the clouds.

Soft breeze all year.

Rainbow often begins here.

Connected to:

- Adventure Trail
- Rainbow Bridge
- Tree Hill

Special:

Perfect for watching sunsets.

## 3. Flower Hill

Most colorful hill.

# World Rules

Nobody owns the forest.

Nobody locks their doors.

Everyone helps everyone.

Children never travel alone after sunset.

Opa always knows where everyone is.

Every location has at least two walking paths.

No sharp edges exist anywhere.

Every building is rounded.

Every tree can be climbed safely.

Flowers bloom all year.

Rainbows appear often.

Magic is subtle.

Kindness is stronger than magic.

---

# Story Logic

## Morning

Everyone gathers near Central Square.

## Afternoon

Children split into adventures.

## Evening

Everyone returns to Story Circle.

## Night

Opa tells a story.

Children walk home under the stars.

This daily rhythm keeps every story connected naturally.

## Ambient Owner-Names (not speaking cast)

Some locations carry an owner-style name for warmth and flavour — **Rosie's Rose Garden,
Milo's Pond, Lily's Lavender Farm, Poppy's Bakery, Benny's Playground, Lulu's Greenhouse**.
Except where an owner is also a member of the main cast (e.g., **Tillo**), these names are
**ambient/background residents only**: they are not part of the 13-character speaking cast and
do not require character bibles in Season 1. They may be developed into full characters in a
later season if needed.
