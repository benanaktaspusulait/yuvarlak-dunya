# Mimo - Voice Prompt

```text
Create a voice for Mimo from Pompom Hills v2.1.

CRITICAL: This must sound like a REAL small child, NOT an adult, NOT a teen. The voice must be genuinely small, high, and childlike — like a real 4-year-old speaking.

Real 4-year-old kind young turtle, slow problem solver, patient nature friend.

Tone:
Small, high, soft, round. A real child's voice — not mature, not polished. Think: actual little kid, short vocal cords, small mouth, genuine child sound.

Performance:
Simple, direct, patient. Short words. Slight softness typical of a 4-year-old. No adult articulation. No professional voice actor quality. Raw, real, small child delivery.

Pace:
70-85 words per minute. A child speaks slower than an adult. Allow small pauses between thoughts.

Pitch:
HIGH child voice, 360-440 Hz. This is NOT an adult pitch. This is a small child. Higher than you think. Tiny voice.

Emotion range:
Calm, patient, curious, happy. Simple emotions only. No adult sass, no maturity, no sophistication.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"Let's take it slow and steady!"

Test lines:
1. "Let's take it slow and steady!"
2. "One little step."
3. "Slow is okay."

WHAT THIS VOICE IS NOT:
- NOT an adult
- NOT a teen
- NOT a voice actor doing "cute"

WHAT THIS VOICE IS:
- A real small child
- High pitch
- Simple words
```
