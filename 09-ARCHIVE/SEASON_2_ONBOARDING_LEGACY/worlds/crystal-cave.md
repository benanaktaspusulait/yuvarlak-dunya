## World Description

A small, hidden cave filled with soft glowing crystals.

The crystals glow in gentle pastel colours — pink, blue, lavender.

The cave is not scary — it is magical.

The walls shimmer. The floor is smooth stone. The air feels cool and quiet.

---

## Visual Identity

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Entrance | Small, round, partially hidden by flowers |
| Walls | Smooth stone with embedded crystals |
| Crystals | Soft pastel glow — pink, blue, lavender |
| Floor | Smooth, flat, safe for walking |
| Light | Soft crystal glow, no harsh shadows |
| Sound | Quiet hum, soft echo |

---

## Hero View Concept

Wide shot of cave entrance from outside. Flowers frame the opening. Soft crystal glow visible from within. Warm light on the stone.

---

## World Introduction Video Concept

### Title: "Welcome to Crystal Cave"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Stone Hill, flowers, a hidden opening | Soft wind |
| 5–10 sec | Camera moves toward the entrance | Quiet, mysterious |
| 10–15 sec | Inside the cave — crystals glow softly | Soft hum |
| 15–20 sec | Camera pans across crystal wall | Gentle shimmer |
| 20–25 sec | A crystal pulses with light | Soft chime |
| 25–30 sec | Camera pulls back to show the full cave | Warm, magical |
| 30–35 sec | Luca enters, looks up in wonder | Quiet awe |
| 35–40 sec | Luca touches a crystal — it glows brighter | Soft chime |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Crystal Cave" | Magical jingle |

**Key Moment:** The crystal glow when Luca touches it — this becomes the cave's signature.

## First Story Use

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Episode | Season 2, Episode 6 |
| Title | "The Crystal Cave" |
| Characters | Luca, Kiko |
| World Role | Main location |

**Story beat:** Luca finds a hidden cave. He calls Kiko. They enter together. The crystals glow. They touch one — it glows brighter. They sit quietly, watching the light.
