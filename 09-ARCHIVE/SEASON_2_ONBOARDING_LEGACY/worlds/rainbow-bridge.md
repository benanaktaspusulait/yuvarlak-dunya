## World Description

A gentle stone bridge that glows with soft rainbow colours.

When the sun shines after rain, the bridge shimmers with pink, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple light.

The bridge is safe and wide. Flowers grow along the edges. The air feels warm and hopeful.

## World Introduction Video Concept

### Title: "Welcome to Rainbow Bridge"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Flowers, soft light, a bridge appears | Gentle wind |
| 5–10 sec | Camera moves toward the bridge | Warm, hopeful |
| 10–15 sec | The bridge glows with rainbow light | Soft chime |
| 15–20 sec | Camera pans across the bridge | Gentle shimmer |
| 20–25 sec | Kiko steps onto the bridge | Soft footsteps |
| 25–30 sec | Mimi joins, they walk together | Warm, friendly |
| 30–35 sec | They stop, look at the rainbow glow | Quiet wonder |
| 35–40 sec | They make a wish | Soft chime |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Rainbow Bridge" | Magical jingle |

**Key Moment:** The rainbow glow when they make a wish — this becomes the bridge's signature.
