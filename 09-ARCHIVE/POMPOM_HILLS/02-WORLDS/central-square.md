# Central Square — World Bible

---

## Purpose

Central Square, PomPom Hills'in kalbi.

Herkes burada buluşur.

Tüm yollar buraya çıkar.

---

## Visual Elements

- Giant Pompom Tree (center)
- Round colorful stepping stones in circle
- Soft green grass
- Round benches
- Warm lamp posts
- Pastel dome houses in background
- Flower gardens
- Winding paths

---

## Camera Rules

- Environment first, character second
- 35mm lens
- Eye level
- Wide compositions

---

## Forbidden

```
✗ Sharp edges
✗ Dark lighting
✗ Realistic textures
✗ City elements
✗ Cars
✗ Technology
```

---

## Consistency Checklist

```
✓ Big Pompom Tree in center
```
