# Brave Little Days EP03 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** shy body language at first (hugging herself, small steps, peeking), turning to
  curious wonder when she hears her heartbeat, then proud at the height chart and relieved,
  beaming with the sticker.
- **Mimi:** gentle, reassuring — takes Kiko's hand, stays close by the mat, happy ear-wiggles.
- **Remi:** big, slow, soft, calm — gentle deliberate movements, warm smile, a reassuring
  "big soft" presence. Never sudden, never looming despite being large.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

shy/nervous (01–03) → warm welcome (04) → curious wonder (05) → proud growing-up (06) →
relief + pride (07) → confident, happy (08).

## Safe-Exposure Motion Rules

- Remi never forces or restrains; Kiko sits freely, Mimi beside her.
- Only gentle actions: soft listening heart on the chest, a soft warm ear light, standing at a
  height chart. **No needle, no shot, no sharp instrument.**
- No pain, no crying, no panic. The tone stays warm and calm throughout.
