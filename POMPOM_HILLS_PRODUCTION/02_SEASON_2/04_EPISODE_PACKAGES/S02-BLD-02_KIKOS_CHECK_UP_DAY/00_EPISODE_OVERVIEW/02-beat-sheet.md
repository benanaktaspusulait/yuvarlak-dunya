# Brave Little Days EP03 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm (6-Step Spine over 8 Shots)

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01) — PROBLEM
Central Square, check-up day. Kiko and Mimi head to the Care Corner; Kiko is nervous.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02) — FEELING
Kiko: "I feel shy." She hesitates.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03) — SUPPORT
Mimi: "It's okay to feel shy. I'll stay with you." A small brave step.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04) — ACTION (arrival)
Remi the Care Helper greets warmly: "I'll be very gentle."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05) — ACTION (listen to heart)
Remi listens with the soft listening heart: "Thump, thump!" Kiko listens too.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06) — ACTION (ears + height)
A gentle ear light and a height check: "Look how big you've grown!"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07) — RELIEF
"All strong and healthy!" A star sticker. Kiko: "That wasn't scary!"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08) — LESSON (warm close)
Together: "It's okay to feel shy. Helpers keep us strong and healthy."
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Nervous/shy → naming the feeling → a friend's support → a warm welcome → curious wonder
(heartbeat) → proud growing-up moment → relief + reward → warm, confident close.
Shown through action, never preached.

---

## Series Continuity

Second "helper" episode after EP01 (Nella, Tooth Helper). Same gentle-helper pattern; new
Care Helper Remi. Optional callback line possible but not required.

## Safe-Exposure Anchors (per `00-CORE/32_..._FORMAT.md` §4)

- **No needles, no shots, no vaccination.** Only: listen to heart, gentle ear look with a soft
  warm light, height measure, star sticker.
- No sharp tools, no mask/gloves, no bright surgical lamp, no clinical white.
- No crying, panic, or restraint. Kiko is always free and supported by Mimi.
- The Care Corner is cozy, rounded, soft blue & cream — a warm nook, not a clinic.
