# Brave Little Days EP03 — Coordinate Map

Scale reference: **Kiko = 100 units** (canon height baseline).

## Character Scale

| Character | Height (units) | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Kiko | 100 | Baseline (3–4 yr child) |
| Mimi | ~70 | Small rabbit |
| Remi | ~135 | Big, soft grown-up bear helper; large but round and gentle, never looming |

Remi is the largest character in the series so far — this is intentional (a big, safe,
huggable presence). Keep him soft, rounded, and low/leaning toward the child, never towering
or intimidating.

  height chart, Kiko on the mat, Mimi beside Kiko (screen-left). Layout identical across 04–08.
