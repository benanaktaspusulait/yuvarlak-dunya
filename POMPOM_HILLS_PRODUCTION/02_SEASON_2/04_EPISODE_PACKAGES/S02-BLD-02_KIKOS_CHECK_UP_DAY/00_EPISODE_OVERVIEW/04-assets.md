## New Set-Piece — Care Corner

A cozy, rounded nook at the edge of Central Square (a sibling to the Tooth Helper Corner), not a clinic:

- Soft blue-and-cream rounded awning / small tent
- One cushioned round mat / low soft bench
- A friendly height chart on a soft board
- A soft "listening heart" (rounded stethoscope), star stickers, potted pastel flowers
- Warm morning light, dappled tree-shadow

**Forbidden in this set:** needles, syringes, sharp tools, bright surgical lamp, metal trays,
clinical white. See `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md` §4.
