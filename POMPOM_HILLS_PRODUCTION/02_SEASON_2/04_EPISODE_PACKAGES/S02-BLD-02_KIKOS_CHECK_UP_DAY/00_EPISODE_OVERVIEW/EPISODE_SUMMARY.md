# Brave Little Days EP03 — "Kiko's Check-Up Day" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Central Square — Care Corner (optional set-piece)
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi, Remi (Care Helper)
**Theme:** It's okay to feel shy; a check-up is gentle and helpers keep us strong and healthy.

It's check-up day in Central Square and Kiko feels shy about it. Mimi holds her hand — "It's
okay to feel shy. I'll stay with you." — and they visit the cozy Care Corner. Remi the big,
gentle bear welcomes them: "I'll be very gentle." He lets Kiko listen to her own heartbeat
("Thump, thump!"), gently checks her ears with a soft light, and measures how tall she's grown.
"All strong and healthy!" — and a star sticker. Kiko beams: "That wasn't scary!" The episode
closes warm: it's okay to feel shy, and helpers keep us strong and healthy.

**Repeated phrase:** "It's okay to feel shy."
**Learning point:** Feeling shy is normal; check-ups are gentle and helpers keep us healthy.
**Safe exposure:** No needles/shots — only heart-listen, gentle ear look, height, sticker.
