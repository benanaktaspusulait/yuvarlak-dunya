## Story Summary

Kiko is exploring Little Forest when she spots a quiet cat sitting perfectly still under a tree. Kiko sits nearby, not too close, giving the cat space. The cat glances at her, then stands and walks slowly down a sunlit path. Kiko follows at a gentle distance. The cat leads her through ferns and past mossy stones to a cozy spot — a soft, warm hollow at the base of an old tree, covered in soft leaves and bathed in golden light. The cat settles into the spot, purring softly. Kiko sits beside it. The cat leans in and nuzzles Kiko's hand. Kiko smiles — she followed a quiet friend and discovered a cozy secret place and a warm connection.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

The cat nuzzling Kiko's hand in the cozy hollow — the moment quiet trust becomes warm connection, and Kiko realizes that following gently and being patient leads to something beautiful.

## Repeated Phrase

```text
Follow quietly...
```

(Kiko whispers it in Shot 04 when she starts following, Mirnik's purring is the "answer" in Shot 07.)

## Learning Point

Some friends don't need loud words to show they care. When you follow gently and give space, a quiet friend might lead you somewhere warm and wonderful.

## Colour Notes

```text
Warm forest palette. Soft greens, golden light, earthy browns.
Identical white balance, exposure, colour temperature, saturation, contrast across all shots.
No cool shift. No desaturated look. No green tint. No magenta tint.
The cozy hollow must have a warm golden glow that stands out from the rest of the forest.
```

---

## Lighting Notes

```text
Soft, dappled forest light. Sunbeams through trees.
Identical light direction, intensity, shadow softness across all shots.
The cozy hollow should feel like a warm pocket of golden light.
No harsh shadows. No dramatic lighting shifts.
```
