# Brave Little Days EP01 — Coordinate Map

Scale reference: **Kiko = 100 units** (canon height baseline).

## Character Scale

| Character | Height (units) | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Kiko | 100 | Baseline (3–4 yr child) |
| Mimi | ~70 | Small rabbit, shorter than Kiko |
| Nella | ~120 | Gentle grown-up helper; taller than Kiko but soft/round, never looming |

Nella must read as a warm, approachable helper — taller than the children but with soft,
rounded proportions and a gentle posture (slight lean-in), never towering or intimidating.

## Staging

- **Shots 01–04 (Central Square open grass):** Kiko and Mimi near the Big Pompom Tree;
  tree centred/background. Tooth Helper Corner visible at square edge from Shot 03.
- **Shots 05–08 (Tooth Helper Corner):** smile chair camera-left, Nella camera-right by the
  shelf, Kiko in the chair, Mimi beside the chair (screen-left of Kiko). Layout stays
  identical across 05–08.

## Continuity

- Big Pompom Tree position stable in all square shots.
- Tooth Helper Corner set-piece identical once established (Shot 05 onward).
- Apple prop consistent in Shots 01–02.
