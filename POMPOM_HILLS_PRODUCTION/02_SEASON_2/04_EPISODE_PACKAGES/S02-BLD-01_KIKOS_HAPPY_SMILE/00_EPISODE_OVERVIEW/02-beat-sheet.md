# Brave Little Days EP01 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm (6-Step Spine over 8 Shots)

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01) — PROBLEM
Kiko happily bites a crunchy apple; her tooth suddenly feels "funny."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02) — FEELING
Kiko touches her cheek, a little worried. Mimi notices.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03) — FEELING / FEAR
Kiko sees the Tooth Helper Corner and hesitates: "I don't want to go."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04) — SUPPORT
Mimi takes her hand: "It's okay to feel scared. I'll come with you."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05) — ACTION (arrival)
They reach the warm corner; Nella greets them gently.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06) — ACTION (gentle check)
Kiko sits in the soft smile chair; Nella slowly counts her smile.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07) — RELIEF
"All happy and healthy!" Kiko relaxes: "That wasn't so scary!"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08) — LESSON (warm close)
Together and smiling: "It's okay to feel scared. Helpers help us feel better."
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Happy play → tooth feels funny → gentle worry → fear of going → a friend's support →
a brave little step → a kind, gentle check → relief → a happy, proud smile.

The lesson is shown through action, never explained. No preaching.

---

## Safe-Exposure Anchors (per `00-CORE/32_..._FORMAT.md` §4)

- Discomfort stays mild ("funny"), never painful or dramatic.
- No needle, drill, sharp tool, mask, gloves, surgical lamp, or clinical white.
- No crying, panic, or restraint. Kiko is always free and supported.
- The Tooth Helper Corner is cozy, rounded, mint & cream — a warm nook, not a clinic.
