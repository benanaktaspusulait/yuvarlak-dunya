# Share and Play — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Amara are at Benny's Playground. A soft purple ball rolls out of the ball pit. Kiko notices it with curiosity. Amara watches with interest.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko picks up the ball and holds it close: "It's mine." Amara's smile fades slightly. The sharing problem is established.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Amara steps closer and asks gently: "Can I play too?" Kiko pauses, really seeing Amara for the first time.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko makes the first sharing move — she lowers the ball to the grass between them: "You can play too." Amara's smile returns.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Both characters kneel and roll the ball together in a gentle circle on the grass. Cooperative play begins.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): They continue rolling. Kiko looks at Amara and says: "Sharing is more fun." Amara agrees. Pure joy.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Kiko pushes the ball toward Amara, then they bring it back together. Kiko feels proud — sharing feels right.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): They hold the ball together and wave to the camera: "Sharing is more fun!" Warm closing.
```
