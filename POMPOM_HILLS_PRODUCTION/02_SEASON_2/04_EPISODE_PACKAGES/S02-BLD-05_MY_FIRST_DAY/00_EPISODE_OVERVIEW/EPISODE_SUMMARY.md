# Brave Little Days EP17: My First Day — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning, Kiko arrives at the Learning Room for the first time with Opa. At the
doorway, Amara waves softly from inside. Kiko hesitates and holds Opa's wing-tip: "Stay?"
Opa kneels to her level and says: "I'll be back soon." Kiko takes a small step inside. Amara
shows her large round crayons; they draw together. Opa returns as promised: "I came back."
Kiko's joy is warm and genuine. The Learning Room is now a trusted space.

## Learning

Separation anxiety • Trust • Keeping promises

## Characters

Kiko, Opa, Amara
