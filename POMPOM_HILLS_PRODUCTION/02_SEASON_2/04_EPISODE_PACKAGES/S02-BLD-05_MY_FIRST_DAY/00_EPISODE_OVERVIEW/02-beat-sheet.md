# My First Day — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Opa arrive at the Learning Room doorway. The warm cottage interior is visible — LR-TABLE-01, LR-BOARD-01. Amara sits at the table and gives a soft wave. Kiko looks inside with curiosity and mild hesitation.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko reaches for and holds Opa's wing-tip. She looks up and asks softly: "Stay?" A brief, natural moment of wanting a trusted person nearby.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Opa slowly kneels to Kiko's eye level. Their eyes meet. Opa says warmly: "I'll be back soon." Kiko's face softens from hesitation to quiet trust.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko glances back at Opa one last time, then takes a small step into the Learning Room. Amara makes space at the table. The physical commitment to staying.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Amara rolls large round crayons toward Kiko across the table. "These are fun!" Kiko's curiosity grows: "What colors?" Hesitation fades into engagement.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Kiko and Amara draw together on the same paper — Kiko draws a circle, Amara draws a square. Friendship forms through shared creation.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa reappears at the doorway, calm and smiling. Kiko's face lights up: "You came back!" Opa: "I came back." The promise was kept.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Kiko stands between Opa and Amara. She echoes: "I'll be back soon." Warm closing — the Learning Room is now a trusted space.
```
