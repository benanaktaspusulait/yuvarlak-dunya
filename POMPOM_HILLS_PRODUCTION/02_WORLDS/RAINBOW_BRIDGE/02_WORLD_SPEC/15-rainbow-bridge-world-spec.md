# Rainbow Bridge — World Creation Specification

> Source of truth: `15-rainbow-bridge-bible.md` (canonical, creative). This document
> is a production-oriented extraction for AI-assisted world generation. It does
> not replace the Bible and does not duplicate its storytelling, educational,
> character, or workflow content.

---

## Purpose

Rainbow Bridge is a magical passage between Cloud Hill and Rainbow Creek — a rounded colourful bridge where the rainbow touches down after rain. It is the most magical transition point in Pompom Hills, representing hope, wishes, and imagination through colour and sparkle.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Rainbow Bridge and must never change:

- Rounded colourful bridge spanning between hills
- Rainbow in the sky touching the bridge
- Six rainbow colours: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple
- Smooth wishing stones along the bridge
- Water flowing beneath
- Sparkle and magical light effects
- No broken bridge, no sharp stones, no storm
- Safe, rounded railings
- Clean, hopeful atmosphere after rain

---

## Visual Identity

A rounded colourful bridge connecting two green hills, with a vibrant rainbow arching in the sky above and touching the bridge. Smooth wishing stones line the bridge edges, each a different pastel colour. Water flows gently beneath. Sparkle effects drift through the air. The overall feeling is magical and hopeful — a place where wishes come true and every colour tells a story.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous crossing. Cloud Hill on one side leads to the bridge, which spans over Rainbow Creek below, and arrives at the other side. The rainbow arches overhead throughout the entire journey. There are no separate structures — just one flowing magical passage from hill to hill.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- Cloud Hill connects naturally to the bridge entrance.
- The bridge spans continuously over the creek.
- The rainbow is always visible overhead.
- The wishing stones are always along the bridge edges.
- The water is always visible below.
- The sparkle effects are distributed throughout.

---

## Playable Areas

**Bridge Entrance (Cloud Hill side)** — Purpose: the beginning of the magical crossing. Visual identity: rounded bridge start, rainbow colours beginning, first wishing stones. Important props: bridge start, rainbow. Typical use: arrival, first colour discovery.

**Bridge Middle** — Purpose: the heart of the crossing — highest point over the water. Visual identity: full rainbow overhead, water below, sparkle effects. Important props: bridge, rainbow, water. Typical use: crossing, looking down, colour counting.

**Bridge Exit (Rainbow Creek side)** — Purpose: arrival and wish-making. Visual identity: bridge end, wishing stone collection, calm water. Important props: wishing stones, water edge. Typical use: wishing, resting, reflection.

**Wishing Stones Area** — Purpose: the ritual of wishing. Visual identity: smooth colourful stones arranged along the bridge edge. Important props: wishing stones. Typical use: stone selection, wish-making.

---

## Materials

Bridge: smooth, rounded, colourful surface with soft matte finish. Wishing stones: polished smooth, each a different pastel colour. Water: clear, gentle flow. Sparkle: thin, natural light effects. All surfaces read as soft, magical, and touchable. No sharp edges, no rough surfaces, no dark materials.

---

## Lighting

Primary light comes from the rainbow itself — a natural, colourful glow that illuminates the bridge. Additional sparkle effects create magical light patterns. The overall lighting is warm, colourful, and dreamlike. Shadows are very soft and tinted with rainbow colours.

## Scale

Everything is sized for small preschool children. The bridge is low and wide, with secure railings. The wishing stones are small and smooth, easy to pick up. The crossing feels safe and manageable — not high, not long, not scary. The rainbow above feels close enough to touch.

---

## Atmosphere

Magical. Hopeful. Colourful. Sparkling. Clean. Every surface reads as smooth and enchanting. The mood is consistently bright and optimistic — a place where children feel "anything is possible here."

---

## Consistency Rules

- The bridge is always rounded and colourful — never straight, never grey.
- The rainbow is always visible — never hidden, never faded.
- Wishing stones are always smooth and colourful — never sharp, never dull.
- Water is always clear and flowing — never stagnant, never dark.
- No broken structures, no sharp edges, no dark atmosphere.
- The colour palette stays vibrant and rainbow-spectrum throughout.

---

## Generation Considerations

The bridge must be generated as a magical, colourful crossing — not as a普通 stone bridge or concrete structure. Keep all shapes rounded. Keep colours vibrant and rainbow-spectrum. The bridge's identity — its magic, its hope, its colour — must be preserved exactly across every generation.

## Navigation

Default flow: Cloud Hill (approach) → Bridge Entrance → Bridge Middle → Bridge Exit → Rainbow Creek.
The crossing journey — the colour immersion — should feel magical and unhurried.
**Alternative openings are permitted when story continuity requires them.**

## Generation Failures — Quick Reference

| Failure | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Rainbow not visible | Add rainbow — it is the identity |
| Bridge not colourful | Add rainbow spectrum colours |
| Sharp stones present | Smooth all wishing stones |
| Dark or stormy atmosphere | Clear to hopeful, post-rain |
| Broken bridge structure | Repair — bridge must be whole |
| Muted or dull colours | Increase saturation, add vibrancy |
| No sparkle effects | Add magical sparkle |
| Sharp edges anywhere | Smooth all forms |

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Rainbow Bridge must never be reduced to "a colourful bridge."

This layer protects Rainbow Bridge from becoming too plain. Keep the
six-colour rainbow arching overhead and touching the bridge always visible
and readable — add only the canon-approved charm details below. Do not add
random objects. Do not remove the magical wish-crossing emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** RB-RAINBOW-01, the six-colour rainbow arching
overhead and touching the bridge, is the primary landmark and must be
visible in every wide shot. RB-BRIDGE-01, the rounded colourful bridge
surface, and the smooth pastel RB-STONES-01 wishing stones lined along the
edges, plus RB-RAILING-01, the colourful rounded railings, add landmark and
small-prop charm. RB-SPARKLE-01, the drifting magical sparkle effects, and
RB-WATER-01, the gentle flowing water beneath, keep the world feeling alive
with motion charm.

**Forbidden clutter:** darkness or storm atmosphere, broken or damaged
bridge structure, sharp or rough stones, dull or desaturated colours, sharp
corners anywhere, and fast or jarring movement.

### Hero View Quality Tests

Before accepting a Hero View, it must pass:

1. **Silhouette Test** — recognisable as Rainbow Bridge from the rounded bridge shape and rainbow arc alone?
2. **Colour Test** — recognisable from the six-colour rainbow spectrum and pastel wishing stones alone?
3. **Charm Test** — does it make a child want to cross the bridge and make a wish?

If any test fails, reject.
