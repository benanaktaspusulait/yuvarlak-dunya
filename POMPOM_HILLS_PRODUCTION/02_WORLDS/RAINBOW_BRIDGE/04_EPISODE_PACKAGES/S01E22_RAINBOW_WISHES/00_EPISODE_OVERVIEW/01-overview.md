## Story Summary

Kiko and Mimi stand at Rainbow Bridge after rain. They see the rainbow and each make a secret wish. Kiko whispers "more adventures" and Mimi whispers "more friends." As they wish together, the rainbow shimmers and changes — new colours appear that weren't there before. They hold hands and imagine a world made of rainbows. Their shared imagination makes the bridge glow with magical warmth. They smile, knowing their wishes feel stronger when shared.
