# Rainbow Bridge — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A beautiful arched bridge spanning a gentle stream, painted in six soft rainbow colours — red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple — each colour a gentle pastel shade. The bridge arch is smooth and rounded. Colourful wishing stones sit at each end. Soft sparkles shimmer in the air around the bridge. Warm golden sunlight, gentle mist from the water below. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, magical and hopeful atmosphere. No harsh colours, no realistic textures, no dark shadows, no modern structures.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the bridge from the stream bank
- Image 2 (back): view from the other side
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the arch and colours
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of wishing stones

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and rainbow palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
