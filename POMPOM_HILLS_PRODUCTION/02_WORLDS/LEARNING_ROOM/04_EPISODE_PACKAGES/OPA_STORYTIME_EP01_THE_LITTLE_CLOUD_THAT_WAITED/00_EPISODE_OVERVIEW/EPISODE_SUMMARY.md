# Opa's Storytime: The Little Cloud That Waited — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a quiet story-time day, Luca and Mimi sit with Opa in the warm Learning Room
Reading Corner. Opa opens a picture book and tells a gentle story about a little
cloud that wanted to rain right away, but learned to wait until the flowers were
ready. Luca and Mimi ask questions and react throughout the story.

## Learning

Patience • Listening • Gentle helping

## Characters

Opa, Luca, Mimi
