# The Little Cloud That Waited — Beat Sheet v2.0 (5-Minute Version)

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Mimi arrive at the Reading Corner, sit near Opa. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa opens the book. Children lean in with curiosity.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): "Once there was a little cloud." Luca asks if it was tiny.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Cloud had a big wish — to help the flowers below.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Cloud sees flowers waiting. Mimi asks if they were thirsty.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Cloud wants to rain right away. Opa pauses.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Wind whispers "Wait." Luca asks why.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Cloud feels unsure but keeps watching the flowers.
Beat 9 (Shot 09): Flowers slowly begin to wake. Luca notices.
Beat 10 (Shot 10): "They were not ready." Opa explains gently.
Beat 11 (Shot 11): Cloud waits one more moment. Mimi wonders what happens next.
Beat 12 (Shot 12): The sky becomes quiet. The right moment is coming.
Beat 13 (Shot 13): One gentle drop falls from the cloud. Luca watches.
Beat 14 (Shot 14): "And the flowers opened?" Mimi asks with hope.
Beat 15 (Shot 15): Yes — the flowers open softly, one by one.
Beat 16 (Shot 16): "Sometimes waiting helps us help better." Opa smiles.
Beat 17 (Shot 17): Luca reflects quietly on the story.
Beat 18 (Shot 18): "I can wait too." Micro-moment.
Beat 19 (Shot 19): Opa softly: "That is a kind thing."
Beat 20 (Shot 20): Quiet warm hold. Book closes gently. No new dialogue.
```
