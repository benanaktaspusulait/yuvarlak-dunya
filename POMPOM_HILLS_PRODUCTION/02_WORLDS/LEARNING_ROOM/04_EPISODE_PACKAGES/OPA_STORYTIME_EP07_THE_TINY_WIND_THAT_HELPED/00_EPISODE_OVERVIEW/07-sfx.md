Forbidden: loud music, sudden stingers, aggressive sound design, strong wind, gusts,
storm/thunder sounds, whistling wind (per `00-CORE/31_OPA_INTERACTIVE_STORYTELLING_FORMAT.md` §11
and `00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11). The breeze must always sound
soft, warm and safe.
