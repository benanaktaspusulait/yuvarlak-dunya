Forbidden: loud music, sudden stingers, aggressive sound design, strong wind/storm/gust
sounds (per `00-CORE/31_OPA_INTERACTIVE_STORYTELLING_FORMAT.md` §11 and
`00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11). The breeze must stay gentle and
cozy — never a whistling or howling wind.
