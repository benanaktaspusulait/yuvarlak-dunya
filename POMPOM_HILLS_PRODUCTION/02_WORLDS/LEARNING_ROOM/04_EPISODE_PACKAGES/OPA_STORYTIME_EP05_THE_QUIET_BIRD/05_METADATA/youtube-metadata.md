## YouTube Description

Come close, little friends — it's a cozy rainy-day story with Opa. 💛

In this gentle Opa's Storytime episode from Pompom Hills, Opa tells a warm preschool story
about one quiet bird in a forest full of chatty birds — a bird who listened so carefully she
noticed a tiny hidden nest no one else could see. Luca and Mimi listen, ask questions, and
discover that being quiet helps us notice more.

This calm story supports early learning through gentle themes like listening, noticing, and
valuing quiet.
