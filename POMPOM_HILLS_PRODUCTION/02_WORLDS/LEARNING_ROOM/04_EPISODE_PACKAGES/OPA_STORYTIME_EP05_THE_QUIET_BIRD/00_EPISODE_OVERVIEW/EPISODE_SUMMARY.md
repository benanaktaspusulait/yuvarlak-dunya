# Opa's Storytime: The Quiet Bird — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a rainy day, Luca and Mimi sit with Opa in the Learning Room's Reading Corner
while soft rain falls outside. Opa tells a gentle story about a quiet bird in a
forest full of chatting birds, who listens more than she talks and notices a tiny
hidden nest that no one else had seen. Luca and Mimi ask questions and react
throughout the story.

## Learning

Listening • Noticing small things • Being gentle

## Characters

Opa, Luca, Mimi

## World

Learning Room — Reading Corner (rainy day)
