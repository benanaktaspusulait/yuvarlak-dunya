# The Little Lantern — Beat Sheet v2.0 (5-Minute Version)

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Mimi arrive at the cozy evening Reading Corner, sit near Opa. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa opens the book. Children lean in with curiosity.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): "Once there was a little lantern." Luca asks if it was little.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): The little lantern wished it could shine big and bright. Mimi asks if it wanted to be like the big lamps.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The lantern felt its light was too small to matter. Luca echoes softly.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): "Is a little light enough?" Mimi asks the key question. Opa: "Let us see."
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Evening falls softly; the sky turns gentle and dim. A friend is still outside. Luca asks if it was dark.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): The friend cannot find the way home. Mimi asks if the friend was scared.
Beat 9 (Shot 09): The little lantern's warm glow lights the path. Luca wonders if its little light was enough.
Beat 10 (Shot 10): The friend follows the glow, step by step, safely home. Mimi asks if it was safe and cozy.
Beat 11 (Shot 11): "A little light helps a friend find the way." Luca asks: even a small one?
Beat 12 (Shot 12): "The little light was enough!" Luca's warm reaction. Opa agrees.
Beat 13 (Shot 13): "I can be a little light for my friend." Mimi's micro-moment. Opa: "That is a kind thing."
Beat 14 (Shot 14): Book closes gently. Warm closing begins. No new dialogue.
Beat 15 (Shot 15): Children smile. Quiet warm hold.
Beat 16 (Shot 16): Warm lantern / evening window glow. Cozy stillness.
Beat 17 (Shot 17): Gentle pull-back. The Reading Corner widens softly.
Beat 18 (Shot 18): Quiet warmth. Shared calm.
Beat 19 (Shot 19): Final warm hold. Characters settle peacefully.
Beat 20 (Shot 20): Quiet warm closing hold. No new dialogue; transition to closing bumper.
```
