# The Little Lantern — Beat Sheet v1.0 (5-Minute Version)

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Mimi arrive at the cozy evening Reading Corner, sit near Opa by the glowing lantern. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa opens the book. Children lean in with curiosity in the warm lantern glow.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): "Once there was a little lantern." Its light seemed too dim. Luca asks if it was little.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): The big lanterns shone bright and far. Mimi wonders about the little one.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): "Is a small light enough?" Luca asks the gentle question.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): A little friend wanders in the soft evening light, gently unable to find the way. Mimi notices.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): The little lantern glows its warmest best. Luca asks how bright.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): "Will the friend see it?" Mimi asks with hope.
Beat 9 (Shot 09): One small warm glow shines in the soft evening. Luca watches.
Beat 10 (Shot 10): The friend follows the little light safely home. Mimi asks if the way was found.
Beat 11 (Shot 11): "A little light helps a friend find the way." Opa shows the lesson through story.
Beat 12 (Shot 12): "The little lantern helped!" Mimi's warm reaction.
Beat 13 (Shot 13): "I can be a little light for a friend." Luca's micro-moment.
Beat 14 (Shot 14): Book closes gently. Warm closing begins. No new dialogue.
Beat 15 (Shot 15): Children smile softly at each other.
Beat 16 (Shot 16): Warm evening window and lantern light fill the corner.
Beat 17 (Shot 17): Gentle pull-back; the cozy corner widens.
Beat 18 (Shot 18): Quiet shared warmth.
Beat 19 (Shot 19): Final warm hold.
Beat 20 (Shot 20): Quiet warm closing hold. No new dialogue; transition to closing bumper.
```

## Interaction Check

Her shot en az bir çocuk tepkisi (soru/tahmin/gözlem) içerir — Opa hiçbir shot'ta
10-15 saniyeden uzun tek başına konuşmaz. Bkz. `00-CORE/31_OPA_INTERACTIVE_STORYTELLING_FORMAT.md` §7.

"Lost" beat (Shot 06) gentle-safe kalır: arkadaş asla panik/korku içinde değil, yumuşak
akşam ışığında sadece yolunu arar. Bkz. `00-CORE/CHILD_SAFETY_RULES.md`.
