# The Little Lantern — SFX v1.0

---

## Sound Design

| Element | Sound | Volume |
|---|---|---|
| Ambiyans | Soft cozy indoor room tone, faint evening crickets/soft breeze through window | -20 dB |
| Müzik | Gentle soft piano / music-box underscore | -14 dB |
| SFX | Page turn (soft paper) | -12 dB |
| SFX | Very soft chime on warm lantern-glow moments (shots 02, 06, 07, 09, 10) | -14 dB |
| Diyalog | Karakter sesleri | -6 dB |

Forbidden: loud music, sudden stingers, aggressive sound design, storm/thunder sounds,
spooky/night-horror ambience (per `00-CORE/31_OPA_INTERACTIVE_STORYTELLING_FORMAT.md` §11
and `00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11). Evening ambience must stay
gentle and reassuring — nothing eerie or dark.
