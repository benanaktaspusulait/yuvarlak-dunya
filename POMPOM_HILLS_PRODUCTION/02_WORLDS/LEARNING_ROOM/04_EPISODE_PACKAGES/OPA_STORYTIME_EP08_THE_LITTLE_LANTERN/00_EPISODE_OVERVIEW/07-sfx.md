## Sound Design

| Element | Sound | Volume |
|---|---|---|
| Ambiyans | Soft indoor room tone, faint gentle evening ambience through window | -20 dB |
| SFX | Page turn (soft paper) | -12 dB |
| SFX | Very soft warm shimmer on gentle glow moments (shots 02, 07, 09) | -14 dB |
| Diyalog | Karakter sesleri | -6 dB |

Natural ambience only. No music, no background music, no soundtrack — per the episode brief
and the shot-level Sound sections.

Forbidden: music, background music, melody, song, soundtrack, musical bed, loud effects,
sudden stingers, aggressive sound design, storm/thunder sounds, nighttime spooky ambience
(per `00-CORE/31_OPA_INTERACTIVE_STORYTELLING_FORMAT.md` §11 and
`00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11).

Note: The shared closing bumper adds its own soft music tail in edit — the episode body itself
carries natural ambience only.
