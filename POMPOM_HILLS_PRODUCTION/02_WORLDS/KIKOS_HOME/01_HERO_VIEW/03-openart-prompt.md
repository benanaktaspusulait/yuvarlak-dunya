# Kiko's Home — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A round, single-story coral pink cottage with a white arched door and round windows. Warm morning sunlight, soft green garden with small flowers in front. A round stepping-stone path leads to the door. Soft pastel colours, children's storybook style, warm and safe atmosphere. Matte clay-plush surfaces, no sharp edges, no modern objects, no dark shadows.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the cottage from the garden
- Image 2 (back): view from behind showing the backyard
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the door and windows
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the round door and flowers

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and coral pink palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
