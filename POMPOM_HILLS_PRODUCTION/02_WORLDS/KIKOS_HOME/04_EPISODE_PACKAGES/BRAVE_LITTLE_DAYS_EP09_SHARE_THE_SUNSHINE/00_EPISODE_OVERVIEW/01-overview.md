## Story Summary

Kiko is happily playing with her favourite soft ball in the living area when her friend Arda arrives. Arda sees the ball and wants to play too. Kiko hesitates — "It's mine." Arda waits patiently without pushing. Mimi the butterfly appears briefly and gently suggests, "What if you play together?" Kiko thinks about it, then tries rolling the ball to Arda. Arda rolls it back. They discover the joy of playing together. By the end, they are laughing and rolling the ball back and forth. Kiko declares, "Together is more fun!" Both wave as the episode ends.
