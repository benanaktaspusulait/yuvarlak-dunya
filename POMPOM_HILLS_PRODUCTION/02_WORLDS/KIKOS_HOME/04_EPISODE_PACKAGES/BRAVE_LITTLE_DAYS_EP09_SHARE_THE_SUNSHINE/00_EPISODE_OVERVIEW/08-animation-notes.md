## Animation Rules

1. **No rapid movements**: All motion is gentle and childlike
2. **No sharp transitions**: Smooth fades between emotions
3. **Ball physics**: Gentle rolling, soft catches, no dramatic bouncing
4. **Facial expressions**: Subtle, natural, not exaggerated
5. **Timing**: Allow pauses for emotion to land
6. **Consistency**: Characters maintain proportions and colours throughout
7. **Safe exposure**: No conflict animation, no anger expressions
