## Frame Descriptions

### Shot 01 — My Favourite Ball
**Frame Start**: Kiko sitting/kneeling on a soft rug in the living area. She holds a soft pastel ball. Warm daylight streams in. She is smiling.

**Frame End**: Kiko bouncing the ball gently. She is content and happy playing alone.

**Key Moment**: Kiko's face lights up as she plays. The ball is clearly hers — she holds it close.

### Shot 02 — I Want to Play
**Frame Start**: Kiko playing. Arda enters from screen-right, curious and friendly.

**Frame End**: Arda asks to play. Kiko hesitates, pulling the ball closer.

**Key Moment**: Kiko's hand tightens on the ball. "It's mine." Not angry — just reluctant.

### Shot 03 — Let's Play Together
**Frame Start**: Mimi appears briefly above. A gentle presence, not overwhelming.

**Frame End**: Kiko considers. She looks at Arda, then at the ball.

**Key Moment**: Mimi's suggestion is gentle. Not a command. Just an idea.

### Shot 04 — One Roll
**Frame Start**: Kiko holds the ball. She looks at Arda. She makes a decision.

**Frame End**: She rolls the ball gently across the rug toward Arda.

**Key Moment**: The ball rolling is the first act of sharing. Small but significant.

### Shot 05 — Back and Forth
**Frame Start**: Arda catches the ball. He rolls it back to Kiko.

**Frame End**: They continue rolling. Both laughing. The rhythm begins.

**Key Moment**: The back-and-forth establishes the game. Fun is shared.

### Shot 06 — Our Game
**Frame Start**: They've found a pattern. Roll, catch, roll back.

**Frame End**: Both characters fully engaged, smiling, enjoying.

**Key Moment**: The game is now "theirs" — not just Kiko's ball anymore.

### Shot 07 — More Fun Together
**Frame Start**: Arda pauses mid-game. Speaks warmly.

**Frame End**: Kiko beams. She agrees.

**Key Moment**: Arda articulates the lesson: "This is more fun together!"

### Shot 08 — Together Is More Fun
**Frame Start**: Kiko speaks the repeated phrase with full conviction.

**Frame End**: Both wave goodbye. The ball sits between them.

**Key Moment**: The episode's lesson lands. Both characters embody the joy of sharing.
