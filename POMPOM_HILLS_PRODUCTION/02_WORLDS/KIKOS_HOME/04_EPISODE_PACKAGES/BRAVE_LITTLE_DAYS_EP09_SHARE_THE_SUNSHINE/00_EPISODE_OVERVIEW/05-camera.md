# EP09 — Camera

## Camera Identity

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Focal Length | 28–35mm range |
| Eye Level | Child eye level (approx. 80–100cm from ground) |
| Style | Warm, intimate, stable |
| Movement | Minimal — mostly static with gentle reframing |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 |
| Frame Rate | 24fps |

## Camera Rules

1. **Child Eye Level**: All shots at child height. No looking down on characters.
2. **Static Preferred**: Minimal camera movement. Let action fill the frame.
3. **Warm Framing**: Characters comfortably within frame, not crowded.
4. **Ball Tracking**: Ball always visible when in play. Smooth tracking if needed.
5. **Consistent Depth**: Same depth of field across all shots for continuity.

## Shot-by-Shot Camera Notes

### Shot 01 — My Favourite Ball
- **Framing**: Kiko centred, soft ball visible in her hands
- **Focus**: Kiko's face and the ball
- **Lighting**: Warm daylight from screen-left
- **Background**: Soft, out-of-focus living area

### Shot 02 — I Want to Play
- **Framing**: Two-shot — Kiko left, Arda enters right
- **Focus**: Both characters, especially Kiko's reaction
- **Movement**: Gentle pan right as Arda enters
- **Ball**: Visible in Kiko's hands, she pulls it closer

### Shot 03 — Let's Play Together
- **Framing**: Wider shot to include Mimi above
- **Focus**: All three characters (Mimi briefly)
- **Movement**: Static — let Mimi's entrance be the motion
- **Ball**: In Kiko's lap/hands

### Shot 04 — One Roll
- **Framing**: Medium shot, Kiko centred
- **Focus**: Kiko's face, then the rolling ball
- **Movement**: Static — ball moves within frame
- **Ball**: Rolling from Kiko toward Arda (off-screen or screen-right)

### Shot 05 — Back and Forth (Quality Reset Anchor)
- **Framing**: Wide shot, both characters visible
- **Focus**: Ball moving between them
- **Movement**: Static — ball is the motion
- **Ball**: Rolling back and forth between Kiko and Arda
- **Quality Check**: Re-verify all character positions, colours, lighting

### Shot 06 — Our Game
- **Framing**: Medium two-shot, both characters
- **Focus**: Their faces and the ball
- **Movement**: Static
- **Ball**: Continuing to roll between them

### Shot 07 — More Fun Together
- **Framing**: Medium two-shot, both characters
- **Focus**: Arda speaking, Kiko's reaction
- **Movement**: Static
- **Ball**: Paused mid-roll or in Arda's hands

### Shot 08 — Together Is More Fun
- **Framing**: Medium shot, both characters
- **Focus**: Kiko speaking, then both waving
- **Movement**: Static
- **Ball**: Between them on the rug, still
