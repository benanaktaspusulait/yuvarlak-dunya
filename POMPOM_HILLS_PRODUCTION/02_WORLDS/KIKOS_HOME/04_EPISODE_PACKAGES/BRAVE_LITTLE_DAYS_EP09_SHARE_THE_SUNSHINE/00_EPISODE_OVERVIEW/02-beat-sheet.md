## Beat Details

### Beat 1 — PROBLEM (Shots 01-02)

**Setup**: Kiko is in her living area, playing with a soft ball. She bounces it gently, rolls it, catches it. She is happy and content.

**Inciting Incident**: Arda enters the scene. Sees the ball. Wants to play too.

**Kiko's Reaction**: She pulls the ball closer. "It's mine." Not angry — just reluctant. She likes playing alone with her ball.

**Emotional State**: Content → Protective → Hesitant

### Beat 2 — FEELING (Shot 02)

**Arda's Response**: He doesn't push. He doesn't get upset. He waits. He is patient and kind.

**Kiko's Internal Conflict**: She sees Arda waiting. She feels a little tug. But the ball is hers.

**Emotional State**: Torn between wanting to play alone and seeing a friend waiting

### Beat 3 — SUPPORT (Shot 03)

**Mimi Appears**: Briefly. Lightly. Just a suggestion — not an order.

**The Suggestion**: "What if you play together?" Not "You should share." Not "Give Arda the ball." Just an idea.

**Kiko's Response**: She thinks. She looks at Arda. She looks at the ball. She considers.

**Emotional State**: Open to trying

### Beat 4 — ACTION (Shots 04-05)

**First Roll**: Kiko gently rolls the ball to Arda. It's a small gesture but huge for her.

**Arda's Reaction**: He catches it (gently). He smiles. He rolls it back!

**Back and Forth**: They roll it back and forth. Each roll is a moment of connection.

**Emotional State**: Tentative → Joyful discovery

### Beat 5 — RELIEF (Shots 06-07)

**Game Develops**: They find a rhythm. Roll, catch, roll back. They laugh.

**Arda Says**: "This is more fun together!"

**Kiko Feels**: She was right to try. Sharing didn't lose her the ball — it gained her a friend.

**Emotional State**: Connected, happy, validated

### Beat 6 — LESSON (Shot 08)

**Kiko Declares**: "Together is more fun!" The repeated phrase lands with full weight.

**Both Wave**: A gentle goodbye. The episode ends on warmth.

**Final Frame**: Both characters waving, ball between them, warm lighting.

**Emotional State**: Full joy, lesson embodied
