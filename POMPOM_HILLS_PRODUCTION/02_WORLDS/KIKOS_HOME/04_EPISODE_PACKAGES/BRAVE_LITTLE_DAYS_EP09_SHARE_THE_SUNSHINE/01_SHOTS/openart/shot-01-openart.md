## Take Shot Prompt

A young girl with brown pigtails in two braids, wearing a coral-pink short-sleeved top and white shorts, sits on a light-coloured soft rug in a warm, cozy living room with cream walls. She holds a soft pastel blue ball with a faint pattern, smiling happily with bright eyes. Warm natural daylight streams in from a window on the left. The girl bounces the ball gently in her hands, catches it, and giggles with joy. Soft, gentle 3D animation style. Child eye level camera at 30mm focal length. Warm colour palette with soft shadows.
