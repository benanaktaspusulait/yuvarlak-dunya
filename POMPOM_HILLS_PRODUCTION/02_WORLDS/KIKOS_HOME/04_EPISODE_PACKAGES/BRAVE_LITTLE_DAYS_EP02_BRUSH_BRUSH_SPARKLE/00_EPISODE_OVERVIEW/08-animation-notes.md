# Brave Little Days EP02 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat. Never a
static talking pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** sleepy and reluctant at first (slow blinks, small yawn, slumped shoulders, hugging
  her toy), then brightening curiosity in Shot 03, playful brushing energy in 05–06, and proud,
  fresh delight at the mirror in 07. Pigtails bounce with the brushing rhythm.
- **Mimi:** gentle and encouraging — a little playful "sparkle" bounce in Shot 03, brushing
  alongside Kiko for fun in 05–06, happy ear-wiggles. Never pushy.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

reluctance (01–02) → playful spark (03) → getting ready (04) → happy rhythm (05–06) →
proud sparkle (07) → cozy, sleepy-happy (08).

## Brushing Motion (Safe / Realistic)

- Pea-size toothpaste only; brush moves gently up-and-down then small circles.
- No aggressive scrubbing, no gagging, no swallowing. Rinse-and-spit shown lightly and cleanly.
- Water trickle is soft; sink stays tidy. No mess, no splashing chaos.

## World Notes

Keep Kiko's Home rounded/soft. Warm evening light stays golden and cozy (never dark).
The Washing Corner is small, rounded, pink+white — matches the toy-cottage material language.
