# Brave Little Days EP02 — "Brush, Brush, Sparkle!" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Washing Corner (optional set-piece)
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi (Nella referenced verbally only)
**Theme:** Brushing your teeth every day; a "boring" routine can be fun.

It's evening at Kiko's Home and sleepy Kiko doesn't want to brush her teeth — "Brushing is
boring." Mimi turns it into a game and remembers Nella the Tooth Helper: "Let's make it
sparkle — like Nella said!" At the little Washing Corner they take their brushes, add a pea
of toothpaste, and brush in a happy rhythm: "Brush, brush, sparkle!" Kiko rinses and sees her
sparkly clean smile in the mirror — "Look — sparkle!" The episode closes cozy and sleepy-happy:
"Brush, brush, sparkle — every day!"

**Repeated phrase:** "Brush, brush, sparkle!"
**Learning point:** Brushing every day keeps our smile happy and sparkly; routines can be fun.
**Continuity:** Verbal callback to EP01 "Kiko's Happy Smile" (Nella). No new characters.
