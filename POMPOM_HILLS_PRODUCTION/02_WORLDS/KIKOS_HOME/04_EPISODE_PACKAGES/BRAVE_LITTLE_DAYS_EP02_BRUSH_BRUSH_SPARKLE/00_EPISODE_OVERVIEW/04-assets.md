## New Set-Piece — Washing Corner (Optional)

A small, cozy washing nook inside Kiko's rounded toy-cottage (not a separate room):

- Low round white sink (child height), rounded edges
- Round white-framed mirror above the sink
- A little cup holding two small toothbrushes (coral for Kiko, soft blue for Mimi)
- A small friendly toothpaste tube
- Warm evening light, coral-pink accents matching the home palette

**Keep it on-canon:** all rounded/soft, pink+white toy-cottage material language, no sharp
corners, no cold metal/glass look, no dark shadows. See Kiko's Home bible § Material Language
and `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md`.
