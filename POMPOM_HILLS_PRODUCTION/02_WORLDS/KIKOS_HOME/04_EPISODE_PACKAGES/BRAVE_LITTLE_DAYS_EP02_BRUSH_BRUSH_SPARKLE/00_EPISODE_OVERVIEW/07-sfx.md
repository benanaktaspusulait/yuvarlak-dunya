# Brave Little Days EP02 — Sound Design

Natural ambience only. **No background music, no melody, no soundtrack** in the episode body.
(Music lives only in the shared opening/closing bumpers.)

| Shot | Sound |
|---|---|
| 01 | Cozy evening room tone, soft toy rustle, a small sleepy yawn |
| 02 | Soft room tone, gentle Mimi hop |
| 03 | Playful little bounce, soft cheerful Mimi tone (no music) |
| 04 | Soft footsteps to the sink, gentle cup/brush pickup, tiny paste squeeze |
| 05 | Soft brushing sound (gentle, friendly), light water trickle, playful chant |
| 06 | Soft brushing, small happy giggles |
| 07 | Gentle water rinse, cup set down, a happy breath |
| 08 | Warm cozy room tone, soft goodnight, gentle settle |

---

## Forbidden Sounds

```
✗ harsh, sudden, or scary sounds
✗ loud running water / plumbing clatter
✗ background music inside the episode body
```
