## Story Summary

Kiko wants to use Mimi's special cup, but Mimi is drinking from it. "I want it now!" Kiko says. Mimi gently asks, "Can you wait a little?" Kiko is impatient but Mimi shows her how to look at clouds out the window while waiting. Kiko starts seeing shapes in the clouds — a bunny, a flower, a heart. Distraction works! When Mimi finishes, she hands the cup to Kiko. "All done! Your turn!" Kiko drinks from the cup and smiles. "That was worth the wait!" Both agree: "Waiting is okay."

## Micro-Moment

Kiko's foot taps impatiently while she watches Mimi use the cup. The tapping is brief but real — a small physical expression of impatience that carries the emotional weight of the episode. When she turns to the window, the tapping stops. The shift is subtle but meaningful.

## Learning Point

Good things come to those who wait. When you can't have something right away, finding something fun to do while you wait makes the time go faster — and the reward feels even better.
