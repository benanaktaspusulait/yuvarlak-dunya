## Frame Descriptions

### Shot 01 — Waiting for Mimi
**Frame Start**: Kitchen nook, warm daytime. Mimi holds the special cup. Kiko enters from screen-left, noticing the cup.

**Frame End**: Kiko's eyes fix on the cup. She is curious and interested.

**Key Moment**: Kiko's face lights up when she sees the special cup. She wants it.

### Shot 02 — I Want It Now
**Frame Start**: Kiko approaches Mimi. Her foot taps gently on the floor.

**Frame End**: Kiko reaches slightly toward the cup. "I want it now!" Not angry — just impatient.

**Key Moment**: Kiko's foot tapping is the physical expression of her impatience. Brief but real.

### Shot 03 — Can You Wait
**Frame Start**: Mimi holds the cup gently, not rushing.

**Frame End**: Mimi points to the window. Kiko hesitates, then begins to turn.

**Key Moment**: Mimi's question is gentle — "Can you wait a little?" Not a command, an invitation.

### Shot 04 — Looking Out the Window
**Frame Start**: Kiko turns to look out the window. Blue sky fills the frame.

**Frame End**: White fluffy clouds drift across the sky. Kiko's expression softens.

**Key Moment**: The shift from impatience to curiosity. The clouds begin to work their magic.

### Shot 05 — The Clouds Are Fun
**Frame Start**: Kiko points at a cloud. Her face is bright with discovery.

**Frame End**: She sees shapes — a bunny, a flower, a heart. She laughs softly.

**Key Moment**: "That cloud looks like a bunny!" — distraction has fully worked. Kiko is enjoying the wait.

### Shot 06 — Almost Time
**Frame Start**: Mimi holds out the cup toward Kiko. "All done!"

**Frame End**: Kiko gasps with happy surprise. The wait is over!

**Key Moment**: The cup being offered. The reward arrives.

### Shot 07 — That Was Worth It
**Frame Start**: Kiko holds the special cup carefully. She brings it to her lips.

**Frame End**: She drinks. She smiles with pure satisfaction. "That was worth the wait!"

**Key Moment**: The reward is even better because she waited for it.

### Shot 08 — Waiting Is Okay
**Frame Start**: Both characters smile at each other. The cup rests between them.

**Frame End**: Both agree: "Waiting is okay." Warm final frame.

**Key Moment**: The lesson lands. Patience is rewarded. The episode ends on warmth.
