# EP11 — Episode Summary

**Title:** A Little Patience
**Series:** Brave Little Days
**World:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook
**Duration:** 120 seconds (8 shots × 15 s)

## Story

Kiko sees Mimi using a special pastel cup with a unique star or flower rim. "Ooh, the special cup!" she says, wanting it immediately. "I want it now!" Kiko says, her foot tapping impatiently. Mimi gently asks, "Can you wait a little?" but Kiko pushes back: "But I want it!"

Mimi doesn't force her to wait. Instead, she offers something fun — "Look at the clouds while you wait." Kiko turns to the window and sees blue sky with fluffy white clouds. "That cloud looks like a bunny!" she says, pointing excitedly. A flower. A heart. She's having fun waiting!

When Mimi finishes, she hands the cup to Kiko. "All done! Your turn!" Kiko drinks from the cup and smiles with pure satisfaction. "That was worth the wait!" Both friends agree: "Waiting is okay."

## Key Messages

- Waiting is okay — good things come to those who wait
- Finding something fun to do while waiting makes time go faster
- Patience is rewarded naturally, not forced
- Friends can help each other be patient
- The reward feels even better when you've waited for it

## Learning Point

Good things come to those who wait. When you can't have something right away, finding something fun to do while you wait makes the time go faster — and the reward feels even better.

## Safe-Exposure Compliance

No tantrum, no anger, no scolding. Kiko's impatience is mild and brief. Mimi offers a gentle distraction, not a command. Patience is rewarded naturally.
