## Beat Details

### Beat 1 — PROBLEM (Shots 01-02)

**Setup**: Mimi is in the kitchen nook, happily using the special cup. It's a beautiful pastel cup with a unique shape. Kiko notices and wants it.

**Inciting Incident**: Kiko sees the cup and wants to use it too. She doesn't want to wait.

**Kiko's Reaction**: She approaches Mimi, tapping her foot gently. "I want it now!" Not angry — just impatient. She's a child who wants things right away.

**Emotional State**: Curious → Impatient

### Beat 2 — FEELING (Shot 02)

**Kiko's Expression**: Her foot taps. Her hands reach slightly forward. She wants the cup. She doesn't understand why she can't have it right now.

**Mimi's Response**: She holds the cup gently. She doesn't rush. She doesn't get upset. She is patient and kind.

**Kiko's Internal Conflict**: She sees Mimi using the cup. She feels the impatience building. She wants it now.

**Emotional State**: Impatient, wanting

### Beat 3 — SUPPORT (Shot 03)

**Mimi Speaks**: "Can you wait a little?" Not a command. A gentle question.

**Kiko Responds**: "But I want it!" — honest, direct, childlike.

**Mimi's Offer**: She doesn't force Kiko to wait. She offers something fun instead — "Look at the clouds while you wait."

**Emotional State**: Resistant → Beginning to shift

### Beat 4 — ACTION (Shots 04-05)

**The Window**: Mimi points to the window. Kiko looks. She sees blue sky and fluffy white clouds.

**Discovery**: "That cloud looks like a bunny!" Kiko starts seeing shapes in the clouds. A flower. A heart. She's having fun!

**Distraction Works**: Kiko forgets about waiting. She's enjoying the clouds. Time passes happily.

**Emotional State**: Curious → Delighted → Engaged

### Beat 5 — RELIEF (Shots 06-07)

**Mimi Finishes**: "All done! Your turn!" She hands the special cup to Kiko.

**Kiko's Joy**: She takes the cup carefully. She drinks from it. It's everything she hoped for.

**The Lesson Moment**: "That was worth the wait!" — the reward feels even better because she waited for it.

**Emotional State**: Anticipation → Satisfaction → Pride

### Beat 6 — LESSON (Shot 08)

**Both Agree**: "Waiting is okay." — the repeated phrase lands with full weight.

**Final Frame**: Both characters smiling, the cup between them, warm lighting.

**Emotional State**: Contentment, lesson embodied
