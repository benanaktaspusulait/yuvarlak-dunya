## Action Notes — Patience Mechanics

1. **Foot tapping**: Brief, childlike. 2-3 taps maximum. Not aggressive — just a physical expression of wanting something.
2. **Cloud watching**: Kiko's body relaxes as she looks. Shoulders drop. Expression shifts from tense to curious.
3. **Pointing at clouds**: Excited but controlled. She sees shapes — bunny, flower, heart. Each discovery brings a small smile.
4. **Cup transfer**: Mimi holds it out gently. Kiko takes it carefully. The cup is treated as something special.
5. **Drinking**: Kiko sips slowly. Not rushed. She savours it. The reward is earned.

## Safe-Exposure — Animation

- No frustrated expressions from Kiko (impatience is mild, not angry)
- No eye-rolling, sighing, or aggressive body language from anyone
- Mimi never rushes Kiko — she offers a distraction, not a command
- Final expressions: genuine contentment, not exaggerated celebration
- Kiko's impatience is brief (shots 02-03) and resolves quickly
