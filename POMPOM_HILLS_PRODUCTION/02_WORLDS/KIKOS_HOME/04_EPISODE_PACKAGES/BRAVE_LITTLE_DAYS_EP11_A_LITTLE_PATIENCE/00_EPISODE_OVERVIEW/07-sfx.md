## Forbidden Sounds

| Sound | Reason |
|---|---|
| Background music | Rule: NO background music |
| On-screen text sounds | Rule: NO on-screen text |
| Harsh impatience sounds | Kiko's impatience is mild, not aggressive |
| Scolding tone | Violates safe-exposure |
| Clock ticking | Would create urgency — opposite of patience |
| Cartoon boing / slapstick | Too silly for the warm tone |
| Adult voices | No adult characters |
| Door slamming | Too aggressive |

## Sound Design Principles

1. **Impatience sounds are gentle** — foot tapping is soft, not aggressive. The physical expression of impatience should feel childlike, not frustrated.
2. **Room tone is warm** — never cold or clinical. The kitchen nook should feel alive but calm.
3. **Silence is allowed** — gaps between dialogue are fine. Don't fill every second.
4. **Cloud scene has air** — slightly brighter ambience when Kiko looks at the window. A sense of openness.
5. **Cup sounds are satisfying** — soft sip, gentle placement. The reward should sound rewarding.
