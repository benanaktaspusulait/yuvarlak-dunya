# Dialogue — Kiko's Home Tour

> **15 saniye. 7 replik.**

---

## Complete Dialogue

```
Shot 01 (0–4 sn):
Kiko: I hope you'll visit again soon!

Shot 02 (4–8 sn):
Kiko: Oh! I almost forgot!
(Kiko pauses, smiles.)
Kiko: Come on... let me show you inside!
(Kiko opens door, walks inside, camera follows. No further dialogue after entering.)

Shot 03 (14–22 sn):
Kiko: This is where I read stories.
Kiko: I love looking outside from here.

Shot 04 (22–24 sn):
Kiko: Next time I'll show you even more!
```

---

## Voice Direction

| Shot | Tone | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| 01 | Excited, inviting | "Come on!" rises with enthusiasm |
| 02 | — | Movement only, no speech |
| 03 | Proud, sharing | Points while speaking, gentle pauses |
| 04 | Warm, welcoming | Final greeting, smile in voice |

---

## Total Word Count

7 lines × ~5 words = ~35 words

Perfect for preschool audience.
