## Action Notes

### Coat-Putting-On Sequence

**Step 1: Position Coat (Shot 02)**
- Kiko holds coat in front of her
- Tries to find armholes
- Gets tangled, arms flailing slightly
- Coat is half-on, one sleeve dangling

**Step 2: First Arm (Shot 04)**
- Mimi guides Kiko's arm toward sleeve
- Kiko slides arm in smoothly
- Arm goes all the way through
- Kiko looks amazed

**Step 3: Second Arm (Shot 05)**
- Mimi guides second arm
- Kiko slides arm in
- Both arms now in sleeves
- Coat hangs properly but unsnapped

**Step 4: Snap (Shot 06)**
- Mimi reaches for snap
- Kiko's hands help
- Snap closes with click-click
- Both smile at the sound

**Step 5: Settle (Shot 07)**
- Coat settles on Kiko's body
- She stands tall, feeling cozy
- Arms relax at sides
- Comfortable, warm feeling

### Animation Timing Notes

- **Pacing**: Slow to moderate — child learning speed
- **Anticipation**: Small wind-up before key actions
- **Follow-through**: Natural settling after actions
- **Overlap**: Hair and clothing move slightly after body stops
- **Secondary action**: Facial expressions animate with body

## Calm but Alive

**The animation should feel:**
- **Calm**: No frantic energy, no rapid movements
- **Alive**: Characters breathe, blink, shift weight naturally
- **Present**: Characters feel like they exist in the space
- **Connected**: Kiko and Mimi respond to each other

**Avoid:**
- Robot-like stillness
- Over-animated expressiveness
- Exaggerated cartoon physics
- Looney Tunes energy
