# Storyboard — EP08 "Cozy Coat, Click Click"

## Location

**Kiko's Home — Entryway**

- **Setting**: Front door area of a cozy, warm home
- **Time**: Warm daytime (late morning)
- **Key elements**: Front door with glass panels (screen-right), wooden coat rack, small bench, potted plant, warm cream walls, honey-toned wood floor
- **Atmosphere**: Safe, familiar, inviting — this is Kiko's comfort zone

## Storyboard Table

| Shot | Frame Description | Camera | Duration |
|------|-------------------|--------|----------|
| 01 | **A Coat on the Chair** — Wide shot of entryway. Coat rack with Kiko's terracotta coat hanging on it. Kiko (screen-left) looks at coat with excitement. Mimi (screen-right) stands near door, smiling. Warm light from door glass. | Medium-wide, eye level, static | 15s |
| 02 | **I Can't Do It** — Medium shot. Kiko tries to put on coat, gets tangled. Arms flailing slightly, face shows mild frustration. Mimi watches with gentle concern. Coat half-on, one sleeve dangling. | Medium, eye level, slight push-in | 15s |
| 03 | **Let's Try Together** — Two-shot. Mimi moves closer to Kiko. Kiko looks up at Mimi, expression shifting from frustrated to hopeful. Mimi gestures toward the coat. Warm light wraps both characters. | Medium two-shot, eye level, static | 15s |
| 04 | **One Arm In** — Medium shot focused on Kiko. She slides one arm into the sleeve. Mimi guides from the side. Kiko's face lights up with success. Coat now has one arm in, other sleeve hanging. | Medium, eye level, static | 15s |
| 05 | **The Other Arm** — Medium shot. Kiko slides second arm in. Both arms now in sleeves. Mimi beams with pride. Kiko looks down at herself, amazed. Coat hangs properly but unsnapped. | Medium, eye level, static | 15s |
| 06 | **Click Click** — Close-up on coat front. Mimi's paw reaches to snap. Kiko's hands help. Snap closes with satisfying click. Both characters smile. Focus on the snap mechanism. | Close-up, slightly low angle, static | 15s |
| 07 | **All Cozy** — Medium-full shot. Kiko stands in fully worn coat, arms slightly out to show it off. She looks cozy and comfortable. Mimi stands beside her, warm smile. Coat fits perfectly. | Medium-full, eye level, static | 15s |
| 08 | **I Can Do It Myself** — Medium shot. Kiko waves at camera, proud expression. Mimi stands beside her, nodding. Kiko's posture is confident, standing tall in her coat. Final frame: both characters smiling. | Medium, eye level, static | 15s |

## Continuity Chain

### Coat State Progression
```
Shot 01: On coat rack (hanging)
Shot 02: Half-on (one arm attempting, one sleeve dangling)
Shot 03: Still half-on (Mimi approaches to help)
Shot 04: One arm in (one sleeve on, one hanging)
Shot 05: Both arms in (sleeves on, unsnapped)
Shot 06: Snapped (click-click moment)
Shot 07: Fully worn (snapped, cozy)
Shot 08: Fully worn (standing proud)
```

### Character Position Continuity
```
Shot 01: Kiko screen-left (30%), Mimi screen-right (70%)
Shot 02: Kiko screen-left (35%), Mimi screen-right (65%)
Shot 03: Kiko screen-left (40%), Mimi screen-right (60%) — closer together
Shot 04: Kiko screen-left (45%), Mimi screen-right (55%) — close

Shot 06: Both centered for close-up
Shot 07: Kiko screen-left (40%), Mimi screen-right (60%)

```

### Emotional Continuity
```
Shot 01: Excited → Shot 02: Frustrated → Shot 03: Hopeful → Shot 04: Encouraged →
Shot 05: Confident → Shot 06: Delighted → Shot 07: Satisfied → Shot 08: Proud
```

### Light Continuity
- All shots: Warm golden light from screen-right (door glass)
- No changes in light direction or quality
- Consistent warm tone throughout

### Prop Continuity
- Coat: Terracotta/rust colour, visible throughout
- Coat rack: Present in shots 01-02, may be partially visible in others
- Door: Always screen-right background
- Potted plant: Visible in wide shots (01, 07, 08)

## Visual Flow

```
[Wide Establishing] → [Medium Action] → [Two-Shot Support] → [Medium Success] →
[Medium Success] → [Close-Up Detail] → [Medium-Full Result] → [Medium Resolution]
```

The visual flow moves from establishing the space to focusing on the action, then pulls back to show the result, and finally settles on the emotional resolution.

## Transition Notes

- **Between shots**: Soft dissolve or gentle cut (no hard cuts)
- **Time progression**: Real-time, no time skips
- **Camera movement**: Minimal — mostly static shots to maintain calm feel
- **Focus**: Sharp on characters, slightly soft on background
