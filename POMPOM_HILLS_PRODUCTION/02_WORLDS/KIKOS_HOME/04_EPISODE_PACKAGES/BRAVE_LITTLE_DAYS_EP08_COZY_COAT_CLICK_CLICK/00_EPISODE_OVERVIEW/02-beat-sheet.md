## Safe-Exposure Anchors

### Anchor 1: Frustration is Normal (Shot 02)
- **What**: Kiko expresses "I can't do it..." without tears or tantrum
- **Why**: Normalizes frustration as part of learning
- **How**: Voice is disappointed but not distressed; body language shows trying, not giving up
- **Safe**: No crying, no yelling, no throwing, no running away

### Anchor 2: Help is Welcome (Shot 03)
- **What**: Mimi offers help, Kiko accepts it
- **Why**: Models that asking for/receiving help is okay
- **How**: Mimi's tone is warm and patient; Kiko's "Okay..." shows trust
- **Safe**: No reluctance, no resistance, no power struggle

### Anchor 3: Step-by-Step Success (Shots 04-05)
- **What**: Kiko succeeds one step at a time
- **Why**: Shows that big tasks become easy when broken down
- **How**: Each arm-in is celebrated; Mimi guides without doing it for her
- **Safe**: No rushing, no pressure, no comparison to others

### Anchor 4: Joy in Achievement (Shots 06-07)
- **What**: Click-click moment and cozy satisfaction
- **Why**: Reinforces that effort leads to reward
- **How**: Kiko giggles; coat feels warm and comfortable
- **Safe**: No exaggeration, no unrealistic expectations

### Anchor 5: Independence Declared (Shot 08)
- **What**: Kiko proudly says "I can do it myself!"
- **Why**: Models the learning outcome
- **How**: Confident but not arrogant; waving, not showing off
- **Safe**: No bragging, no put-downs, no "I told you so"
