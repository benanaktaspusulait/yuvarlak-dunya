## Ambience Notes

### Base Ambience (All Shots)
- **Room tone**: Quiet, warm home atmosphere
- **Exterior sounds**: Very faint birdsong through door glass
- **No**: TV, music, appliances, traffic, voices, pets
- **Level**: Very low — barely noticeable, just enough to feel "alive"

### Per-Shot Adjustments
- **Shots 01-03**: Slightly more exterior birdsong (door is focal point)
- **Shots 04-07**: More interior focus (action is happening)
- **Shot 08**: Return to balanced interior/exterior

## Key Sound Effects

### The Click-Click (Shot 06)

**This is the signature sound of the episode.**

- **Sound**: Two quick, satisfying snaps in succession
- **Quality**: Bright, clear, tactile — like a real coat snap
- **Timing**: 0.8-1.8s in Shot 06
- **Volume**: Slightly louder than other SFX — it's the payoff
- **Character**: Satisfying, not harsh — like a puzzle piece clicking into place

**Sound design notes:**
- Use real coat snap recording, not synthetic
- Two clicks, not one — "click-click" is the phrase
- Slightly reverberant (indoor space)
- Kiko's giggle follows immediately after

## Forbidden Sounds

### Absolutely No:
1. **Background music** — Only shared bumpers have music
2. **Instruments** — No piano, guitar, strings, etc.
3. **Singing/humming** — Characters don't sing
4. **Electronic sounds** — No beeps, buzzes,合成 sounds
5. **Cartoon effects** — No boings, whooshes, zaps
6. **Exaggerated sounds** — No overly dramatic SFX
7. **Danger sounds** — No crashes, breaks, thuds
8. **Crying sounds** — No tears, no sniffles
9. **Angry sounds** — No yelling, no stomping
10. **Adult sounds** — No deep voices, no complex speech

### Sound Level Rules
- **Dialogue**: Primary — always clear and audible
- **SFX**: Secondary — supports dialogue, never overpowers
- **Ambience**: Tertiary — barely noticeable background

## Sound Design Notes

### Emotional Sound Arc
```
Shot 01: Peaceful (establishing)
Shot 02: Slightly tense (struggling)
Shot 03: Calm (support offered)
Shot 04: Hopeful (first success)
Shot 05: Building (second success)
Shot 06: DELIGHTFUL (click-click!)
Shot 07: Comfortable (cozy settling)
Shot 08: Cheerful (waving goodbye)
```

### Sound Mixing Notes
- **Dialogue level**: 0dB (reference)
- **SFX level**: -6dB to -12dB (supporting)
- **Ambience level**: -18dB to -24dB (background)
- **No compression**: Keep dynamic range natural
- **No limiting**: Let sounds breathe

### Recording Notes
- Record dialogue in quiet studio
- Record SFX separately (foley)
- Mix in post-production
- No live recording on set (animation)
