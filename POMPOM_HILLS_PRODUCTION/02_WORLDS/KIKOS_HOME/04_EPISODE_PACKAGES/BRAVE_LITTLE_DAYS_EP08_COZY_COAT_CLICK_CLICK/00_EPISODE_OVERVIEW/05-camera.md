# Camera Plan — EP08 "Cozy Coat, Click Click"

## Camera Identity

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Lens Range | 28-35mm | Wide to normal, child-friendly perspective |
| Height | 0.75m | Child eye level — seeing the world as Kiko sees it |
| Movement Style | Static or minimal | Calm, patient, no distractions |
| Depth of Field | Moderate | Characters sharp, background slightly soft |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 | Standard widescreen |
| Frame Rate | 24fps | Cinematic but smooth |

## Camera Philosophy

The camera is a **patient observer** at child height. It never rushes, never zooms dramatically, never disorients. It watches Kiko's journey with the same patience Mimi shows. The 28-35mm lens range keeps both characters in frame while maintaining intimacy.

**Key principles:**
- Always at Kiko's eye level (0.75m)
- Never above looking down (adult perspective)
- Static or very slow movements only
- Let the action happen within the frame
- Trust the characters to fill the space

## Detailed Camera Notes

### Shot 01 — Establishing
- **Purpose**: Set the scene, show the coat on the rack
- **Framing**: Wide enough to see door, coat rack, both characters
- **Focus**: Deep — everything in focus
- **Movement**: None — let audience absorb the space

### Shot 02 — Frustration
- **Purpose**: Follow Kiko's attempt and mild frustration
- **Framing**: Medium on Kiko, Mimi slightly out of focus in background
- **Focus**: Shallow — Kiko sharp, background soft
- **Movement**: Very subtle push-in (5% over 15s) — almost imperceptible

### Shot 03 — Support
- **Purpose**: Show Mimi approaching, Kiko accepting help
- **Framing**: Two-shot, both characters in focus
- **Focus**: Medium — both characters sharp
- **Movement**: None — stable, safe framing

### Shot 04 — First Success
- **Purpose**: Celebrate one arm going in
- **Framing**: Medium on Kiko, Mimi's arm/guidance visible
- **Focus**: Sharp on Kiko's face and arm
- **Movement**: None — let the success happen

### Shot 05 — Second Success
- **Purpose**: Both arms in — building momentum
- **Framing**: Medium on Kiko, Mimi's proud expression visible
- **Focus**: Sharp on Kiko
- **Movement**: None — consistent with Shot 04

### Shot 06 — Click-Click
- **Purpose**: The satisfying snap moment
- **Framing**: Close-up on coat front, both sets of hands
- **Focus**: Very sharp on snap mechanism
- **Movement**: None — static for detail clarity
- **Special**: Slightly lower angle (0.65m) to see snap clearly

### Shot 07 — Cozy Result
- **Purpose**: Show the fully worn coat
- **Framing**: Medium-full — Kiko from head to knees
- **Focus**: Sharp on Kiko, Mimi slightly soft
- **Movement**: None — let audience see the result

### Shot 08 — Resolution
- **Purpose**: Kiko's proud declaration
- **Framing**: Medium — Kiko from head to waist
- **Focus**: Sharp on Kiko, Mimi beside
- **Movement**: None — final static frame

## Camera Continuity Rules

1. **Height**: Always 0.75m (except Shot 06 at 0.65m)
2. **Lens**: Stay within 28-35mm range
3. **Movement**: Max 5% push-in per shot, no other moves
4. **Focus**: Characters always sharp, background can soften
5. **Framing**: Kiko screen-left, Mimi screen-right (except close-ups)

## Camera禁忌 (What NOT to Do)

- ❌ No zooms (except subtle push-in in Shot 02)
- ❌ No pans or tilts
- ❌ No Dutch angles
- ❌ No shaky cam
- ❌ No crane shots
- ❌ No overhead shots
- ❌ No extreme close-ups (except Shot 06)
- ❌ No wide establishing shots after Shot 01
- ❌ No rapid cuts
- ❌ No camera movement that draws attention to itself

## Focus Pulling Notes

- **Shot 01**: Deep focus (f/8 or equivalent)
- **Shot 02**: Shallow focus on Kiko (f/2.8 or equivalent)
- **Shot 03**: Medium focus on both (f/4 or equivalent)
- **Shot 04-05**: Medium focus on Kiko (f/3.5 or equivalent)
- **Shot 06**: Very sharp on snap (f/2 or equivalent)
- **Shot 07-08**: Medium focus (f/4 or equivalent)

## Lighting Integration

- Camera positioned to capture warm light from screen-right (door)
- No fill lighting from screen-left (keeps natural feel)
- Characters lit from side, not front (avoids flat look)
- Soft shadows only, no harsh lines
