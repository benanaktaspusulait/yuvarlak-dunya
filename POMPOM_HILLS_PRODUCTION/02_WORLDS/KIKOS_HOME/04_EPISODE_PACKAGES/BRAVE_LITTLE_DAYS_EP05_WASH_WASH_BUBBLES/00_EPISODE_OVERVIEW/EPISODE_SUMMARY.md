# Brave Little Days EP05 — "Wash, Wash, Bubbles!" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Washing Corner (reused from EP02)
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi
**Theme:** Washing hands before we eat; a "boring" routine can be fun with bubbles.

Kiko comes in from playing and reaches for an apple with slightly dirty hands. Mimi reminds her —
"Wash first?" — but Kiko is hungry. Mimi makes it fun: "Let's make bubbles!" At the Washing Corner
they turn on gentle warm water, rub soft soap between their fingers, and chant "Wash, wash, bubbles!"
They rinse and dry, and Kiko holds up sparkly clean hands. Now they can enjoy the apple. The episode
closes warm: clean hands before we eat.

**Repeated phrase:** "Wash, wash, bubbles!"
**Learning point:** Washing hands before eating keeps them clean; routines can be fun.
**Continuity:** Reuses the EP02 Washing Corner; optional apple callback to EP01. No new canon.
