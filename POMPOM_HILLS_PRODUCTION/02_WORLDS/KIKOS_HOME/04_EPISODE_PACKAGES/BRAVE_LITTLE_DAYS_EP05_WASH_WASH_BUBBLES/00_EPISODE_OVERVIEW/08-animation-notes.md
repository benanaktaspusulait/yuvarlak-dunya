# Brave Little Days EP05 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat. Never a static
talking pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** hungry and impatient at first (reaching for the apple), mild reluctance, then brightening
  at "bubbles," playful bubbly washing, and happy pride at clean hands + the apple bite.
- **Mimi:** gentle and encouraging — points at the hands, playful bubble gesture in Shot 03, washes
  alongside Kiko, happy ear-wiggles. Never pushy.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

hungry impatience (01) → mild reluctance (02) → playful spark (03) → getting ready (04) →
bubbly fun (05) → clean satisfaction (06) → happy snack (07) → warm, content (08).

## Washing Motion (Safe / Realistic)

- Gentle warm water; a little soap; rub palms and between fingers; soft bubbles.
- Rinse and dry with a soft towel. **No flooding, no splashing chaos, no mess.** Tidy sink.

## World Notes

Keep Kiko's Home rounded/soft. Washing Corner identical to EP02. Warm daytime light stays consistent.
