## Emotional Progression

Playful resistance → a telltale yawn → a calm routine → snuggling in → slow soft breaths →
heavy eyes → peaceful drift → cozy goodnight. Shown through action, never preached.

gentle wind-down routine. Loosely echoes core S01E04 "Mimi's Big Yawn" (sleepiness) but from Kiko's
side. No new characters; no new canon.
