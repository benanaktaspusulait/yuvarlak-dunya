# Brave Little Days EP06 — Animation Notes

Calm and settling — energy winds down across the episode. Every 2–3 seconds still has a soft beat
(a yawn, a snuggle, a slow blink). Never a frozen pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** bouncy resistance at first (playing, "not sleepy"), a big telltale yawn, then softening
  into snuggles, slow blinks, slower movements, and a peaceful drift toward sleep.
- **Mimi:** gentle and cozy — knowing head tilt, guiding Kiko to bed, snuggling in, slow calm breathing.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

playful resistance (01) → sleepy tell (02) → agreeable (03) → snug (04) → calm breathing (05) →
heavy eyes (06) → drifting (07) → asleep/peaceful (08).

## Wind-Down Motion Rules

- Movements slow down progressively; breathing is gentle and visible (soft chest rise/fall).
- No sudden motion. The soft-blue shift is gradual, never a jarring cut to dark.

## World Notes

Keep Kiko's Home rounded/soft. Evening → soft blue night, cozy, never black or scary.
