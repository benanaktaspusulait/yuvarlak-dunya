# Brave Little Days EP06 — Sound Design

Natural ambience only. **No background music** in the episode body (music only in shared bumpers).
A very quiet, cozy soundscape getting softer toward sleep.

| Shot | Sound |
|---|---|
| 01 | Warm evening room tone, soft toy sounds |
| 02 | A big soft yawn, gentle room tone |
| 03 | Soft blanket rustle, gentle Mimi hop |
| 04 | Cozy snuggle sounds, soft blanket |
| 05 | Very soft slow breathing, quiet room tone |
| 06 | Barely-there room tone, slow calm breaths |
| 07 | A tiny sleepy murmur, near silence |
| 08 | Very soft, warm quiet; a whispered goodnight |

---

## Forbidden Sounds

```
✗ harsh, sudden, or scary sounds
✗ loud noises that would break the calming mood
✗ background music inside the episode body
```
