# Brave Little Days EP06 — "Cozy Down, Sleepy Time" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Sleeping Corner (evening → soft blue night)
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi
**Theme:** A calm bedtime routine; winding down and drifting peacefully to sleep.

Kiko doesn't want to stop playing — "I'm not sleepy!" — but a big yawn gives her away. Mimi
suggests they cozy down. The lights soften, Kiko snuggles into bed with Mimi, and together they
take soft slow breaths and count a few gentle stars: "Cozy down, sleepy time." Kiko's eyes get
heavy and she drifts peacefully to sleep. Goodnight.

**Repeated phrase:** "Cozy down, sleepy time."
**Learning point:** Sleep keeps us strong and happy; a calm wind-down helps us drift off.
**Safe exposure:** Playful (not tantrum) resistance; warm→soft-blue light (never black); calm and cozy.
**Note:** Distinct from EP04 (fear of the dark) — this is about the wind-down routine.
