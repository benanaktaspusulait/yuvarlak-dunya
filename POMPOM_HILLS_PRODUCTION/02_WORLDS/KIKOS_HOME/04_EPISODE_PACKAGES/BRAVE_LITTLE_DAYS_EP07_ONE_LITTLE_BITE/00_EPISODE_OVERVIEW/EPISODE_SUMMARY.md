# Brave Little Days EP07 — "One Little Bite" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi
**Theme:** Trying a new vegetable; being brave enough for "just one little bite."

There's a carrot on Kiko's plate, and without tasting it she says "I don't like it." Mimi — who
loves carrots — makes it playful: "Just one little bite?" Kiko takes a tiny brave bite... crunch!
It's sweet and crunchy — "It's yummy!" She happily takes another bite with Mimi. The episode closes
warm: trying a little bite is brave, and new tastes can be a happy surprise.

**Repeated phrase:** "Just one little bite!"
**Learning point:** Try before deciding; a small brave bite can turn into a happy new favorite.
**Safe exposure:** No force feeding, no disgust/gagging/spitting; gentle invitation and genuine delight.
