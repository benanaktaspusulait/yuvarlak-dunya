# Brave Little Days EP07 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm (6-Step Spine over 8 Shots)

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01) — PROBLEM
A new veggie (a carrot) is on the plate at the Kitchen Nook.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02) — FEELING
Kiko, without tasting: "I don't like it." (hesitant)
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03) — SUPPORT
Mimi (who loves carrots) makes it inviting: "Just one little bite?"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04) — ACTION (brave bite)
Kiko takes one tiny, brave bite.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05) — ACTION (crunch!)
A surprising crunch — it's sweet and crunchy!
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06) — RELIEF
"It's yummy!" Delighted surprise.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07) — RELIEF (again)
Another happy bite together with Mimi.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08) — LESSON (warm close)
Trying a little bite is brave — "Just one little bite!"
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Hesitant refusal → naming it → a friend's playful invitation → a tiny brave bite → surprised crunch
→ delight → happy second bite → proud, warm close. Shown through action, never preached.

---

## Series Continuity

No new characters; no new canon. Mimi's carrot-love (rabbit) makes her the natural encourager.
Uses the canon Kitchen Nook.

## Safe-Exposure Anchors (per `00-CORE/32_..._FORMAT.md` §4)

- **No force feeding.** Mimi invites; Kiko chooses the bite herself.
- No disgust faces, no gagging, no spitting food, no mess.
- Reluctance stays mild ("I don't like it" before tasting); resolves into genuine delight.
