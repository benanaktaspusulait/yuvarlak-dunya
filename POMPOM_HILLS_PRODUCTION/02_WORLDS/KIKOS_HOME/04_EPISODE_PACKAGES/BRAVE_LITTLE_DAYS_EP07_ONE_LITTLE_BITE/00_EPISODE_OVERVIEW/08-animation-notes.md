# Brave Little Days EP07 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat. Never a static
talking pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** unsure at first (small wary face, leaning back a little), curious consideration, a tiny
  brave bite with wide eyes, a surprised "crunch" reaction, then a big happy smile and eager second bite.
- **Mimi:** playful and encouraging — excited carrot-love bounce, inviting gesture, munching alongside
  Kiko happily. Never pushy.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

hesitant (01–02) → curious (03) → brave anticipation (04) → surprised (05) → delight (06) →
happy enjoyment (07) → proud, warm (08).

## Eating Motion (Safe / Positive)

- One small, believable bite; visible crunch; carrot shrinks slightly with each bite.
- **No force feeding**, no disgust face, no gagging, no spitting. The turn is genuinely positive.

## World Notes

Keep Kiko's Home rounded/soft, Kitchen Nook canon. Warm daytime light consistent.
