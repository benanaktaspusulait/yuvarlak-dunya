## Purpose

Establish the messy playroom after a big play session. Show the viewer the full extent of the toy scatter. Kiko stands in the middle, taking it in. This is the "before" state that makes the final clean room so satisfying.

## Video Prompt

Wide establishing shot of Kiko's playroom, child eye level, 28mm lens. Warm diffused daylight through window. Cream walls, light wood floor. Toys scattered everywhere — colourful building blocks (red, blue, green, yellow) spread across the floor, small toy cars near and under a low table, picture books stacked sideways and leaning against furniture. Natural wood toy box in corner, open and empty. Wooden shelf against wall, empty tiers. Low book rack, empty slots. Kiko (coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails) stands in center of room, looking around at the mess. Camera slowly pans left to right to reveal the full extent of the toy scatter. Soft shadows, warm golden lighting, preschool animation style.
