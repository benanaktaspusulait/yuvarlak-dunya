## Video Prompt (MANDATORY SPOKEN DIALOGUE)

Wide establishing shot of a child's playroom, child eye level, 28mm lens. Warm diffused daylight through window. Cream walls, light wood floor. Toys scattered everywhere — colourful building blocks (red, blue, green, yellow) spread across the floor, small toy cars near and under a low table, picture books stacked sideways and leaning against furniture. Natural wood toy box in corner, open and empty. Wooden shelf against wall, empty tiers. Low book rack, empty slots. A young girl with coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails stands in center of room, looking around at the mess. Camera slowly pans left to right to reveal the full extent of the toy scatter. Soft shadows, warm golden lighting, preschool animation style. No spoken dialogue.
