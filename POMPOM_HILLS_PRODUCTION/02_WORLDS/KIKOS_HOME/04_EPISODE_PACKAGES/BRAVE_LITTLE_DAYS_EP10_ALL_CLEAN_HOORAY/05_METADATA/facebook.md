## Post Copy

Kiko's playroom is a wonderful mess after a big play session! Blocks, cars, and books are everywhere. "Oh, it's so messy!" she says, feeling overwhelmed.

But her friend Mimi has a great idea: "Let's tidy up together! One at a time."

Watch as Kiko and Mimi put blocks in the toy box, cars on the shelf, and books in the rack. "A place for everything!" they say as each toy finds its special home.

The room transforms from cluttered to clean — and it feels so good!

**What your child will learn:**
- Mess is a normal result of playing
- Tidying up one step at a time is satisfying
- Having a friend help makes any task easier
- Everything has a special place where it belongs
