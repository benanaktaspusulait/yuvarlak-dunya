# EP10 — Beat Sheet

## 6-Step Spine

| Step | Shot(s) | Beat | Emotion |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. PROBLEM | 01 | Playroom is a mess after playing | Neutral → recognition |
| 2. FEELING | 02 | "Oh, it's so messy!" Overwhelm | Overwhelm, slight anxiety |
| 3. SUPPORT | 03 | "Let's tidy up together! One at a time." | Hope, comfort |
| 4. ACTION | 04–06 | Putting toys away one category at a time | Progress → satisfaction |
| 5. RELIEF | 07 | Room is clean! "All clean!" | Pride, joy, relief |
| 6. LESSON | 08 | "A place for everything!" Wave | Confidence, warmth |

## Safe-Exposure Rules

| Rule | Application |
|---|---|
| No scolding | Neither character scolds or criticises the mess |
| No shame | Kiko is overwhelmed, not ashamed. Mimi normalises the mess. |
| No punishment | Tidying is framed as a fun, satisfying activity together |
| Mess is normal | Mess is presented as a natural result of play |
| Positive framing | Focus on the joy of a clean space, not the wrongness of mess |
| Friend support | Mimi offers help without being asked — normalises asking for help |
| One step at a time | Overwhelm is solved by breaking into small, doable steps |
