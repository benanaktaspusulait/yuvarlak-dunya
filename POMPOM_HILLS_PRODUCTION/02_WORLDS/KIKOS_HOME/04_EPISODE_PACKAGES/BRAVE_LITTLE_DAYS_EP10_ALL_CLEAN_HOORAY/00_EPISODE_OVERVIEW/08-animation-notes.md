## Action Notes — Tidying Mechanics

1. **Blocks**: Kiko picks up 2–3 blocks, walks to toy box, drops them in gently. Repeat. Not rushed.
2. **Cars**: Kiko picks up cars one at a time, places on shelf deliberately. Each car placed with care.
3. **Books**: Kiko slides books into rack slots. Upright, neat. Satisfying placement.
4. **Pace**: Tidying is calm and methodical. Not frantic. The pace says "this is nice, not a chore."
5. **Mess decreases visibly**: Each shot, the floor has fewer items. This is the visual storytelling core.

## Safe-Exposure — Animation

- No frustrated expressions from Kiko (overwhelm is gentle, not angry)
- No eye-rolling, sighing, or impatient body language from anyone
- Mimi never takes over — Kiko does the tidying with Mimi's encouragement
- Final expressions: genuine pride, not exaggerated celebration
