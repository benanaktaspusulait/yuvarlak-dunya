# EP10 — Episode Summary

**Title:** All Clean, Hooray!
**Series:** Brave Little Days
**World:** Kiko's Home — Playroom
**Duration:** 120 seconds (8 shots × 15 s)

## Story

After a big play session, Kiko's playroom is a wonderful mess — blocks on the floor, cars everywhere, books stacked sideways. "Oh, it's so messy!" she says, feeling overwhelmed. Her friend Mimi bounces in: "That's okay! Let's tidy up together. One at a time."

They start with the blocks — into the toy box. "A place for everything!" Mimi says. Then the cars — onto the shelf. Then the books — into the rack. Each item finds its special home. The room transforms from cluttered to clean.

"All clean!" Kiko beams with pride. Both friends say together: "A place for everything!" Kiko waves goodbye from her sparkling-clean playroom.

## Key Messages

- Mess is a normal result of playing — nothing to feel bad about
- Tidying up, done one step at a time, becomes satisfying and fun
- Having a friend help makes any task easier
- Everything has a special place where it belongs
- The reward of a clean, organized space

## Learning Point

Tidying up is presented as a positive, satisfying activity — not a punishment for making a mess. The episode teaches children that mess is normal, and putting things away one by one feels great.

## Safe-Exposure Compliance

No scolding, no shame, no punishment. Mess is normal. Tidying is satisfying. Friend support is encouraged.
