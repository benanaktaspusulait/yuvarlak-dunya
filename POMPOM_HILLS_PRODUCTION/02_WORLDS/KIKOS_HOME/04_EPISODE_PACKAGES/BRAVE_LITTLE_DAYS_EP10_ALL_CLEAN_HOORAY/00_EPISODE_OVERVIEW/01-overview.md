## Story Summary

Kiko has been playing with many toys in her playroom. The room is a wonderful mess — blocks on the floor, cars everywhere, books stacked sideways. "Oh, it's so messy!" Kiko says, overwhelmed. Mimi, her soft-blue rabbit friend, bounces in and says, "That's okay! Let's tidy up together. One at a time." They start with the blocks — into the toy box. Then the cars — onto the shelf. Then the books — into the rack. Each item finds its home. The room transforms. "All clean!" Kiko beams. Mimi: "A place for everything!" Kiko waves goodbye to the audience from her sparkling-clean playroom.

## Learning Point

Mess is a natural result of playing and is nothing to feel ashamed about. Tidying up, done one step at a time with a friend, becomes a satisfying and rewarding activity. Each toy has a special home where it belongs.
