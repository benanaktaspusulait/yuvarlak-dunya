## YouTube Description

Come close, little friends — it's story time with Opa. 💛

In this gentle Opa's Storytime episode from Pompom Hills, Opa tells a warm preschool story
about a little seed deep in the soil that listened to the world around it and grew slowly
into a small green shoot. Kiko and Mimi listen, ask questions, and discover that listening
with your ears and your heart helps you grow.

This calm story supports early learning through gentle themes like listening, patience, and
growing at your own pace.
