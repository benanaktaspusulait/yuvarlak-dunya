# The Little Seed That Listened — Beat Sheet v2.0 (5-Minute Version)

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko welcomes Opa and Mimi into her home reading corner. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa opens the book. Children lean in with curiosity.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): "Once, deep in the soil, there was a little seed." Kiko asks if it was scared in the dark.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Opa explains it was a little scared but not alone. Kiko wonders what was with it.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The seed hears many sounds — rain, worms, wind.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Mimi asks which sound it liked best. Kiko wonders which sound to follow.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa: "It listened to all of them, gently." It didn't rush.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): The seed waits, still listening, deep in the soil.
Beat 9 (Shot 09): Kiko: "That's patient." She connects to herself.
Beat 10 (Shot 10): A tiny drop of water falls from above toward the seed.
Beat 11 (Shot 11): Mimi: "Did it feel it?" Opa: "Yes, it felt the cool water."
Beat 12 (Shot 12): The seed feels the water and begins to grow a tiny root.
Beat 13 (Shot 13): "It's growing!" Kiko and Mimi react with soft joy.
Beat 14 (Shot 14): A small green shoot pushes through the soil.
Beat 15 (Shot 15): Opa: "It grew when it was ready." The lesson is revealed.
Beat 16 (Shot 16): Mimi smiles softly, feeling the warmth of the story.
Beat 17 (Shot 17): Kiko connects it to herself: "I listen with my ears and my heart." Micro-moment.
Beat 18 (Shot 18): Opa: "That is a wonderful way to listen."
Beat 19 (Shot 19): Quiet warm hold. No new dialogue, just warmth.
Beat 20 (Shot 20): Closing hold. Book stays open on the lap. No new dialogue.
```
