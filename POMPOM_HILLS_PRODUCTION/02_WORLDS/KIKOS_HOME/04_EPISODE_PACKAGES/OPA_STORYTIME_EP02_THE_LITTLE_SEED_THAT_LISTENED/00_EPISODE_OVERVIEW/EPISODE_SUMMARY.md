# Opa's Storytime: The Little Seed That Listened — Episode Summary

---

## Story

Kiko invites Opa and Mimi into her home's reading corner. Opa tells a gentle story
about a little seed underground that hears many sounds and learns to listen
patiently until it is ready to grow. Kiko and Mimi ask questions and react
throughout the story.

## Learning

Listening • Patience • Growing slowly

## Characters

Opa, Kiko, Mimi

## World

Kiko's Home — Reading Corner
