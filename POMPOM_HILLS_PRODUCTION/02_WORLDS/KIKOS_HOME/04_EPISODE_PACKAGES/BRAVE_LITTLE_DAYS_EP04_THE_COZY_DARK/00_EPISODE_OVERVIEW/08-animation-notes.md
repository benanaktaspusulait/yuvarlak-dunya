# Brave Little Days EP04 — Animation Notes

Calm, still, cozy — the gentlest episode. Every 2–3 seconds still has a soft beat (a blink, a
blanket pull, a glow, a yawn). Never a frozen pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** uneasy at first (blanket pulled up, small worried glances), softening as the light
  glows, wonder at the moon, then snuggling, slow blinks, and a gentle yawn into sleepy calm.
- **Opa:** slow, warm, wise — a gentle lean-in, a soft gesture to light the star and to the moon.
  A calm, unwavering safe presence.
- **Mimi:** silent, asleep, cuddled beside Kiko — only soft breathing motion.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

uneasy (01–02) → reassured (03) → warm wonder (04–05) → snug and safe (06) → sleepy calm (07)
→ cozy goodnight (08).

## Safe-Exposure / Night Rules

- Night stays **soft blue, never black.** The dimming in Shots 01–02 is gentle; the room stays
  readable. **No scary shadows, monsters, silhouettes, or ghosts.**
- The star nightlight glow is warm and soft, blending with the moonlight. The friendly moon is
  round and kind. No pitch darkness at any moment.
