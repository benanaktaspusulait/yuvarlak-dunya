# Brave Little Days EP04 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm (6-Step Spine over 8 Shots)

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01) — PROBLEM
Night at Kiko's Home. Opa is there for goodnight; the light dims and the room grows dim.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02) — FEELING
Kiko pulls the blanket up: "I'm scared of the dark."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03) — SUPPORT
Opa, calm: "It's okay to feel scared. The dark can be soft and friendly."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04) — ACTION (little light)
Opa lights a small glowing star nightlight; a warm glow fills the room.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05) — ACTION (friendly moon)
Through the round window they see the friendly moon and gentle stars.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06) — RELIEF
The room feels soft and safe; Kiko snuggles, Mimi cuddled beside her.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07) — RELIEF (settle)
Kiko yawns, calm and cozy: "The dark can be cozy."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08) — LESSON (warm close)
Opa: "Goodnight, little light." Warm, sleepy resolution.
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Uneasy in the dimming room → naming the fear → a wise elder's calm → a warm little light →
wonder at the friendly moon → snug and safe → sleepy contentment → cozy goodnight.
Shown through action, never preached.

---

## Series Continuity

An emotional (non-helper) episode; support comes from Opa the guide. No new characters.
Mimi appears as a silent sleeping cuddle.

## Safe-Exposure Anchors (per `00-CORE/32_..._FORMAT.md` §4 and `SERIES_STRUCTURE.md` §11)

- Night is **soft blue, never black.** The room may dim but stays soft and readable.
- Friendly moon; gentle stars. **No scary shadows, monsters, silhouettes, or ghosts.**
- A warm nightlight glow makes the room cozy. No pitch darkness at any point.
- Fear stays gentle (blanket up), no crying/panic; Opa is calm and present throughout.
