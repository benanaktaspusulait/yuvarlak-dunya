# Brave Little Days EP04 — "The Cozy Dark" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Sleeping Corner (night)
**Characters:** Kiko, Opa (Mimi as a silent sleeping cuddle)
**Theme:** It's okay to be scared of the dark; with a little light and someone you love, the dark can be cozy.

At bedtime, Opa is there to say goodnight. As the room dims, Kiko is scared of the dark. Opa
stays calm: "It's okay to feel scared. The dark can be soft and friendly." He lights a small
glowing star nightlight, and a warm glow fills the room. Through the round window the friendly
moon says goodnight. The room feels soft and safe; Kiko snuggles in with Mimi, yawns, and says
"The dark can be cozy." Opa smiles: "Goodnight, little light."

**Repeated phrase:** "The dark can be cozy."
**Learning point:** Fear of the dark is normal; a little light and a loved one make it cozy.
**Safe exposure:** Night is soft blue (never black), friendly moon, warm nightlight, no scary
shadows or monsters.
