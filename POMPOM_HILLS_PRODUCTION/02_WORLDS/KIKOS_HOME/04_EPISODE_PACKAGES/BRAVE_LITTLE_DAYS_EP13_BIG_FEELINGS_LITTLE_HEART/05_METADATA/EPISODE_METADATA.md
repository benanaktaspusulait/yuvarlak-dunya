## Content Description

### Logline
Kiko's block tower falls down and she feels sad. With Mimi's gentle guidance, she learns that it's okay to feel sad, takes a deep breath, and together they rebuild — stronger than before.

### Synopsis
In this episode, Kiko experiences sadness when her carefully built block tower collapses. Rather than dismissing her feelings, Mimi validates them with the phrase "It's okay to feel sad." Together, they practice a simple breathing technique that helps Kiko regulate her emotions. The episode concludes with Kiko and Mimi rebuilding the tower together, demonstrating that feelings pass and that support from friends helps us through difficult moments.
