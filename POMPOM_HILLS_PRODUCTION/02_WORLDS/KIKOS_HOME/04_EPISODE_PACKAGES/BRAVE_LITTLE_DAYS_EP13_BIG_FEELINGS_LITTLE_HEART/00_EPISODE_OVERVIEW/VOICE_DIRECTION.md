## Kiko — Voice Direction

### General Tone
- **Age:** 4-6 years old
- **Quality:** Soft, clear, childlike
- **Accent:** Neutral, easy to understand
- **Energy:** Varies with emotion (sad → hopeful → joyful)

### Line-by-Line Direction

#### Shot 01: "Oh no!"
- **Emotion:** Surprise, mild distress
- **Volume:** Medium (not shouting)
- **Pitch:** Slightly higher (surprise)
- **Speed:** Quick exclamation
- **Note:** Not scared, just surprised

#### Shot 02: "I feel sad."
- **Emotion:** Genuine sadness
- **Volume:** Soft, quiet
- **Pitch:** Lower than usual
- **Speed:** Slow, deliberate
- **Note:** She's really feeling it — not whining

#### Shot 04: "Okay." (response to Mimi)
- **Emotion:** Trusting, willing
- **Volume:** Soft
- **Pitch:** Neutral
- **Speed:** Normal
- **Note:** Simple acceptance

#### Shot 06: "I feel better now!"
- **Emotion:** Relief, emerging happiness
- **Volume:** Medium, growing
- **Pitch:** Rising, brighter
- **Speed:** Slightly faster
- **Note:** Genuine surprise at feeling better

#### Shot 07: "Let's build it again! Together!"
- **Emotion:** Excitement, determination
- **Volume:** Medium-loud (enthusiastic)
- **Pitch:** Higher, joyful
- **Speed:** Energetic
- **Note:** She's taking action!

#### Shot 08: "It's okay to feel!"
- **Emotion:** Confident, joyful
- **Volume:** Clear, projected
- **Pitch:** Bright
- **Speed:** Deliberate (teaching moment)
- **Note:** She believes it now

## Mimi — Voice Direction

### General Tone
- **Quality:** Warm, soothing, slightly deeper than Kiko
- **Energy:** Consistently calm and supportive
- **Approach:** Never preachy — always empathetic

### Line-by-Line Direction

#### Shot 03: "It's okay to feel sad, Kiko."
- **Emotion:** Gentle validation
- **Volume:** Soft, intimate
- **Pitch:** Lower, warm
- **Speed:** Slow, caring
- **Note:** This is the key line — make it count

#### Shot 04: "When we feel sad, we can breathe together. Can you breathe with me?"
- **Emotion:** Patient, guiding
- **Volume:** Soft
- **Pitch:** Gentle
- **Speed:** Slow, clear
- **Note:** Simple language, easy to follow

#### Shot 05: "In... and out..."
- **Emotion:** Peaceful, meditative
- **Volume:** Very soft
- **Pitch:** Low, calming
- **Speed:** Very slow (2 seconds per word)
- **Note:** She's breathing with Kiko — not just instructing

#### Shot 06: "That's wonderful!"
- **Emotion:** Happy for Kiko
- **Volume:** Warm, medium
- **Pitch:** Slightly higher (pleased)
- **Speed:** Normal
- **Note:** Genuine happiness, not patronizing

#### Shot 07: "Together!"
- **Emotion:** Encouraging, playful
- **Volume:** Enthusiastic
- **Pitch:** Higher
- **Speed:** Quick
- **Note:** Matching Kiko's energy

#### Shot 08: "It's okay to feel!"
- **Emotion:** Joyful, confident
- **Volume:** Clear, together with Kiko
- **Pitch:** Warm
- **Speed:** Deliberate
- **Note:** United message

## Recording Notes

### Session Setup
- Record Kiko and Mimi separately
- Both voices should be clean, no background noise
- Consistent mic distance throughout

### Post-Processing
- Minimal EQ — keep voices natural
- No heavy reverb (playroom is small)
- Slight compression for consistency
- Breathing sounds: Record separately if needed

### Quality Check
- [ ] All lines are clearly understandable
- [ ] Emotional arc is evident in delivery
- [ ] No line feels forced or unnatural
- [ ] Breathing sounds are authentic
- [ ] Final message is empowering, not preachy
