# Music Direction — EP13

## Overall Musical Approach

The music in EP13 supports the emotional journey without overwhelming it. It's warm, simple, and child-friendly — like a gentle friend in the background.

---

## Musical Structure

```
Shot 01: [Playful → Shock]
Shot 02: [Sad, gentle]
Shot 03: [Warm, validating]
Shot 04: [Calming, preparing]
Shot 05: [Meditative, breathing]
Shot 06: [Hopeful, rising]
Shot 07: [Playful, building]
Shot 08: [Joyful, celebratory]
```

## Musical Themes

### Main Theme (EP13 Variation)
- Simple, memorable melody
- Played softly throughout
- Returns in full at Shot 08
- Should be hummable by children

### Sadness Motif
- Descending notes
- Minor key hints
- Soft piano only
- Brief — doesn't linger

### Breathing Motif
- Two notes: up (inhale), down (exhale)
- Very simple
- Could become a series signature
- Children can learn it

---

## Instrument Palette

| Instrument | Use | Character |
|------------|-----|-----------|
| Piano | Primary melody | Warm, clear |
| Xylophone | Playful moments | Bright, childlike |
| Strings | Emotional support | Warm, sustaining |
| Soft pads | Atmosphere | Gentle, ambient |
| Chime | Key moments | Magical, affirming |

### Instruments to Avoid
- No drums (too energetic)
- No brass (too bold)
- No electronic sounds (too modern)
- No minor keys for long (keep hopeful)

## Production Notes

### Recording
- Record piano first (melody foundation)
- Add strings for emotion
- Xylophone for playfulness
- Mix gently — music supports, doesn't dominate

### Mix Balance
- Voice: -6 dB (priority)
- Music: -12 dB (support)
- SFX: -10 dB (complement)

### Quality Check
- [ ] Music doesn't overpower voices
- [ ] Emotional arc is clear musically
- [ ] Breathing shot (05) is appropriately quiet
- [ ] Final moment is warm and celebratory
- [ ] No jarring transitions
