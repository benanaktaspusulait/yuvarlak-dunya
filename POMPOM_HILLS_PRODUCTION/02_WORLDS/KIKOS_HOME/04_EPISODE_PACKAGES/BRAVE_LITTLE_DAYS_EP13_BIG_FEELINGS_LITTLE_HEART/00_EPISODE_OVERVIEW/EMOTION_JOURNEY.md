## Emotional Regulation Techniques Modeled
1. **Naming the emotion** ("I feel sad")
2. **Validation** ("It's okay to feel sad")
3. **Breathing together** ("In... and out...")
4. **Peer support** (Mimi's presence)
5. **Action after calm** (rebuilding together)

## Child Takeaway
When you feel sad, you can:
1. Say what you feel
2. Know it's okay
3. Take deep breaths
4. Feel better with help
