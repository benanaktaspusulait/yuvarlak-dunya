## Child-Friendly Elements

### What Children Learn
1. **Sadness is normal** — even when something small happens
2. **Naming helps** — saying "I feel sad" makes it better
3. **Breathing works** — a concrete tool they can use
4. **Friends help** — it's okay to need support
5. **Feelings pass** — sadness doesn't last forever

### What Children Should NOT Take Away
- ✗ That sadness is bad or should be avoided
- ✗ That they must always be happy
- ✗ That they need someone else to fix their feelings
- ✗ That big emotions are dangerous
