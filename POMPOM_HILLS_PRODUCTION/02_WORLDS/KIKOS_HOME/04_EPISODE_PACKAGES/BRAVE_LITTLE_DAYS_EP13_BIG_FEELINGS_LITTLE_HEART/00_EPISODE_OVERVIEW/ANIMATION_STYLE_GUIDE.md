# Animation Style Guide — EP13

## Overall Style
**Brave Little Days** uses a soft, friendly 2D animation style designed for toddlers and preschoolers. Characters are expressive but not exaggerated. Backgrounds are warm and inviting.

---

## Character Animation

### Kiko
| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| Proportions | Large head, small body (chibi-inspired) |
| Eyes | Large, expressive, simple shapes |
| Mouth | Simple curve, easily readable emotions |
| Movement | Gentle, slightly bouncy |
| Idle animation | Subtle breathing, occasional blink |

### Mimi
| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| Proportions | Slightly larger than Kiko, round body |
| Ears | Floppy, with subtle bounce animation |
| Eyes | Warm, kind, slightly smaller than Kiko's |
| Movement | Smooth, reassuring gestures |
| Idle animation | Ear twitch, gentle sway |

---

## Emotion Animation Keyframes

### Sadness (Shots 02-04)
```
Key pose: Eyebrows up-center, mouth downturned
Secondary: Shoulders drop, head tilts down
Micro: Eyes slightly glossy (subtle)
Timing: Slow, weighted movement
```

### Calm (Shot 05)
```
Key pose: Eyes closed, relaxed face
Secondary: Chest rises/falls with breath
Micro: Slight smile forming
Timing: Very slow, meditative pace
```

### Joy (Shots 06-08)
```
Key pose: Wide smile, eyes bright
Secondary: Slight bounce, arms up
Micro: Sparkle in eyes (subtle)
Timing: Energetic but controlled

```

## Background Style

### Kiko's Playroom
| Element | Style |
|---------|-------|
| Walls | Soft cream, slight texture |
| Floor | Warm wood grain, simple |
| Window | Round or arched, soft light |
| Furniture | Rounded edges, no sharp corners |
| Toys | Simple shapes, bright colors |
| Lighting | Warm, golden, no harsh shadows |

### Color Temperature
- Warm overall (~3200K feel)
- Slightly brighter during happy moments
- Slightly softer during sad moments
- Returns to warm for resolution

---

## Motion Principles

### Timing
| Emotion | Speed | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Happy | Medium-fast | Bouncy, energetic |
| Sad | Slow | Weighted, deliberate |
| Calm | Very slow | Meditative, breathing pace |
| Joyful recovery | Medium | Building energy |

### Squash and Stretch
- **Subtle** throughout
- **Minimal** during sad moments
- **Slight** during happy moments
- **None** during breathing (stillness is important)

### Anticipation
- Small wind-up before big emotions
- Kiko's face shows feeling before body reacts
- Mimi's ears anticipate her expressions

## Lip Sync Guidelines

### Kiko's Lines
| Line | Mouth Shape | Duration |
|------|-------------|----------|
| "Oh no!" | O → open | 1s |
| "I feel sad." | Closed → open → closed | 2s |
| "I feel better now!" | Open → wide smile | 2s |
| "Let's build it again!" | Open, energetic | 2s |

### Mimi's Lines
| Line | Mouth Shape | Duration |
|------|-------------|----------|
| "It's okay to feel sad." | Gentle open-close | 2.5s |
| "In... and out..." | Slow, deliberate | 4s |
| "That's wonderful!" | Warm smile | 1.5s |

---

## Visual Effects

### Minimal Effects
- **Breathing glow:** Subtle warm glow during Shot 05
- **Sparkle:** Very subtle eye sparkle on joy moments
- **Block fall:** Simple motion blur, no debris
- **Heart:** Optional small heart icon on validation (Shot 03)

### What NOT to Use
- No flashing lights
- No rapid cuts
- No screen shake
- No exaggerated expressions (tantrum-like)
