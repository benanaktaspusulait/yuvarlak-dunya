## Dialogue Rules

- Maximum 2 short lines per shot. Soft, playful preschool voices. No shouting.
- Encouragement is warm and inviting, never forceful. Natural childlike speech; simple words.
- "Not perfect" is acknowledged warmly, never corrected or criticized.
