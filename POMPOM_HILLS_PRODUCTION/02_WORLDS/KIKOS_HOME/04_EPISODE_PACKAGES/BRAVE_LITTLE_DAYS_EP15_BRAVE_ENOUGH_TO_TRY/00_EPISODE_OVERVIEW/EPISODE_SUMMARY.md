# Brave Little Days EP15 — "Brave Enough to Try" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Kiko's Home — Playroom
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi
**Theme:** Trying a new activity; courage to attempt something new.

Mimi is building a colourful block tower in the Playroom. Kiko watches but says "I can't do that."
Mimi gently encourages her: "You don't have to be perfect. Just one small try!" Kiko places one
block... then another. "Look, I'm doing it!" Her tower is wobbly and not perfect, but it's hers.
"It's not perfect... but it's mine! And I tried it!" The episode closes warm: trying is brave,
and imperfect is beautiful.

**Repeated phrase:** "One small try!"
**Learning point:** Trying new things takes courage, and you don't have to be perfect to succeed.
**Safe exposure:** No failure humiliation, no comparison, no tantrum, no force. "Not perfect" is
celebrated. Gentle encouragement and genuine pride.
