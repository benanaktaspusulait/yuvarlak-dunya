# Brave Little Days EP15 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm (6-Step Spine over 8 Shots)

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01) — PROBLEM
Mimi builds a colourful block structure. Kiko watches.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02) — FEELING
Kiko: "I can't do that." She doubts herself.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03) — SUPPORT
Mimi: "You don't have to be perfect. Just one small try!"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04) — ACTION (first block)
Kiko places one block. First step!
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05) — ACTION (building)
Another block! "Look, I'm doing it!" Growing confidence.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06) — RELIEF
The structure is wobbly but standing. "It's not perfect..."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07) — RELIEF (again)
"...but it's mine! And I tried it!" Proud smile.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08) — LESSON (warm close)
"One small try!" Both smile. Kiko waves proudly.
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Doubt → "I can't" → gentle encouragement → first tentative step → surprise at doing it →
"not perfect" acknowledged → pride in trying → warm proud close. Shown through action, never preached.

---

## Series Continuity

No new characters; no new canon. Uses the canon Playroom in Kiko's Home. Colourful building blocks
are the central prop. Mimi is already skilled; Kiko is the learner.

---

## Safe-Exposure Anchors (per `00-CORE/32_..._FORMAT.md` §4)

- **No failure humiliation.** Kiko's doubt is mild and internal; no one laughs or points.
- **No comparison.** Mimi never says hers is better; she only encourages.
- **"Not perfect" is celebrated.** The wobbly structure is called charming, not corrected.
- No tantrum, no crying, no force, no pressure. Gentle invitation and genuine pride.
