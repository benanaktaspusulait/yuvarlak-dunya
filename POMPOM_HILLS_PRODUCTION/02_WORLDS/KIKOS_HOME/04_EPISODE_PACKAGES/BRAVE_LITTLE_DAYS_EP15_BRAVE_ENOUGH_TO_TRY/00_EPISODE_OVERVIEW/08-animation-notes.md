# Brave Little Days EP15 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat. Never a static
talking pose; never a long empty pause.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** watching with mild uncertainty (Shot 01), small unsure face and slight lean back (Shot 02),
  curious consideration (Shot 03), careful reaching and placing one block with wide eyes (Shot 04),
  surprise and delight at building (Shot 05), head tilt at wobbly tower (Shot 06), proud smile and
  gentle wave (Shots 07–08).
- **Mimi:** focused building with satisfaction (Shot 01), pauses kindly (Shot 02), gentle encouraging
  gesture toward loose blocks (Shot 03), supportive watching (Shot 04), happy bounce (Shot 05),
  warm reassurance (Shot 06), warm smile (Shot 07), proud together (Shot 08). Never pushy.

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

watching uncertainly (01) → doubt (02) → encouragement (03) → tentative first step (04) →
surprise and growing confidence (05) → "not perfect" acknowledgment (06) → pride (07) →
warm proud close (08).

## Block Building Motion (Safe / Positive)

- Mimi's structure: already complete in Shot 01, stays neat and symmetrical throughout.
- Kiko's structure: grows from nothing (Shot 01–03) to 1 block (Shot 04) to 2 blocks (Shot 05)
  to a small wobbly tower (Shot 06–07). Each block placed gently and carefully.
- **No tower collapse, no frustration, no comparison.** The wobble is charming, not a failure.

## World Notes

Keep Kiko's Home rounded/soft, Playroom canon. Warm daytime light consistent.
