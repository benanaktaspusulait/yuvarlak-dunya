# Story Arc — EP14

## Three-Act Structure

### Act 1: The Challenge (Shots 01-02)
**Setup**: Kiko's living area is messy after play.
**Conflict**: Kiko feels overwhelmed—"There's too much!"
**Emotion**: Frustration, feeling alone

### Act 2: The Wisdom (Shot 03)
**Turning Point**: Opa arrives with wisdom.
**Quote**: "Many hands make light work!"
**Shift**: From alone to together
**Action**: Kiko, Mimi, and Opa each choose a task

### Act 3: The Triumph (Shots 04-08)
**Action**: Each character cleans their section
- Shot 04: Kiko picks up stuffed animals
- Shot 05: Mimi folds blankets (Quality Reset)
- Shot 06: Opa arranges books
**Celebration**: "We did it together!"
**Resolution**: "We do it together!" (final wave)

## Emotional Journey
1. Overwhelm → 2. Hope → 3. Action → 4. Joy → 5. Pride
