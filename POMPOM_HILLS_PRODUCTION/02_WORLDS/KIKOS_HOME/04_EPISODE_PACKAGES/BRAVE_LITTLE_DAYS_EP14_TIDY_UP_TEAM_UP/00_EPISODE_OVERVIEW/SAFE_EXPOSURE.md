# Safe-Exposure Guidelines — EP14

## Principle
Mess is normal. No scolding. Teamwork is joyful.

## What We DO NOT Show
- ❌ Any character scolding another about the mess
- ❌ Mess presented as "bad" or "naughty"
- ❌ Cleanliness presented as a moral obligation
- ❌ Punishment or consequences for mess
- ❌ Characters sighing in disapproval

## What We DO Show
- ✅ Mess as a natural result of playing and living
- ✅ Cleaning as a shared, positive activity
- ✅ Each character contributing without complaint
- ✅ Joy in the process, not just the result
- ✅ Gentle encouragement, never pressure

## Dialogue Guidelines
- Kiko's "There's too much!" is about the TASK SIZE, not the mess being wrong
- Opa's wisdom is about TEAMWORK, not about cleaning being necessary
- No character says "You should clean up" or "Look at this mess!"
- Focus is on "how" (together), not "why" (because it's messy)

## Visual Guidelines
- Mess is colorful and playful, not disgusting
- Tidy state is inviting, not sterile
- Characters smile throughout the process
- Body language is relaxed, not tense

## Emotional Safety
- Children watching should never feel ashamed of their own messes
- The episode models asking for help positively
- Friendship is the solution, not discipline
