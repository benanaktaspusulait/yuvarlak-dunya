# Repeated Phrase — EP14

## Episode Phrase
**"We do it together!"**

## Occurrences (3-4 times)

### 1. Shot 04 (Transition)
- **Context**: Kiko and Mimi start their tasks
- **Speaker**: Kiko (suggested)
- **Variation**: "We do it together!"
- **Purpose**: Affirming the decision to work as a team

### 2. Shot 07 (Celebration)
- **Context**: All tasks completed
- **Speaker**: Mimi
- **Variation**: "We did it together!"
- **Purpose**: Celebrating accomplishment

### 3. Shot 08 (Finale)
- **Context**: Final moment, waving to camera
- **Speaker**: All three characters
- **Variation**: "We do it together!"
- **Purpose**: Reinforcing the theme, audience connection

### 4. (Optional) Shot 05 or 06
- **Context**: During the work
- **Speaker**: Could be any character
- **Variation**: "We do it together!" (as encouragement)
- **Purpose**: Mid-task morale boost

## Phrase Usage Guidelines
- Always positive and joyful
- Never forced or repetitive-feeling
- Natural delivery, like a cheer
- Can vary slightly in wording ("did it" vs "do it")
- Should feel earned, not preachy
