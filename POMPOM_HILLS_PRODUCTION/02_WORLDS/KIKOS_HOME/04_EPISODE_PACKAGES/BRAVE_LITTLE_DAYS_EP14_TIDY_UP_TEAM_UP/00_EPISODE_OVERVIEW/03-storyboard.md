## Frame Descriptions

### Shot 01 — So Messy
**Frame Start**: Wide shot of Kiko's living area. Stuffed animals (pastel pink, blue, lavender) are scattered on the rug. A cream blanket is crumpled on the couch. Picture books are spread on the floor.

**Frame End**: The mess is clearly visible. It's colourful and natural — a result of happy play.

**Key Moment**: The viewer sees the full extent of the mess. It's not distressing — just big.

### Shot 02 — Too Much
**Frame Start**: Kiko stands in the middle of the mess. She picks up a stuffed animal. She sees more ahead.

**Frame End**: Her shoulders drop slightly. "There's too much!" She feels overwhelmed.

**Key Moment**: Kiko's physical expression of overwhelm — shoulders dropping, looking around.

### Shot 03 — Opa Arrives
**Frame Start**: Opa enters gently from screen-right. Warm brown shawl, golden glasses. Mimi follows — soft-blue rabbit, yellow top.

**Frame End**: Both look at Kiko with warm smiles. Opa speaks his wisdom. Mimi adds encouragement.

**Key Moment**: "Many hands make light work!" — the turning point from overwhelm to hope.

### Shot 04 — Kiko Picks Up
**Frame Start**: Kiko picks up a pastel pink stuffed animal. She walks to the toy bin.

**Frame End**: She places it inside. She picks up another. The first section is being cleared.

**Key Moment**: Each stuffed animal going into the bin is a small victory.

### Shot 05 — Mimi Folds (Quality Reset)
**Frame Start**: Mimi holds a crumpled cream blanket. She begins to fold it.

**Frame End**: The blanket is neatly folded. She places it on the couch. The second section is done.

**Key Moment**: Mimi's careful folding — she takes pride in doing it well.

### Shot 06 — Opa Organizes
**Frame Start**: Opa holds a picture book. He walks to the shelf.

**Frame End**: He places it upright, cover facing out. He arranges the others beside it. The third section is done.

**Key Moment**: Opa's gentle method — each book placed with care.

### Shot 07 — All Done Together
**Frame Start**: Wide shot. The room is tidy. Three characters stand in it.

**Frame End**: They look at each other. Kiko speaks. "All done together!"

**Key Moment**: The transformation from mess to tidy — and the pride of having done it together.

### Shot 08 — We Do It Together
**Frame Start**: Kiko speaks the repeated phrase with full conviction.

**Frame End**: All three wave goodbye. Tidy room behind them. Warm close.

**Key Moment**: The lesson lands. "We do it together!" — the episode's message.
