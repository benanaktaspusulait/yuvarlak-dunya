### Story Summary

Kiko's living area is messy after a play session — stuffed animals, blankets, and books are everywhere. She tries to tidy up alone but it's too much: "There's too much!" She feels overwhelmed. Opa the wise owl arrives gently: "Many hands make light work!" Mimi the rabbit adds: "We do it together!" Each character takes a section — Kiko picks up stuffed animals, Mimi folds blankets, Opa arranges books on the shelf. Working side by side, the mess disappears quickly. "All done together!" "We do it together!" Three happy faces look at the tidy room.

### Emotion Arc

```
Overwhelm → Encouragement → Determination → Rhythm → Focus → Accomplishment → Celebration
```

### Learning Point

Working together as a team makes big tasks feel small. When friends help each other, even a big mess becomes a shared adventure — and the result feels wonderful.

### Repeated Phrase

"We do it together!" — appears 3-4 times across the episode, building from invitation to full declaration.
