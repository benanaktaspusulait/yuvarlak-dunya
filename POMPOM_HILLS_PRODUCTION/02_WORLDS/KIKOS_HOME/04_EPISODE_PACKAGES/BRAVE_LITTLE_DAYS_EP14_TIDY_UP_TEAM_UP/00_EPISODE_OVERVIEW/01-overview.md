## Story Summary

Kiko's living area is messy after a play session — stuffed animals, blankets, and books are everywhere. She tries to tidy up alone but it's too much: "There's too much!" She feels overwhelmed. Opa the wise owl arrives gently: "Many hands make light work!" Mimi the rabbit chimes in: "We do it together!" Each character takes a section — Kiko picks up stuffed animals, Mimi folds blankets, Opa arranges books on the shelf. Working side by side, the mess disappears quickly. "All done together!" "We do it together!" Three happy faces look at the tidy room.

## Micro-Moment

Kiko's shoulders drop when she looks around the messy room and says "There's too much!" It is a small, real moment of overwhelm — not panic, just the feeling of something being bigger than one person. This micro-moment carries the emotional pivot of the episode: from solo struggle to team solution.
