# Location Guide — EP14

## Primary Location
**Kiko's Home — Living Area**

### Visual Description
- Warm, cozy living space
- Natural daytime lighting (warm tones)
- Comfortable furniture (couch, shelves, small table)
- Windows with soft light streaming in
- Child-friendly decor with colorful elements

### Props & Set Dressing
**Messy State (Shots 01-04):**
- Stuffed animals scattered on floor
- Blankets draped over furniture
- Books piled haphazardly
- Toys here and there

**Tidy State (Shots 07-08):**
- Stuffed animals in basket/bin
- Blankets neatly folded on couch
- Books arranged on shelf
- Floor clear and inviting

### Lighting
- Warm daytime ambient light
- Soft shadows, no harsh contrasts
- Gentle golden tones throughout

### Mood
- Feels like home
- Safe and welcoming
- Playful but not chaotic
- Warm and inviting when tidy
