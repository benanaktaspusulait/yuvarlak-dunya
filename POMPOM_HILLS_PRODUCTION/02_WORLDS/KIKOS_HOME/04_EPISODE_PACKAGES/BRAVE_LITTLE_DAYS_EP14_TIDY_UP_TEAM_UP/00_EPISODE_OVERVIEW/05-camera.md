# EP14 — Camera

## Camera Identity

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Focal Length | 28–35mm range |
| Eye Level | Child eye level (approx. 80–100cm from ground) |
| Style | Warm, intimate, stable |
| Movement | Minimal — mostly static with gentle reframing |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 |
| Frame Rate | 24fps |

## Camera Rules

1. **Child Eye Level**: All shots at child height. No looking down on characters.
2. **Static Preferred**: Minimal camera movement. Let action fill the frame.
3. **Warm Framing**: Characters comfortably within frame, not crowded.
4. **Mess Visibility**: Shot 01 must show full extent of mess clearly.
5. **Consistent Depth**: Same depth of field across all shots for continuity.

## Shot-by-Shot Camera Notes

### Shot 01 — So Messy
- **Framing**: Wide shot, full living area visible
- **Focus**: The mess — stuffed animals, blankets, books
- **Lighting**: Warm daylight from screen-left
- **Background**: Cream walls, couch, shelf, toy bin

### Shot 02 — Too Much
- **Framing**: Medium shot, Kiko centred
- **Focus**: Kiko's face and body language
- **Movement**: Static — let her expression carry the moment
- **Mess**: Visible around her but secondary to her emotion

### Shot 03 — Opa Arrives
- **Framing**: Wide shot, all three characters
- **Focus**: Opa and Mimi entering, Kiko's reaction
- **Movement**: Static — let their entrance be the motion
- **Opa**: Screen-right, warm brown shawl, golden glasses
- **Mimi**: Beside Opa, soft-blue, yellow top

### Shot 04 — Kiko Picks Up
- **Framing**: Medium shot, Kiko centred
- **Focus**: Kiko's hands and the stuffed animals
- **Movement**: Static — Kiko moves within frame
- **Props**: Stuffed animals being placed in toy bin

### Shot 05 — Mimi Folds (Quality Reset Anchor)
- **Framing**: Medium shot, Mimi centred
- **Focus**: Mimi's hands folding blanket
- **Movement**: Static
- **Quality Check**: Re-verify all character positions, colours, lighting

### Shot 06 — Opa Organizes
- **Framing**: Medium shot, Opa centred
- **Focus**: Opa's hands placing books on shelf
- **Movement**: Static
- **Props**: Books being placed upright on shelf

### Shot 07 — All Done Together
- **Framing**: Wide shot, all three characters visible
- **Focus**: The tidy room + three happy faces
- **Movement**: Static
- **Mess**: Gone — transformation visible

### Shot 08 — We Do It Together
- **Framing**: Medium shot, all three visible
- **Focus**: Kiko speaking, then all waving
- **Movement**: Static
- **Final Frame**: All three waving, tidy room behind them
