# Character Guide — EP14

## Kiko
- **Role**: Main protagonist
- **Appearance**: Coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails
- **Screen Position**: Screen-left
- **Personality**: Enthusiastic, can feel overwhelmed, learns to accept help
- **Arc in EP14**: From overwhelmed to empowered through teamwork
- **Lines**: Multiple (main dialogue)

## Mimi
- **Role**: Supportive friend
- **Species**: Soft-blue rabbit
- **Appearance**: Yellow top
- **Personality**: Helpful, cheerful, reliable
- **Arc in EP14**: Steps in to help without being asked, shows quiet leadership
- **Lines**: Supporting dialogue

## Opa
- **Role**: Wise mentor
- **Species**: Owl
- **Appearance**: Warm brown feathers, glasses
- **Personality**: Wise, gentle, encouraging
- **Arc in EP14**: Arrives to offer wisdom, keeps presence minimal
- **Lines**: 1-2 lines only (max)
- **Key Line**: "Many hands make light work!"

## Character Interactions
- Kiko ←→ Mimi: Friends working side by side
- Kiko ←→ Opa: Student learning from mentor
- Mimi ←→ Opa: Mutual respect, shared wisdom
