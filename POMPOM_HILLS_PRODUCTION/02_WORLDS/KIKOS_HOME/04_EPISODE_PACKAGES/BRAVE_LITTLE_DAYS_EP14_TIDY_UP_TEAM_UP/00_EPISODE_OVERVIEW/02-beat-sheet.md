## Beat Details

### Beat 1 — PROBLEM (Shot 01)

**Setup**: Kiko's living area is messy after a play session. Stuffed animals are on the floor, blankets are crumpled, books are scattered. The mess is natural and normal — a result of joyful play.

**Visual**: The room is colourful and cluttered but not distressing. It looks like a child's space after happy activity.

**Emotional State**: Playful chaos — the mess is not bad, it's just big.

### Beat 2 — FEELING (Shot 02)

**Kiko's Reaction**: She looks around. She picks up one stuffed animal, then another. She sees more. Her shoulders drop slightly. "There's too much!"

**Internal Conflict**: She wants to tidy, but doing it alone feels impossible. Not angry — just overwhelmed.

**Emotional State**: Overwhelm — the task is bigger than one person.

### Beat 3 — SUPPORT (Shot 03)

**Opa Arrives**: Gently. He sees the mess and doesn't judge. He speaks one wise line: "Many hands make light work!"

**Mimi Arrives**: Warm and bright. She adds: "We do it together!"

**Kiko's Response**: She looks at them. Hope rises. She considers. She nods.

**Emotional State**: Encouraged — help has arrived.

### Beat 4 — ACTION (Shots 04-06)

**Shot 04 — Kiko's Section**: Kiko picks up stuffed animals. She puts them in the toy bin one by one. Each one is a small victory.

**Shot 05 — Mimi's Section**: Mimi folds blankets neatly. She smooths them out with care. The blankets go on the couch, tidy and warm.

**Shot 06 — Opa's Section**: Opa arranges books on the shelf. He places them upright, covers facing out. Gentle, methodical.

**Emotional State**: Determined → Joyful → Calm — each character finds their rhythm.

### Beat 5 — RELIEF (Shot 07)

**All Tidy**: The room is clean. Three characters stand in it, looking around. The transformation is visible.

**Kiko Declares**: "All done together!" Pride. Relief. Joy.

**Emotional State**: Shared accomplishment — they did it together.

### Beat 6 — LESSON (Shot 08)

**Kiko Says**: "We do it together!" The repeated phrase lands with full weight.

**All Three**: "We do it together!" Echo. Unity.

**Final Frame**: All three wave goodbye. The tidy room is behind them. Warm close.

**Emotional State**: Full joy, lesson embodied.
