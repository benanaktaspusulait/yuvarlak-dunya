## SFX STYLE GUIDE

| Principle | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Butterfly sound | Always soft, delicate, pastel-feeling. Never buzzing or insect-like |
| Character breath | Natural, age-appropriate. No exaggerated breathing |
| Silence | Used deliberately in Shots 01, 04, 05 for tension and wonder |
| Volume arc | Gentle throughout. Peaks only at Kiko's gasp (Shot 05) |
| Garden ambience | Constant thread. Drops low in tense moments, returns in relief |
| Emotional sfx | No overtly "emotional" sounds. Let visuals carry feeling |
