## SHOT RENDER PROMPTS

### Shot 01 — "A Little Butterfly"

**Render prompt:** Warm garden establishing shot. Young girl (coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails, bare feet) mid-step on soft green grass, pausing to notice a pastel butterfly (pink/yellow wings, soft-stylised) landing on a round pink flower. Dappled warm sunlight through garden foliage. Soft green bushes, rounded shapes, pastel flowers in background. Child eye level camera, 35mm lens. Warm daytime, soft natural light. Gentle wing flutter animation. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 02 — "I Want to Touch It"

**Render prompt:** Medium shot. Young girl (coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails) leaning forward excitedly, both hands reaching toward a pastel butterfly on a flower screen-right. Face lit with eager excitement, mouth open. Quick but not rough movement. Child eye level, 30mm lens. Warm garden background, soft bokeh. Only girl's mouth moves for dialogue. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 03 — "Soft and Gentle"

**Render prompt:** Medium two-shot. Soft-blue rabbit (yellow top, long floppy ears) standing screen-right, extending one soft paw in calming open gesture toward young girl (coral-pink top) screen-left. Girl pauses, looks at rabbit, recalibrates. Warm garden setting. Child eye level, 32mm lens. Calm coaching moment. Only speaking character's mouth moves. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 04 — "One Gentle Finger"

**Render prompt:** Close-up. Young girl's hand slowly extending one finger toward a pastel butterfly (pink/yellow wings) resting on a round pink flower. Extreme slow approach. Maximum gentle tension. Child eye level, 28mm lens. Shallow depth of field — finger and butterfly sharp, garden background soft bokeh. Near-silence. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 05 — "It Lands on Me" ★ FRESH ANCHOR

**Render prompt:** Close-up to medium close-up. Pastel butterfly (pink/yellow wings) lifts from flower, hovers briefly, lands softly on young girl's extended fingertip. Girl's eyes widen in pure amazement, mouth small 'o'. Girl freezes completely, holding still. Butterfly rests calmly, wings gentle flutter. HIGHEST PRODUCTION VALUE. Magical moment. Rack focus from butterfly to girl's amazed eyes. Warm golden garden light. Child eye level, 30mm lens. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 06 — "So Beautiful"

**Render prompt:** Medium shot. Young girl (coral-pink top) standing still, pastel butterfly resting on her extended finger. Soft-blue rabbit (yellow top) nearby, beaming with warm joy. Warm golden garden light connecting all three. Child eye level, 32mm lens. Girl transfixed, barely breathing. Rabbit has proud smile. Butterfly calm on finger. Only speaking character's mouth moves. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 07 — "Soft and Gentle" (whispered)

**Render prompt:** Close-up profile. Young girl's face in profile screen-left, pastel butterfly resting on her fingertip in soft foreground. Peaceful garden bokeh behind. Girl's lips move to whisper. Eyes soft, peaceful. Extremely shallow depth of field — girl's eye and butterfly sharp, rest soft. Child eye level, 28mm lens. Intimate, whispered moment. No music, no text.

---

### Shot 08 — "Gentle Hands, Kind Heart"

**Render prompt:** Wide shot. Pastel butterfly lifts off from girl's finger, graceful upward arc, flies away into soft sky. Young girl (coral-pink top) and soft-blue rabbit (yellow top) watch from below with peaceful smiles. Warm garden setting. Child eye level, 35mm lens. Butterfly departing is beautiful, not sad. Eyes open on final frame. Warm golden light fading gently. No music, no text.
