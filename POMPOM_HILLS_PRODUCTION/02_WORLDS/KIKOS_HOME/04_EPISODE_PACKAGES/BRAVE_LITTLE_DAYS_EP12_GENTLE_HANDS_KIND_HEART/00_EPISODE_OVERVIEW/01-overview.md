# EP12 — Gentle Hands, Kind Heart

## Series
**Brave Little Days** — Pompom Hills Production

## Episode
EP 12 — "Gentle Hands, Kind Heart"

## Logline
Kiko discovers a butterfly in the garden and learns that reaching out gently — not grabbing eagerly — is the way to connect with living things.

## Theme
Gentle touch with living things. Learning that kindness opens doors that force cannot.

## Repeated Phrase
**"Soft and gentle."** (3–4 times across the episode)

## Characters
| Character | Position | Description |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Kiko** | Screen-left | Coral-pink top, white shorts, brown pigtails, bare feet |
| **Mimi** | Screen-right | Soft-blue rabbit, yellow top, long floppy ears |

## Location
**Kiko's Home — Garden / Window Area**
Warm daytime, soft natural light, rounded garden shapes, soft green foliage, pastel flowers.

## Narrative Arc (Spine Steps)
1. **PROBLEM** (Shot 01): Kiko notices a beautiful butterfly in the garden
2. **FEELING** (Shot 02): Excited eagerness — "I want to touch it!" Reaches eagerly
3. **SUPPORT** (Shot 03): Mimi coaches: "Soft and gentle." Kiko recalibrates
4. **ACTION** (Shot 04): Kiko extends one gentle finger toward the butterfly
5. **ACTION** (Shot 05): Butterfly lands on Kiko's finger — magic moment (FRESH RESET ANCHOR)
6. **RELIEF** (Shot 06): "It likes you!" — Beauty of gentle connection
7. **RELIEF** (Shot 07): Butterfly rests. Kiko whispers: "Soft and gentle."
8. **LESSON** (Shot 08): Butterfly flies away. Kiko: "Gentle hands, kind heart."

## Emotion Curve
```
Excitement → Eagerness (too fast) → Calm (coaching) → Curiosity →
AMAZEMENT (fresh anchor) → Warmth → Gratitude → Peaceful understanding
```

## Duration
- 8 shots × 15 seconds = **120 seconds (2 minutes)**
- Chain A: Shots 01–04 (Discovery → Coaching)
- Chain B: Shots 05–08 (Magic → Lesson)
- Shot 05 = Fresh Quality Reset Anchor

## Safety Notes
- No hurting the butterfly. No rough touch shown.
- Kiko's "rough" is eagerness — never actual grabbing or harm.
- Butterfly is unharmed throughout. It flies away freely at the end.
- Gentle from the very start. The "rough" moment is purely about speed/eagerness.

## Technical Specs
- Camera: 28–35mm, child eye level
- Eyes: Open in first and final frames
- Background music: None
- On-screen text: None
- Butterfly: Pastel colors, friendly, soft stylised (not realistic-insect-like)
- Flower: Soft, round petals, pastel pink/yellow
- Garden: Warm, green, rounded shapes, soft light
