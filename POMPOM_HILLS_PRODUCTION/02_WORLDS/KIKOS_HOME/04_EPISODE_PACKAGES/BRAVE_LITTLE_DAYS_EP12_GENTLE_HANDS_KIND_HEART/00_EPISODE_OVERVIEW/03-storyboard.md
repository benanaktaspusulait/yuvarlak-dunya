# EP12 — Storyboard

## Episode: "Gentle Hands, Kind Heart"
## Format: 8 panels × 15 seconds

---

### PANEL 01 — "A Little Butterfly"
**Frame:** Wide garden. Kiko mid-step, pausing. Butterfly (pastel pink/yellow wings) lands on a round soft-pink flower. Warm green foliage everywhere.
**Action:** Butterfly flutters in from screen-right, settles on flower. Kiko notices.
**Camera:** Wide establishing → slow push-in toward butterfly
**Duration:** 15s
**SFX:** Soft wing flutter, garden ambience
**Notes:** Eyes open on Kiko. Butterfly is soft-stylised, not insect-realistic. Pastel palette.

---

### PANEL 02 — "I Want to Touch It"
**Frame:** Medium shot. Kiko leaning forward, both hands reaching out quickly, face lit with excitement. Butterfly visible in background on flower.
**Action:** Kiko reaches eagerly toward butterfly — fast, excited movement. Hands open wide.
**Camera:** Medium, slightly low (child eye level)
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** "I want to touch it!"
**SFX:** Excited footstep, quick rustle
**Notes:** Eagerness is clear but NOT rough. She's reaching too fast, not too hard. No grabbing.

---

### PANEL 03 — "Soft and Gentle"
**Frame:** Two-shot. Mimi steps in screen-right, placing a soft paw near Kiko's reaching hands. Calming gesture. Kiko pauses, looks at Mimi.
**Action:** Mimi demonstrates slow, open-hand motion. Kiko pulls back slightly, recalibrates.
**Camera:** Two-shot at child eye level
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** Mimi: "Soft and gentle." Kiko: "Like this?"
**SFX:** Soft movement, calm tone
**Notes:** Mimi is warm, not scolding. Coaching tone. Kiko is receptive, not upset.

---

### PANEL 04 — "One Gentle Finger"
**Frame:** Close-up. Kiko's hand slowly extending one finger. Butterfly in soft focus on flower, watching. Tension of the approach.
**Action:** One finger moves toward butterfly. Slow, careful, deliberate. Butterfly tilts head curiously.
**Camera:** Close-up, split focus between finger and butterfly
**Duration:** 15s
**SFX:** Near-silence. Soft breath. Tiny wing twitch.
**Notes:** This is the suspense beat. Maximum gentle tension. No rush. 28mm lens feel.

---

### PANEL 05 — "It Lands on Me" ★ FRESH ANCHOR
**Frame:** Close-up. Butterfly lifts from flower, flutters briefly, lands softly on Kiko's extended fingertip. Pull to Kiko's face — eyes wide, mouth small 'o' of wonder.
**Action:** Butterfly lands. Kiko gasps. Pure magic moment.
**Camera:** Close-up landing → slow pull to Kiko's amazed expression
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** Kiko: (soft gasp)
**SFX:** Wing flutter. Silence of wonder.
**Notes:** This is the quality reset anchor. Highest production value. Magical. Time seems to stop.

---

### PANEL 06 — "So Beautiful"
**Frame:** Medium shot. Kiko standing still, butterfly on finger. Mimi nearby, beaming with joy. Warm garden light.
**Action:** Mimi celebrates quietly. Kiko holds perfectly still, transfixed.
**Camera:** Medium two-shot with butterfly visible
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** Mimi: "It likes you!" Kiko: (whispered) "So beautiful."
**SFX:** Happy tone from Mimi. Whispered wonder from Kiko.
**Notes:** Warmth and gentle pride. Connection between all three characters.

---

### PANEL 07 — "Soft and Gentle" (whispered)
**Frame:** Close-up. Kiko's face in profile, butterfly on finger in foreground. Peaceful garden bokeh behind.
**Action:** Kiko barely breathes, watching butterfly. Lips move to whisper the phrase.
**Camera:** Close-up profile, shallow depth of field
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** Kiko: (whispered) "Soft and gentle."
**SFX:** Whisper. Garden stillness. Butterfly wing micro-movement.
**Notes:** The phrase returns — now owned by Kiko. Deep internalization moment.

---

### PANEL 08 — "Gentle Hands, Kind Heart"
**Frame:** Wide shot. Butterfly lifts off, flies upward and away into soft sky. Kiko and Mimi watch from below. Kiko's peaceful smile.
**Action:** Butterfly departs gracefully. Kiko watches, then turns to Mimi with understanding smile.
**Camera:** Wide → tilt up following butterfly → settle on Kiko and Mimi
**Duration:** 15s
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Gentle hands, kind heart."
**SFX:** Wing flutter fading. Warm garden ambience.
**Notes:** Lesson learned. Eyes open on final frame. Peaceful resolution. No sadness — the butterfly leaving is beautiful.

---

## Panel Flow Summary
```
01: DISCOVER → 02: REACH → 03: COACH → 04: APPROACH →
05: MAGIC ★ → 06: CONNECTION → 07: INTERNALIZE → 08: RELEASE
```

## Visual Continuity Notes
- Butterfly color palette: pastel pink, soft yellow, light lavender accents
- Flower: round petals, pastel pink/yellow, soft-stylised
- Garden: warm green, rounded shapes, dappled soft light throughout
- Kiko's finger: clean, small, childlike — the vehicle of gentleness
- Light progression: warm → warmer → golden → peaceful
