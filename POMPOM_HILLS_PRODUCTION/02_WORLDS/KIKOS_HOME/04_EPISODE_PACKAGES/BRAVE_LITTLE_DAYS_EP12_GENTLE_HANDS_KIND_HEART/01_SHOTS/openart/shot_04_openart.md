# OpenArt Prompt - Shot 04

## Visual Description
Kiko (coral-pink top, brown pigtails) extending one gentle finger toward a butterfly on a flower. Butterfly watches the approaching finger with curiosity. Tension builds as finger gets closer. Garden setting with soft focus on finger and butterfly.

## Style
Children's animation, detailed close-up, building anticipation

## Mood
Tense, anticipatory, careful

## Key Elements
- Kiko's gentle finger extended
- Butterfly watching with curiosity
- Flower with butterfly
- Soft focus background
- Warm garden lighting

## Composition
Close-up on finger approaching butterfly, shallow depth of field, tension in the space between
