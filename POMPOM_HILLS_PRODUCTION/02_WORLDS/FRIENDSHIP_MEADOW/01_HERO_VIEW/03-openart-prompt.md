# Friendship Meadow — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A wide open green meadow with soft short grass and a gentle breeze. A few round-topped trees with soft green canopies at the edges providing shade. Clusters of round colourful flowers — red, yellow, purple — along the path edges. Smooth round grey stepping stones marking the entrance. A round woven picnic basket and blanket on the grass. Two or three colourful round kites drifting in the sky above. A soft rounded hill at the meadow's edge. Warm golden sunlight, open sky. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, joyful and friendly atmosphere. No narrow spaces, no sharp corners, no competitive markings, no dark areas, no modern objects.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide panorama of the meadow with kites in the sky
- Image 2 (back): view from the hill looking across the meadow
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the entrance, trees, and picnic area
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the picnic blanket and flowers

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
