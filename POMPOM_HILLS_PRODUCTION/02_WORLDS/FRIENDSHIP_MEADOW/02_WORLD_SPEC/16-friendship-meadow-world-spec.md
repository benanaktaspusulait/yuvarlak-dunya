# Friendship Meadow — World Creation Specification

---

## Purpose

Friendship Meadow is a wide, round green meadow that functions as the
communal heart of Pompom Hills. It is where friendship, sharing, and
togetherness come alive through the simple joy of group play and picnics.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Friendship Meadow and must never change:

- Wide, round green meadow
- Soft short grass
- Round trees at edges
- Colourful flowers along paths
- Soft rounded hills
- Open sky for kite flying
- Warm golden sunlight
- No sharp corners anywhere

---

## Visual Identity

A wide, fully open meadow with no rectangles, no straight edges, no sharp
corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every element a natural,
handcrafted quality. The palette is bright and cheerful, centred on green
grass with colourful flowers and warm sunlight. The overall feeling is a
"living toy meadow" — approachable, joyful, and visually simple enough for
a preschool audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous open space rather than a set of separate
areas. The entrance leads into the meadow, which flows naturally from kite
flying zone to picnic area to group game space to hill viewpoint to rest
circle. All zones are open and connected without fences or barriers between
them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The meadow entrance is visible from the path.
- The kite flying zone can be seen from the hill viewpoint.
- The picnic area is visible from the kite zone.
- The group game space is visible from all areas.
- The hill viewpoint provides a view of the entire meadow.
- The rest circle is visible from the hill but feels separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Meadow Entrance** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the meadow. Visual identity: colourful flowers, welcome stones. Important props: flowers, stones. Typical use: arrival, greeting.

**Kite Flying Zone** — Purpose: kite flying and sky watching. Visual identity: wide open sky, gentle wind. Important props: kites. Typical use: flying, freedom.

**Picnic Area** — Purpose: shared eating and gathering. Visual identity: soft grass, shade from trees. Important props: picnic blanket, basket. Typical use: sharing, conversation.

**Group Game Space** — Purpose: group play and activities. Visual identity: wide open grass, natural arena. Important props: open space. Typical use: games, dancing, holding hands.

**Hill View Point** — Purpose: viewing the entire meadow. Visual identity: soft rounded hill, panoramic view. Important props: hill, view. Typical use: perspective, calm.

**Rest Circle** — Purpose: rest and quiet sitting. Visual identity: smooth stones under tree shade. Important props: stones, tree. Typical use: resting, star watching.

---

## Materials

Grass: soft, short, green. Trees: round, wide, providing shade. Flowers:
soft, round, colourful. Stones: smooth, round, grey. Hills: soft, rounded,
green. No hard surfaces, no sharp edges, no artificial materials anywhere.
Everything reads as natural and safe.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is bright daylight. Morning brings soft golden light; midday is
bright and even; evening turns golden-orange with longer soft shadows; night
is soft moonlight with stars visible. Outdoor lighting is always natural
daylight. Shadows are always soft — never hard, never dark.

---

## Colour Palette

Grass: bright green. Trees: darker green with brown trunks. Flowers: red,
yellow, purple. Stones: warm grey. Sky: blue. The palette stays within this
bright, cheerful set across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. The meadow is wide but
intimate — open enough to run and fly kites, but contained enough to feel
safe. Trees provide shade without blocking views. Hills are gentle and
climbable.

---

## Atmosphere

Bright. Soft. Safe. Joyful. Every surface reads as natural and gentle.
The mood is always playful and inviting — full of laughter and movement.
The meadow never feels empty or quiet during the day.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The meadow is always wide, round, and green — never narrow, never brown.
- Grass stays bright green across every generation.
- Trees stay round and green.
- Flowers stay colourful and soft.
- No sharp corners, no thorns, no threatening elements.
- No dark or high-contrast lighting.
- The meadow's core shape, colours, and proportions are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The meadow must be generated as a single wide open space with natural
elements, not as a confined or threatening landscape — this is the defining
trait of this world and the most common failure point to watch for. Keep all
shapes rounded with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour palette
limited to the bright cheerful set defined above. Favor a soft, matte,
natural material language over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering.
Treat the meadow's shape, colours, and proportions as a fixed identity to
preserve exactly, not to reinterpret, across every generation.

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/WORLD_STANDARDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Friendship Meadow must never be reduced to "an open field."

This layer protects Friendship Meadow from becoming too plain. Keep the wide
round meadow silhouette readable and the open, togetherness feeling clear —
add only the canon-approved charm details below. Do not add random objects.
Do not remove the joyful, shared-play emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** FM-KITE-01, a cluster of 2-3 colourful round
kites drifting overhead, is the primary landmark and must be visible against
the open sky in every wide shot. The gentle rounded hill silhouette at the
meadow's edge adds landmark charm. FM-GRASS-01 (soft green grass with a
natural wave pattern) and clusters of FM-FLOWERS-01 (red, yellow, purple)
marking the path edges add surface charm. A round woven picnic basket and
blanket, when the scene calls for a picnic, and smooth grey stepping stones
marking the entrance path are the central reusable small props. Gentle
motion — kites drifting and dipping in the wind, and soft waves moving
through the grass — keeps the world feeling alive without adding clutter.

**Forbidden clutter:** an empty bare field with nothing to anchor the eye,
narrow or cramped space, sharp corners anywhere, modern playground
equipment, competitive game markings (goals, lines, scoreboards), large
crowds, and dark or gloomy lighting.

### Hero View Quality Tests

Before accepting a Hero View, it must pass:

1. **Silhouette Test** — recognisable as Friendship Meadow from the wide round meadow with its rounded hill edge alone?
2. **Colour Test** — recognisable from the bright green grass, colourful flowers, and warm sunlight alone?
3. **Charm Test** — does it make a child want to run in and fly a kite with friends?

If any test fails, reject.
