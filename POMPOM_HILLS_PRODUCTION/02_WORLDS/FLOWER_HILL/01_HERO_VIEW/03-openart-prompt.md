# Flower Hill — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A soft rounded green hill covered in dense clusters of pastel flowers in five colours — red, yellow, pink, purple, and orange — forming a readable flower carpet. A curved stepping-stone path of smooth grey round stones winds through the flowers in a clear S-curve. Short soft green grass between flower clusters. Warm morning sunlight from the upper left, soft and bright. Distant soft rolling hills on the horizon. No bare soil, no sharp stones, no dark atmosphere. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, colourful and inviting. Five flower colours always visible in wide shots, path always readable.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the flower hill with the S-curve path and all five colours
- Image 2 (back): view from the hill looking back along the path
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the hill's soft rounded silhouette
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of flower clusters and stepping stones

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
