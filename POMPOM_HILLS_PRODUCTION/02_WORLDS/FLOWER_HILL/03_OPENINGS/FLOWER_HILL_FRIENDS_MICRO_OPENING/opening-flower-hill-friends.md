# Flower Hill Friends — Micro-Opening

> Reusable, story-external micro-opening for Flower Hill episodes. Optional lead-in placed
> before the main story. It does not change the story length.
>
> Type: **World Micro-Opening** (not a Series Bumper). See
> `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §2B for the
> rule set this follows. Unlike a series bumper (e.g. Opa's Storytime), this has **no matching
> reusable closing** — normal Flower Hill episodes close on their own final shot (see
> `MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §3).
>
> Render prompts: `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/FLOWER_HILL/03_OPENINGS/FLOWER_HILL_FRIENDS_MICRO_OPENING/render-prompts.md`
> World canon: `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/FLOWER_HILL/00_CANON/25-flower-hill-bible.md`

## ⚠️ Status: Pending Canon Hero View Approval

Flower Hill's own canon bible states: `Hero View: 🟡 Legacy images exist, Canon Hero View
approval pending` (see `25-flower-hill-world-spec.md` and `25-flower-hill-bible.md`
§ Generation Workflow). This micro-opening spec is written and ready, but should not be
rendered into a final reusable clip until Flower Hill's own Canon Hero View is approved —
otherwise the micro-opening would lock in an identity that the world itself hasn't locked
in yet. Treat this file as ready-to-render once the world's Hero View is approved, not
before.

## Duration

3–4 seconds.

## Use

Optional opening for 90–120 second Flower Hill episodes. Applies to the **Home Cluster
Zone** specifically (Kiko's Home / Mimi's Burrow neighbourhood feeling), not the Nature
Field Zone — see zone distinction in `25-flower-hill-bible.md` § Sub-Zones. If a future
Nature Field Zone micro-opening is needed, it should be a separate file, not a variant of
this one.

## Characters

None by default.

## Text & Safety

- No readable text generated inside the AI video.
- No subtitles.
- No logo animation.

## Visual

Flower Hill Home Cluster Zone: flower-covered hill, pink main dome home, two smaller
pastel dome homes, central stone path, flower fences, flower pots, warm home charm, warm
morning daylight from the upper left.

## Motion

Very slow camera glide along the stone path toward the homes, or a gentle push-in. Soft
vine sway. No fast movement.

## Mood

Warm, colourful, homely, inviting, preschool-friendly.

## Sound

A light, cheerful 3–4 second music motif: bells or glockenspiel, a soft flower-breeze sound.
Calm and cheerful but childlike, never busy. No loud logo sting, no vocals, no fast whoosh.
Follows the general music motif rule in
`POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §2C and the
volume/instrument limits in `00-CORE/AUDIO_GUIDE.md`. Like the rest of this file, this sound
note is draft — subject to the same Hero View approval gate (see header).

## Avoid

Readable sign, top-down map view, large bridge, dominant river, ducks, fish, rainbow
unless later canonised, realistic village, sharp fences, busy clutter, random non-canon
props, redesigned Kiko's Home or Mimi's Burrow silhouettes.

## Transition

The final frame should visually connect into the first story shot of the episode — same
Home Cluster Zone layout, same warm morning daylight, same Episode Colour Master.
