# Lulu's Greenhouse — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A round greenhouse with curved glass panels held by warm wooden frames. Inside, lush green plants and colourful flowers fill every shelf. Warm golden light glows from within. The glass panels reflect soft sunlight. Small potted plants sit outside the entrance. A wooden sign hangs above the door. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, warm and alive atmosphere. No modern materials, no sharp glass edges, no cold lighting, no dark shadows.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the greenhouse from the garden path
- Image 2 (back): view from behind showing the plant-filled interior through glass
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the curved structure
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of plants inside through glass

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and warm green palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
