# Poppy's Bakery — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A round, warm orange-brown bakery building with a curved roof and a large round display window showing cookies and pastries. A small wooden sign hangs above the door. Warm golden light spills from the windows. The scent of fresh bread seems to fill the air. Soft cobblestone path leads to the entrance. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, cosy and inviting atmosphere. No modern signage, no sharp edges, no dark shadows, no realistic food textures.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the bakery from the path
- Image 2 (back): view from behind showing the kitchen area
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the display window
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the pastries in the window

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and warm orange palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
