## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — Golden Forest (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest, golden autumn. Kiko walks among falling leaves, amazed.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "So many leaves..."
**Camera:** Wide, 35mm, static. Kiko centred.

### Shot 02 — A Leaf in Her Hand (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko holds a golden leaf, looks sad.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "I miss the green..."
**Camera:** Medium, 50mm, static. Focus on Kiko's face.

### Shot 03 — Finding Opa (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko finds Opa under a big tree.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Opa! Why do leaves fall?"
**Camera:** Two-shot, medium. Kiko approaches, Opa pats ground.

### Shot 04 — Opa's Gentle Answer (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Opa explains gently.
**Dialogue:** Opa: "So the tree can rest. And grow new ones when spring comes."
**Camera:** Medium, gentle focus shift between Opa and Kiko.

### Shot 05 — Changing Clothes (60-75 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Opa uses a metaphor.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Like changing clothes?" / Opa: "Exactly."
**Camera:** Two-shot, warm close-up.

### Shot 06 — Dancing Leaves (75-90 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko watches leaves dance.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "They look like they're dancing!"
**Camera:** Wide, tracking Kiko's movement.

### Shot 07 — A Leaf Falls Fast (90-105 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. A leaf spirals down quickly.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "That one's going fast!"
**Camera:** Follow the leaf, then Kiko reaching up.

### Shot 08 — Caught! (105-120 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko catches a leaf mid-air.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Got it! Hello, little leaf!"
**Camera:** Medium close-up, Kiko's delighted face.

### Shot 09 — So Many Colors (120-135 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko examines different colored leaves.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "This one is red... this one is gold!"
**Camera:** Medium, focus on leaves in her hands.

### Shot 10 — The Colorful Carpet (135-150 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko sees the ground covered in leaves.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "The forest is so beautiful..."
**Camera:** Wide, showing the golden landscape.

### Shot 11 — Letting Go (150-165 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. Kiko places leaf down, Opa smiles.
**Dialogue:** Opa: "Everything changes gently."
**Camera:** Medium, warm two-shot.

### Shot 12 — Walking Together (165-180 sn)
**Location:** Little Forest. They walk through golden forest together.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Let's come back tomorrow."
**Camera:** Wide, slow tracking. Warm ending.

---

*Learning: A small discovery, noticed together and shared with everyone, becomes something special.*
