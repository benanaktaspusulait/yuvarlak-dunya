# S01E12 — Why Do Leaves Fall — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1:  Kiko walks through golden forest, sees leaves falling. (Curious)
Beat 2:  She holds a leaf and feels sad — misses the green summer. (Sad)
Beat 3:  She finds Opa and asks why leaves fall. (Questioning)
Beat 4:  Opa explains: trees need rest to grow new leaves. (Understanding)
Beat 5:  Opa compares it to changing clothes. (Metaphor)
Beat 6:  Kiko watches leaves dance in the wind. (Wonder)
Beat 7:  A leaf spirals down fast — Kiko reaches for it. (Excitement)
Beat 8:  She catches it mid-air! (WOW Moment)
Beat 9:  She discovers leaves in many colors. (Discovery)
Beat 10: She sees the beautiful carpet of leaves. (Acceptance)
Beat 11: She places her leaf down gently. Opa says the repeated phrase. (Resolution)
Beat 12: They walk through the golden forest together. (Warm ending)
```

---

## Duration Check

12 shots × 15 seconds = 180 seconds (3 minutes).
