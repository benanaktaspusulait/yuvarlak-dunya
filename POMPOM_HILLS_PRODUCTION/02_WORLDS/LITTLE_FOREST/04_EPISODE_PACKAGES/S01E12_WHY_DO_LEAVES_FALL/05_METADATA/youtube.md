## Description
Why do the leaves fall from the trees? 🍂

In this gentle Pompom Hills preschool animation, Kiko walks through the golden autumn forest and wonders why the leaves are falling. Wise old Opa explains that the tree is simply changing its clothes — and in spring, it will wear its most beautiful outfit again.

This episode supports early social-emotional learning through themes like nature discovery, curiosity, gentle change, and the beauty of autumn. Children learn that change can be beautiful and that letting go is part of nature's cycle.
