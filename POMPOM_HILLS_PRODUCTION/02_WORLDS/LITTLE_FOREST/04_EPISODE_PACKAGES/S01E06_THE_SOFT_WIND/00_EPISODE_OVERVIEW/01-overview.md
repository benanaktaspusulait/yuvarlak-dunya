## Story Summary

Kiko stands in Little Forest and feels something soft brush her cheek. She looks around — where did it come from? A leaf floats past her nose. She reaches for it but it drifts away. She follows it deeper into the forest. The wind plays tricks — it tickles her ears, makes the flowers sway, lifts her scarf. She laughs and keeps following. Mimi appears on the path ahead, ears flapping in the breeze. "Where is it going?" he asks. Together they chase the wind through the trees, past a stream, around a big oak. Then they stop — and realize the wind isn't going anywhere. It's everywhere at once, playing with everything. "It's all around us," Kiko whispers. They stand still and feel it together — the soft wind, touching everything, going everywhere.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Kiko chasing the wind and finally stopping to realize it's not going somewhere — it's already everywhere. The moment she stops chasing and starts feeling. The second wow moment is when they both stand still and the wind plays with their hair, ears, and scarves simultaneously — visible proof that nature is always there.

## Colour Notes

```text
Fresh, airy forest palette. Soft greens, light browns, gentle sky blue, warm sunlight.
Identical white balance, exposure, colour temperature, saturation, contrast across all shots.
No cool shift. No desaturated look. No green tint. No magenta tint.
Leaves and wind-blown elements must move naturally and consistently across shots.
Little Forest should feel alive and breezy throughout.
```

---

## Lighting Notes

```text
Dappled forest sunlight. Light filtering through leaves, creating soft patterns.
Identical light direction, intensity, shadow softness across all shots.
The wind should be visible through movement of leaves, flowers, and characters' hair/ears.
No dramatic lighting changes between shots.
```
