## Description
Feel the soft wind with Kiko and Mimi! 🍃

In this gentle Pompom Hills preschool animation, Kiko feels a soft breeze on her cheek and begins to follow it through Little Forest. Mimi joins her, and together they chase the wind, laugh, rest by a stream, and discover something beautiful: the wind isn't going somewhere — it's already everywhere, playing with everything.

This calm story supports early learning through nature observation, senses, curiosity, and the joy of slowing down to feel what's always been there.
