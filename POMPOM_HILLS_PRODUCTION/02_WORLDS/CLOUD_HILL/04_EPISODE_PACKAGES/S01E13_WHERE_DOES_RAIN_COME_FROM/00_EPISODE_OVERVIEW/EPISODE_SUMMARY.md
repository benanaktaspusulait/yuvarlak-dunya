# Where Does Rain Come From — Episode Summary

---

## Story

Luca and Mimi climb Cloud Hill on a cloudy day. Wind moves the grass, and the clouds drift slowly overhead. Luca wonders if rain comes from the clouds. Mimi does not explain; she watches with him. One cloud becomes darker and fuller. The hill grows quiet, and Luca holds out his hand while they wait. Then the first raindrop lands on Luca's hand. They look at the drop, smile, and look back up as gentle rain begins.

## Learning

Rain comes from clouds.

Children discover this through watching, wondering, waiting, and noticing together.
