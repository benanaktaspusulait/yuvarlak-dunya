## CH-STONE-01 Spatial Continuity

The stone is the spatial memory of Cloud Hill. It must be used deliberately to track the children's approach and arrival.

| Shot | Stone Position in Frame | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Visible in distance, upper hill | Establishes the destination |
| 02 | Mid-distance, slightly closer | Journey continues toward it |
| 03 | Nearby — children sit beside it | Arrival at the observation point |
| 04 | Same — close, anchor of the frame | Waiting together at the stone |
| 05 | Same — discovery happens here | First drop lands beside the stone |
| 06 | Wide — stone anchors the closing frame | Both children + stone + rain |

Do not move, resize, or reposition the stone between shots 3–6.
