# Cloud Hill Moments — Micro-Opening

> Reusable, story-external micro-opening for Cloud Hill episodes. Optional lead-in placed
> before the main story. It does not change the story length.
>
> Type: **World Micro-Opening** (not a Series Bumper). See
> `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §2B for the
> rule set this follows. Unlike a series bumper (e.g. Opa's Storytime), this has **no matching
> reusable closing** — normal Cloud Hill episodes close on their own final shot (see
> `MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §3).
>
> Render prompts: `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/CLOUD_HILL/03_OPENINGS/CLOUD_HILL_MOMENTS_MICRO_OPENING/render-prompts.md`
> World canon: `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/CLOUD_HILL/00_CANON/01-world-bible.md`

## Duration

3–4 seconds.

## Use

Optional opening for 90–120 second Cloud Hill episodes. The final frame should hand off
into the first story shot (typically the climb path or summit arrival beat — see
`01-world-bible.md` § World Navigation).

## Characters

None by default.

## Text & Safety

- No readable text generated inside the AI video.
- No subtitles.
- No logo animation.

## Visual

Smooth dome hill silhouette, CH-STONE-01 summit viewing stone slightly right of centre,
2–3 large soft white clouds close in the sky, gentle cloud shadows drifting, tiny grass
tufts, warm open sky occupying at least 60% of frame, warm morning daylight from the
upper left.

## Motion

Gentle camera glide across the summit area, or a very slow push-in toward the sky. Clouds
drift very slowly left to right. No fast movement.

## Mood

Warm, calm, sky-dominant, quiet wonder, preschool-friendly.

## Sound

A soft, airy 3–4 second music motif: airy pad, one soft chime, and a light breath-like wind
sound. Calm, open, unhurried. No loud logo sting, no vocals, no fast whoosh. Follows the
general music motif rule in
`POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §2C and the
volume/instrument limits in `00-CORE/AUDIO_GUIDE.md`.

## Avoid

Generic empty hill with no landmark, houses on the summit, cliffs, storm clouds, too many
flowers (that is Flower Hill's identity, not Cloud Hill's), dark or dramatic lighting,
more than 4 clouds or zero clouds, hill silhouette becoming pointed or jagged.

## Transition

The final frame should visually connect into the first story shot of the episode — same
dome silhouette, same sky ratio (~60%+), same Episode Colour Master (lighting direction,
cloud count, warm pastel palette).
