# Central Square Friends Micro-Opening

> Reusable, story-external Central Square Friends Micro-Opening for Central Square episodes.
> Optional lead-in placed before the main story. It does not change the story length.
>
> Type: **World Micro-Opening** (not a Series Bumper). See
> `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/MICRO_OPENING_AND_CLOSING_STANDARD.md` §2B.
> This has **no matching reusable closing**. Normal Central Square episodes close on
> their own final story shot.
>
> Frame-to-video prompt: `central-square-openart-frame-to-video-prompt.md`
> World canon: `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/CENTRAL_SQUARE/00_CANON/01-central-square-bible.md`

## Duration

4 seconds.

## Use

Optional Central Square Friends Micro-Opening for 90-120 second Central Square episodes. Recommended for the
2-minute `S01E05 — Colours Everywhere` refactor.

The final frame should hand off into Shot 01. It stays outside an episode's
`01_SHOTS/` folder and is not counted as a story shot.

## Characters

None. This is a calm empty Central Square world asset.

## Text & Safety

- No readable text generated inside the AI video.
- No subtitles.
- No captions.
- No logo animation.
- No title card.

## Visual

Calm Central Square environment only: Big Pompom Tree centered as the main
landmark, rounded paths, coloured stepping-stone ring, soft carpet-like grass,
one visible red rounded planter or one visible red bunting accent, fixed in the
set, pastel blue bench, yellow flower planter, small soft purple bunting or
flags visible as a subtle fixed set detail, low pastel houses only at the far
edge of the square, warm morning daylight.

No readable text, no gibberish text, no signposts with writing, no fake signs,
no rainbow, no literal rainbow, no magical rainbow, no glow beam, no sparkles,
no fantasy sky effects.

These colour objects (red, blue, yellow, green and purple anchors) are stable
physical set pieces from the first frame onward. They are not optional and must
not be described as conditional on composition. Keep them small, pastel and
preschool-safe so the shot does not feel crowded.

## Motion

A single very slow camera glide or soft push-in. The movement should feel like
entering the square gently, not like a trailer or title sequence. The
composition must be a clean, stable, reusable continuity frame — not a
poster-like showcase image, not a hero collage, not a promotional image.

## Mood

Warm, friendly, communal, preschool-safe, calm but alive.

## Sound

A soft warm 4-second music motif with a light friendship feeling, very gentle
percussion if needed, light ambient wind and distant birds. No loud logo sting,
no vocals, no fast whoosh.

## Transition

The final frame should visually connect into the first story shot of the
episode. It should show the same Central Square set, warm morning light and
camera calm that Shot 01 will use.

The approved final frame can be used as `@image1` for Shot 01 only as an
environment continuity reference. It does not define the Episode Colour Master.
Shot 01's approved Kiko-visible still becomes the Episode Colour Master.

## Hard Background Lock

This is an empty environment shot. No characters appear. Because there are no
characters, the main approval focus is object stability.

The Central Square set must behave like a fixed physical stage. The first frame defines
the locked physical layout for the whole 4-second Central Square Friends Micro-Opening,
and the final frame must preserve the same Central Square layout so it is safe to use as
Shot 01 `@image1`.

The following visible objects must not move, swap, resize, disappear, duplicate, repaint
or change position during the shot: Big Pompom Tree, all visible tree trunks, benches,
planters, bushes, flowerbeds, rounded paths, coloured stepping-stone ring, paving
stones, grass patches, bunting or flags, distant houses, low walls and any visible
landmark established in the first frame.

The camera movement must not cause tree position shift, bench position shift, planter
position shift, bush position shift, path redesign, stepping-stone ring redesign,
background morphing, new objects appearing or objects disappearing.

## Object Identity Lock

Every visible object keeps its identity. A bench cannot become a planter. A bush cannot
become a tree. A flowerbed cannot become a grass mound. A path cannot become a road. A
house cannot become a shop. A planter cannot move to a new side of the frame. The Big
Pompom Tree cannot change size, trunk position, canopy shape or location.

## Production Workflow

This Central Square Friends Micro-Opening may be produced either still-first or
shot-first.

Preferred practical workflow: shot-first.

- Treat this as `Central Square Friends Micro-Opening Shot`.
- Do not place it inside an episode's `01_SHOTS/` folder.
- It is not part of the main story duration.
- It does not replace Shot 01.
- Characters begin in Shot 01, not in this Central Square Friends Micro-Opening.
- Use original downloaded/exported production media only for continuity; never
  use screenshots.
- After rendering, export:
  - first frame
  - final frame
  - final video clip
- Save these assets in this opening package.

## Camera Direction

A single very slow stable establishing move, child-eye-level, 28-35mm. Keep the
Big Pompom Tree centered or clearly dominant. The camera may glide gently toward
the rounded path and stepping-stone ring where Shot 01 can begin.

No fast zoom. No shake. No whip pan. No reveal of a different place.

## Reject The Generation If

- Any tree, bench, planter or bush changes position.
- The Big Pompom Tree shifts, resizes, duplicates or changes shape.
- Paths or the stepping-stone ring change layout.
- Objects disappear, duplicate, morph or change identity.
- Camera resets, reveals a different part of Central Square or causes background morphing.
- The camera movement causes tree, bench, planter or bush position shift.
- New objects appear or established objects disappear.
- Lighting or colour grading changes.
- Any character appears.
- Text, logo, captions, subtitles, signs, roads, cars, shops, fountains, water features, crowds or extra characters appear.
- Any sign with readable or gibberish text appears.
- A rainbow appears in the sky.
- Purple bunting or flags are missing.
- Houses dominate the frame or are too large.
- Composition looks like a poster or showcase rather than a clean continuity frame.
