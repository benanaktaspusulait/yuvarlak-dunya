# Central Square — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A warm welcoming village square with a large round Pompom Tree at the centre, soft green canopy and warm brown trunk. A ring of colourful round stepping stones — pink, yellow, blue, green — circling the tree. Soft carpet-like green ground with dappled tree-shadow patterns. Low pastel-coloured rounded houses visible at the edges of the square. Warm golden morning sunlight from the upper left, soft shadows. A small wooden bench near the tree. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, warm community atmosphere. No market stalls, no signage, no sharp-edged paving, no dark lighting, no modern objects, no large crowds.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the square with the Big Pompom Tree centred
- Image 2 (back): view from under the tree looking outward at the houses
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the stepping stone ring and entrance
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the tree canopy and stepping stones

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
