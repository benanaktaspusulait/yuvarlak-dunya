## Story Summary

Kiko wakes up and walks into Central Square. She discovers the world — the trees, the houses, the flowers. She meets Mimi, who waves hello. Then Opa appears and welcomes her. One by one, all the friends gather: Luca, Noah, Arda. They all say hello to each other. The episode ends with everyone together under the Giant Pompom Tree, smiling.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

The moment when all friends gather together and say hello — the feeling of belonging and community.
