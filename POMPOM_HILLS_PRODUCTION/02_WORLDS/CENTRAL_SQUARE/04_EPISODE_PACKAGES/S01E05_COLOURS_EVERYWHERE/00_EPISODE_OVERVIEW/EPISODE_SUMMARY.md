## Story

Kiko notices red in Central Square. Mimi joins from the same path, and together they
notice blue, yellow, green and purple details already around them. They do not travel to
another world and they do not collect objects. The story stays in one physical Central
Square set and ends with the colours feeling warm and rainbow-like together.

---

## Shot Summary

1. Kiko sees red.
2. Mimi joins.
3. They see a blue bench.
4. They see yellow flowers.
5. They see green grass.
6. They see purple flags.
7. They remember the colours together.
8. They end with all colours together in Central Square.
