child-eye-level. No camera reset between continuation shots.

## Locked Camera Rules

- Child-eye-level 28-35mm feel.
- Wide or medium-wide framing.
- Slow settle only.
- No fast zoom.
- No shake.
- No whip pan.
- No new establishing shot after Shot 01.
- The first 1 second should visually hold close to @image1 before new action begins.
- In Shot 01, this applies to the Central Square environment only; Kiko must already
  be visible and `@image1` must not be copied as an empty frame.
