## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — They See the Ball (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — A round yellow ball sits on the grass. Luca arrives and sees it, then Noah arrives and sees the same ball.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "A ball!" / Noah: "I see it!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, child eye level, 0.85m.
**Emotion:** Discovery.

### Shot 02 — Both Want It (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Both reach for the ball. Luca holds one side, Noah the other. They look at each other.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Can I try?" / Noah: "I want to try too."
**Camera:** Medium, child eye level, 0.85m.
**Emotion:** Mild tension.

### Shot 03 — Taking Turns (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Luca hands the ball to Noah. Noah bounces it (boop!), smiles, and hands it back.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Your turn." / Noah: "Your turn!"
**Camera:** Medium, child eye level, 0.85m.
**Emotion:** Sharing.

### Shot 04 — Playing Together (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Together they throw and catch the ball (boop! boop!). It flies up, both watch it land, and they laugh.
**Dialogue:** Noah: "That was fun!" / Luca: "Again!"
**Camera:** Medium-wide, child eye level, 0.85m.
**Emotion:** Joy.

### Shot 05 — The Ball Rolls Away (60-75 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — The ball bounces and rolls away across the grass. Luca and Noah chase after it together.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Oh no!" / Noah: "Let's get it!"
**Camera:** Medium-wide, child eye level, 0.85m, gentle pan right.
**Emotion:** Mild surprise.

### Shot 06 — Found It Together (75-90 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — The ball stops near a small bush. Noah picks it up. Both children smile with relief.
**Dialogue:** Noah: "We found it!" / Luca: "Together."
**Camera:** Wide → medium, child eye level, 0.85m, gentle settle.
**Emotion:** Warm ending.

## Ball Continuity

The same round yellow ball appears in every shot.

Shot 01: Ball is seen on the grass.
Shot 02: Both children reach for the same ball.
Shot 03: They decide to take turns.
Shot 04: They play together with the same ball.
Shot 05: The same ball rolls away.
Shot 06: They find the same ball together.

The ball must not change colour, size, texture, or shape.
