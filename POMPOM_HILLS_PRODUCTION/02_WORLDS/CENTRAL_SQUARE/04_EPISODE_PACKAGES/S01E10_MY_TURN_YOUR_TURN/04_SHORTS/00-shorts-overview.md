## Shorts Edit Standardı

```text
Format: 9:16 vertical
Length: 15–30 seconds
No extra narrator
No new voice
No colour filter
No auto-enhance
No screenshot reference
Use original exported video only
Keep faces and ball inside safe area
Keep world identity visible when possible
Do not add loud music
Do not add meme-style text
Do not overcut
```

## Renk Koruma

```text
Use the final approved long video as source.
Do not re-grade.
Do not apply auto colour.
Do not increase contrast.
Do not add filters.
Do not export from a screen recording.
Use original exported file.
```
