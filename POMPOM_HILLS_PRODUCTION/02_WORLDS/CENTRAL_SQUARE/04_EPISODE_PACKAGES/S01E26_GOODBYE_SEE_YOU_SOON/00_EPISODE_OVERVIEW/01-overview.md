## Theme

Friendship and promises, saying goodbye with love, the joy of knowing friends return.

## Story Summary

All friends gather at Central Square as the sun begins to set. Each character says goodbye in their own special way — Noah plays a soft melody, Sofia draws a heart in the air, Mimi gives a warm hug, Opa shares a wise word. They all come together in a group circle, holding hands. The Pompom Tree watches over them, its leaves glowing gently. They promise to meet again. Stars begin to appear. Everyone waves goodnight. The season ends with warmth and the promise of new adventures.
