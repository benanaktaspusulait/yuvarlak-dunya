## Story Summary

Kiko and Opa sit on the bench in Central Square under a starry sky. They start counting stars together — one, two, three... As they count, Opa shows Kiko how to find a pattern: the Big Dipper made of stars. Kiko is amazed. Opa shares bedtime wisdom: "The moon watches over us while we sleep." The moon seems to smile back. Kiko yawns, feeling safe and warm. Opa hums softly. Kiko falls asleep under the starlight, counting still glowing in her mind.
