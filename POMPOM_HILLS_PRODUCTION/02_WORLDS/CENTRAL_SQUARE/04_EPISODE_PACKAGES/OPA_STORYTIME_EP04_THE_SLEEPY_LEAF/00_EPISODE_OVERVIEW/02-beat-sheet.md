## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Noah settle on a story blanket near the Big Pompom Tree at sunset. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa begins: "Once, there was a busy little leaf who never wanted to stop." Noah laughs, asks what it was so busy doing.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): The leaf dances on branches, dances in the breeze. Kiko asks "all day long?"
Beat 4 (Shot 04): The leaf dances and dances, even when it feels tired. Noah asks if it wanted to rest.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The leaf keeps going, faster and faster. Kiko wonders what happens next.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Kiko and Noah both ask why the leaf won't rest. Opa explains gently: it thought resting meant the fun was over.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Noah guesses the leaf was having too much fun. Opa adds it wanted to keep playing forever.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): The sun goes down, the wind slows. Kiko and Noah notice the change.
Beat 9 (Shot 09): The little leaf starts to feel sleepy. Noah points out it's slowing down.
Beat 10 (Shot 10): The leaf tries one last little twirl. Kiko asks "and then?"
Beat 11 (Shot 11): The leaf settles softly onto the grass. Kiko says it looks peaceful.
Beat 12 (Shot 12): The leaf is calm at last. Noah says it found a nice spot.
Beat 13 (Shot 13): Micro-moment — Kiko: "Resting helps me play better tomorrow." Opa: "Yes, little one. Rest is part of play too."
Beat 14 (Shot 14): The story ends. Group warmth on the blanket. No new dialogue.
Beat 15 (Shot 15): Children smile softly. No new dialogue.
Beat 16 (Shot 16): Sunset glow deepens over the story blanket. No new dialogue.
Beat 17 (Shot 17): Gentle pull-back, the scene widens. No new dialogue.
Beat 18 (Shot 18): Quiet warmth, no movement. No new dialogue.
Beat 19 (Shot 19): Final warm hold, group settles. No new dialogue.
Beat 20 (Shot 20): Closing hold, bumper transition. No new dialogue.
```
