## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Noah settle on a story blanket near the Big Pompom Tree at sunset. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa begins: "Once, there was a busy little leaf who never wanted to stop." Noah laughs, asks what it was so busy doing.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): The leaf keeps dancing in the wind all day, refusing to rest. Kiko reacts, a little worried.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko and Noah both ask why the leaf won't rest. Opa explains gently.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The wind slows down at sunset. Noah notices the leaf getting sleepy.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): The leaf finally settles softly onto the grass, calm at last. Kiko smiles softly.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa gives the lesson. Kiko connects it to herself: "Resting helps me play better tomorrow."
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Quiet golden hold on the story blanket. No new dialogue, just warmth.
```
