# Brave Little Days EP01 — Animation Notes

Calm but alive. Every 2–3 seconds has a clear action, reaction, or dialogue beat.
Never freeze characters into a static talking pose; never leave long empty pauses.

## Character Motion

- **Kiko:** curious then nervous body language — hand to cheek, hugging herself, small
  hesitant steps, then softening shoulders and a growing smile by Shot 07–08. Pigtails
  bounce gently with movement.
- **Mimi:** gentle, reassuring — ears attentive, leans in, takes Kiko's hand, small
  supportive hops. Ears down slightly when Kiko is worried, up when she relaxes.
- **Nella:** slow, warm, deliberate — soft smile, gentle head tilt, slow careful hand
  movement with the round mirror. Never sudden. Models "gentle and safe."

## Emotional Curve (must read on faces)

worry (01–03) → reassurance (04) → shy curiosity (05) → calming (06) → relief (07) →
happy pride (08).

## Safe-Exposure Motion Rules

- Nella never forces, pins, or restrains. Kiko is always free to move.
- The mirror moves slowly and gently near the smile; no poking, no fast motion.
- No wincing in pain, no crying, no panic. Discomfort is mild and passes quickly.
