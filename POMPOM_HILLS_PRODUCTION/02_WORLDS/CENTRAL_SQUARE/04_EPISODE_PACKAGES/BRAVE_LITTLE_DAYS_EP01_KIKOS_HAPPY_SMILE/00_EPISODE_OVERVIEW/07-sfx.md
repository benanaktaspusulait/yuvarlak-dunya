# Brave Little Days EP01 — Sound Design

Natural ambience only. **No background music, no melody, no soundtrack** inside the episode body.
(Music lives only in the shared opening/closing bumpers.)

| Shot | Sound |
|---|---|
| 01 | Birds chirping, gentle morning ambience, soft crunch of apple bite |
| 02 | Soft ambience, gentle rustle of Pompom Tree canopy |
| 03 | Soft ambience, a small hesitant breath from Kiko |
| 04 | Warm ambience, soft footsteps starting on grass |
| 05 | Soft footsteps arriving, gentle friendly greeting tone (no music) |
| 06 | Very soft, cozy ambience; a tiny gentle "tap" of the round mirror (soft, non-clinical). NO drill, NO metal clatter. |
| 07 | Warm ambience, soft happy breath, light birdsong |
| 08 | Warm ambience, gentle birdsong, soft wave of a hand |

---

## Forbidden Sounds

```
✗ drilling / dental drill sound
✗ sharp metal instrument clatter
✗ any harsh, sudden, or scary sound
✗ background music inside the episode body
```
