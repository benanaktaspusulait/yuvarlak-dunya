## Story Summary

Kiko, Central Square'de Mimi ile mutlu mutlu çıtır bir elma yerken dişi "funny" hisseder.
Endişelenir. Mimi fark eder ve Tooth Helper Nella'ya gitmeyi önerir. Kiko önce korkar
("I don't want to go."). Mimi elini tutar ve "It's okay to feel scared. I'll come with you."
der. Birlikte meydanın kenarındaki sıcak, yumuşak Tooth Helper Corner'a giderler. Nazik
beaver Nella onları güler yüzle karşılar, küçük yuvarlak bir aynayla dişlerini yavaşça sayar
("One, two, sparkle!"). Kiko rahatlar. Nella "All happy and healthy!" der. Kiko gülümser:
"That wasn't so scary!" Bölüm sıcak bir gülümseme ve tekrarlanan cümleyle kapanır.

Voice continuity rule: The speaking voice for each character MUST remain identical

## Colour Notes

```text
The full episode must appear colour graded as one continuous film.
Identical white balance, exposure, colour temperature, saturation, contrast, brightness and pastel palette across all shots.
No cool shift. No warm shift. No green tint. No magenta tint. No orange grading. No HDR look. No cinematic LUT.
The viewer must not perceive a shot boundary.
```

---

## Lighting Notes

```text
Lighting continues across shots without reinterpretation.
Identical light direction, intensity, shadow softness, ambient lighting, highlight behaviour, canopy brightness and grass brightness.
The episode must not become brighter, darker, warmer, cooler, harsher or more cinematic between shots.
```
