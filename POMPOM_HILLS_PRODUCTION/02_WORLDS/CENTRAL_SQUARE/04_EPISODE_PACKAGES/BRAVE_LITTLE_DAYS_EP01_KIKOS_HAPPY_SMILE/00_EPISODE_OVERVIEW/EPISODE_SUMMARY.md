# Brave Little Days EP01 — "Kiko's Happy Smile" (Summary)

**Series:** Brave Little Days | Gentle Everyday Skills
**Length:** 120s (8 × 15s) + shared opening/closing bumpers
**Location:** Central Square — Tooth Helper Corner
**Characters:** Kiko, Mimi, Nella (Tooth Helper)
**Theme:** It's okay to feel scared; visiting a gentle helper keeps our smile happy.

Kiko is happily eating a crunchy apple with Mimi when her tooth feels "funny." She's a
little worried, and Mimi suggests visiting the tooth helper. Kiko is scared and doesn't
want to go. Mimi holds her hand — "It's okay to feel scared. I'll come with you." — and
they walk together to the cozy Tooth Helper Corner. Nella the beaver welcomes them warmly
and gently counts Kiko's smile with a little round mirror. Kiko relaxes: "That wasn't so
scary!" The episode closes warm and smiling: "It's okay to feel scared. Helpers help us
feel better."

**Repeated phrase:** "It's okay to feel scared."
**Learning point:** Feeling scared is normal; helpers are gentle and asking for help is good.
