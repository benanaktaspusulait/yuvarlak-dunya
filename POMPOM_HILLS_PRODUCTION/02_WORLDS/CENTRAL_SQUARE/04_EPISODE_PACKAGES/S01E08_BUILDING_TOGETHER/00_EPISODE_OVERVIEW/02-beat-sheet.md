# S01E08 — Beat Sheet

> 8 beats × 15s = 120s. Message: "It's easier when we build together, and if it breaks we can try again."

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Noah finds the blocks; Arda arrives. "Look at these blocks." / "Can we build?"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2 (Shot 02): They choose shapes — round vs flat. "This one?" / "The flat one first."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3 (Shot 03): First tower rises but wobbles. "Careful..." / "I am careful!"
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Tower falls softly, blocks scatter gently. "Oh no!" / "It fell."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Arda feels discouraged; Noah reassures. "I can't do it." / "We can try together."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6 (Shot 06): New plan — big block bottom, small on top. "Big block first." / "Small block on top?" / "Yes."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 7 (Shot 07): They rebuild together — one holds, one places. "You hold it." / "I've got it."
────────────────────────────────
Beat 8 (Shot 08): The tower stands; they celebrate. "We did it!" / "Together!"
```

---

## Emotional Arc

Curiosity → Planning → Rising tension → Setback → Feeling (empathy centre) → Problem-solving → Cooperation → Warm resolution
