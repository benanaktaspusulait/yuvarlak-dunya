## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — Let's Build (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Under the big pompom tree, colorful blocks sit on the ground. Noah is examining them. Arda arrives and sits beside him.
**Dialogue:** Arda: "Want to build?" / Noah: "Yes!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 02 — It Fell (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Noah stacks a block, another, another. Arda adds one too. The tower topples.
**Dialogue:** Noah: "Oh no!" / Arda: "It fell!"
**Camera:** Medium → close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 03 — Try Again (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Noah smiles and suggests trying again. Together, carefully and slowly, they stack the blocks.
**Dialogue:** Noah: "Let's try again." / Arda: "Okay!"
**Camera:** Medium → close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 04 — We Did It (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — The tower stands tall. Both look proud as the light turns golden.
**Dialogue:** "We did it!" / "Together!"
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

---

*Learning: Falling down sometimes just means trying again — and trying together is best of all.*
