These four files are the original 60-second (4 shots × 15s) version of "Building Together".

## Why superseded

The 1-minute version told the story correctly but too fast (gather → build → fall → retry →
success), leaving the emotional "try again together" beat thin. The episode was expanded to a
proper 8-beat, 2-minute structure instead of stretching the 4 shots (which would cause dead air
and OpenArt drift).
