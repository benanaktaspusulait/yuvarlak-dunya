# Milo's Pond — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A clear, round, calm pond with soft blue-green water. Lily pads float gently on the surface. Smooth round stones line the edge. Tall green reeds sway softly at the borders. A small wooden dock extends into the water. Soft reflections shimmer on the surface. Warm golden sunlight, gentle breeze. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, peaceful and safe atmosphere. No dark water, no deep drops, no sharp rocks, no modern objects.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the pond from the bank
- Image 2 (back): view from across the pond
- Image 3 (left): side view showing reeds and dock
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of lily pads

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and blue-green palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
