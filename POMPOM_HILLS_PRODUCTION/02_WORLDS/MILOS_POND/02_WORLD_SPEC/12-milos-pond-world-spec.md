# Milo's Pond — World Creation Specification

> Source of truth: `12-milos-pond-bible.md` (canonical, creative). This document
> is a production-oriented extraction for AI-assisted world generation. It does
> not replace the Bible and does not duplicate its storytelling, educational,

---

## Purpose

Milo's Pond is a small, calm, round pond that functions as the meditative
heart of Pompom Hills. It is where silence, nature, and quiet discovery come
alive through the simple beauty of still water and lily pads.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Milo's Pond and must never change:

- Small, round, clear pond
- Clear blue-green water
- Round green lily pads floating on surface
- Tall reeds along the edges
- Smooth round stones around shore
- Small stream feeding into the pond
- Calm, meditative atmosphere
- Shallow, safe water (0.3-0.5m depth)

---

## Visual Identity

A small, fully round pond with no rectangles, no straight edges, no sharp
corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every element a natural,
handcrafted quality. The palette is calm and natural, centred on blue-green
water with green lily pads and earthy reeds. The overall feeling is a "living
toy pond" — approachable, calm, and visually simple enough for a preschool
audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous natural space rather than a set of separate
areas. The stone path leads to the pond edge, which flows naturally to lily
pads, reeds, observation point, shore rest, and stream entry. All zones are
open and connected without barriers between them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The pond edge is visible from the stone path.
- The lily pad zone can be seen from the shore.
- The reed area frames the view from the observation point.
- The stream is visible from the pond edge.
- The observation point provides a view of the entire pond.
- The shore rest area is visible from all zones but feels separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Pond Edge** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the water. Visual identity: smooth round stones, clear water edge. Important props: stones, water. Typical use: arrival, first water contact.

**Lily Pad Zone** — Purpose: water surface life zone. Visual identity: round green lily pads floating on clear water. Important props: lily pads. Typical use: observation, quiet discovery.

**Reed Area** — Purpose: natural vegetation and hiding zone. Visual identity: tall green-brown reeds along the edge. Important props: reeds. Typical use: hiding, listening to wind.

**Observation Point** — Purpose: viewing the entire pond. Visual identity: elevated position with panoramic view. Important props: smooth stones, wide view. Typical use: perspective, calm observation.

**Shore Rest Area** — Purpose: rest and quiet sitting. Visual identity: smooth stones under tree shade. Important props: stones, tree. Typical use: resting, listening to water.

**Stream Entry** — Purpose: water source flowing into pond. Visual identity: small stream with smooth stones. Important props: stream, stones. Typical use: water flow observation, discovery.

---

## Materials

Water: clear, soft, natural. Lily pads: soft, round, textured. Reeds: natural,
tall, thin. Stones: smooth, round, grey. Tree: soft, natural, providing shade.
Stream: clear, flowing, gentle. No hard surfaces, no sharp edges, no artificial
materials anywhere. Everything reads as natural and safe.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is soft daylight. Morning brings warm golden light reflecting on
water; midday is bright with water sparkle; evening turns golden with long
shadows; night is soft moonlight on calm water. Outdoor lighting is always
natural daylight. Shadows are always soft — never hard, never dark.

---

## Colour Palette

Water: clear blue-green. Lily pads: green. Reeds: green-brown. Stones: grey.
Grass: soft green. Tree: natural green. The palette stays within this calm,
natural set across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. The pond is intimate, not
vast — small enough to see across, large enough to feel like a real pond.
Water is shallow and safe. Stones are smooth and child-sized. Reeds are
tall enough to create gentle framing but not overwhelming.

---

## Atmosphere

Calm. Soft. Safe. Meditative. Every surface reads as natural and gentle.
The mood is always peaceful and inviting — full of quiet sounds and gentle
movement. The pond never feels threatening or overwhelming.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The pond is always small, round, and clear — never deep, never murky.
- Water colour stays clear blue-green across every generation.
- Lily pads stay round and green.
- Reeds stay tall and natural.
- Stones stay smooth and round.
- No deep water, no fast currents, no threatening creatures.
- No dark or high-contrast lighting.
- The pond's core shape, colours, and proportions are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The pond must be generated as a single calm water space with natural
elements, not as a large lake or dangerous body of water — this is the
defining trait of this world and the most common failure point to watch for.
Keep all shapes rounded with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour
palette limited to the calm natural set defined above. Favor a soft, matte,
natural material language over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering.
Treat the pond's shape, colours, and proportions as a fixed identity to
preserve exactly, not to reinterpret, across every generation.

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/02_WORLDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Milo's Pond must never be reduced to "a pond with lily pads."

This layer protects Milo's Pond from becoming too plain. Keep the round
clear pond readable and the calm silhouette clear — add only the
canon-approved charm details below. Do not add random objects. Do not
remove the quiet, meditative emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** MP-POND-01, the round, clear blue-green pond, is
the primary landmark and must be visible as the calm centre of every shot.
MP-LILY-01, the 5-7 round green lily pads floating on the surface, add
landmark and surface charm, together with gentle ripples spreading from the
lily pads and stream entry and soft reflections of sky and reeds on the
water. Smooth round grey stepping stones along the edge and tall reeds
framing one side of the pond are the central reusable small props, with
MP-DUCK-01 glimpsed occasionally near the shore. Gentle motion — MP-LILY-01
drifting slowly across the surface and the reeds swaying in the breeze —
keeps the world feeling alive without adding clutter.

**Forbidden clutter:** deep or murky water, fast current, scary creatures,
dirty or polluted water, sharp stones, tall waves, a dark or stormy
atmosphere, and characters or logos in the Hero View.

### Hero View Quality Tests

Before accepting a Hero View, it must pass:

1. **Silhouette Test** — recognisable as Milo's Pond from the small round pond with drifting lily pads alone?
2. **Colour Test** — recognisable from the clear blue-green water, green lily pads, and soft reed tones alone?
3. **Charm Test** — does it make a child want to sit quietly and watch the water?

If any test fails, reject.
