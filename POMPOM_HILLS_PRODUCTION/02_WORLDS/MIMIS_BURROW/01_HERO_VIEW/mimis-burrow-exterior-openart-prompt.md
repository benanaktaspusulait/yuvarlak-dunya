## Hero View QA Checklist

**APPROVE if ALL are YES:**

- [ ] **Entrance:** Round? Blue (#90CAF9 or close)? Open with no door panel?
- [ ] **Glow:** Warm interior glow visible?
- [ ] **Stones:** Exactly three round grey stepping stones (not 2, not 4, not more)?
- [ ] **Flowers:** Visible on top of entrance? Not covering entrance?
- [ ] **Composition:** Exterior view only? Entrance is central hero element (15-25% frame height)?
- [ ] **Scale:** Mimi-sized, small and cozy? Not human-house-sized?
- [ ] **Mood:** Warm, safe, inviting? Not dark or cave-like?
- [ ] **Identity:** Would a child recognise this as Mimi's Burrow from silhouette alone?

**REJECT if ANY are YES:**

- [ ] Entrance looks like a door, cave, tunnel, or dark hole
- [ ] Wrong number of stepping stones
- [ ] Flowers cover or obscure entrance
- [ ] Entrance too small (less than 10% frame height)
- [ ] Full interior room visible (should be only glow + soft blue hint)
- [ ] Hobbit-house or adult-sized scale
- [ ] Dark, cold, or scary atmosphere
