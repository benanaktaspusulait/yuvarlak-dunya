# Mimi's Burrow — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Bu dosya Mimi's Burrow için standart OpenArt prompt formatıdır.
> Detaylı dış ve iç mekan prompt'ları ayrı dosyalarda mevcuttur.

---

## Dış Mekan (Hero View)

Ana referans: `mimis-burrow-exterior-openart-prompt.md`

### Kısa Prompt (1 image mode)

```
A cozy hidden underground burrow beneath soft green grass. Small round open entrance 
embedded in a soft grassy mound, framed in soft matte blue (#90CAF9), no door panel. 
Warm golden interior light glows from inside. Exactly three small round light-grey 
stepping stones lead toward the entrance. Small soft flowers on top of the mound. 
Soft green grass surrounds the burrow. Mimi-sized scale — small, hidden, intimate, 
not human-house-sized. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, 
warm and safe atmosphere. No sharp edges, no dark interior, no modern objects.
```

---

## İç Mekan

Ana referans: `mimis-burrow-interior-openart-prompt.md`

### Kısa Prompt (1 image mode)

```
Inside Mimi's cozy underground burrow. Warm soft blue walls with rounded edges. 
A small round cushion bed with soft blankets. A tiny shelf with a few small toys. 
Warm golden light from a soft lamp. Everything is small, child-proportioned, 
round, soft and touchable. No sharp edges, no dark corners, no modern objects. 
Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, cosy and safe atmosphere.
```

---

## 2–4 Image Mode

Dış ve iç mekan prompt'ları ayrı ayrı kullanılır. Text prompt devre dışı kalır.

- Image 1: Dış mekan (giriş, çimen, taşlar)
- Image 2: İç mekan (yatak, lamba, raflar)
- Image 3 (opsiyonel): Girişten içeri bakış
- Image 4 (opsiyonel): Detay (yastık, oyuncak)
