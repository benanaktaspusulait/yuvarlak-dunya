## Production Safety Notes

### Critical Changes from Original:
1. **Location moved** from Flower Hill → Mimi's Burrow (better fits sleep theme)
2. **Reduced from 8 to 5 shots** (lower risk, cleaner flow)
3. **Stream removed** (high risk: water motion, sound, landmark spawning)
4. **Bench removed** (unnecessary complication)
5. **Master Setup Shot 01** seeds ALL objects upfront
6. **No camera tracking/panning** (static shots only)
7. **Consistent lighting** (evening dusk throughout)
8. **Simplified object count** (only flower patch, tree, leaves)

### OpenArt Safety Rules Applied:
1. **@image1 continuity:** All shots use Shot 01 as @image1 reference
2. **No camera movement:** Static shots only, no pan/track/pull-back
3. **Object seeding:** All interaction objects visible in Shot 01
4. **Lighting consistency:** Same dusk lighting throughout
5. **Character limits:** Only Kiko and Mimi, simple interactions
6. **No complex audio:** Natural ambience only, no music/melody
