# Butterfly Meadow — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A wide soft green meadow with gentle rolling hills at the edges. Dense clusters of round pastel flowers in red, yellow, pink, purple, and orange scattered across the field. A cloud of pastel butterflies — pink, purple, and yellow — drifting in slow curved paths just above the flowers. Short soft wavy green grass. A few round-topped trees with soft canopies at the meadow's edge. Warm soft morning sunlight from the upper left. Smooth round stepping stones winding through the flowers. Distant soft hills on the horizon. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, magical and peaceful atmosphere. No dark colours, no scary insects, no sharp corners, no clutter, no modern objects.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the meadow with butterflies above the flowers
- Image 2 (back): view from the observation hill looking across the meadow
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the flower garden and wind path
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of butterflies dancing above pastel flowers

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
