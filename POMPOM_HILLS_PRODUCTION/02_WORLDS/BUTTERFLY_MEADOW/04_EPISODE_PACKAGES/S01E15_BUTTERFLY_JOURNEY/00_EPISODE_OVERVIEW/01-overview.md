## Story Summary

Luca and Kiko are playing in Butterfly Meadow when a bright blue butterfly flutters past. Luca's eyes follow it — he's curious where it's going. He gently follows the butterfly through fields of wildflowers. Kiko runs to catch up. The butterfly leads them past tall daisies, over a tiny wooden bridge, and into a hidden corner of the meadow they've never seen before. The butterfly lands on Luca's open hand. He holds his breath. Kiko leans in close. They watch the butterfly rest peacefully. Then it opens its wings and flies away toward a cluster of flowers where more butterflies gather. Luca and Kiko stand together, amazed — they followed one small butterfly and discovered a secret garden.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

The butterfly landing on Luca's open hand — he holds perfectly still, eyes wide with wonder — and discovering that one small curiosity can lead to a whole hidden world.
