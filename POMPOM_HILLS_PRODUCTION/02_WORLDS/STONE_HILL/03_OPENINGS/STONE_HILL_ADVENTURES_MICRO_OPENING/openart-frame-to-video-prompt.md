# Stone Hill Adventures — OpenArt Frame-to-Video Prompt

> Copy-paste ready. This is the exact prompt to use in OpenArt when an approved Stone Hill
> opening frame already exists. It does not replace `render-prompts.md` (Option A / Option B) —
> it is the practical next step once a frame is already approved and uploaded as `@image1`.

## Use

Use this when:

- an approved Stone Hill opening frame already exists
- OpenArt labels the uploaded frame as `@image1`
- the goal is to generate the final 4-second reusable Stone Hill micro-opening video

## Duration

4 seconds.

## Main Prompt

```text
@image1

Use this image as the first frame for a 4-second Stone Hill Adventures micro-opening.

Keep the same natural Stone Hill discovery area from the reference image: rounded stones, pebble path, warm moss, small grass tufts, gentle slope and warm morning daylight.

Create a very slow, stable camera glide or soft push-in moving gently forward along the pebble path between the rounded stones.

Small grass and moss tufts may move very slightly in a soft breeze.

No characters appear. No story action. No reveal. No building. No house. No door. No roof. No bench. No cave. No waterfall. No tower. No entrance. No signs. No readable text. No logo. No animals. No modern objects. No sharp rocks. No cliffs.

Camera: extremely slow, stable, child-eye-level, 28–35mm, no fast zoom, no shake, no whip pan.

Duration: 4 seconds.

The final frame should still feel like the same Stone Hill location and should be easy to cut into Shot 01.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
characters, extra characters, text, readable text, logo, title card, subtitles, captions, speech bubbles, house, building, door, roof, bench, cave, waterfall, tower, entrance, sign, hut, stone house, doorway, window, chimney, constructed structure, wooden structure, bridge, pond, river, stream, water feature, sharp rocks, cliffs, quarry, dark lighting, sunset, moonlight, fast zoom, camera shake, whip pan, morphing, blurry, low quality, modern objects, clutter
```

## OpenArt Settings

- Duration: 4 seconds
- Motion: low / subtle / gentle
- Camera: slow push-in or gentle glide

## Approval Checklist

**Accept if:**

- same approved Stone Hill frame is preserved
- slow stable movement only
- no characters
- no text
- no buildings
- no waterfall
- no cave
- no sharp rocks
- no fast camera movement
- final frame can cut naturally into Shot 01

**Reject if:**

- background morphs too much
- camera moves too fast
- buildings, doors, caves or waterfalls appear
- characters appear
- text or logo appears
- it no longer feels like the same Stone Hill location

## Save As

After approval, save the final video as:

- `stone-hill-adventures-opening-video.mp4`

Also export:

- `stone-hill-adventures-opening-first-frame.png`
- `stone-hill-adventures-opening-final-frame.png`
