Look what Luca found on Stone Hill! 🌈

In this gentle Pompom Hills preschool animation, Luca finds a smooth round stone and shows

on Stone Hill, and decide to share their discovery so everyone can see it.

This calm story supports early learning through noticing shapes, curiosity, gentle
observation and the joy of sharing a discovery with a friend.
