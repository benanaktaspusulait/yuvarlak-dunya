## Stone Hill Visual Identity

Stone Hill is the gentle stone-discovery hill of Pompom Hills: smooth rounded toy-like
stones, a soft pebble path, warm moss patches, small grass tufts, a gentle slope and
child-safe rounded rock clusters. Calm, tactile and safe.

Required anchors: smooth rounded stone clusters, soft pebble path, gentle slope,
warm moss between stones, child-safe rounded rock shapes, warm morning daylight.

Never: sharp rocks, cliffs, realistic quarry, dark cave, dangerous boulders,
generic rocky field.

## Opening Hook

The first 3-5 seconds should immediately create curiosity.
Begin with an interesting visual, a surprising action, a curious question, or an
emotional expression (e.g. "What's this?").

## Scale

Characters should feel small and childlike within the environment, but must remain
readable for facial expression and action. Use wide framing for environment identity
and medium framing for emotional beats.
