## Characters

- Kiko (friend)

## Location

Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area

## Time of Day

Morning

## Lighting

Warm morning daylight

## Theme

Curiosity, noticing shapes, sharing discovery, friendship.

## Core Story Summary

Luca finds a smooth round stone on Stone Hill and shows it to Kiko. Together they examine
it, notice how round it is, and see that Stone Hill is full of round shapes. Instead of
keeping the stone for themselves, they gently place it where everyone can see it, ending
on a warm, shared discovery.

## Main Prop

A smooth, round, flat stone.
