## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — Discovery (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Luca walks the pebble path among rounded stones, spots a smooth round flat stone, bends down, picks it up and examines it with a curious smile.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Oh! What's this?"
**Camera:** 35mm wide → gentle push-in to medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 02 — Showing Kiko (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Kiko enters naturally from the same path. Luca turns and shows the stone.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Hi Luca!" / Luca: "Look what I found!" / Kiko: "Wow!"
**Camera:** Continue from Shot 01, medium-wide, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 03 — So Round (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Kiko holds the stone, Luca points to its round shape, they turn it slowly and wonder.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "It's so round." / Luca: "Like a tiny moon!"
**Camera:** Medium, gentle focus shift, eye level, 0.80m. (Moon is imaginative only.)

### Shot 04 — What Else Is Round? (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — They look around and notice other rounded stones already in the set. Kiko points to one; Luca gestures to the hill.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "This one is round too." / Luca: "Stone Hill has so many shapes!"
**Camera:** Gentle pan across visible stones → back to characters, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 05 — A Place for the Stone (60-75 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — They find a small safe mossy spot and gently place the stone flat on the moss.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Should we keep it?" / Kiko: "Maybe we can share it."
**Camera:** Medium → medium-low, placement visible, 0.75m. Stone placed flat (no stacking).

### Shot 06 — Together (75-90 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — They step back and look at the placed stone together, smiling. Warm shared hold.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Now everyone can see it." / Kiko: "Together." / Luca: "Together."
**Camera:** Gentle pull-back → wide quiet hold, eye level, 0.80m. ~2s final hold.

---

*Learning: A small discovery, noticed together and shared with everyone, becomes something special.*
