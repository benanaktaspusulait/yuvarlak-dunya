# S01E07 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1: Luca discovers a smooth round stone on Stone Hill.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2: Luca shows it to Kiko, who joins from the same path.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3: Together they examine the stone and notice how round it is.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4: They look around and see Stone Hill is full of round shapes.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5: They gently place the stone where others can see it — sharing over keeping.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6: They step back together. A warm, shared discovery.
```
