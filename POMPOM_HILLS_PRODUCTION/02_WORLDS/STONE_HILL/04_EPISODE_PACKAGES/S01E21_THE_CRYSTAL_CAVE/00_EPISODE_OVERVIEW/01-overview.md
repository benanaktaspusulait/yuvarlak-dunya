## Story Summary

Luca is climbing gently on Stone Hill when he notices a warm glow coming from behind some soft moss. He parts the moss and finds the entrance to a cozy cave — round, inviting, and glowing with soft warm light. He calls Kiko over. They peek inside together. The cave is small and safe, like a cozy room made of stone. Inside, crystals catch the light and scatter tiny rainbows across the walls. Luca reaches out and gently touches a crystal — it glows a little brighter. Kiko touches another one, and it glows too. They move deeper, touching crystals, watching rainbows dance. The cave feels like a warm, sparkling secret. Luca and Kiko sit together in the soft glow, eyes wide with wonder — they found a hidden world of beauty right under their feet.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Luca touching a crystal and watching it glow brighter — the moment a hidden cave becomes a living, sparkling wonder, and both characters realize that the most beautiful things are often hidden in cozy, unexpected places.

## Colour Notes

```text
Warm cave palette. Soft golden light, crystal colors (pink, blue, amber), stone textures.
Identical white balance, exposure, colour temperature, saturation, contrast across all shots.
No cool shift. No desaturated look. No green tint. No magenta tint.
The cave interior should feel warm and golden, never cold or dark.
Crystal colors must remain consistent across all shots.
```

---

## Lighting Notes

```text
Warm, soft glow inside the cave. Crystals scatter gentle rainbow light.
Identical light direction, intensity, shadow softness across all shots.
The cave should feel cozy and inviting, like a warm room.
No harsh shadows. No dark corners. No cold blue light.
Always warm. Always safe. Always glowing.
```
