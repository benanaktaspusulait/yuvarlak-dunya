## Visual Richness & World Charm

> See `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/WORLD_STANDARDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Stone Hill must NOT be reduced to "a hill with rocks." If Stone Hill looks like
> a generic rock pile, reject it.

Stone Hill is the round pastel stone climbing-and-discovery hill of Pompom Hills.
It should feel like: **"I want to hop across every stone and peek inside the
little cave."**

### Iconic Landmark

SH-STONES-01 — the clusters of round pastel stones (warm grey #BDBDBD, pink
#F48FB1, purple #CE93D8) that cover the hillside. This is the primary visual
anchor: the object that turns "a hill" into "the hill made of colourful round
stones you can climb."

### Allowed Charm Details

**Landmark Charm**
- SH-STONES-01 — clusters of round pastel stones in warm grey, pink and purple, scattered across the slope (already a Locked Element, see World Identity Lock below)
- SH-CAVE-01 — small round, friendly cave tucked into the hillside, safe to peek into

**Surface Charm**
- soft green grass (#C8E6C9) growing between and around the stones
- gently curved, rounded climbing paths winding between the stone clusters

**Small Prop Charm**
- varied stone sizes (0.2–0.5 m) grouped naturally along the path, never in a uniform pile

**Motion Charm**
- small şelale (waterfall) trickling gently near the cave, soft continuous water sound
- soft morning-to-evening light shift across the pastel stones

### Forbidden Clutter

```
✗ sharp-edged or jagged stones
✗ dark, scary or maze-like cave
✗ generic grey rock pile with no colour identity
✗ muddy or murky water
✗ modern objects, ropes, or climbing gear
✗ harsh or dramatic lighting inside the cave
✗ realistic/photoreal rock texture
```

### Thumbnail Hook

A sunny hillside covered in round pastel stones with a friendly little cave
and a soft trickling waterfall.

### Child Recognition Test

**"The hill with the colourful round stones and the little cave."**
