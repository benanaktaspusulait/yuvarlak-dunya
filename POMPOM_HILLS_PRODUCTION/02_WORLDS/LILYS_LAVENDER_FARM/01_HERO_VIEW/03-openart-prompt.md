# Lily's Lavender Farm — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

> Use this short plain-text prompt in the OpenArt World Builder text field.

---

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
Rows of deep purple lavender stretching gently across a soft rolling meadow. A small white windmill with rounded blades stands at the centre. Tiny bee houses dot the edges. Warm golden sunlight filters through the lavender. Soft green grass between the rows. Distant pastel hills on the horizon. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, warm and peaceful atmosphere. No modern objects, no sharp edges, no dark shadows.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:

- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the lavender rows from the path
- Image 2 (back): view from the windmill looking across the field
- Image 3 (left): side view showing bee houses and flowers
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of lavender and bees

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and purple palette.

---

## After the world is built

Use the bible at `00_CANON/` as your production reference when framing shots, placing characters, and running QA inside the generated world.
