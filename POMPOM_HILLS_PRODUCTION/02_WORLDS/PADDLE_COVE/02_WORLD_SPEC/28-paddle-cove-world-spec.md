---

## Purpose

Paddle Cove is a small, round, shallow water edge that functions as the
discovery heart of Pompom Hills. It is where feet touch water, stones skip,
and gentle exploration comes alive through the simple joy of wading and
observing.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Paddle Cove and must never change:

- Small, round, shallow water edge
- Maximum 10 cm water depth (ankle level)
- Clear light blue water (#81D4FA)
- Smooth round pebbles (grey, pink, yellow)
- Soft green grass shore
- Round stepping stones extending into water
- No swimming, no deep water
- No sharp corners anywhere

---

## Visual Identity

A small, fully round water edge with no rectangles, no straight edges, no
sharp corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every element a natural,
handcrafted quality. The palette is light and inviting, centred on light blue
water with grey-pink-yellow pebbles and green grass. The overall feeling is a
"living toy cove" — approachable, safe, and visually simple enough for a
preschool audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous water edge rather than a set of separate
areas. The entrance leads to the cove, which flows naturally from wading zone
to stone skipping area to observation shore to flower edge to quiet beach.
All zones are open and connected without barriers between them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The cove entrance is always visible from the path.
- The wading zone can be seen from the entrance.
- The stone skipping area is visible from the wading zone.
- The observation shore provides a view of the entire cove.
- The flower edge is visible from the observation shore.
- The quiet beach is visible from the flower edge but feels separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Cove Entrance** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the cove. Visual identity: round stones, shallow water visible. Important props: stones, water. Typical use: arrival, first glimpse.

**Wading Zone** — Purpose: feet in water zone. Visual identity: clear shallow water, soft grass edge. Important props: water, stones. Typical use: wading, touching.

**Stone Skipping Area** — Purpose: throwing stones and watching ripples. Visual identity: round pebbles, calm water surface. Important props: pebbles, water. Typical use: throwing, observing.

**Observation Shore** — Purpose: sitting and watching the water. Visual identity: soft grass, quiet water view. Important props: grass, view. Typical use: resting, observing.

**Flower Edge** — Purpose: flowers growing near water. Visual identity: colourful flowers at water's edge. Important props: flowers. Typical use: discovering, picking.

**Quiet Beach** — Purpose: rest and calm. Visual identity: soft sand, gentle water. Important props: sand, water. Typical use: sitting, breathing.

## Materials

Water: clear, shallow, light blue. Pebbles: smooth, round, colourful. Grass:
soft, green, plush. Stones: smooth, round, grey. Flowers: soft, round,
colourful. Sand: soft, warm. No hard surfaces, no sharp edges, no artificial
materials anywhere. Everything reads as natural and safe.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is warm natural sunlight. Morning brings soft golden light on
water; midday is bright with water sparkle; evening turns golden-orange with
longer soft shadows; night is soft moonlight on calm water. Cove lighting
is always natural daylight. Shadows are always soft — never hard, never dark.

---

## Colour Palette

Water: light blue (#81D4FA). Pebbles: grey, pink, yellow. Grass: soft green.
Flowers: pink, yellow, purple. Sand: warm beige. The palette stays within
this light, inviting set across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. The cove is intimate, not
vast — small enough to wade across, large enough to feel like a real beach.
Water is shallow and safe. Pebbles are child-sized. The cove never feels
overwhelming.

---

## Atmosphere

Light. Soft. Safe. Inviting. Every surface reads as natural and gentle.
The mood is always curious and calm — full of gentle sounds and soft
movement. The cove never feels deep or threatening.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The cove is always shallow, round, and safe — never deep, never vast.
- Water stays light blue and clear across every generation.
- Pebbles stay smooth and round.
- Grass stays soft and green.
- No swimming, no deep water, no fast currents.
- No sharp corners, no dirty water, no threatening creatures.
- The cove's core shape, colours, and proportions are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The cove must be generated as a single shallow water edge with safe
elements, not as a deep lake or dangerous beach — this is the defining trait
of this world and the most common failure point to watch for. Keep all shapes
rounded with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour palette limited to
the light inviting set defined above. Favor a soft, matte, natural material
language over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering. Treat the
cove's shape, colours, and proportions as a fixed identity to preserve
exactly, not to reinterpret, across every generation.

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `POMPOM_HILLS_PRODUCTION/00_GLOBAL_RULES/WORLD_STANDARDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Paddle Cove must never be reduced to "shallow water with pebbles."

This layer protects Paddle Cove from becoming too plain. Keep the round
shallow cove shape and the clear light-blue water always readable — add only
the canon-approved charm details below. Do not add random objects. Do not
remove the gentle, curious, water-discovery emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** the round shallow cove with clear light-blue
water (#81D4FA) is the primary landmark and must be visible in every wide
shot. Smooth round pebbles in grey, pink, and yellow along the shore and in
the water, plus the round stepping stones extending toward the water, add
landmark and surface charm. Small clusters of pink, yellow, and purple
flowers at the water's edge add small-prop charm. Gentle water ripples from
skipped stones and the soft trickle of the shallow water add motion charm.

**Forbidden clutter:** deep or dark water, fast currents, sharp or rough
stones, dirty or murky water, scary creatures, and sharp corners anywhere.

2. **Colour Test** — recognisable from the light-blue water and pastel pebbles alone?
