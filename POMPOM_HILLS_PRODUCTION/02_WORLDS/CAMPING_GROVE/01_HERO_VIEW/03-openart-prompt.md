# Camping Grove — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A cosy camping grove at twilight with a round stone fire circle at the centre, glowing warm orange. Three to four round colourful domed tents — red, yellow, and green — arranged around the fire. Smooth round sitting stones near the fire. Deep starlit night sky overhead with many soft glowing stars and a round white moon. Warm firelight patterns dancing on nearby grass and tent walls. Round-topped trees with soft green canopies at the edges. Soft green grass underfoot. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, warm and safe nighttime atmosphere. No open flame, no scary forest, no cold colours, no sharp corners, no modern camping gear.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the camp with fire circle and tents under stars
- Image 2 (back): view from inside the fire circle looking outward at the tents
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the entrance path and camp layout
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the campfire stones with warm embers

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
