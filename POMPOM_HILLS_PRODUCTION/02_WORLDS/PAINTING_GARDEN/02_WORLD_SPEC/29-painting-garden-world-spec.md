# Painting Garden — World Specification

> This specification defines the canonical Painting Garden world.
> This document is a dense generation instruction set.

---

## Identity

- One gentle flat garden with colourful flowers in 4 distinct colours
- Flowers are large, round, painterly — like brushstrokes, not realistic
- Soft white fence surrounds the garden — rounded, safe
- Paint pots and brushes scattered naturally — pastel colours
- Canvas on an easel — ready for painting
- Trees fresh green #66BB6A with soft brown trunks
- Path warm sand #FFE0B2
- Sky clear blue #81D4FA — warm, open
- No sharp edges, no dark corners, no realistic textures.
- Warm pastel palette. Handcrafted, soft, never photorealistic.

---

## Hero View Composition

| Zone | Frame % |
|------|:-------:|
| Sky | 50% |
| Garden (flowers + fence) | 35% |
| Ground (path + grass) | 15% |

- Garden fills the mid-ground — flowers visible edge to edge
- 4 colour zones: red, blue, yellow, purple — not mixed, grouped naturally
- Fence visible at garden edge — soft white, rounded
- Canvas on easel near centre-right
- Path enters from bottom-left
- Trees frame the garden on left and right
- Horizon at 45–50% from frame bottom

## Must Preserve

1. Flowers must be in 4 distinct colours — red, blue, yellow, purple
2. Flowers must be round and painterly — like brushstrokes
3. Fence must be soft white #FAFAFA — rounded, safe
4. Path must be warm sand #FFE0B2
5. Canvas and paint pots visible but not dominant
6. No realistic art supplies — everything is pastel and toy-like
7. No crowd, no competition
8. Soft shadows only — no harsh lighting

---

## Atmosphere

- Colourful, joyful, artistic
- Every flower is a brushstroke
- Every leaf is a palette
- Safe, calm, creative

---

## Consistency Rules

1. Every generation must match this specification
2. Flowers must be in 4 colours — never mixed in one cluster
3. Fence must surround the garden — not absent
4. Canvas must be visible but not centre-frame
5. No modern objects — no buildings, no signs, no vehicles
6. Characters must be small (10–12% of frame) — environment is the hero

---

## Video Rules

- Camera: 35mm–50mm, child eye-level
- Movement: slow pan, gentle push-in, locked-off shots
- No fast cuts, no shaky cam
- Lighting: warm diffused daylight always
- Characters may paint among flowers but flowers never move
