## Story Summary

Sofia walks through Painting Garden and notices a red flower. She picks it up gently and looks at its colour. Kiko joins her. They discover more flowers — yellow, purple, orange, pink. Sofia starts painting the flowers on a big leaf. Kiko helps mix colours from flower petals. They create a beautiful flower painting together. They show their art to the garden — other flowers seem to lean in to look. The garden feels proud. Sofia and Kiko smile, knowing nature inspired their best art.
