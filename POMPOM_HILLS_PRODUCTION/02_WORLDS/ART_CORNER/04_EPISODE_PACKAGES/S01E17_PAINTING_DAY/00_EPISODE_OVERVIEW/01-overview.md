## Story Summary

Sofia is painting a tree at Art Corner, mixing colors carefully. Kiko joins with her own brush. They paint side by side — Sofia works on a big tree, Kiko paints a small flower. Sofia's brush slips and a splash of orange paint lands on her tree. She stops, worried it's ruined. Kiko looks at it closely — the orange splash looks like a sun behind the tree. She adds a small yellow circle around it. Sofia sees it and smiles. They step back together to admire their painting: a tree with a warm sun behind it, a flower at its base, and tiny birds Sofia adds in the branches. They high-five gently. Their painting is more beautiful because they made it together — and because of the happy accident.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Kiko turning Sofia's paint splash into a sun — the moment an accident becomes art, and collaboration makes it even better.
