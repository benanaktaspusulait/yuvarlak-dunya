# Art Corner — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
An open-air painting studio in nature with round wooden easels holding white canvases. Vividly colourful paint palettes with many hues sit on round warm wooden tables. Round-handled paintbrushes rest in water cups. A round wooden display wall shows finished colourful artwork. Soft dappled sunlight filtering through nearby trees, warm natural light. Short green grass underfoot, soft pastel flowers at the edges. Warm, creative, inspiring atmosphere. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look. No sharp corners, no dirty paint, no dark lighting, no modern art tools, no clutter.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the studio area showing easels and palettes
- Image 2 (back): view from the display wall looking outward
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the studio entrance and surrounding nature
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of a paint palette with vivid colours

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
