# Benny's Playground — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A bright, round outdoor playground with a central smooth blue slide. Two round swings with red and yellow seats sway gently on the left. An orange climbing hill with soft round steps rises nearby. A round ball pit filled with red, yellow, blue, green, and purple balls sits in the corner. Soft plush green grass covers the entire ground surface. A colourful welcome gate with rounded lettering marks the entrance. Warm golden sunlight, soft shadows. A shaded rest corner with a soft wooden bench. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, fun and energetic atmosphere. No metal equipment, no sharp corners, no tall structures, no dark areas, no modern objects.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide panorama of the entire playground showing all equipment
- Image 2 (back): view from the climbing hill looking down at the playground
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the entrance gate and swings
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of the ball pit with colourful balls

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
