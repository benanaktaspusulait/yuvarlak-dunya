## Story Summary

Noah walks through Music Hill and notices that when the wind blows through the hollow trees, they make different sounds — some low, some high, some soft. He tries blowing gently into a hollow branch and it hums like a flute. Arda arrives and they discover different trees make different sounds. Noah blows into a tall tree — deep sound. Arda blows into a small bush — high sound. They start playing together, creating a rhythm. Wind joins them. They dance and play — a joyful wind-orchestra made of trees.
