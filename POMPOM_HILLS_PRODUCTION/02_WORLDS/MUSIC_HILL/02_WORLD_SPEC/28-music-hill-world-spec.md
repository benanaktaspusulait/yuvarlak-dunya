# Music Hill — World Specification

> This specification defines the canonical Music Hill world.
> This document is a dense generation instruction set.

---

## Identity

- One gentle grass dome with musical trees — trees that chime when wind passes
- Trees have round, colourful leaf clusters (not realistic foliage)
- Leaf clusters act as natural chimes — visual rhythm, not literal instruments
- Path winds gently up the hill — warm sand colour
- Stepping stones in soft coral — round, flat, safe
- Flowers melody pink #F48FB1 scattered naturally
- Sky clear blue #81D4FA — warm, open
- No structures, no fences, no modern objects. No thorns, no sharp stones.
- Warm pastel palette. Handcrafted, soft, never photorealistic.

---

## Hero View Composition

| Zone | Frame % |
|------|:-------:|
| Sky | 55% |
| Hill with trees | 35% |
| Ground (path + grass) | 10% |

- Hill fills the width — dome visible edge to edge
- 3–5 music trees spaced naturally on the slope
- Path winds from bottom-left to summit
- Stepping stones along the path
- Flowers scattered around tree bases
- Horizon at 40–45% from frame bottom

## Must Preserve

1. Trees must look musical — round leaf clusters, not realistic foliage
2. Path must be warm sand #FFE0B2
3. Flowers must be melody pink #F48FB1
4. Sky must be clear blue #81D4FA
5. No realistic instruments — music comes from nature
6. No crowd, no competition
7. Soft shadows only — no harsh lighting

---

## Atmosphere

- Warm, rhythmic, inviting
- Every tree feels like it could sing
- The air feels alive with gentle sound
- Safe, calm, creative

---

## Consistency Rules

1. Every generation must match this specification
2. Tree leaf clusters must be round and colourful
3. Path must wind naturally — not straight
4. Flowers must be scattered — not in rows
5. No modern objects — no fences, no buildings, no signs
6. Characters must be small (10–12% of frame) — environment is the hero

---

## Video Rules

- Camera: 35mm–50mm, child eye-level
- Movement: slow pan, gentle push-in, locked-off shots
- No fast cuts, no shaky cam
- Lighting: warm diffused daylight always
- Characters may move among trees but trees never move
