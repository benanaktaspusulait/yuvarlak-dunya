# Adventure Trail — OpenArt World Builder Prompt

## If uploading 1 image

Use the image as the primary reference and paste this prompt into the text field:

```
A long winding nature trail curving gently through soft green hills, marked by small round smooth grey stones along the edges. The path flows in soft S-curves, never straight, disappearing around bends into lush greenery. Warm golden morning sunlight from the upper left, soft shadows on the path. Short plush green grass on both sides, clusters of small pastel flowers along the trail edges. A few round-topped trees with soft green canopies at the forest section. A small gentle creek with clear water and a round stone bridge crossing it. Distant soft rolling hills on the horizon. Children's storybook style, soft matte handcrafted look, warm and adventurous atmosphere. No buildings, no fences, no sharp rocks, no dark clouds, no modern objects.
```

## If uploading 2–4 images

Do not rely on the text field — it will be disabled. Instead:
- Image 1 (front): wide shot of the trail winding from below, showing the first bend
- Image 2 (back): view from a bend looking back along the trail
- Image 3 (left): side view showing the trail curving through different terrain
- Image 4 (right, optional): close-up of round trail marker stones and flowers

All four images must share the same lighting direction, sky color, and colour palette.
