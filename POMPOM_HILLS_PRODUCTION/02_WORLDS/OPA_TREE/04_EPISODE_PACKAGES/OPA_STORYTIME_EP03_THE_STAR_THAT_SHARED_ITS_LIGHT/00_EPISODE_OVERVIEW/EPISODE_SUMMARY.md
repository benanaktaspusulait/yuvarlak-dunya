# Opa's Storytime: The Star That Shared Its Light — Episode Summary

---

## Story

Luca and Noah arrive under Opa's Tree at night as stars begin to appear. Opa tells a
gentle story about a little star that wanted to keep all its light for itself, until
it discovers that sharing its light makes the whole sky brighter. Luca and Noah ask
questions and react throughout the story.

## Learning

Sharing • Kindness • Togetherness

## Characters

Opa, Luca, Noah

## World

Opa's Tree (night)
