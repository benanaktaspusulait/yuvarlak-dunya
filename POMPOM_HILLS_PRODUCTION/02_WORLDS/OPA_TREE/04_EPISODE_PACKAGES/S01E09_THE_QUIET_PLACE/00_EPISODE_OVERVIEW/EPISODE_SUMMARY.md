## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — A Quiet Morning (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — A calm morning. The big tree's leaves sway slowly and wind chimes ring softly. Kiko arrives and looks up at the tree.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Hi Opa!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 02 — Coming Down (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — Opa sits on a branch above, looks down, and gently glides down beside Kiko, wings opening and closing.
**Dialogue:** Opa: "Hello, little one." / Kiko: "Can I stay?" / Opa: "Of course."
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 03 — The Pretty Leaves (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — The two sit on the ground and watch the leaves. A leaf falls, then another, quietly, both smiling.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "The leaves are pretty." / Opa: "They are."
**Camera:** Close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 04 — Thank You (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — The sun begins to set and golden light fills the scene. Kiko stands and looks at Opa.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "Thank you, Opa." / Opa: "Anytime."
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.
