# Brave Little Days EP12: Gentle Hands — Episode Summary

---

## Story

In the warm living room of Kiko's Home, Kiko and Arda both reach for the same soft toy car.
Kiko's hands tense up — the hitting impulse appears but she stops herself. She remembers the
breathing technique from EP11: "Breathe in, breathe out." After breathing, her hands soften
and she speaks instead of hitting: "I want a turn." Arda shares willingly, and the two friends
play together — Kiko traces a path, Arda drives the car along it. Kiko discovers: "Hands are
for helping, not hurting."

## Learning

Anger Management • Self-Calming • Verbal Expression • Cooperation
