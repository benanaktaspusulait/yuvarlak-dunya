# Gentle Hands — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — One Toy Car

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Wide establishing shot. The warm living room with soft rounded furniture is visible. One soft rounded toy car sits in the centre of the play rug. Kiko and Arda both notice it and reach for it gently.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Big Feeling

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on Kiko's hands — they tense up briefly. The hitting impulse is visible but does NOT become contact. Her face shows big feeling. Arda watches with mild concern.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — EP11 Callback: Breathe

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on Kiko's face. Her eyes soften as she remembers the breathing technique. She whispers: "Breathe in, breathe out." A moment of self-recollection.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Hands Soften

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on Kiko's hands — tension drops, fingers uncurl, palms open. Camera tilts up to her face as she speaks calmly: "I want a turn."

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Problem Solved

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium two-shot. Arda pushes the toy car to the centre: "Here you go." Both children begin to guide the car together on the rug.

## Shot 06 (63-78 sn) — Helping Hands

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on Kiko's hands tracing a path, then medium shot as Arda drives the car along it. Cooperative play — hands helping, not hurting.

## Shot 07 (78-90 sn) — Hands Are for Helping

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium two-shot as they park the car. Kiko looks at her hands, then at Arda: "Hands are for helping, not hurting." Arda smiles.

## Shot 08 (90-102 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Both characters sit beside the parked car, face the camera. They say: "Hands are for helping!" and give a small gentle wave. Soft fade.
