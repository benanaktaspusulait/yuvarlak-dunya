## Purpose

Open with a simple, readable toy desire: Kiko and Arda both reach for the same soft toy car on
the play rug. The problem is small — not a fight, but a gentle sharing challenge between friends.

---

## Opening Hook

First 3–5 seconds: the soft rounded toy car sits in the middle of the play rug. Kiko and Arda
both notice it at the same time and reach for it — not aggressively, but with eager curiosity.

---

## Continuity

First story shot, after the shared Brave Little Days opening bumper. This shot establishes the
living room setting, the toy rug, and the two characters' relationship. Background: warm daytime
light, soft rug, rounded furniture, safe home environment.
