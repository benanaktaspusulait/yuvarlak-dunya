## Purpose

Show the physical transformation: after breathing, Kiko's hands soften — tension drops, fingers
open, hands lower to her lap. She can now speak calmly. This is the moment where "vurma
yerine konuşma" (speaking instead of hitting) becomes visible.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Close-up on Kiko's hands — the tension visibly releases. Fingers uncurl,
palms open, hands settle onto her knees. The transformation is physical and immediate.
