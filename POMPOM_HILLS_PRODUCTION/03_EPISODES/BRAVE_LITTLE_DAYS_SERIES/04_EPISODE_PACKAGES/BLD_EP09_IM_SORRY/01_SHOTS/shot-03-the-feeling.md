## Purpose

The emotional reaction shot: Arda's smile fades as he sees his toppled tower; Kiko freezes
with the "Uh-oh" feeling. This shot establishes the emotional cost of the accident and
creates the opening for an apology.

blocks. A small, soft sadness. The world pauses.

"Uh-oh" — but no crying meltdown, no blame. Both freeze: "What should I do?" This moment
