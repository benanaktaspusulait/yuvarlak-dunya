# I'm Sorry — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Arda are at Central Square near Big Pompom Tree. They build a small tower together with 8 soft blocks. Both smile — cooperative play.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko reaches for a block and lightly bumps the tower. It softly topples — blocks scatter on the grass. "Oops!" The accident happens.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Arda's smile fades as he sees his toppled tower. Kiko freezes with the "Uh-oh" feeling. The emotional cost is established.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko slowly moves toward Arda and speaks sincerely: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" The apology moment — genuine and warm.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Kiko bends down and picks up a block: "Let's fix it." Both gather the scattered blocks together. Repair begins.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): They rebuild — this time shorter and wider, with a strong base. "Stronger!" The collaborative rebuild succeeds.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Arda smiles: "We did it!" Kiko relaxes and warmly reiterates: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" Arda: "I'm okay." Reconciliation.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): They sit beside the new tower, face the camera: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" Small wave. Warm closing.
```
