# Brave Little Days EP09: I'm Sorry — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning at Central Square near the Big Pompom Tree, Kiko and Arda build a small
tower together with 8 soft rounded blocks. Kiko accidentally bumps the tower while reaching
for a block, and it softly topples. Arda's smile fades. Kiko freezes, then moves toward
Arda and says: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" They rebuild together — this time shorter and
stronger. Arda smiles: "We did it!" The friendship is restored, and the lesson is clear:
saying sorry and making it right brings friends back together.

## Learning

Apologizing • Making It Right • Friendship Repair

Kiko, Arda
