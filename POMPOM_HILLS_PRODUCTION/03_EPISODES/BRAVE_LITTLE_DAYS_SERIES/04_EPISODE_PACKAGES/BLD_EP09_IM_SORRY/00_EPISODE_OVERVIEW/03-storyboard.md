# I'm Sorry — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — Building Together

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Wide establishing shot. Big Pompom Tree visible in background, soft green grass. Kiko and Arda kneel on the grass, placing soft rounded pastel blocks together to build a small tower. Both smile as they cooperate.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — The Soft Topple

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot. Kiko reaches for the next block; her arm lightly touches the tower. The tower softly topples — blocks scatter gently on the grass. Kiko pulls back: "Oops!" Arda watches, surprised.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — The Feeling

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Close-up on Arda's face as his smile slowly drops — small, soft sadness. Then medium on Kiko frozen with the "Uh-oh" feeling. A quiet emotional pause. Both look at the scattered blocks.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — I'm Sorry

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot following Kiko as she slowly moves toward Arda. She looks at him and says: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" Her voice is warm and sincere. Arda looks up; his expression softens.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Building Again

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot. Kiko bends down and picks up a block: "Let's fix it." Both kneel and gather the 8 scattered blocks together. Arda's small smile returns. Repair begins.

## Shot 06 (63-75 sn) — A Better Tower

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot, both characters side by side. They build a new tower — shorter, wider, with a strong base. "Stronger!" Both smile as the new tower is completed.

## Shot 07 (75-87 sn) — Making Up

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Close-up on Arda's smile: "We did it!" Medium on Kiko's relieved face: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" Close-up on Arda: "I'm okay." A warm, quiet exchange. Reconciliation.

## Shot 08 (87-99 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot at child eye level. Kiko and Arda sit beside the new tower, facing camera. Both say together: "I'm sorry. Are you okay?" Small gentle wave. Warm smiles. Soft fade.
