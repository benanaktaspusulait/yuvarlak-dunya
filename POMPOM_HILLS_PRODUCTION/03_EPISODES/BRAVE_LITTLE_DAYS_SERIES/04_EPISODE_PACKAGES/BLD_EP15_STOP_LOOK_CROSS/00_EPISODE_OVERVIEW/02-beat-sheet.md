# Stop, Look, Cross — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Opa arrive at the Pompom Crossing — a wide soft stepping-stone band on grass with two round colour discs. Kiko wants to step forward eagerly. Learning need established.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa gently says "Stop first." Kiko pauses — mild impatience, then acceptance. Eagerness meets patience.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Opa extends his wing-tip. Kiko takes it. They hold hands — the physical commitment to crossing together.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): The soft coral stop disc glows, then fades to soft green go disc. Kiko brightens: "Green!" Colour teaches without words.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Kiko and Opa slowly look right, then left. "Right... left!" — the looking ritual becomes a playful game.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): They step onto the stepping-stone band together, holding hands, stepping slowly. Kiko counts: "One step... two steps..."
Beat 7 (Shot 07): They reach the far side safely. Kiko smiles proudly: "We did it safely!"
Beat 8 (Shot 08): They face the camera together and say: "Stop, look, hold a hand." Warm closing with a small wave.
```
