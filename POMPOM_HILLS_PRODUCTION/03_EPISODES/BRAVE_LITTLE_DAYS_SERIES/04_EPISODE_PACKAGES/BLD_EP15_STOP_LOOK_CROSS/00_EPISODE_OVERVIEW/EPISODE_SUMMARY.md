# Brave Little Days EP15: Stop, Look, Cross — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning, Kiko and Opa arrive at the Pompom Crossing — a wide soft stepping-stone band
on grass with two round colour discs. Kiko wants to step forward eagerly, but Opa gently says
"Stop first." Kiko pauses, then takes Opa's wing-tip. Together they watch the colour change from
coral to green, look both ways, and step across the stones slowly and carefully. "We did it
safely!" Kiko smiles proudly. The episode closes with both characters saying: "Stop, look, hold
a hand."

## Learning

Safe crossing • Stop-look-hold ritual • Walking together

## Characters

Kiko, Opa

## World

Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path (on grass, NOT a real street)
