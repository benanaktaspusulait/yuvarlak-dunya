# Stop, Look, Cross — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — The Soft Crossing Path

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Wide establishing shot. The wide soft stepping-stone band stretches across green grass. Two round
colour discs sit at the near end — one coral, one green. Kiko and Opa stand at the start. Kiko
leans forward, one foot ready to step.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Wait First

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium two-shot. Kiko takes a small eager step forward. Opa gently raises his hand: "Stop first."
Kiko pauses — mild impatience, then acceptance. She says: "Okay."

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Hold My Hand

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Close-up on Opa's extended wing-tip and Kiko's small hand reaching for it. Their hands/wing-tips
connect gently — a moment of trust. Camera pulls back to medium two-shot.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Watch the Colour

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium shot showing both characters and the colour discs. The coral stop disc fades; the green go
disc lights up. Kiko brightens: "Green!" Opa smiles.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Look Both Ways

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium two-shot. Kiko and Opa slowly look right, then left. "Right... left!" — playful and
rhythmic. The world is calm and clear.

## Shot 06 (63-78 sn) — Crossing Together

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium shot, gentle tracking. Kiko and Opa hold hands and step onto the stepping-stone band.
They take slow, careful steps — one stone at a time. Kiko counts softly.

## Shot 07 (78-90 sn) — Safe Arrival

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium shot. Kiko and Opa reach the far side. Kiko's face lights up: "We did it safely!"
Opa smiles warmly. They are still holding hands.

## Shot 08 (90-102 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Pompom Crossing — soft crossing practice path

Medium shot, both characters centered. They say together: "Stop, look, hold a hand." Then a
small gentle wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
