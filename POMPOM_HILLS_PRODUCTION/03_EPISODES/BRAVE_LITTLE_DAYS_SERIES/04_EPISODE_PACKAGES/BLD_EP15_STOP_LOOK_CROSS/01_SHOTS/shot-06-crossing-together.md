## Purpose

The core crossing action: Kiko and Opa step onto the stepping-stone band together, holding
hands, and cross slowly and carefully. Each step is deliberate — this models safe crossing
behavior.

stepping-stone band. The stone is wide, flat, and safe underfoot.
