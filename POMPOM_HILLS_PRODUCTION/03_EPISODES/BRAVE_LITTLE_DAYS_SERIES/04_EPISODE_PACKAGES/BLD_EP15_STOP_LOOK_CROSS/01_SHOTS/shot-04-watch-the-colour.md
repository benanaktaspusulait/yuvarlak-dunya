## Purpose

Teach the colour signal: the soft coral disc (stop) transitions to soft green (go). Kiko and
Opa watch the colour change together — no text, only colour. This teaches "wait for green."

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: The soft coral stop disc glows gently. Kiko and Opa stand together,
holding hands, watching the disc. The coral colour is warm and clear.

---

## Continuity

Direct continuation from Shot 03. Kiko and Opa are now holding hands at the path start.
They watch the colour disc together. Background remains locked.

---

## Environment Anchors

- Same Pompom Crossing practice path; stepping-stone band visible.
- Two round colour discs: soft coral (currently lit) and soft green (off).
- Kiko and Opa holding hands, facing the discs.
- No road, no asphalt, no cars, no traffic.
- No new props, no new characters, no background movement.

---

## Background Object Lock

Identical to Shots 01–03. Background locked from first frame. Only Kiko, Opa, and the
colour discs' glow change move. The stepping-stone band and soft grass remain stable.

## Camera Direction

Medium shot showing both characters and the colour discs. Camera holds steady on the disc
as it transitions from coral to green. A gentle close-up on Kiko's face as she notices the
change. Then back to medium two-shot.

Kiko: Green!

## Sound

A soft, gentle "tick" as the colour transitions — natural, not mechanical. Kiko's "Green!"
is warm and delighted. No music, no electronic beep, no traffic signal sound.

---

## Emotion

Learning to read the colour signal. Kiko discovers that green means "now we can go" —
colour communicates without words. The moment is warm and celebratory in a small, natural way.
