## Purpose

Show that waiting is hard for Kiko — she wants to go, but Opa's gentle "Stop first" makes
her pause. This establishes the emotional beat: eagerness meets patience.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Kiko takes a small eager step forward; Opa gently raises his hand. The
movement is soft and natural — a calm guide, not a command.
