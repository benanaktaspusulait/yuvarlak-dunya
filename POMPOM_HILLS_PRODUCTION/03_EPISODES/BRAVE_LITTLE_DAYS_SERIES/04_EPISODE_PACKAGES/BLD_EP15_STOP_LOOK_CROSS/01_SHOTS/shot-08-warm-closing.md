## Purpose

The warm closing shot: Kiko and Opa stand together on the far side of the crossing path.
They deliver the repeated phrase "Stop, look, hold a hand" one final time, sealing the lesson
with a friendly goodbye.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Kiko and Opa stand together on soft grass, the stepping-stone band
behind them. Both face the camera with warm, relaxed smiles. They are still holding hands.

---

## Continuity

Direct continuation from Shot 07. They have crossed safely; now they deliver the final
lesson together. Background remains locked.

that safety is a learned ritual, not a rule. The lesson is complete, and the feeling is one
of gentle accomplishment.
