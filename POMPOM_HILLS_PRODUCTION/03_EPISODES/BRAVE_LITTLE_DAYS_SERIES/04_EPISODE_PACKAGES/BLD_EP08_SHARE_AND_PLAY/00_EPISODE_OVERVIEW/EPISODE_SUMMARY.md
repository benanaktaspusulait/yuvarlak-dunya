# Brave Little Days EP08: Share and Play — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning at Benny's Playground, Kiko finds a soft purple ball in the Ball Pit Area
and holds it close: "It's mine." Amara gently asks: "Can I play too?" Kiko pauses, sees
Amara, and chooses to share. The two friends roll the ball together in a gentle circle,
discovering that sharing is more fun than playing alone.

## Learning

Sharing • Cooperation • Empathy

## Characters

Kiko, Amara

## World

Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area
