## Purpose

The first shared activity: Amara shows Kiko the large round crayons on the table. Kiko's
attention shifts from hesitation to curiosity. The crayons are the bridge between "I'm new here"
and "I can do something here."

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Amara reaches across the table and gently rolls a few large, round,
soft-edged crayons toward Kiko. The crayons are bright, friendly, and safe — no sharp points,
no small pieces.
