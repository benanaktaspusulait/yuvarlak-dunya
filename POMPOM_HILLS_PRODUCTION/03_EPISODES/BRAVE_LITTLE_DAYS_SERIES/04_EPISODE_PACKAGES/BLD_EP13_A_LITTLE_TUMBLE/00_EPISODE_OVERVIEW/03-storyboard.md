# A Little Tumble — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — Walking Near the Rest Corner

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Wide establishing shot. The Rest Corner is visible with the low rest bench and soft grass.
Opa sits calmly on the bench. Kiko and Mimi walk slowly on the soft grass edge. Warm morning
light, safe and inviting.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — A Little Tumble

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Medium shot. Kiko walks slowly; her foot catches on a soft surface and she sits down gently
on the grass — a "soft sit-down." She pauses: "Ouch." Expression is mild surprise, not pain.
Mimi watches nearby, concerned but calm. Opa on the bench in background.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Asking for Help

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Medium shot on Kiko sitting on the grass. She turns toward Opa on the bench and says calmly:
"Help, please." Close-up on Kiko's face — calm and clear, not panicked. Opa begins to rise.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Gentle Check

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Medium shot. Opa reaches Kiko and kneels down to her eye level. Close two-shot as he says
gently: "I'm here." Kiko's expression softens from surprise to relief. Warm, calm moment.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Comfort Care

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Close-up on Opa's hands as he pulls a soft star sticker from his pocket. Gentle close-up as
he places it on Kiko's knee: "A little star for a brave girl." Kiko watches with curiosity,
then a small smile.

## Shot 06 (63-75 sn) — I'm Okay

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Close-up on Kiko's face as she looks at the star sticker on her knee. She takes a breath:
"I'm okay." Pull back to medium three-shot showing Opa and Mimi's warm reactions.

## Shot 07 (75-87 sn) — Back to Play

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Medium shot. Kiko slowly stands up from the grass. Star sticker visible on her knee. Mimi
asks: "Ready?" Kiko smiles: "Ready." They take a small step together — back to play, slow
and controlled. Opa watches from the bench.

## Shot 08 (87-99 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge

Wide three-shot. All three stand together, facing the camera with warm smiles. Kiko delivers
the repeated phrase: "It's okay — I can ask for help." They give a small gentle wave. Warm
smiles, soft fade.
