# A Little Tumble — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Mimi walk slowly near the Rest Corner. Opa sits calmly on the low bench. The scene is safe, warm, and calm — help is nearby.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko's foot catches on a soft surface and she sits down gently on the grass — a "soft sit-down." She says softly: "Ouch." Mild surprise, not pain.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Kiko doesn't panic. She turns toward Opa on the bench and says calmly: "Help, please." This is the core message moment.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Opa reaches Kiko and kneels to her eye level. He says gently: "I'm here." Kiko's expression softens from surprise to relief.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Opa pulls a soft star-shaped sticker from his pocket: "A little star for a brave girl." He places it gently on Kiko's knee. Kiko watches with curiosity, then a small smile.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Kiko looks at the star sticker, takes a breath, and says calmly: "I'm okay." The relief is genuine and natural.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Kiko slowly stands up. Mimi asks: "Ready?" Kiko smiles: "Ready." They take a small step together — back to play.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): All three stand together. Kiko delivers the repeated phrase: "It's okay — I can ask for help." They share a warm smile and a small wave.
```
