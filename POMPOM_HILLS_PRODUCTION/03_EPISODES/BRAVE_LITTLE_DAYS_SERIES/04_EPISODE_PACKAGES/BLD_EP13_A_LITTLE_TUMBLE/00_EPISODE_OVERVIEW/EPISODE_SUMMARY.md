# Brave Little Days EP13: A Little Tumble — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning at Benny's Playground, Kiko and Mimi walk slowly near the Rest Corner while
Opa sits on the nearby bench. Kiko's foot catches on a soft surface and she sits down gently
on the grass — a "soft sit-down," not a hard fall. She says softly: "Ouch." Without panic,
Kiko turns to Opa and asks calmly: "Help, please." Opa kneels to her eye level, says "I'm here,"
and places a soft star bandage sticker on her knee: "A little star for a brave girl." Kiko looks
at the sticker, takes a breath, and says: "I'm okay." She slowly stands up, Mimi asks "Ready?"
and Kiko says "Ready" — they take a small step together, back to play. All three stand together
and Kiko delivers the repeated phrase: "It's okay — I can ask for help."

## Learning

Asking for help • Calm response to small accidents • Comfort and reassurance

## Characters

Kiko, Mimi, Opa

## World

Benny's Playground — Rest Corner / soft grass edge
