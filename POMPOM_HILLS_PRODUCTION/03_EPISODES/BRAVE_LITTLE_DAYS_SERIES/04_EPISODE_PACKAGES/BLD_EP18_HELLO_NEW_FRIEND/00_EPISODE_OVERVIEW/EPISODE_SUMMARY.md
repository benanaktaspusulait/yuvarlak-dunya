# Brave Little Days EP18: Hello, New Friend — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm day at Friendship Meadow, Kiko and Mimi discover Sofia playing alone with colour
cards on a small round play blanket. Kiko feels shy and turns to Mimi: "I feel shy." Mimi
gently encourages: "Let's say hi." Kiko takes a step forward and says: "Hi, I'm Kiko. Can
I play too?" Sofia immediately smiles and extends a colour card. The three children play
together, arranging cards on the blanket. Sofia shows a small creation: "Look what I made!"
Kiko responds: "Wow!" The episode closes with Kiko recalling "Can I play too?" as a shared
memory — now a celebration of new friendship.

## Learning

Shyness • Courage • Friendship

## Characters

Kiko, Mimi, Sofia

## World

Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge
