# Hello, New Friend — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — A New Friend Nearby

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Wide establishing shot. The soft green meadow and low rest-circle seating are visible. Sofia
sits cross-legged on a small round play blanket, arranging large soft colour cards. Kiko and
Mimi stand a few steps away, noticing her with quiet curiosity.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — I Feel Shy

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Medium two-shot. Kiko looks at Sofia, then turns to Mimi with gentle hesitation. She says
softly: "I feel shy." Mimi listens with a warm, understanding expression.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Let's Say Hi

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Two-shot. Mimi looks at Kiko with a warm, encouraging smile and says: "Let's say hi."
Kiko listens; her face begins to open. A quiet moment of support.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Hi, I'm Kiko

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Medium shot. Kiko takes a small step toward Sofia and speaks warmly: "Hi, I'm Kiko. Can I
play too?" Sofia looks up from the colour cards, her face brightening. Mimi watches from
behind with a proud smile.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — The Invitation

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Close-up on Sofia's face as she smiles brightly. She holds a large soft colour card toward
Kiko: "Hi! Yes!" Kiko takes it; her face lights up with relief and happiness.

## Shot 06 (63-78 sn) — Playing Together

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Medium three-shot. All three children kneel around the small round play blanket. Kiko places
a red card, Mimi places blue, Sofia places yellow. They work together, smiling, creating a
gentle pattern.

## Shot 07 (78-90 sn) — New Friendship

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Close-up on Sofia's creation — a small arrangement from the colour cards. She holds it up:
"Look what I made!" Kiko responds: "Wow!" — genuine wonder. Mimi watches with a warm smile.

## Shot 08 (90-102 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Friendship Meadow — Rest Circle / Group Game Space edge

Medium three-shot. All three sit together on the soft grass near the blanket. Kiko says:
"Can I play too?" — now as a shared memory. Sofia & Mimi respond: "Yes!" Warm smiles, soft fade.
