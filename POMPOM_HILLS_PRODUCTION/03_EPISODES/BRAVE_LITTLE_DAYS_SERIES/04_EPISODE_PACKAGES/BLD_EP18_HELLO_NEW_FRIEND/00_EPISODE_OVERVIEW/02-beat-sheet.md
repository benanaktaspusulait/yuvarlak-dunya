# Hello, New Friend — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Mimi discover Sofia playing alone with colour cards on a small round play blanket at Friendship Meadow. Curiosity begins.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko turns to Mimi with gentle hesitation: "I feel shy." Mimi listens with warmth. The social courage challenge is established.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Mimi encourages softly: "Let's say hi." Kiko listens; her face begins to open. Support arrives without pressure.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko takes a step toward Sofia and says: "Hi, I'm Kiko. Can I play too?" Sofia looks up, her face brightening. The core courage moment.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Sofia smiles and extends a colour card toward Kiko: "Hi! Yes!" The fear of rejection dissolves. Relief and joy.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): All three children kneel around the blanket, placing colour cards together — red, blue, yellow. Cooperative play, shared creation.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Sofia shows a small creation from the colour cards: "Look what I made!" Kiko responds: "Wow!" The new friendship deepens.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): All three sit together. Kiko says: "Can I play too?" — now as a shared memory. Sofia & Mimi respond: "Yes!" Warm closing.
```
