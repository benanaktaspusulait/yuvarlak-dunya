# Thank You! — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — The Rolling Ball

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Wide establishing shot. The Big Pompom Tree is visible with soft roots. One soft yellow ball
rolls slowly across the grass toward the tree roots and stops just out of reach. Kiko watches
with surprise. Mimi stands nearby.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Reaching

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot. Kiko leans down and reaches toward the ball with her fingertips. The ball is
close but nestled between soft roots — not quite touchable. She pulls back slightly: "Oh no."
Mimi watches, concerned but calm.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Mimi Helps

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot following Mimi. She steps closer, bends down, and gently pushes the yellow ball
from the roots back toward Kiko. The ball rolls softly across the grass. Kiko watches with
a grateful smile.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Thank You!

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Close-up on Kiko. She looks at the ball at her feet, then turns to Mimi with a bright,
sincere smile: "Thank you!" Cut to close-up on Mimi's warm, happy response.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Warm Feeling

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Close-up on Mimi: "You're welcome!" Pull-back to medium two-shot. Kiko holds the ball,
feeling the warmth of the exchange. Both characters rest in a comfortable, warm silence.

## Shot 06 (63-75 sn) — Kiko Helps Back

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium two-shot. Mimi carries the ball; it's a little big and she wobbles slightly. Kiko
steps in: "Let's carry it together!" Both characters hold the ball and walk side by side.

## Shot 07 (75-87 sn) — The Thank-You Chain

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium two-shot tracking both characters as they walk. Mimi looks at Kiko: "Thank you!"
Kiko responds: "You're welcome!" Both smile — the thank-you chain is complete.

## Shot 08 (87-99 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree area

Medium shot. Both hold the yellow ball together, facing the camera. They say: "Thank you!"
and give a small gentle wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
