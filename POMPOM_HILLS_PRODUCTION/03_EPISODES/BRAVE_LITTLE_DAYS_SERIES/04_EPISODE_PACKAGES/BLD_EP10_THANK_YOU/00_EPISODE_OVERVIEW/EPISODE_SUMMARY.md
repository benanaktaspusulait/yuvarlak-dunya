# Brave Little Days EP10: Thank You! — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning at Central Square, Kiko's soft yellow ball rolls slowly toward the Big Pompom
Tree roots and stops just out of reach. Mimi gently pushes the ball back to Kiko. Kiko smiles
and says: "Thank you!" — discovering that gratitude makes both people feel warm. When Mimi
later needs help carrying the ball, Kiko offers: "Let's carry it together!" The thank-you
chain completes: both characters have experienced both sides of gratitude.

## Learning

Gratitude • Kindness • Reciprocity

## Characters

Kiko, Mimi

## World
