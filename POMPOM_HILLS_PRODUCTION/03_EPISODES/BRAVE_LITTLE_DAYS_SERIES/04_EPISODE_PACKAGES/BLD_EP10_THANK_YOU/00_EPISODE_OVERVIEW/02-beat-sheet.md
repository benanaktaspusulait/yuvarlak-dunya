# Thank You! — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Mimi are at Central Square near the Big Pompom Tree. Kiko's soft yellow ball rolls slowly toward the tree roots and stops just out of reach. Kiko says: "Oh, my ball!" Mimi watches and offers: "I can help."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko leans down and reaches toward the ball with her fingertips. It's close but not quite touchable — nestled between soft roots. She pulls back slightly: "Oh no." Mimi watches, ready to help.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Mimi steps closer, bends down, and gently pushes the soft yellow ball from between the tree roots back toward Kiko. The ball rolls softly across the grass. Kiko watches with a grateful smile.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko looks at the ball at her feet, then turns to Mimi with a bright, sincere smile: "Thank you!" Mimi's face lights up with warmth. The core action beat.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Mimi responds: "You're welcome!" Kiko holds the ball, feeling the warmth of the exchange. Both characters rest in a comfortable, warm silence. The gratitude seed is planted.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Mimi now carries the ball — it's a little big and she wobbles slightly. Kiko steps in: "Let's carry it together!" Both characters hold the ball and walk side by side. The roles reverse.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Mimi looks at Kiko and says: "Thank you!" Kiko responds: "You're welcome!" The thank-you chain is complete — both characters have experienced both sides of gratitude.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Kiko and Mimi hold the ball together, facing the camera. They say: "Thank you!" and give a small gentle wave. Warm closing.
```
