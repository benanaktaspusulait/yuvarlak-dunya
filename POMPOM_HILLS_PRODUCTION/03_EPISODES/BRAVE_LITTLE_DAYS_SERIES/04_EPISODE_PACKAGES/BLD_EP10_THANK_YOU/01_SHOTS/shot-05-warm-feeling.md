## Purpose

Show the emotional payoff: Mimi responds with "You're welcome!" and both characters feel the
warmth of the gratitude exchange. Kiko begins to understand that thanking someone makes both
people feel good.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Mimi responds to Kiko's "Thank you!" with a bright "You're welcome!" — her
face is warm and happy. The reciprocal feeling is immediate and genuine.

## Emotion

Warmth — thanking and being thanked both feel good. Kiko begins to understand the cycle:
help → thank → both feel warm. This is the emotional seed that will grow into the thank-you
chain later in the episode.
