## Purpose

Show Kiko's small struggle — the ball is resting near the tree roots, just out of her reach.
This is a gentle, age-appropriate difficulty that establishes the need for help without drama.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Kiko leans down and reaches toward the ball with her fingertips. The ball
is close but not quite touchable — nestled between soft roots. The effort is natural and calm.
