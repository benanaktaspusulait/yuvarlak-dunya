## Purpose

Open with a simple, readable need: Kiko's soft yellow ball rolls slowly toward the Big Pompom
Tree roots and stops just out of reach. This creates a gentle problem that naturally invites
help from a friend.

---

## Opening Hook

First 3–5 seconds: The soft yellow ball rolls gently across the warm morning grass and comes
to rest near the tree roots. The movement is slow, safe, and instantly readable — a ball that
needs retrieving.
