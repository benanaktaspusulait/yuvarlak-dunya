## Purpose

Show the patience skill in action: Opa and Kiko sit together, hands in their laps, waiting for
the soup to cool. The steam gradually fades — a visual reward for their patience. This is the
core life-skill moment: when something is hot, we wait.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Opa and Kiko sit side by side, hands resting in their laps, a comfortable
distance from the soup bowl. The faint steam still rises. It's a quiet, patient moment — two
people simply waiting together.
