# Brave Little Days EP16: Careful, It's Hot! — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm day in Kiko's Home Kitchen Nook, Kiko notices a rounded soup bowl on the low round table
with faint steam rising. "What's that?" she asks. Opa gently says: "Warm soup." Kiko reaches toward
the bowl but stops before touching. Opa calmly says: "Careful — it's hot." Kiko pulls her hand back
and says: "Hot. We don't touch." They wait together as the steam fades. When the soup cools, Opa
confirms it's safe. Kiko smells and tastes the warm soup: "Mmm, warm!" The episode closes with Kiko's
proud discovery: "Hot! We don't touch."

## Learning

Patience • Safety • Waiting skill
