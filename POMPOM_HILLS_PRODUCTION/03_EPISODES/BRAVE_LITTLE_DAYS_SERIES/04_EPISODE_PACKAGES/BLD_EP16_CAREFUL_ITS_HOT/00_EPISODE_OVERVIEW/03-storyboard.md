# Careful, It's Hot! — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — Warm Soup on the Table

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Wide establishing shot. The cozy kitchen nook with warm daytime light. A low round table holds a
single rounded soup bowl with very faint steam wisps rising. Kiko approaches with bright curiosity.
Opa stands nearby, calm and present.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Reaching for Warmth

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Close-up on Kiko's face as she watches the steam. She slowly reaches her hand toward the bowl —
her fingers hover near but don't touch. Her expression is curious and gentle. Opa watches calmly.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Careful, It's Hot

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Close-up on the hands — Opa's hand gently near Kiko's — then slow tilt up to Kiko's face as she
listens. Opa speaks calmly: "Careful — it's hot." Kiko pauses, understanding.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Hand Pulls Back

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Close-up on Kiko's hand pulling back to her chest. She says: "Hot. We don't touch." Her face shows
thoughtful understanding. Opa watches with a warm, approving smile.

## Shot 05 (51-66 sn) — Waiting

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Medium two-shot. Opa and Kiko sit side by side, hands in their laps, a comfortable distance from
the soup bowl. Opa: "We wait." Kiko repeats: "We wait." The steam gradually fades.

## Shot 06 (66-78 sn) — Cooling Down

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Close-up on the soup bowl as Opa gently stirs with a soft spoon. Steam has nearly disappeared.
"See? It's cooling down." Kiko watches with patient satisfaction.

## Shot 07 (78-90 sn) — Safe to Try

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Medium shot. Opa confirms the soup is safe. Kiko leans in carefully, smells the warm soup, takes
a small gentle taste. Her face lights up: "Mmm, warm!" Delight and quiet pride.

## Shot 08 (90-102 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Kitchen Nook / low round table

Medium two-shot. Both sit together, sharing a warm smile. Kiko: "Hot! We don't touch." Opa: "That's
right." Both smile — a gentle, complete closing moment.
