# Careful, It's Hot! — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko notices a rounded soup bowl on the low round table with faint steam rising. She looks curious. Opa is nearby, calm and warm.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko watches the steam, then slowly reaches her hand toward the bowl — her fingers hover but don't touch. Opa watches calmly.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Opa gently places his hand near Kiko's and says: "Careful — it's hot." Kiko pauses, listening and understanding.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko pulls her hand back to her chest and says: "Hot. We don't touch." Opa smiles warmly — this is her own discovery.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Opa and Kiko sit side by side, hands in laps, waiting for the soup to cool. Steam gradually fades. Opa: "We wait." Kiko repeats.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Opa gently stirs the soup with a soft spoon. The steam has nearly disappeared. "See? It's cooling down." Kiko watches with patient satisfaction.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa confirms the soup is safe. Kiko leans in, smells the warm soup, tastes it gently. "Mmm, warm!" Delight and quiet pride.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Kiko and Opa share a warm smile. Kiko says: "Hot! We don't touch." Opa: "That's right." Warm closing.
```
