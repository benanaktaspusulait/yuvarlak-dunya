# Brave Little Days SP02: Guessing Game — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning in Central Square, Noah and Arda stand together. Noah looks at the viewer
with a playful smile and asks: "Can you guess where we are?" Arda throws his hands up
excitedly and answers: "Pompom Hills!" Both smile warmly at the viewer. No props — the game
is purely verbal.
