# Brave Little Days SP01: Big Tree Excitement — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning in Central Square, Noah and Arda stand together near the Big Pompom Tree.
Noah points toward the tree with a friendly gesture: "The big tree is this way." Arda bounces
excitedly in place: "I know! Come on!" Both share a moment of pure excitement and anticipation.
No running, no walking — just the joy of discovering something wonderful together.

## Learning

Excitement • Friendship • Shared Discovery
