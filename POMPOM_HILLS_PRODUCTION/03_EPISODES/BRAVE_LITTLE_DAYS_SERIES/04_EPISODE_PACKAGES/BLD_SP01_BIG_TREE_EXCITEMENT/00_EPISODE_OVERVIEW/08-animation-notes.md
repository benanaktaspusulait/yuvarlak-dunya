# Big Tree Excitement — Animation Notes v1.0

---

## Animation Rules

- Hareketler yavaş ve doğal; squash-stretch max %4.
- Noah: point gesture toward Big Pompom Tree — friendly, inviting, not dramatic.
- Arda: tiny excited in-place body bob — bouncing without changing position.
- Both stay in place — NO walking, NO running, NO jumping, NO repositioning, NO crossing.
- Her 3-6 saniyede küçük bir mikro-aksiyon: bakış, gülümseme, baş eğme, el hareketi.
- Final 0.5 seconds: calm still hold — no new action begins.
- Both blink naturally throughout.
- Excitement is expressed through facial expressions and small in-place movements only.
