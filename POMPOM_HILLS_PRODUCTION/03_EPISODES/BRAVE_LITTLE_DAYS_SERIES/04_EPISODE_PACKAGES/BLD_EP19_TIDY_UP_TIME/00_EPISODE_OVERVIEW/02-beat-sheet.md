# Tidy-Up Time — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko's living room toy rug has 5-6 large soft toys scattered across it. Kiko walks in slowly and pauses, noticing the clutter. Mimi stands nearby.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko looks around at the scattered toys: "Uh-oh, too messy." A soft, natural recognition moment — no blame, no urgency.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Mimi hops excitedly toward KH-TOYBOX-01 and asks: "Tidy game?" Kiko's surprised expression shifts to a smile — the mess becomes a game.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): The tidy game begins. Kiko picks up the soft yellow ball: "Yellow one!" and places it in the box. Mimi picks up the large blue block: "Blue one!" — gentle, cheerful.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The final toys. Kiko places the soft green book on the shelf: "Almost done!" Mimi places the last toy in the box: "Last one!" The rug is nearly clear.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Wide view of the clear rug. "All clean!" / "So tidy!" — relief and quiet pride. The room feels warm and safe.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Kiko and Mimi stand together on the clean rug and say: "Tidy up, all done!" — the repeated phrase, delivered as shared completion.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): They sit on the clean rug facing the camera, give a small wave: "Tidy up, all done!" Warm closing.
```
