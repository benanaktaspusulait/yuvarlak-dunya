heavy thuds, crowd noise (per `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md`
and `00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11).
