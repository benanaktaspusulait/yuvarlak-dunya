# Tidy-Up Time — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — Toys on the Rug

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Wide establishing shot. The round toy rug is visible with 5-6 large soft toys scattered across it.
KH-TOYBOX-01 sits at the rug's edge. Kiko walks in slowly and pauses, noticing the clutter.
Mimi stands nearby.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Too Messy

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko looks around at the scattered toys. Her expression shifts to mild surprise:
"Uh-oh, too messy." Mimi watches quietly. A natural recognition moment.

## Shot 03 (27-42 sn) — Tidy Game!

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium two-shot. Mimi hops excitedly toward KH-TOYBOX-01 and asks: "Tidy game?" Kiko's
surprised face shifts to a smile. The mess becomes a game.

## Shot 04 (42-54 sn) — Colour by Colour

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko picks up the soft yellow ball: "Yellow one!" and places it gently into
KH-TOYBOX-01. Mimi picks up the large blue block: "Blue one!" — gentle, cheerful.

## Shot 05 (54-66 sn) — Finishing Together

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko places the soft green book on the shelf: "Almost done!" Mimi holds the last
toy and places it in the box: "Last one!" The rug is nearly clear.

## Shot 06 (66-81 sn) — Open and Safe

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Wide shot. The round toy rug is completely clear. KH-TOYBOX-01 sits full at the edge. Kiko
and Mimi look at the clean space: "All clean!" / "So tidy!"

## Shot 07 (81-93 sn) — Tidy Up, All Done!

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium two-shot. Kiko and Mimi stand side by side on the clean rug, facing each other. Both
smile and say together: "Tidy up, all done!"

## Shot 08 (93-105 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko and Mimi sit together on the clean rug, facing the camera. They say:
"Tidy up, all done!" Then they give a small gentle wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
