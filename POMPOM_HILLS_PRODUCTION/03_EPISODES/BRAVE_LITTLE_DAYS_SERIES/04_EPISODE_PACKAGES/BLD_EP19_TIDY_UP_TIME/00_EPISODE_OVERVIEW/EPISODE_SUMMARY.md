# Brave Little Days EP19: Tidy-Up Time — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm day, Kiko's living room toy rug has 5-6 large soft toys scattered across it. Kiko
walks in and pauses: "Uh-oh, too messy." Mimi hops to the toy box and proposes: "Tidy game?"
Kiko smiles. Together they pick up the toys by colour — "Yellow one!" / "Blue one!" — placing
each gently into the box. The last book goes on the shelf, the last toy in the box. The rug
is clear, the room is safe and warm. Kiko and Mimi say together: "Tidy up, all done!"

## Learning

Order • Cooperation • Safety

## Characters

Kiko, Mimi

## World

Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug
