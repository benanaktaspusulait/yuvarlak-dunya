# Brave Little Days SP04: More Friends Coming — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning in Central Square, Noah and Arda stand together. Noah looks around
quietly and says: "More friends are coming." Arda responds excitedly: "I can't wait!"
Both look toward the viewer with eager smiles. This is a bridge beat — the anticipation
moment before the final full-cast.
