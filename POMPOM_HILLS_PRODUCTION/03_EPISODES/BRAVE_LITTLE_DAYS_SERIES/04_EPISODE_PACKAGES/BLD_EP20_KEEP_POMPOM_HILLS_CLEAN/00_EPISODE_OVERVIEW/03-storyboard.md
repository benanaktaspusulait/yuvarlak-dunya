# Keep Pompom Hills Clean — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — A Little Leftover

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Wide establishing shot. Soft green grass, rounded tree shapes, warm morning light. Kiko walks slowly along the trail, holding one small, clean snack paper in her hand. Mimi walks beside her. The forest is clean and warm.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Where Does It Go?

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Medium shot. Kiko lowers the clean snack paper toward the soft grass, then stops. Her hand hovers. She looks around, thinking: "Where?" Mimi watches calmly. The moment is quiet and sincere.

## Shot 03 (27-42 sn) — Find a Bin

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Two-shot. Mimi points toward the pastel clean-up basket at the path edge: "Find a bin." The basket is soft, rounded, toy-like. Kiko follows her gesture, understanding dawns.

## Shot 04 (42-54 sn) — Walking to the Basket

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Medium wide shot. Kiko and Mimi walk slowly together toward the basket. Kiko still holds the clean snack paper. Soft leaves drift gently around them. Kiko says: "Let's go!"

## Shot 05 (54-66 sn) — In the Bin

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Medium shot. Kiko reaches the basket and gently places the clean snack paper inside: "In it goes!" Mimi watches with a warm smile. The paper drops softly into the basket.

## Shot 06 (66-81 sn) — Leaf Trail Stays Clean

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Wide shot. The Leaf Trail is clean and beautiful. Soft leaves drift gently. Mimi: "The trail is clean!" Kiko looks at the trail and smiles. The forest is pristine.

## Shot 07 (81-93 sn) — In the Bin It Goes!

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Medium two-shot. Kiko and Mimi face each other. Kiko says: "In the bin it goes!" Mimi repeats: "In the bin it goes!" Both smile. The phrase feels natural and shared.

## Shot 08 (93-105 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Little Forest — Leaf Trail

Medium shot. Kiko and Mimi stand together on the clean trail, facing the camera. They say: "In the bin it goes!" and give a small gentle wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
