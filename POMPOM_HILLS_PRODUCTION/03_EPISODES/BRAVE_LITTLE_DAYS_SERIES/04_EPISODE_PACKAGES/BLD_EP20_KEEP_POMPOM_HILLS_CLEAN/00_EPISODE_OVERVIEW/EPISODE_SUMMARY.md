# Brave Little Days EP20: Keep Pompom Hills Clean — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning in Little Forest, Kiko and Mimi walk along the Leaf Trail. Kiko holds a clean snack paper/peel and wonders where to put it. Mimi points out a soft pastel clean-up basket. Together, they walk to the basket and Kiko gently places the paper inside. The Leaf Trail stays clean and beautiful, and they share the phrase: "In the bin it goes!"

## Learning

Nature responsibility • Cleanliness • Cooperation
