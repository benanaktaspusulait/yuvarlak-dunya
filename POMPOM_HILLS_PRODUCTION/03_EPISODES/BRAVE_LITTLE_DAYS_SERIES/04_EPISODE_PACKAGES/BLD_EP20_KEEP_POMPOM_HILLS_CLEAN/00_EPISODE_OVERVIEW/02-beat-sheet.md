# Keep Pompom Hills Clean — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Mimi walk along the Leaf Trail. Kiko holds one small, clean snack paper/peel in her hand. The forest is clean and warm.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko lowers the clean snack paper toward the soft grass, then pauses. She looks around, thinking: "Where?" Mimi watches.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Mimi spots the pastel clean-up basket at the path edge and points: "Find a bin." Kiko understands.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko and Mimi walk slowly together toward the basket. Kiko says: "Let's go!"
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Kiko reaches the basket and gently places the clean snack paper inside: "In it goes!" Mimi smiles.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Wide view of the Leaf Trail — clean and beautiful. Mimi: "The trail is clean!" Both smile.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Kiko and Mimi face each other. Kiko says: "In the bin it goes!" Mimi repeats: "In the bin it goes!"
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Both stand on the clean trail, facing the camera. They say: "In the bin it goes!" and give a small wave.
```

---

## Interaction Check

Her shot en az bir karakter tepkisi (soru/gözlem/gülümseme) içerir — Kiko ve Mimi hiçbir shot'ta 10-15 saniyeden uzun tek başına konuşmaz.
