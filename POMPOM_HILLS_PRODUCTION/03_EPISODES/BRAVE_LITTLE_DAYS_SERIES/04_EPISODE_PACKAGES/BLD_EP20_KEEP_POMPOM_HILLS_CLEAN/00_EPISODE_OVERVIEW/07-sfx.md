## Sound Design

| Element | Sound | Volume |
|---|---|---|
| Ambiyans | Soft forest ambience, birdsong, light breeze, gentle leaf rustle | -20 dB |
| Müzik | None — natural ambience only | — |
| SFX | Soft leaf rustle underfoot (gentle "hush hush") | -14 dB |
| SFX | Very soft "pof" as paper drops into basket | -16 dB |
| SFX | Gentle leaf drift sounds | -18 dB |
| Diyalog | Karakter sesleri | -6 dB |

Forbidden: loud music, sudden stingers, aggressive sound design, competitive sound effects,
forest pollution sounds, crowd noise (per `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md`
and `00-CORE/18_VIDEO_STYLE_AND_RETENTION_STANDARD.md` §11).
