## Purpose

Show Kiko's moment of uncertainty — she considers placing the leftover on the ground, then
pauses and wonders where it should go. This is the emotional seed: Kiko doesn't litter, she
genuinely doesn't know the right place yet.

## Opening Hook

Her hand hovers. She looks around, thinking. The moment is quiet and sincere.
