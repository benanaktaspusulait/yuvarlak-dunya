# If I Get Lost — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko and Opa walk together near the Big Pompom Tree in a slightly livelier Central Square. Two silent background figures wave at the far edge. Opa reminds: "Stay close, Kiko."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko stops. Opa is no longer visible — behind the tree trunk or out of frame. Kiko says: "Opa?" — a calm question, not a cry. Mild concern, not panic.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Kiko places her hands on her chest, takes a deep breath, whispers: "Stay calm." She uses the breathing technique from EP11 independently. Quiet strength.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko spots Remi near the Care Corner edge — a familiar face. She walks toward Remi calmly: "Excuse me." Finding a helper, not approaching a stranger.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Remi kneels to Kiko's eye level: "I can help." They wait together. Companionship, not a dramatic rescue.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Opa steps into view from behind the Big Pompom Tree. He walks calmly toward them: "There you are." Kiko's face relaxes with relief.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa thanks Remi: "Thank you, Remi." Remi: "Anytime." A warm, natural exchange between trusted adults. Kiko witnesses community.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): The three stand together. Kiko: "Stay calm, find a helper." They wave gently. Warm closure.
```
