## Purpose

Open the episode in a slightly livelier Central Square — not a crowd, but a gentle buzz of
activity. Kiko and Opa walk together near the Big Pompom Tree. Two silent background figures
are visible at the far edge, adding life without chaos. This shot establishes the "busy day"
feeling that sets up the separation in Shot 02.

---

## Opening Hook

First 3–5 seconds: Central Square is warm and bright, a touch more alive than usual. Two
silent background figures are visible at the far edge, waving gently at each other. Kiko
and Opa walk together near the Big Pompom Tree.
