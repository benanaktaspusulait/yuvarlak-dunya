## Purpose

Show the core breathing technique: Opa and Kiko breathe in together (like smelling a flower)
and breathe out together (like puffing a pompom). The metaphor is visual and intuitive — no
candle, no flame. The breathing is slow, rhythmic, and shared.

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Opa and Kiko sit together on the toy rug, the puzzle board between them.
Opa begins the breathing — a slow, visible inhale through the nose. Kiko watches and begins
to follow.
