## Purpose

Show Kiko's growing frustration — the puzzle piece still won't fit after a second attempt.
Kiko's body language shifts: shoulders tighten, brows furrow slightly. She pauses and says
"It won't fit." This creates the emotional gap that breathing will fill.

---

## Opening Hook

First 2–3 seconds: Kiko pushes the puzzle piece again — it still doesn't fit. The repeated
failure creates a small, readable frustration that any child recognises.
