# Brave Little Days EP11: Slow Breaths — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm day at home, Kiko tries to place a large puzzle piece into its board on the toy rug.
The piece won't fit — frustration builds. Opa gently kneels beside her: "Let's breathe."
Together they inhale like smelling a flower and exhale like puffing a pompom. Kiko's body
softens. She returns to the puzzle with fresh eyes, turns the piece, and it clicks into place.
Calming down is a skill anyone can learn.

## Learning

Breathing • Calming Down • Persistence
