# Slow Breaths — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko is at home on the toy rug, trying to place a large soft puzzle piece into a round puzzle board. The piece is slightly tilted — almost right but not fitting. Opa sits nearby, watching calmly.
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Kiko tries again — it still won't fit. Her eyebrows furrow, shoulders tighten. She pauses: "It won't fit." The frustration is real but contained.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): Opa moves closer, kneels to Kiko's eye level, and offers gently: "Let's breathe." Kiko looks at him — her brows begin to soften.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Opa and Kiko breathe together — inhale like smelling a flower, exhale like puffing a pompom. Slow, rhythmic, shared. No candle, no flame.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Kiko's shoulders drop, face relaxes, brows unfurrow. She looks at Opa: "I feel better." The calm is earned.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): Kiko returns to the puzzle with fresh eyes. She turns the piece to a new angle: "Maybe like this?" Curiosity replaces frustration.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): The puzzle piece clicks softly into place. Kiko smiles: "It fits!" Quiet satisfaction — the calm breathing led to clarity.
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Kiko and Opa sit beside the completed puzzle, facing the camera: "Breathe in, breathe out." Warm closing.
```
