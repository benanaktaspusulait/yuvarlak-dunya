# Slow Breaths — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — The Puzzle Piece

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko sits on the soft toy rug, holding a large soft round puzzle piece above a matching round puzzle board. The piece is slightly tilted — almost right but not quite fitting. Opa sits nearby at child eye level, watching calmly.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — It Won't Fit

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on hands as the puzzle piece resists. Medium on Kiko — eyebrows furrow slightly, shoulders tighten. She pauses: "It won't fit." Opa remains calm and nearby.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Let's Breathe

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium two-shot. Opa kneels closer to Kiko's eye level. He speaks gently: "Let's breathe." Kiko looks at him — brows begin to soften. A quiet moment of invitation.

## Shot 04 (39-54 sn) — Breathing Together

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close two-shot. Opa and Kiko sit facing each other on the toy rug, the puzzle board between them. They breathe in together (like smelling a flower) and breathe out together (like puffing a pompom). Slow, rhythmic, shared.

## Shot 05 (54-66 sn) — Body Softens

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on Kiko's face and shoulders. Shoulders drop, face softens, brows unfurrow. She looks at Opa: "I feel better." The calm is earned and natural.

## Shot 06 (66-78 sn) — Trying Again

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot on Kiko as she calmly picks up the puzzle piece. She studies it, turns it gently to a new angle, and tries placing it: "Maybe like this?" Curiosity replaces frustration.

## Shot 07 (78-90 sn) — It Fits!

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Close-up on the puzzle as the piece clicks in softly — "paf." Medium on Kiko's small, genuine smile: "It fits!" Opa smiles warmly.

## Shot 08 (90-102 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Kiko's Home — Living Room / toy rug

Medium shot. Kiko and Opa sit side by side on the toy rug, the completed puzzle in front. Both face the camera: "Breathe in, breathe out." Small gentle wave. Warm fade.
