# OpenArt Prompt — Shot 01 — Kiko's Welcome

## Visual Prompt

```text
Use Kiko's Home (world reference) as the LOCKED environment. The shot takes place outside Kiko's Home, near the entrance/front area, not in Central Square. Preserve the approved Kiko's Home identity: child-safe cozy outdoor home setting, warm rounded shapes, simple path, soft grass, flowers if present in the world reference, and a handcrafted preschool toy-set look. Do not redesign the location.

Insert Kiko (character reference) only. Kiko is already visible from the first frame and remains the only character for the whole shot. No other character enters, appears, or is revealed later. Kiko stands near the front of Kiko's Home, looking warmly toward the viewer with a bright welcoming smile.

Kiko's Home must remain an exterior home-front setting. The shot stays outside the home at all times. Do not show an indoor room, bedroom, furniture, lamps, toys, shelves, or interior details through the door or windows. The door or entrance may be visible, but it must not reveal a furnished interior.

The environment must stay clean and uncluttered. No props are used in this shot. Do not add any signposts, wooden signs, direction signs, arrows, written boards, labels, place names, readable signs, road signs, path signs, map signs, banners, plaques, benches, fences, lamp posts, barrels, crates, street furniture, or any object containing letters or words. If the world reference contains any sign, signpost, label, board, arrow, bench, fence, street furniture, or written text, ignore it completely and keep it out of the generated frame.

No other character appears in the background, foreground, edge of frame, window, path, reflection, or shadow.

Do not display dialogue as on-screen text. No speech bubbles. No captions. No subtitles. No text. No written text anywhere in the image.

The shot begins immediately with Kiko already alive and moving from frame one. Kiko does not wait silently. Kiko gives the first warm wave within the first 1 second and begins speaking early, within the first 1-2 seconds. Do not hold a silent idle pose at the beginning.

The moment should feel like a true opening welcome: warm, cheerful, inviting, and friendly. Kiko feels excited that the viewer has arrived in Pompom Hills and is happy to welcome them from Kiko's own home.

Handcrafted preschool toy-set look, rounded shapes, {style} {camera} {lighting}
```

## Camera Direction

Medium-wide, eye-level, child-friendly composition, height around 0.85 m. Frame Kiko comfortably in front of Kiko's Home so the home setting is clearly readable. The camera remains locked. No pan, no zoom, no pull-back, no push-in, no tilt, no orbit, no reframe, no camera reveal. Kiko's small gestures and expressions provide the motion; the camera does not move to create interest.

## Dialogue

```text
Kiko: Hello! Welcome to Pompom Hills!
Kiko: I'm Kiko, and I'm so happy you're here.
Kiko: This is my little home.
Kiko: I'm ready to start this bright little day with you.
```
Kiko is the only speaking character. Each line is spoken once by Kiko only. No echo dialogue. No repeated lines. No overlapping speech. No other voice appears.

## Natural Character Motion Rule

Calm but alive from the first frame: Kiko is already subtly alive at frame one with gentle breathing, blinking, warm eye contact, and a bright smile. Kiko gives a small wave within the first 1 second and begins speaking within the first 1-2 seconds. Kiko speaks all four lines with small natural gestures: one friendly wave, one tiny open-hand gesture toward Kiko's Home, one warm nod, and one cheerful inviting smile. The gesture does not introduce props, objects, signs, or other characters. Keep every movement small and in place. No walking, no running, no jumping, no hopping, no repositioning, no crossing, no long pause, no silent idle hold, no delayed wave, no delayed dialogue, no waiting before action, no empty opening seconds.

## Sound

Soft outdoor home ambience: gentle birds chirping, light breeze, subtle cozy village warmth around Kiko's Home. Natural ambience only. No music, no background music, no melody, no song, no soundtrack, no chimes, no whooshes.

## Lighting

Warm morning sunlight, soft ambient, no hard shadows.
