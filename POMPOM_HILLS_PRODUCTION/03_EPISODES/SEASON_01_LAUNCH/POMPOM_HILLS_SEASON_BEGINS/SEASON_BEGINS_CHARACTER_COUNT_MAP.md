# Season Begins — Character Count Map

## Purpose

This map prevents surprise characters, missing characters, duplicate characters, and background crowd errors during OpenArt generation.

Every shot must pass its character count gate from the first frame.

No character may enter later. No character may be revealed later. No character may disappear.

## Global Character Count Rules

- Approved character count must be correct from the first frame.
- No character may appear later.
- No character may enter from off-screen.
- No character may be revealed by camera movement.
- No character may disappear.
- No duplicate character may appear.
- No background crowd may appear.
- No animal, toy creature, silhouette character, reflection character, or shadow character may appear.

---

## Reject Conditions

Reject the shot immediately if:

- the approved character count is wrong
- any approved character is missing
- any extra character appears
- any duplicate character appears
- any character enters later
- any character appears in a window, path, background, reflection, or shadow
- the camera reveals a new character
- a character is redesigned or wrong scale
- the shot adds a crowd or background friend
