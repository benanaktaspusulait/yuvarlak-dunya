## Concept
A gentle season-opening welcome that builds up the group — from solo hellos to a calm full-cast
tableau — inviting the child into Pompom Hills.

## Structure & Safety (No Surprise Elements)
Each shot's character and object set is LOCKED from the first frame. No later-entering characters,
no new props, no camera reveals. Every shot doc carries a No Surprise Elements Rule + Approved
Characters and Props list. Shots are independent generations joined in edit with soft crossfades
and a unified warm daylight grade.

**Shot 06 (full cast) is the highest-risk shot** — all six must be present in the first frame.
Generate a first-frame preview and approve (exactly six correct characters, correct scales, no
duplicates/extras/props) before full video generation.
