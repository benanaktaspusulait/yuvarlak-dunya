# Season Begins — Storyboard

> Static/gentle camera; soft crossfades between shots; unified warm daylight grade.
> Character/object set locked from each shot's first frame (no surprises). Counts: 1/1/2/2/3/6.

## Shot 01 — Kiko's Welcome (0-12s) · Central Square · Kiko
Wide Central Square in warm morning → settle on Kiko who waves brightly to viewer. No props.
Dialogue: "Hi! Welcome to Pompom Hills!"

## Shot 02 — Mimi's Cozy Hello (12-24s) · Mimi's Burrow · Mimi
Round blue entrance, warm glow, 3 stones. Mimi peeks out, sleepy soft wave. No new objects.
Dialogue: "Hello... come in, it's cozy here."

## Shot 03 — Noah & Arda Build Together (24-39s) · Central Square · Noah + Arda
Noah by a small block set, Arda beside him. Noah invites viewer, Arda waves. Blocks stay small.
Dialogue: "Want to build something with us?" / "Yeah! Come play!"

## Shot 04 — Luca & Opa Wonder and Wisdom (39-54s) · Opa's Tree · Luca + Opa
Luca looks up with wonder then to viewer; Opa warm nod. No book/lantern/magical object.
Dialogue: "There's so much to discover here!" / "And I'll be here to help you explore."

## Shot 05 — Kiko, Noah & Arda Play Together (54-69s) · Central Square · trio
Three friends in a natural group, wave/bounce/gesture to viewer. Prop-free.
Dialogue: "We're all here!" / "Come join us." / "Let's play!"

## Shot 06 — Full Cast Tableau (69-84s) · Central Square · all six
All six already in the first frame (Kiko center-forward, Mimi front, Noah+Arda one side, Luca other,
Opa behind). Warm daylight (not sunset). Tiny gestures only; end-screen-safe hold.
Dialogue: "Welcome to Pompom Hills!" / "Come and play with us, little friends." (others smile & wave)
