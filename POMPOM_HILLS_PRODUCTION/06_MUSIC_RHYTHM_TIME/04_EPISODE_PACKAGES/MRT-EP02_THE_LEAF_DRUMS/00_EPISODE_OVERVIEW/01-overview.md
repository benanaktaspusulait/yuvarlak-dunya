## Characters
- Noah (fox) — #8EC5F0
- Arda (turtle) — #8CC9F0

## Setting
Little Forest — bright daytime, dappled sunlight through trees, colorful leaves scattered.

## Hook
Noah taps a large leaf. A soft boom sound resonates.

## Story Beat
1. Noah explores Little Forest, curious about the sounds around him. He spots a large, flat leaf.
2. He taps it gently — a soft boom sound resonates. His eyes widen: "A drum!"
3. Noah taps again: boom, boom, boom. He starts a steady beat.
4. Arda walks by, intrigued. "That's a cool sound!" He finds a different leaf — tap, tap — a higher pitch.
5. Together they gather more leaves of different sizes: big leaves for deep booms, small leaves for sharp taps.
6. They create a leaf drum circle, each playing their own leaf drum, building a forest rhythm together.

## Learning Objective
Nature provides instruments. Different materials make different sounds. Making music together creates joy.
