## Characters
- Noah (fox) — #8EC5F0
- Kiko (bear) — #F8BBD0
- Mimi (rabbit) — #90CAF9

## Setting
Central Square — sunny day, bright open space, colorful Pompom Hills buildings in background.

## Hook
Noah claps once. A clear, bright sound rings out.

## Story Beat
1. Noah walks through Central Square, bored and looking around. He claps once absent-mindedly.
2. The clap sound surprises him — it's clear and bright! He claps again. Same sound.
3. Noah starts clapping a simple rhythm: clap-clap, clap-clap-clap. His tail wags with excitement.
4. Kiko hears the rhythm and bounces over: "That sounds fun!" She adds her deeper, softer claps.
5. Mimi hops in, ears perked, adding quick light claps: "I want to join too!"
6. All three create a clapping song together — simple, joyful, rhythmic. They clap and laugh.

## Learning Objective
Clapping is a simple way to make music. Rhythm can be created by anyone. Music is more fun together.
