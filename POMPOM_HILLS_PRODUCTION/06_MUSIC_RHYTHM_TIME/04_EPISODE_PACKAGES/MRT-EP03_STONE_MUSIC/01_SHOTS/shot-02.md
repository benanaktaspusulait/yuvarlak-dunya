# Shot 02 - First Tap

## Shot Info
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Type:** Close-up + Reaction

## Scene
Noah holds two stones up, ready to tap them together.

## Visual
- Close-up of Noah's hands holding stones
- Cut to Noah's face showing anticipation
- Sparkle effect when stones connect

## Action
Noah taps the two stones together. A clear, beautiful ringing note rings out. Noah's eyes go wide with surprise and delight.

## Audio
- Clear stone tap sound (high pitch)
- Magical sparkle sound effect
- Noah gasps softly

## Camera Notes
Quick cut between hands and face. Use slow motion on the tap moment for emphasis.
