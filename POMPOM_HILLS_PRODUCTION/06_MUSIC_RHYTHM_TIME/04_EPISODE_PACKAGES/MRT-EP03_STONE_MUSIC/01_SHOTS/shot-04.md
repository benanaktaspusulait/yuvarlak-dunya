# Shot 04 - Experimenting Together

## Shot Info
- **Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Type:** Discovery Montage

## Scene
Noah and Kiko gather more stones from the creek.

## Visual
- Split screen or quick cuts showing different stones
- Each stone has unique color/size
- Musical notes appear as floating graphics when tapped

## Action
Noah and Kiko tap different stones, discovering each makes a different pitch. Large stones = low notes, small stones = high notes.

## Audio
- Series of stone taps at different pitches
- Ascending/descending notes as they experiment
- Excited giggles from both characters

## Camera Notes
Quick montage style cuts. Use visual effects to show the pitch differences.
