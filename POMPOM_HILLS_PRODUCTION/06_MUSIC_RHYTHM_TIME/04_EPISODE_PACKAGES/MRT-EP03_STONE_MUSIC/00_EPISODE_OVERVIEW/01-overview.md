## Characters
| Character | Color |
|-----------|-------|

## Location
Rainbow Creek

## Hook
Noah taps two stones together and produces a clear ringing note. The sound surprises him and catches Kiko's attention immediately.

## Story Summary
Noah discovers that different stones make different musical notes when tapped together. Together with Kiko, they experiment with various stones found along Rainbow Creek. They discover that larger stones make lower notes and smaller stones make higher notes. The episode concludes with Noah and Kiko building a stone xylophone arranged by size, creating a complete musical scale they can play together.

## Educational Goals
- Understanding pitch (high vs low sounds)
- Size and sound relationship
- Basic musical scale concept
- Cooperative music making

## Key Sounds
- Stone tapping (various pitches)
- Water flowing
- Stone xylophone melody
