# MRT-EP05: Water Rhythm

## Package
`MRT-EP05_WATER_RHYTHM`

## Episode Info
- **Series:** Music & Rhythm Time
- **Episode:** 05
- **Duration:** ~60 seconds
- **Target Age:** 3-6 years

## Characters
| Character | Color |
|-----------|-------|
| Noah | #8EC5F0 |
| Mimi | #90CAF9 |

## Location
Rainbow Creek

## Hook
Water drips rhythmically from a moss-covered rock into a small pool below. The steady drip creates a perfect beat that catches Noah's attention.

## Story Summary
Noah discovers that dripping water creates a natural rhythm. He begins to count the beats, finding patterns in the drip timing. Mimi joins and starts hopping to the beat. Together they experiment with different water sources to create complex rhythms. The episode culminates in a full water rhythm performance using various dripping, flowing, and splashing sounds.

## Educational Goals
- Rhythm recognition and counting
- Beat and tempo concepts
- Pattern recognition
- Body movement to music
- Counting skills (1-10)

## Key Sounds
- Water dripping (steady beat)
- Splashing sounds
- Flowing water variations
- Character counting
- Rhythmic hopping
