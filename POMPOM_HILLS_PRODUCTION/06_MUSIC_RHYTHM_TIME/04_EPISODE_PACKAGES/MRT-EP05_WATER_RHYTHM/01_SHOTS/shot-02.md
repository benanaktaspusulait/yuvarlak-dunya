# Shot 02 - Counting the Beat

## Shot Info
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Type:** Educational Moment

## Scene
Noah begins to count the water drips out loud.

## Visual
- Noah's face showing concentration
- Numbers appear visually with each drip
- 1, 2, 3, 4 displayed on screen
- Noah's finger points with each count

## Action
Noah counts "One... two... three... four..." matching each number to a drip. He smiles when he recognizes the pattern.

## Audio
- Water drips continue
- Noah's counting voice
- Musical accent on each number

## Camera Notes
Split screen: drip on one side, Noah counting on other.
