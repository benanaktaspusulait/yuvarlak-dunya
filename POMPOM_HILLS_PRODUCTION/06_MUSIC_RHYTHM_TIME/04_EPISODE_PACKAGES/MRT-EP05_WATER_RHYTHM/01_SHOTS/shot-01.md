# Shot 01 - Rhythmic Discovery

## Shot Info
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Type:** Establishing + Hook

## Scene
Rainbow Creek, close-up on a mossy rock. Water drips steadily into a pool below.

## Visual
- Close-up of water droplet falling
- Slow motion capture of the drip
- Ripple effect in the pool
- Noah (#8EC5F0) enters frame, watching

## Action
Water drips in perfect rhythm. Noah notices the pattern, head tilting as he listens to the beat.

## Audio
- Steady water drip (metronome-like)
- Gentle water ambience
- Soft, curious music begins

## Camera Notes
Extreme close-up on drip, then pull back to reveal Noah.
