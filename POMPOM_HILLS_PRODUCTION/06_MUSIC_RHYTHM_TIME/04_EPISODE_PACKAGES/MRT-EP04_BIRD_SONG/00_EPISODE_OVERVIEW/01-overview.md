## Hook
A bird sings a beautiful, complex melody from a treetop. The melody is so enchanting that Noah stops in his tracks to listen.

## Story Summary
Noah discovers a bird singing a beautiful melody in Little Forest. He tries to copy the bird's call, getting better with practice. Luca joins in, and together they create harmonies with the bird. More birds join, creating a full bird choir. The episode celebrates the joy of vocal music and the magic of natural sounds.

## Educational Goals
- Vocal imitation and pitch matching
- Call and response patterns
- Harmony and layering sounds
- Appreciation of nature's music

## Key Sounds
- Birdsong (primary melody)
- Character vocalizations
- Multiple bird harmonies
- Forest ambience
