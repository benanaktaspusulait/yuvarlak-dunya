# Shot 02 - Bird Discovery

## Shot Info
- **Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Type:** POV + Reveal

## Scene
Noah looks up into the trees. A colorful bird sits on a branch.

## Visual
- POV shot looking up into canopy
- Camera finds the bird on a branch
- Bird is brightly colored, animated
- Sparkles around bird as it sings

## Action
The bird begins to sing a beautiful, complex melody. Noah watches in wonder, head tilted.

## Audio
- Birdsong begins (clear, melodic)
- Magical shimmer effect
- Forest sounds fade slightly

## Camera Notes
Slow tilt up to reveal bird. Hold on bird as it sings.
