# Pompom Cloud — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Cloud
- **Type**: Environmental object (sky)
- **Primary use**: Outdoor daytime scenes, sky background
- **First appearance**: Any outdoor scene

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, fluffy cloud — like a cotton ball
- 3-5 connected round puffs
- No flat bottom, no anvil shape
- Soft, organic outline — no hard edges
- Width: varies, but always looks "round overall"

### Texture
- Cotton-like, fluffy surface
- Very soft edges — clouds blend into sky
- No sharp outlines
- Looks huggable and warm

### Color
- **Primary**: Pompom White (#FAFAFA)
- **Underside**: Very subtle Pompom Cream (#FFF8E1) — warm shadow
- No gray, no dark clouds (unless rain scene — then Pompom Blue tint)
- Sun-facing side is brighter, underside is warmer

### Surface Details
- Very subtle fluff texture on surface
- No rain drops (unless rain scene)
- No lightning
- No hard edges anywhere

---

## Behavior

### Animation
- Drifts very slowly across sky — barely noticeable movement
- Puffs expand and contract very subtly — breathing effect
- No sudden movement, no storm behavior
- Soft, peaceful, meditative movement

---

## Lighting

- Backlit by sun — warm glow around edges
- Underside has soft warm shadow
- No harsh shadows anywhere
- Translucent at edges — light passes through slightly

## Forbidden

- ❌ No gray/dark clouds (except rain scene)
- ❌ No flat bottoms
- ❌ No anvil/storm cloud shapes
- ❌ No lightning
- ❌ No rain (unless rain scene)
- ❌ No hard edges or outlines
- ❌ No realistic cloud texture
