# Pompom Stone — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Stone
- **Type**: Environmental object
- **Primary use**: Stone Hill, stepping stones, paths
- **First appearance**: Stone Hill episodes

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, smooth, fluffy stone — NOT realistic rock
- Like a soft pebble or plush stone
- Slightly flattened on bottom (sits stably)
- No sharp edges, no angular faces
- Size: ~10cm in character scale (stepping stone ~30cm)

### Texture
- Soft, matte surface — like velvet or felt
- No rough rock texture
- No cracks, no chips
- Looks warm and huggable

### Color Variants

| Variant | Color | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Pompom Cream (#FFF8E1) | Default stone |
| Warm | Pompom Orange (#FFCC80 tint) | Sunlit stone |
| Cool | Pompom Blue (#90CAF9 tint) | Shadow stone |
| Mossy | Pompom Green (#A5D6A7 tint) | Forest stone |

### Surface Details
- Very subtle felt-like texture
- No mineral patterns, no rock grain
- Uniform softness

---

## Behavior

### Physics
- Heavy enough to stay in place
- Characters can sit on them — no deformation
- Characters can step on them — stable
- Cannot be thrown (too heavy for that)

---

## Lighting

- Soft, warm light on top surface
- Gentle shadow underneath
- No harsh highlights — matte surface
- Subtle warm glow in sunlight
