# Pompom Flower — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Flower
- **Type**: Environmental object
- **Primary use**: Flower Hill, nature scenes, background detail
- **First appearance**: Background in Flower Hill episodes

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, fluffy flower head — like a pom-pom
- 5-7 rounded petals, each petal is soft and puffy
- Center is a small round pom (slightly different color)
- Stem is thin, soft green, slightly curved
- Total height: ~15cm in character scale

### Texture
- Petals look like soft fabric or cotton
- No sharp edges on petals
- Center pom is extra fluffy — like a tiny pom-pom
- Stem is smooth and soft — not rigid

### Color Variants

| Variant | Petals | Center | Stem |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pink | Pompom Pink (#F8BBD0) | Pompom Yellow (#FFF59D) | Soft Green (#A5D6A7) |
| Purple | Pompom Purple (#CE93D8) | Pompom White (#FAFAFA) | Soft Green (#A5D6A7) |
| Orange | Pompom Orange (#FFCC80) | Pompom Pink (#F8BBD0) | Soft Green (#A5D6A7) |
| Blue | Pompom Blue (#90CAF9) | Pompom Yellow (#FFF59D) | Soft Green (#A5D6A7) |

### Surface Details
- Petals have very subtle fiber texture
- No veins, no realistic flower details
- Center pom has visible fluff
- Uniform softness across all petals
