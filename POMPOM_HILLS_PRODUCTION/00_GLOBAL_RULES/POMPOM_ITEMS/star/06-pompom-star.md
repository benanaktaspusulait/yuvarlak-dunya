# Pompom Star — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Star
- **Type**: Environmental object (sky/night)
- **Primary use**: Night scenes, bedtime episodes
- **First appearance**: Night sky episodes

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, fluffy star — NOT realistic star shape
- 5 soft, rounded points — each point is puffy
- Center is extra fluffy — like a pom-pom
- Overall round silhouette
- Size: varies (distant: tiny dot, close: ~15cm)

### Texture
- Cotton-like, fluffy surface
- Soft glow — not sharp light
- Looks warm and cozy
- Like a plush nightlight

### Color
- **Primary**: Pompom Yellow (#FFF59D) — warm glow
- **Center**: Pompom White (#FAFAFA) — brightest point
- **Glow**: Soft warm halo around star
- No blue/white cold stars — always warm

### Surface Details
- Very subtle fluff texture
- Soft glow emanates from center
- Points are soft and rounded

---

## Behavior

### Animation
- Twinkles gently — soft pulse of glow
- Very slow, peaceful rhythm
- No flashing, no rapid changes
- Glow expands and contracts subtly

---

## Lighting

- Self-illuminated — emits soft warm glow
- No external light source needed
- Glow creates soft halo effect
- Warmer than realistic stars
