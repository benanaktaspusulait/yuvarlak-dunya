# Pompom Ball — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Ball
- **Type**: Handheld object
- **Primary use**: Play/game episodes
- **First appearance**: S01E02-B (Bounce, Bounce, Kiko!)

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Perfectly round sphere
- No flat spots, no angular edges
- Slightly compressed at bottom when resting (very subtle)
- Diameter: ~12cm in character scale (fits in two small hands)

### Texture
- Soft, fluffy surface — like a plush ball
- Visible fiber/fuzz detail on surface
- No hard shell, no rubber, no plastic
- Light diffuses softly — no harsh highlights
- Looks squeezable and huggable

### Color
- **Primary**: Pompom Pink (#F8BBD0) — 40%
- **Secondary**: Pompom Purple (#CE93D8) — 30%
- **Accent**: Pompom Orange (#FFCC80) — 20%
- **Base**: Pompom White (#FAFAFA) — 10%
- Colors blend softly into each other — no hard boundaries
- Pattern: gentle swirl or marble-like blend

### Surface Details
- Very subtle sparkle/matte finish — not glossy
- No printed patterns, no numbers, no logos
- No seams visible
- Uniform fluffiness all around

---

## Behavior

### Physics
- Bounces softly — floaty, slow bounce (like a plush ball)
- Returns to perfect round shape after every bounce
- Never deflates, never pops, never breaks
- Light and airy — floats slightly when dropped
- Rolls slowly on flat surfaces

### Interaction
- Characters can hold it in two hands
- Characters can bounce it gently
- Characters can squeeze it — it deforms slightly then returns
- Characters can hug it — it's soft and warm

---

## Lighting

- Warm, soft diffusion on surface
- No sharp specular highlights
- No metallic reflections
- Subsurface scattering — light penetrates slightly into the fluff
- Shadow underneath is soft and warm
