# Pompom Raindrop — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Raindrop
- **Type**: Environmental object
- **Primary use**: Rain scenes, Cloud Hill
- **First appearance**: Rain/weather episodes

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, fluffy raindrop — NOT realistic teardrop
- Like a small cotton ball in drop form
- Slightly pointed at top, round at bottom
- Soft, organic outline
- Size: ~3cm in character scale (individual drop)

### Texture
- Cotton-like, fluffy surface
- No water/wet texture
- Looks soft and huggable
- Not liquid — solid plush drop

### Color
- **Primary**: Pompom Blue (#90CAF9) — soft pastel blue
- **Highlight**: Pompom White (#FAFAFA) — top highlight
- **Shadow**: Slightly deeper blue — bottom
- No transparent/water-like appearance

### Surface Details
- Very subtle fiber texture
- No water reflections
- No ripple effects
- Uniform softness

---

## Behavior

### Animation
- Falls slowly — floaty, gentle descent
- Bounces softly on ground — plush bounce
- Settles and stays — no splashing
- Group of drops fall together — gentle rain

---

## Lighting

- Soft, diffused light — overcast feel
- No harsh highlights
- Subtle warm glow despite blue color
- Shadow underneath is very soft
