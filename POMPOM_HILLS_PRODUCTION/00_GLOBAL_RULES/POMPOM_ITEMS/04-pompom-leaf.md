# Pompom Leaf — Item Spec

## Overview
- **Name**: Pompom Leaf
- **Type**: Environmental object
- **Primary use**: Little Forest, autumn scenes, nature backgrounds
- **First appearance**: Little Forest episodes

---

## Physical Appearance

### Shape
- Round, fluffy leaf — NOT realistic leaf shape
- Like a small cotton puff in leaf form
- 5 soft lobes, each lobe is round and puffy
- Small soft stem at base
- Size: ~8cm in character scale

### Texture
- Soft, fabric-like surface
- No veins, no realistic leaf texture
- Looks like a plush toy leaf
- Light passes through slightly — warm glow

### Color Variants

| Variant | Main Color | Season |
|---|---|---|
| Spring | Pompom Green (#A5D6A7) | Spring/summer |
| Summer | Pompom Green (#81C784) | Mid-summer |
| Autumn Gold | Pompom Orange (#FFCC80) | Autumn |
| Autumn Red | Pompom Pink (#F8BBD0) | Late autumn |
| Autumn Brown | Pompom Cream (#FFF8E1) | Fall |

### Surface Details
- Very subtle fiber texture — like felt
- No veins, no spots, no holes
- Uniform softness

---

## Behavior

### Animation
- Drifts down slowly when falling — floaty, gentle
- Sways in breeze —柔 soft, fabric-like movement
- Never flutters fast — always slow and peaceful
- Settles gently on ground — no bouncing

---

## Lighting

- Warm, soft light through leaf — slight translucency
- No harsh shadows
- Autumn colors glow warmly in sunlight
