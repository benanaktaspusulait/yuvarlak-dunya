# Full Screenplay — First Shadow

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Sunny Start (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, sunny day. Warm golden sunlight fills the
space. Arda runs into the garden with joyful energy, his feet soft on the grass. He stops in
the middle of the sunny patch, breathing happily.

**Arda:** "What a sunny day!"

He looks around the garden with delight, arms slightly open, feeling the warmth.

### Shot 02 — There It Is (0:15–0:30)

Arda stops running and looks down. A clear, soft-edged shadow stretches on the ground beneath
him, mirroring his standing shape. His eyes go wide with curiosity. He tilts his head to one
side, studying the dark shape.

**Arda:** "What's that?"

He leans forward, looking closely at his own shadow on the grass.

### Shot 03 — Step On It (0:30–0:45)

Arda lifts his right foot and carefully tries to step on the shadow. The moment his foot moves,
the shadow shifts too — it stays just ahead of his foot. Arda's foot lands on empty grass.

**Arda:** "Hey!"

He looks at his foot, then back at the shadow, which now sits a tiny bit further away.

### Shot 04 — The Chase (0:45–1:00)

Arda takes quick, playful steps toward the shadow, trying to trap it under his feet. The
shadow moves with every step he takes, always staying just out of reach. Arda's energy is
playful and determined, never frustrated.

**Arda:** "Come back!"

He laughs as the shadow slides away from each step.

### Shot 05 — Running (1:00–1:15)

Arda runs toward the shadow at full speed. The shadow runs too — always the same distance
ahead. Arda runs faster; the shadow keeps pace. He stops, panting happily, hands on his knees.

**Arda:** "It runs with me!"

He looks at the shadow, which has stopped too, matching his position.

### Shot 06 — Plop (1:15–1:30)

Arda plops down onto the grass with a soft thump, legs spread out. The shadow sits right
underneath him now, perfectly matching his seated shape. Arda looks down at the shadow between
his legs and giggles.

**SFX:** `puff`

**Arda:** "Ha! It's under me!"

### Shot 07 — Still There (1:30–1:45)

Arda looks at his hands, turning them over. The shadow on the ground shows two dark hands
moving exactly the same way. He wiggles his fingers; the shadow wiggles too. His expression
shifts from playful to thoughtful.

**Arda:** "It moves when I move."

A calm beat lets the observation land.

### Shot 08 — Growing Tall (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands up very slowly from the ground. As he rises, his shadow stretches and grows on the
grass beneath him — from a small seated shape to a full standing shape. His eyes follow the
shadow's growth upward. A look of quiet wonder crosses his face.

**Arda:** "It grows with me!"

He stands still, looking at the shadow at his feet, then at the sunny sky above.
