### Shot 01 — Playtime (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home indoor play area — a warm, bright room with soft colourful mats,
rounded shelves, and scattered soft toys. Arda sits on the mat, looking through his toy basket.
He pushes aside a stuffed bear and a fabric block, searching for something new.

**Arda:** "Hmm… what shall I play with?"

He looks around the play area with bright, curious eyes.

### Shot 02 — There It Is (0:15–0:30)

Arda's eyes land on something in the corner of the play area, half-hidden behind a soft cushion.
He crawls over and pulls out a round, colourful soft toy drum — rainbow stripes, squishy to the
touch, lightweight. His eyes go wide with curiosity. He holds it up and turns it in his hands.

**Arda:** "A drum!"

He hugs it gently, feeling the soft fabric surface.

### Shot 03 — First Tap (0:30–0:45)

Arda places the drum on the mat in front of him. He lifts his hand and taps the top of the drum
gently. A soft, gentle 'poof' sound comes out. Arda's eyebrows rise with delight. He taps again.
'Poof.' He taps a third time. 'Poof.'

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Poof!"

He grins, delighted by the sound.

### Shot 04 — Faster Tap (0:45–1:00)

Arda taps the drum faster now — 'poof-poof-poof-poof' — each tap soft and rhythmic. His hand
moves up and down with joyful energy. The sounds create a quick, happy pattern. Arda's face
lights up with excitement.

**SFX:** `poof-poof-poof`

**Arda:** "It goes poof-poof-poof!"

He claps his hands once, then taps the drum again.

### Shot 05 — The Roll (1:00–1:15)

Arda pushes the drum gently with both hands. The drum rolls across the soft mat, making a
rhythmic 'poof-poof-poof-poof' as it rolls over the textured surface. Arda watches it go, his
eyes following the colourful drum as it moves away from him.

**SFX:** `poof poof poof poof`

**Arda:** "It rolls!"

He stands up, excited.

### Shot 06 — Under the Sofa (1:15–1:30)

The drum rolls across the mat and under the sofa with a soft 'poof.' Arda laughs and crawls
after it on his hands and knees. He peeks under the sofa — the drum sits there, rainbow stripes
glowing in the dim light under the furniture.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Ha! Come back!"

He reaches under and pulls the drum out.

### Shot 07 — Got It! (1:30–1:45)

Arda pulls the drum free and holds it in his lap, hugging it. He taps it gently once — 'poof.'
He pats the drum with satisfaction, safe and sound in his arms.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "I got you!"

He looks at the drum with warm, proud eyes.

### Shot 08 — Outside! (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands up, holding the drum, and walks toward the garden door. He pushes the door open with
his foot and steps out into the bright, sunny garden. The warm sunlight fills the frame. Arda
holds the drum up to the sky, grinning.

**Arda:** "Let's go outside!"

The sunny garden stretches before him — soft grass, rounded shrubs, bright sky.
