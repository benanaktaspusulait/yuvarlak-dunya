# Full Screenplay — Drum Circle

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

### Shot 01 — Luca Arrives (0:00–0:15)

Arda walks along the path toward Friendship Meadow, rolling his drum beside him. Luca steps into
view from behind a rounded shrub, holding a small soft drum of his own — green with yellow dots.
Luca's eyes light up when he sees Arda's drum.

**Luca:** "Arda! A drum! I have one too!"

**Arda:** "You do! Let's roll them!"

### Shot 02 — Noah Joins (0:15–0:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, carrying a small soft drum — blue with white stars. He holds it
up proudly. All three children now stand on the path, each with a drum. They look at each other's
drums with wide, happy eyes.

**Noah:** "Look! Mine goes poof too!"

**Arda:** "Three drums!"

### Shot 03 — Drum Circle (0:30–0:45)

The three friends sit in a small circle on the soft grass near the meadow edge. They place their
drums in the middle and tap them gently — 'poof poof poof poof poof poof' — a soft, overlapping
rhythm. They look at each other and laugh with delight.

**SFX:** `poof poof poof`

**Luca:** "We're making a beat!"

### Shot 04 — Rolling Line (0:45–1:00)

The three friends stand up and roll their drums in a line across the meadow grass. Three drums
roll side by side — 'poof poof poof poof poof poof' — each at a slightly different speed, creating
a layered rhythm. The friends walk behind their drums, watching them roll.

**Noah:** "Mine is fast!"

**Arda:** "Mine is steady!"

### Shot 05 — Stop! (1:00–1:15)

The three drums slow down and come to a gentle stop in the middle of the meadow, each making a
final soft 'poof.' The friends catch up to their drums and kneel beside them. A quiet moment of
anticipation.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "They stopped!"

They look at each other, ready for the next game.

### Shot 06 — Together (1:15–1:30)

The three friends push their drums at the same speed. Three drums roll across the meadow together
— 'poof… poof… poof' — perfectly synchronized. The friends walk behind them, smiling, matching
their pace to the shared rhythm. The meadow is alive with gentle, steady sound.

**SFX:** `poof… poof… poof`

**All:** "Poof… poof… poof!"

### Shot 07 — Sitting Together (1:30–1:45)

The three friends sit together in Friendship Meadow, each holding their drum in their lap. They
tap their drums softly — 'poof… poof… poof' — in a gentle, shared rhythm. The warm afternoon
light is golden and soft. They look at each other and smile.

**Arda:** "I love this drum."

**Luca:** "Me too."

**Noah:** "Me three."

### Shot 08 — One More Time? (1:45–2:00)

All three friends face the camera, sitting together in Friendship Meadow with their drums in
their laps. They sway gently side to side. They say the rhyme one last time together, tapping

**All:** "Slow roll? Poof… poof… Fast roll? Poof-poof-poof! Together? Poof poof poof!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda taps his drum one last time — 'poof.' The camera holds on the warm moment as the scene
