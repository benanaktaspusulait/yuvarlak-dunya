# Full Screenplay — Your Turn

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — A Sound in the Meadow (0:00–0:15)

Arda enters Friendship Meadow's Group Game Space holding the ball. He hears a playful, gentle
`poof` from behind a rounded shrub and turns. No one is there. He hears a quiet giggle from the
other side and turns again.

**Arda:** "Who's there?"

### Shot 02 — Adventure Friends (0:15–0:30)

Luca and Noah step into view from the clear side of the shrub. Luca points toward the open game
space; Noah gives a cheerful wave.

**Luca:** "Adventure friends!"

**Noah:** "Let's make it fun!"

### Shot 03 — Shall We Play? (0:30–0:45)

Arda places the ball on the grass between them. All three children can see one another clearly.

**Arda:** "Shall we play together?"

Luca and Noah nod.

**Luca and Noah:** "Yes!"

### Shot 04 — Arda, Luca, Noah (0:45–1:00)

Arda rolls the ball softly to Luca. Luca waits until it reaches him, stops it with both hands, then
rolls it to Noah.

**Arda:** "Luca's turn."

**Luca:** "Noah's turn."

### Shot 05 — To Arda's Foot (1:00–1:15)

Noah studies the ball with a playful smile.

**Noah:** "I have an idea!"

He sends a gentle curved roll toward Arda. The ball arrives beside Arda's shoe and softly taps it.

**Arda:** "It found my foot!"

All three laugh gently.

### Shot 06 — One, Two, Three (1:15–1:30)

The friends form a wide, safe triangle. Each child completes one slow roll.

**Arda:** "One!"

**Luca:** "Two!"

**Noah:** "Three!"

The ball remains within the central safe region throughout.

### Shot 07 — Right, Left, Centre (1:30–1:45)

They repeat the passing game. Arda rolls right to Luca; Luca rolls left to Noah; Noah rolls through
the open centre back to Arda.

**Arda:** "Right!"

**Luca:** "Left!"

**Noah:** "Centre!"

### Shot 08 — One More Time? (1:45–2:00)

Arda holds the ball with both hands. He takes a deep breath, then does a special trick bounce — he
bounces the ball once, claps his hands, and catches it on the way back down. Luca and Noah's eyes
go wide, then they clap excitedly.

**SFX:** `poof` `clap-clap`

**Luca:** "Wow!"

**Noah:** "Do it again!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda rolls the ball softly toward the camera until its fluff fills the frame, matching the opening.
