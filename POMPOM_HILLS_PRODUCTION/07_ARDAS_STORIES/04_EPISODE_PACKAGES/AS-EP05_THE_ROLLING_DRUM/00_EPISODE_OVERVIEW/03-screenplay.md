# Full Screenplay — The Rolling Drum

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — First Tap (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Playtime (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home indoor play area — a warm, bright room with soft colourful mats,
rounded shelves, and scattered soft toys. Arda sits on the mat, looking through his toy basket.
He pushes aside a stuffed bear and a fabric block, searching for something new.

**Arda:** "Hmm… what shall I play with?"

He looks around the play area with bright, curious eyes.

### Shot 02 — There It Is (0:15–0:30)

Arda's eyes land on something in the corner of the play area, half-hidden behind a soft cushion.
He crawls over and pulls out a round, colourful soft toy drum — rainbow stripes, squishy to the
touch, lightweight. His eyes go wide with curiosity. He holds it up and turns it in his hands.

**Arda:** "A drum!"

He hugs it gently, feeling the soft fabric surface.

### Shot 03 — First Tap (0:30–0:45)

Arda places the drum on the mat in front of him. He lifts his hand and taps the top of the drum
gently. A soft, gentle 'poof' sound comes out. Arda's eyebrows rise with delight. He taps again.
'Poof.' He taps a third time. 'Poof.'

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Poof!"

He grins, delighted by the sound.

### Shot 04 — Faster Tap (0:45–1:00)

Arda taps the drum faster now — 'poof-poof-poof-poof' — each tap soft and rhythmic. His hand
moves up and down with joyful energy. The sounds create a quick, happy pattern. Arda's face
lights up with excitement.

**SFX:** `poof-poof-poof`

**Arda:** "It goes poof-poof-poof!"

He claps his hands once, then taps the drum again.

### Shot 05 — The Roll (1:00–1:15)

Arda pushes the drum gently with both hands. The drum rolls across the soft mat, making a
rhythmic 'poof-poof-poof-poof' as it rolls over the textured surface. Arda watches it go, his
eyes following the colourful drum as it moves away from him.

**SFX:** `poof poof poof poof`

**Arda:** "It rolls!"

He stands up, excited.

### Shot 06 — Under the Sofa (1:15–1:30)

The drum rolls across the mat and under the sofa with a soft 'poof.' Arda laughs and crawls
after it on his hands and knees. He peeks under the sofa — the drum sits there, rainbow stripes
glowing in the dim light under the furniture.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Ha! Come back!"

He reaches under and pulls the drum out.

### Shot 07 — Got It! (1:30–1:45)

Arda pulls the drum free and holds it in his lap, hugging it. He taps it gently once — 'poof.'
He pats the drum with satisfaction, safe and sound in his arms.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "I got you!"

He looks at the drum with warm, proud eyes.

### Shot 08 — Outside! (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands up, holding the drum, and walks toward the garden door. He pushes the door open with
his foot and steps out into the bright, sunny garden. The warm sunlight fills the frame. Arda
holds the drum up to the sky, grinning.

**Arda:** "Let's go outside!"

The sunny garden stretches before him — soft grass, rounded shrubs, bright sky.

## Module B — The Beat (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Slow Roll (2:00–2:15)

Arda kneels on the garden grass and places the drum on the ground. He pushes it gently with one
hand. The drum rolls slowly across the grass — 'poof… poof… poof' — each sound spaced evenly,
soft and steady. Arda watches it roll, his head nodding to the rhythm.

**SFX:** `poof… poof… poof`

**Arda:** "Slow roll…"

He smiles, listening to the gentle, steady beat.

### Shot 10 — Fast Roll! (2:15–2:30)

Arda pushes the drum harder. It races across the grass — 'poof-poof-poof-poof-poof' — fast and
energetic. The drum bounces slightly on the soft ground. Arda jumps up and chases after it, his
feet pattering on the grass.

**SFX:** `poof-poof-poof-poof`

**Arda:** "Fast roll!"

He laughs as he chases the speedy drum.

### Shot 11 — Bump! (2:30–2:45)

The fast-rolling drum bumps gently into a rounded shrub and stops with a soft 'poof.' The shrub
absorbs the impact — no harm, no damage. Arda catches up to the drum, panting happily. He picks
it up and looks at the shrub, then at the drum.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Oops!"

He giggles at the gentle collision.

### Shot 12 — Thinking (2:45–3:00)

Arda sits on the grass, holding the drum in his lap. He looks at it thoughtfully, tapping his
chin. He places the drum on the grass again and pushes it — not too slow, not too fast. A medium
push. The drum rolls at a steady, even pace.

**SFX:** `poof… poof… poof`

**Arda:** "Hmm… not too slow… not too fast…"

He watches the drum roll with focused eyes.

### Shot 13 — Just Right (3:00–3:15)

The drum rolls at a perfect medium speed — 'poof… poof… poof' — steady and beautiful. Arda
walks alongside it, matching his pace to the drum's rhythm. He claps softly in time with the
beat. His face is calm and satisfied.

**SFX:** `poof… poof… poof`

**Arda:** "Just right!"

He grins with quiet pride.

### Shot 14 — Experiment (3:15–3:30)

Arda rolls the drum slow, then fast, then medium again. Each speed makes a different beat
pattern. He listens carefully to each one, his head tilting as he compares the sounds. He nods
to himself — the medium roll is the one he likes best.

**SFX:** `poof… poof…` then `poof-poof-poof` then `poof… poof… poof`

**Arda:** "Poof… poof… poof!"

He settles on the medium rhythm with a happy hum.

### Shot 15 — The Rhyme (3:30–3:45)

Arda stands on the garden path, swaying gently side to side. The drum sits at his feet. He looks
at the drum, then at the camera, and recites the rhyme with rhythmic delight, tapping his hand
against his leg in time with the words.

**Arda:** "Slow roll? Poof… poof… Fast roll? Poof-poof-poof! Together? Poof poof poof!"

He points down at the drum with a grin.

### Shot 16 — Rolling Along (3:45–4:00)

Arda rolls the drum along the garden path toward the meadow. The drum rolls beside him —
'poof… poof… poof' — a steady companion beat. Arda walks at the drum's pace, glancing at it
warmly. The path opens toward Friendship Meadow ahead.

**Arda:** "Let's go!"

The drum keeps pace beside him.

## Module C — Drum Circle (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

Arda walks along the path toward Friendship Meadow, rolling his drum beside him. Luca steps into
view from behind a rounded shrub, holding a small soft drum of his own — green with yellow dots.
Luca's eyes light up when he sees Arda's drum.

**Luca:** "Arda! A drum! I have one too!"

**Arda:** "You do! Let's roll them!"

### Shot 18 — Noah Joins (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, carrying a small soft drum — blue with white stars. He holds it
up proudly. All three children now stand on the path, each with a drum. They look at each other's
drums with wide, happy eyes.

**Noah:** "Look! Mine goes poof too!"

**Arda:** "Three drums!"

### Shot 19 — Drum Circle (4:30–4:45)

The three friends sit in a small circle on the soft grass near the meadow edge. They place their
drums in the middle and tap them gently — 'poof poof poof poof poof poof' — a soft, overlapping
rhythm. They look at each other and laugh with delight.

**SFX:** `poof poof poof`

**Luca:** "We're making a beat!"

### Shot 20 — Rolling Line (4:45–5:00)

The three friends stand up and roll their drums in a line across the meadow grass. Three drums
roll side by side — 'poof poof poof poof poof poof' — each at a slightly different speed, creating
a layered rhythm. The friends walk behind their drums, watching them roll.

**Noah:** "Mine is fast!"

**Arda:** "Mine is steady!"

### Shot 21 — Stop! (5:00–5:15)

The three drums slow down and come to a gentle stop in the middle of the meadow, each making a
final soft 'poof.' The friends catch up to their drums and kneel beside them. A quiet moment of
anticipation.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "They stopped!"

They look at each other, ready for the next game.

### Shot 22 — Together (5:15–5:30)

The three friends push their drums at the same speed. Three drums roll across the meadow together
— 'poof… poof… poof' — perfectly synchronized. The friends walk behind them, smiling, matching
their pace to the shared rhythm. The meadow is alive with gentle, steady sound.

**SFX:** `poof… poof… poof`

**All:** "Poof… poof… poof!"

### Shot 23 — Sitting Together (5:30–5:45)

The three friends sit together in Friendship Meadow, each holding their drum in their lap. They
tap their drums softly — 'poof… poof… poof' — in a gentle, shared rhythm. The warm afternoon
light is golden and soft. They look at each other and smile.

**Arda:** "I love this drum."

**Luca:** "Me too."

**Noah:** "Me three."

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

All three friends face the camera, sitting together in Friendship Meadow with their drums in
their laps. They sway gently side to side. They say the rhyme one last time together, tapping
their drums in rhythm.

**All:** "Slow roll? Poof… poof… Fast roll? Poof-poof-poof! Together? Poof poof poof!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda taps his drum one last time — 'poof.' The camera holds on the warm moment as the scene
fades softly.
