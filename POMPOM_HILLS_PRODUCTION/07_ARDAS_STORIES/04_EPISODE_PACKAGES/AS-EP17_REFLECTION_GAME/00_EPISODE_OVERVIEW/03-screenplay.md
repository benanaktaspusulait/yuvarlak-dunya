# Full Screenplay — Reflection Game

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Coming Back (0:00–0:15)

The ripples from Arda's touch slowly fade. The water calms, bit by bit. The distorted
reflection begins to reform — first the blue sky returns, then the green tree branches,
then the white clouds. Arda kneels beside the puddle, watching the image repair itself.
His own face gradually reappears in the water.

**Arda:** "It's coming back…"

He watches with patient, fascinated eyes.

### Shot 02 — Still Water (0:15–0:30)

The puddle is perfectly still again. The reflection is crystal clear — sky, trees, clouds,
Arda's smiling face. Arda waves at the reflection one more time. The reflected Arda waves
back, crisp and undistorted. Arda nods, understanding something.

**Arda:** "Still water. Clear picture."

He waves slowly, watching the perfect mirror image.

### Shot 03 — Gentle Touch (0:30–0:45)

Arda reaches down again, this time more carefully. He touches the water surface with just
one fingertip — a small, single ripple appears, spreading outward in a perfect circle.
Arda watches the ripple travel across the puddle, distorting the reflection in its path.

**SFX:** `bloop`

**Arda:** "One finger…"

He observes the single ripple's effect.

### Shot 04 — Small Ripple (0:45–1:00)

The small ripple from Arda's gentle touch reaches the far edge of the puddle and fades.
The reflection settles back — the image is slightly wobbly, then still. Arda nods to
himself. A small touch makes a small ripple. A small ripple makes a small distortion.

**Arda:** "Small ripple…"

He watches the last wobble fade away.

### Shot 05 — Watching (1:00–1:15)

Arda sits beside the puddle in the warm sunlight, perfectly still. The puddle is calm and
the reflection is clear — the whole sky above is mirrored in the water. Arda watches the
reflection for a long, peaceful moment. A leaf drifts down from a tree and lands gently on
the far edge of the puddle, creating a tiny ripple.

**Arda:** "The sky is in the puddle."

He smiles quietly, at peace with the discovery.

### Shot 06 — The Rhyme (1:15–1:30)

Arda stands up and looks at the puddle, then at the camera. He recites the rhyme with
delight, pointing at the puddle for each line. His body sways gently with the rhythm.

**Arda:** "Jump in? Splash! Look down? Sky! Touch it? Ripples!"

He claps his hands once with satisfaction.

### Shot 07 — The Whole Sky (1:30–1:45)

Arda looks down into the puddle one more time. The reflection shows the entire sky — blue
above, clouds drifting slowly, tree branches swaying. Arda's face is reflected among it
all. He gasps softly with amazement, his eyes wide and sparkling.

**Arda:** "The whole sky!"

He crouches down, nose almost touching the water, gazing at the infinite sky in the puddle.

### Shot 08 — Puddle Walk (1:45–2:00)

Arda walks along the garden path toward the meadow, splashing gently through small puddles
along the way. Each puddle he passes reflects a piece of the sky. He looks down at each one
as he walks, pointing and smiling.

**Arda:** "Puddle… puddle… puddle!"

The path opens toward Friendship Meadow ahead.
