## Logline

When Arda finds a small round acorn under a tree, he discovers that it rolls fast on smooth ground and slow on bumpy grass — then he invites Luca and Noah to test every surface together in a friendly rolling race.

---

## Story Promise

This episode continues the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful movement, meets a small physical surprise, observes closely, discovers a pattern, and carries what he learned into a shared game with friends.

## Repeated Rhyme

```text

```

---

## Learning Outcome

Children see that the same object behaves differently on different surfaces, that observing carefully reveals patterns, and that experimenting with surfaces is a fun way to learn about the world. The final game models sharing discoveries, comparing results, and enjoying a shared activity without competition.
