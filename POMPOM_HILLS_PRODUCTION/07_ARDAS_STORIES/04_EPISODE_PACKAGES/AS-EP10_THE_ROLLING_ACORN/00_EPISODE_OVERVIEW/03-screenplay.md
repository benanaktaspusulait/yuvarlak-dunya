# Full Screenplay — The Rolling Acorn

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — First Roll (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Under the Tree (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on the base of a large rounded tree in Arda's Home garden — warm morning
sunlight, soft green grass, small daisies. Arda crouches and peers under the low branches. He
reaches carefully into the soft soil and lifts out a small round acorn — warm brown, smooth,
about the size of his thumb tip. His eyes light up.

**Arda:** "I found it!"

### Shot 02 — So Round (0:15–0:30)

Arda sits on the grass and holds the acorn between his thumb and finger, turning it slowly. The
camera shows the acorn's smooth round shape in close-up. Arda traces the tiny cap with one
finger, then grins.

**Arda:** "It's so round. I wonder… can it roll?"

A short listening pause invites the viewer to answer.

**Arda:** "Let's try!"

### Shot 03 — First Push (0:30–0:45)

Arda kneels on the smooth garden path — flat, soft-edged, child-safe. He places the acorn on
the path surface and pushes it gently with one finger. The acorn starts to move.

**SFX:** `frrr`

**Arda:** "Go!"

### Shot 04 — Fast on the Path (0:45–1:00)

The acorn rolls quickly along the smooth path, picking up speed. Arda follows with quick but
safe small steps, his eyes wide with excitement. The acorn rolls in a gentle straight line.

**SFX:** `vrr-vrr-vrr`

**Arda:** "It's fast! So fast!"

### Shot 05 — One More Time! (1:00–1:15)

Arda catches the acorn, walks back to the start of the path, and pushes it again. The acorn
rolls fast once more. Arda giggles and claps softly.

**Arda:** "One more time! Fast on the path!"

**SFX:** `vrr-vrr-vrr`

### Shot 06 — Off the Path (1:15–1:30)

Arda pushes the acorn gently, but this time it rolls off the smooth path and onto the bumpy
green grass beside it. Arda follows with curious steps, watching the acorn's direction change.

**Arda:** "Oh — it went onto the grass!"

### Shot 07 — Slow on the Grass (1:30–1:45)

The acorn slows down noticeably on the bumpy grass. Its rolling rhythm changes from fast
`vrr-vrr` to slow `frrr… frrr…`. Arda kneels beside it and watches it come to a gentle stop.
He tilts his head, thinking.

**Arda:** "Slow… now it's slow."

**SFX:** `frrr… frrr…`

### Shot 08 — The First Rhyme (1:45–2:00)

Arda picks up the acorn and holds it up, looking at it with curiosity and a small smile. He
claps a gentle rhythm and says the rhyme, rocking slightly.

**Arda:** "Smooth? Fast! Bumpy? Slow! Different surface, different roll!"

He looks around the garden with excited eyes, as if searching for more surfaces to test.

---

## Module B — Surface Discovery (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Moss Patch (2:00–2:15)

Arda notices a soft green moss patch growing at the base of the tree. He kneels beside it and
touches the moss gently with one finger, feeling its soft, bumpy texture. His expression is
curious.

**Arda:** "Moss! So soft and bumpy."

### Shot 10 — Slow on Moss (2:15–2:30)

Arda places the acorn on the moss and gives it a gentle push. The acorn barely moves — it rolls
very slowly, almost rocking in place. Arda's eyes widen with surprise and delight.

**SFX:** `frrr………`

**Arda:** "So slow on moss! Even slower than grass!"

### Shot 11 — Sandy Patch (2:30–2:45)

Arda walks a few steps and finds a small sandy patch beside the path — fine, soft sand. He kneels
and runs his fingers through it, feeling the texture. He looks at the acorn in his other hand.

**Arda:** "Sand! Let's try sand."

### Shot 12 — Medium on Sand (2:45–3:00)

Arda places the acorn on the sand and pushes it. The acorn rolls — faster than on moss, but
slower than on the smooth path. Arda watches it roll and nods slowly, comparing.

**SFX:** `frrr-frrr`

**Arda:** "Not so fast. Not so slow. Sand is in the middle!"

### Shot 13 — Leaf Patch (3:00–3:15)

Arda walks to where dry fallen leaves gather in a small soft pile near the tree. He crouches and
touches the leaves — they crackle softly under his fingers. He looks at the acorn with a
speculative smile.

**Arda:** "Leaves! What happens on leaves?"

### Shot 14 — Bumpy on Leaves (3:15–3:30)

Arda places the acorn on the leaf pile and pushes gently. The acorn bounces softly from leaf to
leaf, rolling unevenly — sometimes fast, sometimes slow, wobbling. Arda giggles at the funny
movement.

**SFX:** `tink-tink-tink`

**Arda:** "It bounces! The leaves make it wobble!"

### Shot 15 — Three Surfaces (3:30–3:45)

Arda arranges the moss, a small pile of sand, and a leaf patch in a row on the ground. He
places the acorn at the start of each one and pushes it gently along each surface, watching
carefully. His expression is focused and scientific.

**Arda:** "Moss — slow. Sand — middle. Leaves — wobbly."

### Shot 16 — Rhyme Again (3:45–4:00)

Arda stands up, holds the acorn proudly in both hands, and repeats the rhyme with a bigger
smile. He turns toward the meadow path in the background.

**Arda:** "Smooth? Fast! Bumpy? Slow! Different surface, different roll!"

He begins walking toward Friendship Meadow, the acorn cupped safely in his hands.

---

## Module C — Rolling Race (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Meadow Path (4:00–4:15)

Arda enters the Friendship Meadow path — a wide, inviting path with grass on both sides. He
holds the acorn and looks around happily. In the distance, he spots Luca and Noah walking toward
him.

**Arda:** "Luca! Noah! Come see what I found!"

### Shot 18 — Show and Tell (4:15–4:30)

Luca and Noah arrive. Arda holds out the acorn for them to see. Luca leans in close; Noah's
eyes go wide.

**Luca:** "What is it?"

**Arda:** "A round acorn! Watch — it rolls fast on smooth ground."

He places it on the path and pushes it. The acorn rolls quickly.

**Noah:** "Whoa! Fast!"

### Shot 19 — Choose a Surface (4:30–4:45)

Arda points to different surfaces nearby — the smooth path, a grass patch, a mossy area, a
sandy spot. Luca finds a smooth flat stone. Noah picks up a dry leaf.

**Arda:** "Pick a surface! Let's roll together!"

**Luca:** "I'll try the path with my stone!"

**Noah:** "I'll try the leaves with my leaf!"

### Shot 20 — Ready, Set, Roll! (4:45–5:00)

The three friends line up at the same starting point. Arda has his acorn on the smooth path.
Luca places his stone on the path beside it. Noah puts his leaf on a leaf patch nearby. They
look at each other excitedly.

**Arda:** "Ready?"

**Luca and Noah:** "Ready!"

**Arda:** "Roll!"

All three push their objects at the same time.

### Shot 21 — Speed Comparison (5:00–5:15)

Arda's acorn rolls fastest on the smooth path. Luca's stone rolls a bit slower beside it.
Noah's leaf floats and tumbles slowly on the leaf patch. Arda's eyes track all three, comparing.

**Arda:** "My acorn is the fastest! The stone is slower! The leaf is the slowest!"

**Noah:** "The surface makes it different!"

### Shot 22 — Swap and Try (5:15–5:30)

The friends laugh and decide to swap surfaces. Arda rolls his acorn on the grass. Luca rolls
his stone on the sand. Noah places his leaf on the smooth path.

**Arda:** "Let's swap! Try a new surface!"

**Luca:** "Good idea!"

They each push their objects on the new surfaces.

### Shot 23 — Same Pattern (5:30–5:45)

Arda's acorn slows on the grass. Luca's stone rolls medium on the sand. Noah's leaf glides
fast on the smooth path. All three friends look at each other with recognition.

**Arda:** "Smooth? Fast! Bumpy? Slow!"

**Luca and Noah:** "Different surface, different roll!"

They clap together, delighted by the shared discovery.

### Shot 24 — One More Roll (5:45–6:00)

Arda kneels and rolls the acorn gently toward the camera. It rolls smoothly along the path,
getting closer until its warm brown round shape fills the frame — a gentle loop back to the
discovery. Luca and Noah are visible behind him, smiling.

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

The acorn's round shape fills the frame softly.
