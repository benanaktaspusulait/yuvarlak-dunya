# Full Screenplay — Waiting for Wind

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Walking Up (0:00–0:15)

Arda walks along a gentle path that leads up to a higher hilltop. He carries the kite in one hand,
the string trailing behind him. The path winds through soft grass and small wildflowers. The sky
above is open and wide. Arda walks with purpose, looking up at the breeze-less sky as he climbs.

**Arda:** "Up, up, up."

### Shot 02 — The Twitching Leaf (0:15–0:30)

Arda reaches the hilltop. He stands still, looking around. On the ground near his feet, a dry
leaf lies on the grass. Arda crouches down to look at it. The leaf twitches — just barely — a
tiny nudge, as if something invisible touched it. Arda's eyes widen. He watches the leaf closely,
leaning in close, his nose almost touching it.

**Arda:** "A leaf..."

### Shot 03 — Leaf Watching (0:30–0:45)

Arda lies flat on his belly on the grass, watching the leaf like a scientist! The leaf twitches
again — then lifts slightly off the ground! Arda's eyes follow it, his head turning as the leaf
drifts a few inches. He reaches out to touch it, but the leaf skitters away. He army-crawls
after it, giggling.

**Arda:** "Come back, leaf!"

The leaf dances just out of his reach.

### Shot 04 — Kite as a Bird (0:45–1:00)

Arda stands up, holding the kite in front of him. He flaps the kite up and down like a bird's
wings — flap, flap, flap! The kite's pastel panels catch the air and flutter. Arda flaps faster,
running in a circle, making the kite "fly" in his hands. He chirps like a bird — "Cheep cheep!"
The kite flutters but doesn't lift on its own.

**Arda:** "Fly, bird kite! Fly!"

He flaps harder, laughing at his own game.

### Shot 05 — The Spin Experiment (1:00–1:15)

Arda holds the kite above his head and starts spinning in a circle, like a carousel! The kite
lifts slightly as he spins — the tail streams out behind it. Arda spins faster and faster, his
feet barely keeping up. The kite starts to rise! Just as it's about to lift away — Arda gets
dizzy and plops down on the grass, the kite landing softly in his lap.

**Arda:** "Almost... whoa!"

He lies on the grass, dizzy but grinning.

### Shot 06 — Feeling the Breeze (1:15–1:30)

Arda sits up on the hilltop. A gentle breeze reaches him — he feels it on his face. His hair
moves softly. His t-shirt ripples slightly. Arda closes his eyes and smiles, feeling the air
brush against his cheeks. He holds his hand out, palm up, feeling the invisible touch of the wind.
The breeze strengthens — it ruffles the kite's tail in his hands!

**Arda:** "I feel it..."

### Shot 07 — The Wind Arrives! (1:30–1:45)

The wind picks up across the hilltop. The grass sways in soft waves. The wildflowers nod their
heads. Arda's kite tail flutters — the first real movement! Arda looks at his kite with growing
excitement. The wind rustles the shrubs, and the whole hilltop comes alive with gentle motion.
Arda stands up, holding the kite high — it fills with air, the panels puffing out!

**Arda:** "Wind! Wind!"

### Shot 08 — It Flies! (1:45–2:00)

Arda lets go. The kite lifts from his hands — it rises, rising higher and higher into the bright
sky. The pastel colours glow in the sunlight, the fluffy tail dances behind it. Arda's face
lights up with pure joy — his eyes wide, his mouth open in a delighted laugh. The kite soars
above him, steady and free. Arda runs down the hill toward the meadow, the kite trailing above
like a colourful flag.

**Arda:** "It flies! It flies!"
