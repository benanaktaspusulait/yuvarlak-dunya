# Beat Sheet — First Splash

| Shot | Time | Beat | Short potential |
|---:|---:|---|---|
| 01 | 0:00–0:15 | Arda stands at the garden door after rain, looking out at the wet garden | Visual hook — post-rain garden freshness |
| 02 | 0:15–0:30 | Arda steps into the garden, notices shiny wet ground and small puddles everywhere | Discovery moment — puddles revealed |
| 03 | 0:30–0:45 | Arda spots one large, colourful puddle — it shimmers with reflected sky and trees | The puddle discovery |
| 04 | 0:45–1:00 | Arda jumps into the puddle — SPLASH! Water sprays up, his face lights up | First splash — joyful cause and effect |
| 05 | 1:00–1:15 | Arda looks down into the puddle and sees the blue sky and green trees reflected | Reflection discovery |
| 06 | 1:15–1:30 | Arda waves at his own reflection in the puddle — his reflection waves back | Self-recognition beat |
| 07 | 1:30–1:45 | Arda kneels and reaches to touch the reflection — his finger touches the water | Touch attempt — anticipation |
| 08 | 1:45–2:00 | Ripples spread from his finger, distorting the reflection — the image wobbles and breaks | Ripple effect — puzzle introduced |
