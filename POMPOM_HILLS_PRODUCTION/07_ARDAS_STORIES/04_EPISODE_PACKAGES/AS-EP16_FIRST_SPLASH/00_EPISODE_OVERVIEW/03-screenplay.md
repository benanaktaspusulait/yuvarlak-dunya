# Full Screenplay — First Splash

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — After the Rain (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden after rain. The grass is wet and glistening, small
puddles dot the ground, and the sky is clearing — soft blue with fluffy white clouds. Arda
stands at the garden door in his orange t-shirt and blue shorts, looking out at the shiny,
fresh garden. Droplets hang from the rounded shrubs.

**Arda:** "Oooh… rain!"

He steps forward onto the wet grass, eyes wide with wonder.

### Shot 02 — Wet Garden (0:15–0:30)

Arda walks through the garden, his bare feet making soft sounds on the wet grass. He looks
down at the ground — small puddles reflect the sky like little mirrors. He hops from one
small puddle to another, testing the water with his toes. Each puddle shimmers with light.

**Arda:** "So many puddles!"

He looks around the garden with bright, curious eyes.

### Shot 03 — The Colourful Puddle (0:30–0:45)

Arda's eyes land on one large puddle near the garden path. It is the biggest puddle he has
ever seen — perfectly still, its surface reflecting the blue sky, white clouds, green trees,
and warm golden sunlight. The puddle shimmers with all the colours above it. Arda stands
very still, mesmerised.

**Arda:** "A colourful puddle!"

He takes a step closer, his reflection appearing in the water.

### Shot 04 — Splash! (0:45–1:00)

Arda jumps into the puddle with both feet — SPLASH! Water sprays up around his legs in a
joyful burst. Arda laughs with delight, his face lighting up with pure happiness. He jumps
again — SPLASH! The water ripples outward from his feet.

**SFX:** `splash! splash!`

**Arda:** "Splash!"

He giggles, wobbling in the shallow water.

### Shot 05 — Look Down! (1:00–1:15)

Arda stops jumping and looks down into the puddle. The water has calmed, and the reflection
is clear again — he sees the blue sky, white clouds, and green tree branches reflected
perfectly in the water. His own face appears among the clouds. Arda gasps softly.

**Arda:** "Look down… sky!"

He points at the puddle with wonder.

### Shot 06 — Hello, Me! (1:15–1:30)

Arda waves at his reflection in the puddle. The reflected Arda waves back. Arda laughs
delighted — he waves again, bigger this time. The reflection waves in perfect sync. He
tilts his head; the reflection tilts too. He grins; the reflection grins.

**Arda:** "Hi!"

He waves one more time, fascinated by his watery twin.

### Shot 07 — Can I Touch It? (1:30–1:45)

Arda kneels beside the puddle, leaning forward. He reaches one finger toward the surface,
hesitating just above the water. His reflection reaches back from below. Arda's eyes are
wide with curiosity. He moves his finger closer — closer —

**Arda:** "Touch it?"

His fingertip hovers just above the water surface.

### Shot 08 — Ripples! (1:45–2:00)

Arda's finger touches the water — a gentle ripple spreads outward from the point of contact.
The reflection wobbles, distorts, breaks apart into shimmering fragments. The sky and trees
in the puddle scramble and swirl. Arda pulls his hand back quickly, watching the ripples
spread and the reflection scatter.

**SFX:** `bloop`

**Arda:** "Ripples!"

He watches, fascinated and a little surprised.
