## Logline

When Arda's tower of soft blocks keeps falling, he observes that a wider base makes it stand
taller, and turns his solo building success into a shared construction game with Luca and Noah.

---

## Story Promise

This episode extends the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful play,
meets a physical problem — a tower that falls — tries again with a calmer method, discovers that
a wider base creates a stronger tower, and carries what he learned into building with friends.

## Repeated Rhyme

```text
Taller? Wider first!
Stronger? Base it wider!
```

---

## Learning Outcome

Children see that making a base wider makes a tower stronger, that an unsuccessful first try is
safe, and that observing before trying again can reveal a better solution. The final game models
cooperation, turn-taking, and enjoying a shared building activity without competition.
