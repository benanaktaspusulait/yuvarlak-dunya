# Full Screenplay — The Taller Tower

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — The Fall (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — A Block-Sized Plan (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's hands lifting a large, soft, pastel-coloured block above his head with
delighted energy. The block is rounded, colourful, and clearly child-safe. Arda's face beams
behind it.

**Arda:** "I'm going to build a tower!"

He lowers the block and looks at the soft floor where he will build.

### Shot 02 — One (0:15–0:30)

The camera settles into a child-eye-level medium view of Arda's indoor play area. Arda kneels
and places the first block gently on the soft floor. It lands with a soft `thump`.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "One!"

He pats the top of the block and smiles.

### Shot 03 — Two (0:30–0:45)

Arda picks up a second block and carefully places it on top of the first. The tower is now two
blocks tall. It stands still.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Two! It's getting taller!"

He looks up at the tower with growing excitement.

### Shot 04 — Three (0:45–1:00)

Arda lifts a third block with both hands. He places it on top of the second block. The tower
wobbles very slightly but holds.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Three!"

He takes a small step back to look at his tower. It sways gently.

### Shot 05 — Four (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches for a fourth block. He lifts it high and places it on top. The tower wobbles more
noticeably this time. Arda's eyes widen.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Four! It's... wobbly."

He steadies the tower with his hands for a moment, then pulls them away carefully.

### Shot 06 — Tumble (1:15–1:30)

The tower wobbles once more, then gently tumbles. The soft blocks fall with a series of soft
`thump thump thump` sounds and scatter across the floor. No block hits Arda.

**SFX:** `thump thump thump`

**Arda:** "Oh!"

He watches the blocks settle around him.

### Shot 07 — Sitting Back (1:30–1:45)

Arda sits back on the soft floor, legs crossed, looking at the scattered blocks. His expression
is thoughtful, not frustrated. He tilts his head.

**Arda:** "It fell down."

A brief, calm pause lets the moment breathe.

### Shot 08 — Try Again (1:45–2:00)

Arda looks at the blocks, then looks at the camera with a small determined smile.

**Arda:** "Let's try again!"

He stands up and moves toward the blocks with renewed energy.

## Module B — Wider Base (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Gathering (2:00–2:15)

Arda gathers the scattered blocks into a neat pile. He sits back and looks at them, chin
resting on his hand. His expression is curious and thoughtful.

**Arda:** "Hmm..."

He picks up one block and turns it over in his hands.

### Shot 10 — Looking Around (2:15–2:30)

Arda's eyes wander around the room. He notices a small rounded decorative stone sitting on two
tiny flat stones near the wall. The wider base underneath keeps it steady. His eyes light up.

**Arda:** "Oh! That one is standing strong."

He points at the stone on its two-stone base.

### Shot 11 — Two Side by Side (2:30–2:45)

Arda places two blocks side by side on the soft floor. They sit next to each other, forming a
wider base. He looks at the base, then looks at the remaining blocks.

**Arda:** "Wider first!"

**SFX:** `thump thump`

He picks up a third block.

### Shot 12 — Three on Top (2:45–3:00)

Arda places the third block on top of the two-block base. The tower stands. It wobbles very
gently but holds.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Three! It's standing!"

He claps once, softly.

### Shot 13 — Even Wider (3:00–3:15)

Arda looks at the tower. He decides to make the base even wider. He adds a third block to the
bottom row, making a three-block-wide base. He pats it firmly.

**Arda:** "Base it wider!"

**SFX:** `thump thump`

The base now sits solid and wide.

### Shot 14 — Growing Tall (3:15–3:30)

Arda stacks the fourth block, then the fifth block on top. The tower rises — four, five blocks
tall — and stands strong on its wide base. It does not wobble.

**SFX:** `thump thump`

**Arda:** "Four... five! It's tall and strong!"

He steps back with wide eyes and a huge smile.

### Shot 15 — The Rhyme (3:30–3:45)

Arda stands beside his tall, stable tower. He sways gently and recites the rhyme with pride,
looking at the tower and then at the camera.

**Arda:** "Taller? Wider first! Stronger? Base it wider!"

He claps his hands together once.

### Shot 16 — Proud Builder (3:45–4:00)

Arda stands with his hands on his hips, looking at the tower. It stands tall and steady. The
warm indoor light falls on the pastel blocks. He takes one satisfied breath.

**Arda:** "My tower stands!"

He smiles, then turns toward the garden door, hearing a sound outside.

## Module C — Let's Build Together (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

Arda walks through the garden and into Friendship Meadow, passing his tower visible through the
window. Luca steps into view from behind a rounded shrub, eyes wide with excitement at seeing
the tower in the distance.

**Luca:** "Wow, Arda! You built a tower!"

**Arda:** "I did! It fell first, then I made the base wider."

### Shot 18 — Noah Joins (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, waving cheerfully. He looks at the tower through the garden
window, then back at Arda and Luca.

**Noah:** "Can we build together?"

**Arda:** "Yes! Let's build together."

All three children smile at each other.

### Shot 19 — Building Together (4:30–4:45)

The three friends sit in a wide, safe circle in Friendship Meadow. Soft blocks are spread
between them. Arda places a wide base while Luca and Noah hand him blocks one at a time. They
work in rhythm.

**SFX:** `thump thump thump`

**Arda:** "Base it wider!"

### Shot 20 — Two Towers (4:45–5:00)

Two towers stand side by side in the meadow — one tall built by Arda, one slightly shorter built
by all three friends together. The friends stand back and look at both towers.

**Luca:** "Two towers!"

**Noah:** "They're both strong!"

**Arda:** "Because the base is wide."

### Shot 21 — A Bridge Idea (5:00–5:15)

Arda picks up a long, soft block. He looks at the gap between the two towers, then at his
friends.

**Arda:** "What if we connect them?"

Luca and Noah look at each other and nod.

**Luca:** "Let's try!"

### Shot 22 — The Bridge Holds (5:15–5:30)

Very carefully, Arda places the long block across the tops of the two towers. It rests gently
and holds. All three children watch with bated breath, then cheer softly.

**SFX:** `thump`

**All:** "It holds!"

**Noah:** "The wide bases keep them strong!"

### Shot 23 — Standing Together (5:30–5:45)

The three friends stand together behind their construction — two towers connected by a bridge.
They sway gently, admiring what they built. The warm afternoon light falls on the pastel blocks.

**Arda:** "We built it together."

**Luca:** "Together is best."

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

All three friends face the camera. They sway gently side to side and say the rhyme one last
time together. Arda holds a soft block in his hands.

**All:** "Taller? Wider first! Stronger? Base it wider!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda places the block gently on the ground, and the camera holds on their warm smiles as the
scene fades softly.
