# Full Screenplay — Splash Friends

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Luca Splashes! (0:00–0:15)

Luca appears on the path ahead, jumping into a puddle with both feet — SPLASH! He is wearing
his green t-shirt and beige shorts, and he laughs with delight as water sprays up around him.
He sees Arda approaching and waves excitedly.

**Luca:** "Arda! Puddles! Come splash!"

Arda runs toward Luca, excited to share the game.

### Shot 02 — Noah Jumps In! (0:15–0:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, wearing his red t-shirt and dark shorts. He spots a puddle
and jumps in with a big, enthusiastic splash — SPLASH! Water sprays everywhere. Noah laughs,
his face pure joy. All three friends are now on the path, surrounded by puddles.

**Noah:** "Big splash!"

**Arda:** "You found the puddles too!"

### Shot 03 — Three Faces (0:30–0:45)

The three friends gather around one large, still puddle near the meadow edge. They all look
down together. The puddle reflects the blue sky, and three small faces appear in the water —
Arda, Luca, and Noah, reflected among the clouds. All three gasp softly.

**Arda:** "Look! Three faces!"

**Luca:** "That's me!"

**Noah:** "And me!"

### Shot 04 — Triple Wave (0:45–1:00)

All three friends wave at the puddle at the same time. Three reflections wave back in perfect
sync. They laugh with delight, waving faster, watching their watery twins mimic every move.
The puddle shimmers with the movement of six waving hands.

**All:** "Hi!"

They wave in different directions — up, down, side to side — watching the reflections follow.

### Shot 05 — Big Splash! (1:00–1:15)

Arda counts down with his fingers. On three, all three friends jump into the puddle at the
same time — BIG SPLASH! Water sprays up in a joyful burst around all of them. Ripples
overlap, the reflection shatters completely, and all three friends laugh with pure joy.

**SFX:** `BIG SPLASH!`

**All:** "Splash!"

They stand in the puddle, giggling, water dripping from their shorts.

### Shot 06 — Back Again (1:15–1:30)

The ripples from the triple splash slowly settle. The water calms, and the reflection
returns — first the sky, then the trees, then three smiling faces looking down. The three
friends watch their reflections reform, their grins reflected back at them.

**Arda:** "It comes back!"

**Luca:** "Three of us!"

They look at each other and then back at the puddle, amazed.

### Shot 07 — Sitting Together (1:30–1:45)

The three friends sit together beside the large puddle in the warm afternoon light. They
look down at the sky reflected in the water, their three faces among the clouds and trees.
The moment is peaceful and warm. A gentle breeze stirs the tree branches reflected above.

**Arda:** "I love puddles."

**Luca:** "Me too."

**Noah:** "Me three."

### Shot 08 — One More Time? (1:45–2:00)

All three friends face the camera, sitting together beside the puddle. The reflected sky
glitters behind them in the water. They sway gently side to side. They say the rhyme one
last time together, pointing at the puddle with each line.

**All:** "Jump in? Splash! Look down? Sky! Touch it? Ripples!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda reaches one finger toward the puddle — one last tiny ripple. The camera holds on the
warm moment as the scene fades softly.
