### Shot 01 — The Ramp Problem (0:00–0:15)

**Time:** 0:00–0:15

Arda tries to push the car up a small incline made of stacked blocks. The car rolls backward each time.

**ARDA**
Up… up… no!

The car slides back down with a soft "whoooosh."

**Time:** 0:15–0:30

**LUCA**
What are you doing?

**ARDA**

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Time:** 0:45–1:00

**LUCA**

**ARDA**
A ramp!
