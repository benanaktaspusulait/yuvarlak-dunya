# Full Screenplay — Roll, Roll, Roll

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Slow on the Flat Path (0:00–0:15)

The ball turns slowly across the flat, soft-edged path. Arda follows with exaggerated small steps,
matching its pace.

**Arda:** "Slow... slow... slow."

**SFX:** `frrr... frrr...`

### Shot 02 — Faster Down the Gentle Slope (0:15–0:30)

The path becomes a very gentle grassy slope. The ball speeds up slightly; Arda's eyes widen and his
walking rhythm becomes quicker without becoming a chase.

**Arda:** "Now it's faster!"

**SFX:** `vrr-vrr-vrr`

### Shot 03 — Around to the Right (0:30–0:45)

The ball curves around a rounded patch of flowers. It disappears behind a tall bloom for a moment.
Arda's eyes go wide — he thinks it's gone!

**Arda:** "Where did it—"

But then the ball bounces back out from the other side of the flowers, rolling right toward him.

**Arda:** "There it is!"

He leans his body gently in the same direction and points.

**Arda:** "Right!"

He stays behind the ball on the clear path.

### Shot 04 — Tink, Then Left (0:45–1:00)

The ball lightly touches a small, rounded Pompom stone.

**SFX:** `tink`

It changes direction and rolls left. Arda changes his pointing arm.

**Arda:** "Now left!"

### Shot 05 — Too Soon (1:00–1:15)

The ball begins to slow. Arda steps forward and reaches too early. A soft grassy bump lifts the ball
just beyond his hands.

**SFX:** `boing`

**Arda:** "Almost!"

### Shot 06 — Puff and Sit (1:15–1:30)

The ball floats back and softly touches Arda's tummy.

**SFX:** `puff`

Arda takes one surprised step and settles safely onto the thick grass. He looks at the ball, then
giggles.

**Arda:** "The ball bounced. I sat down!"

### Shot 07 — Try Again (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands, brushes one soft blade of grass from his trousers, and watches the moving ball instead
of rushing toward it. He claps a slow rhythm.

**Arda:** "Try, try, try again. Slow down, watch, and try again."

### Shot 08 — Watch, Wait, Catch (1:45–2:00)

Arda tracks the ball with his eyes. As it slows, he walks ahead on the clear path, kneels, and lets
the ball roll gently into both hands.

**Arda:** "I found it!"

He holds it up proudly as Friendship Meadow becomes visible beyond him.
