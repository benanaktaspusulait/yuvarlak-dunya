## Reset Rules

- Do not upload any previous generated final frame as the next shot's primary visual source.
- Build every start image fresh from the approved Arda, Pompom Ball, and Arda's Home references.
- Match the Episode Colour Master; do not colour-match recursively to the previous video frame.
- If a fresh composition cannot express its Clean Start State clearly, repair the composition;
  do not convert the shot to linked mode without updating this plan first.
- A clean editorial cut is preferred to progressive softness, distortion, colour drift, or scale
  drift.
