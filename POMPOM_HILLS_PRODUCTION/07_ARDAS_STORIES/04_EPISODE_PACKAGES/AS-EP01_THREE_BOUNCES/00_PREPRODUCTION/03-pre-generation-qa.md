# AS-EP01 — Pre-Generation QA

## Episode Gate

- [ ] Eight shots × 15 seconds = exactly 120 seconds.
- [ ] All shots are labelled `FRESH QUALITY-RESET SHOT` in the plan, shot file, and OpenArt prompt.
- [ ] Every shot has a Clean Start State, Complete Main Action, Completed End State, Stable Final
      Anchor, and Next-Shot Dependency.
- [ ] No bounce, catch, roll, walk, reach, or camera move is divided across a cut.
- [ ] Shots 05–08 begin from clean completed states, not mid-air or mid-roll states.
- [ ] The final 1–2 seconds of every shot are stable and grounded with only natural micro-motion.
- [ ] Every start image is built from canonical Arda, Pompom Ball, and Arda's Home references.
- [ ] Episode Colour Master is selected and supplied independently to every shot.
- [ ] Previous generated video frames are not used as primary image inputs.
- [ ] Object/prop state matches `02-object-prop-map.md`.
- [ ] Arda and the ball stay in the central 60% safe region for crop-friendly action.
- [ ] Dialogue wording and story intent remain unchanged.
- [ ] No burned-in text, number graphics, arrows, music, chimes, or unsafe chase behaviour.

## Reject Before Credit Spend

Reject the start still or cheap take if Arda, the ball, garden layout, gate, light identity, scale,
colour, or prop state is wrong; if the shot requires an unplanned linked frame; or if the planned
action cannot complete with a stable final 1–2-second anchor inside 15 seconds.
