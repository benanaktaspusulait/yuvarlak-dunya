# Full Screenplay — Where Did It Go?

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Searching (0:00–0:15)

Arda scans the garden slowly. He looks at the shrubs, the flowers, the stones. His eyes move
carefully from place to place. The garden is full of hiding spots — big leaves, colourful flowers,
smooth stones. The golden leaf could be anywhere. Arda takes a deep breath and begins to look
carefully.

**Arda:** "My golden leaf is hiding. Let me look…"

### Shot 02 — Behind the Flower (0:15–0:30)

Arda walks toward a big, colourful flower in the garden corner. The flower is tall with wide
petals — bright pink and yellow. Arda crouches down and peeks behind the flower stem. He looks
left and right behind it. The golden leaf is not there. Arda stands up, looking thoughtful.

**Arda:** "Behind…"

### Shot 03 — Behind? Look! (0:30–0:45)

Arda says the rhyme and looks again — this time he looks more carefully. He leans around the
flower from the other side. He checks under the lowest petals. He looks at the ground around the
stem. The leaf is definitely not behind the flower. Arda stands up, shakes his head gently.

**Arda:** "Behind? Look! Not here…"

### Shot 04 — Under the Mushroom (0:45–1:00)

Arda walks to a cluster of round, soft mushrooms growing beside the path. The mushrooms are
beige with white spots — large and rounded. Arda crouches down and peeks underneath the biggest
mushroom cap. He looks under it, around it, beside it. The space beneath is empty.

**Arda:** "Under…"

### Shot 05 — Under? Peek! (1:00–1:15)

Arda says the rhyme again. He lies on his belly on the soft grass and looks under the mushroom
from a low angle. He sees the ground clearly — no golden leaf. He lifts the mushroom cap gently
and peeks underneath. Nothing. Arda sits up, brushing grass from his t-shirt.

**Arda:** "Under? Peek! Not here either…"

### Shot 06 — Beside the Stone (1:15–1:30)

Arda walks to a smooth, round garden stone near the edge of the garden. The stone is grey with
soft edges, warm from the sunlight. Arda kneels beside it and looks on all sides — left, right,
behind, in front. The golden leaf is not beside the stone.

**Arda:** "Beside…"

### Shot 07 — Found It! Here It Is! (1:30–1:45)

Arda says the rhyme one more time, hopeful. He looks behind the stone, under it, on top of it.
He pats the ground around it. The leaf is not there. Arda sits back on his heels, a small smile
on his face — not frustrated, just thinking. The rhyme didn't work this time.

**Arda:** "Found it! Here it is! …Oh. Not here."

### Shot 08 — Slow Down (1:45–2:00)

Arda sits down on the soft grass in the middle of the garden. He looks around slowly, breathing.
He remembers — the leaf was floating toward the meadow edge when he last saw it. He closes his
eyes for a moment, thinking. Then he opens them, stands up with a calm expression, and starts
walking carefully toward the Friendship Meadow edge.

**Arda:** "Slow down. Watch. Try again."
