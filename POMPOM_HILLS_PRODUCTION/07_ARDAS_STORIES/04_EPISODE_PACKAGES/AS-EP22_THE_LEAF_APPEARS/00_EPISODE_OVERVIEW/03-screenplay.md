# Full Screenplay — The Leaf Appears

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Spotted! (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a warm autumn afternoon. The sky is pale blue, the sun
is golden, and a few leaves drift lazily in the soft breeze. Arda walks along the garden path,
barefoot on the soft grass. He stops. His eyes catch something on the ground — a single golden
autumn leaf, glowing in the sunlight. Arda crouches down, his face full of curiosity.

**Arda:** "A leaf!"

### Shot 02 — A Golden Treasure (0:15–0:30)

Arda picks up the golden leaf carefully. He holds it up to the sunlight — the golden colour glows
warm and bright. The leaf is large and soft, with gentle curves. Arda turns it slowly, admiring the
way the light shines through it. His eyes are wide with wonder. He holds it close to his face, then
holds it out again.

**Arda:** "So pretty!"

### Shot 03 — Leaf Play (0:30–0:45)

Arda stands in the garden and plays with the golden leaf. He tosses it gently into the air — it
floats down slowly, spinning softly. Arda catches it with both hands, giggling. He tosses it again,
higher this time. The leaf dances in the air, catching the light. Arda reaches up and catches it
once more. Pure, simple joy.

**Arda:** "Catch!"

### Shot 04 — The Wind Takes It! (0:45–1:00)

Arda holds the leaf up high, about to toss it again. A gust of wind sweeps through the garden —
the leaf lifts from his open hand and floats away! Arda's hand closes on empty air. His eyes go
wide. The golden leaf drifts upward, carried by the breeze, spinning gently. Arda watches it go,
surprised.

**Arda:** "Hey!"

### Shot 05 — Just Out of Reach (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches up toward the floating leaf — his fingertips stretch but the leaf drifts just beyond
his grasp. It floats to the left, Arda moves left. It floats right, Arda moves right. The leaf
seems to play with him, always one step ahead. Arda jumps gently, trying to catch it. The leaf
floats higher.

**Arda:** "Come back!"

### Shot 06 — The Leaf Dance (1:15–1:30)

The golden leaf dances across the garden — it swoops low over the grass, then rises high, then
dips behind a shrub, then floats up again. The wind carries it in gentle loops. Arda watches from
the centre of the garden, turning to follow its path. The leaf is beautiful in flight — a golden
spark against the green garden and blue sky.

**Arda:** "Where are you going?"

### Shot 07 — The Chase (1:30–1:45)

Arda runs after the leaf. It weaves between the rounded shrubs, floats over the small garden
stones, and dances past the wooden fence. Arda chases with delight — his bare feet on the soft
grass, his arms outstretched. The leaf is fast but playful, always staying just ahead. Arda
laughs as he runs.

**Arda:** "I'm coming!"

### Shot 08 — Where Did It Go? (1:45–2:00)

The leaf settles somewhere in the garden — it drops behind a cluster of shrubs and disappears.
Arda stops running, breathing softly, looking around. The garden is quiet again. He looks left,
then right. The leaf is gone. Where did it hide? Arda stands in the middle of the garden, hands
on his hips, thinking.

**Arda:** "Where did it go?"
