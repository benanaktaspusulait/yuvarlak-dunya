# Full Screenplay — First Float

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — The Boat (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, sunny morning. Green grass, rounded shrubs,
a warm wooden fence, and a small shallow stream running along the garden edge. Arda spots his
soft toy boat sitting on the grass near the stream — it is rounded, colourful, and safe for water
play. His eyes light up with excitement. He picks it up gently, holding it with both hands.

**Arda:** "My boat!"

He turns toward the stream, eager to play.

### Shot 02 — Into the Water (0:15–0:30)

Arda crouches at the edge of the shallow stream. The water is calm, clear, and warm — barely
an inch deep over smooth pebbles. He carefully lowers the soft toy boat onto the surface. His
hands release the boat slowly. The boat settles on the water, bobbing slightly. Arda watches
with wide, focused eyes.

**Arda:** "Go in…"

His hands pull away from the boat gently.

### Shot 03 — Rocking (0:30–0:45)

The soft toy boat rocks gently on the still water — rocking left, rocking right, slow and
rhythmic. Arda crouches beside the stream, mesmerised by the motion. The boat bobs in tiny
waves, its colourful sides catching the sunlight. Arda's face shows pure fascination. He
reaches one hand toward the boat, stopping just above the water.

**Arda:** "It rocks!"

He watches the boat sway back and forth, completely absorbed.

### Shot 04 — Push Too Hard! (0:45–1:00)

Arda pushes the boat with his whole hand — a big, eager push. The boat lurches forward,
wobbles violently, and tips over onto its side. Water fills the soft toy. Arda gasps, his
face dropping with surprise. The boat floats sideways, half-submerged.

**SFX:** `splash! wobble wobble`

**Arda:** "Oh!"

He stares at the tipped boat, confused and a little disappointed.

### Shot 05 — Lift It Out (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches into the water and carefully lifts the boat out. Water drains from the soft toy.
He holds it in both hands, looking at it with concern. He tilts it, watching the last drops
fall. His brow furrows — he is thinking. The boat is safe, just wet.

**Arda:** "It tipped…"

He holds the boat close, examining it with gentle care.

### Shot 06 — Try Again (1:15–1:30)

Arda lowers the boat back onto the water surface. This time, his movements are slower and more
careful. The boat settles on the water again and begins rocking gently. Arda watches it rock,
his expression now more focused and thoughtful. He does not reach for it immediately. He
observes.

**Arda:** "One more time…"

He watches the boat rock back and forth, thinking about what to do.

### Shot 07 — Gentle Push (1:30–1:45)

Arda reaches out slowly, extending one finger. He touches the back of the boat with just the
tip of his finger — a tiny, gentle push. The boat moves forward slowly, smoothly, without
wobbling. Arda's eyes widen. His finger stays on the boat for a moment, guiding it gently.

**Arda:** "Soft…"

He pushes again with one finger — another gentle push. The boat glides forward.

### Shot 08 — It Floats! (1:45–2:00)

The boat glides smoothly across the shallow stream, rocking just slightly as it moves. Arda's
face lights up with pure joy — a big, bright smile. He stands up, watching the boat travel
farther than it ever did with the hard push. The boat is stable, balanced, beautiful in motion.

**Arda:** "It floats!"

He claps his hands once with delight, watching the boat glide.
