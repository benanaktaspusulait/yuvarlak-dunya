# Full Screenplay — The Rocking Boat

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — First Float (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — The Boat (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, sunny morning. Green grass, rounded shrubs,
a warm wooden fence, and a small shallow stream running along the garden edge. Arda spots his
soft toy boat sitting on the grass near the stream — it is rounded, colourful, and safe for water
play. His eyes light up with excitement. He picks it up gently, holding it with both hands.

**Arda:** "My boat!"

He turns toward the stream, eager to play.

### Shot 02 — Into the Water (0:15–0:30)

Arda crouches at the edge of the shallow stream. The water is calm, clear, and warm — barely
an inch deep over smooth pebbles. He carefully lowers the soft toy boat onto the surface. His
hands release the boat slowly. The boat settles on the water, bobbing slightly. Arda watches
with wide, focused eyes.

**Arda:** "Go in…"

His hands pull away from the boat gently.

### Shot 03 — Rocking (0:30–0:45)

The soft toy boat rocks gently on the still water — rocking left, rocking right, slow and
rhythmic. Arda crouches beside the stream, mesmerised by the motion. The boat bobs in tiny
waves, its colourful sides catching the sunlight. Arda's face shows pure fascination. He
reaches one hand toward the boat, stopping just above the water.

**Arda:** "It rocks!"

He watches the boat sway back and forth, completely absorbed.

### Shot 04 — Push Too Hard! (0:45–1:00)

Arda pushes the boat with his whole hand — a big, eager push. The boat lurches forward,
wobbles violently, and tips over onto its side. Water fills the soft toy. Arda gasps, his
face dropping with surprise. The boat floats sideways, half-submerged.

**SFX:** `splash! wobble wobble`

**Arda:** "Oh!"

He stares at the tipped boat, confused and a little disappointed.

### Shot 05 — Lift It Out (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches into the water and carefully lifts the boat out. Water drains from the soft toy.
He holds it in both hands, looking at it with concern. He tilts it, watching the last drops
fall. His brow furrows — he is thinking. The boat is safe, just wet.

**Arda:** "It tipped…"

He holds the boat close, examining it with gentle care.

### Shot 06 — Try Again (1:15–1:30)

Arda lowers the boat back onto the water surface. This time, his movements are slower and more
careful. The boat settles on the water again and begins rocking gently. Arda watches it rock,
his expression now more focused and thoughtful. He does not reach for it immediately. He
observes.

**Arda:** "One more time…"

He watches the boat rock back and forth, thinking about what to do.

### Shot 07 — Gentle Push (1:30–1:45)

Arda reaches out slowly, extending one finger. He touches the back of the boat with just the
tip of his finger — a tiny, gentle push. The boat moves forward slowly, smoothly, without
wobbling. Arda's eyes widen. His finger stays on the boat for a moment, guiding it gently.

**Arda:** "Soft…"

He pushes again with one finger — another gentle push. The boat glides forward.

### Shot 08 — It Floats! (1:45–2:00)

The boat glides smoothly across the shallow stream, rocking just slightly as it moves. Arda's
face lights up with pure joy — a big, bright smile. He stands up, watching the boat travel
farther than it ever did with the hard push. The boat is stable, balanced, beautiful in motion.

**Arda:** "It floats!"

He claps his hands once with delight, watching the boat glide.

## Module B — Gentle Push (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Watching (2:00–2:15)

Arda crouches beside the stream, watching the boat float peacefully. The water carries it
gently along the current. The boat rocks softly, its colourful sides catching the warm
sunlight. Arda's expression is calm and understanding — he is learning something. He nods
slowly to himself.

**Arda:** "Soft push… smooth float."

He watches the boat drift, content.

### Shot 10 — Blow! (2:15–2:30)

Arda leans close to the boat, his face just above the water. He takes a breath and blows
gently — a soft, steady puff of air. The boat responds immediately, moving forward on the
water. Arda's eyes widen with amazement. He pulls back, grinning.

**Arda:** "Blow!"

The boat glides forward from his breath alone.

### Shot 11 — Blow Again (2:30–2:45)

Arda blows again — another gentle, steady puff. The boat moves farther, gliding smoothly
across the stream. Arda follows alongside the stream bank, walking carefully, blowing again
and again. Each breath sends the boat forward. He laughs softly with each movement.

**Arda:** "Blow, blow, blow!"

The boat responds to every breath, floating beautifully.

### Shot 12 — Under the Bridge (2:45–3:00)

The boat drifts toward a small stone bridge that crosses the stream. Arda follows on the
bank, watching the boat approach the arch. The boat passes under the bridge shadow and
emerges on the other side, still floating perfectly. Arda runs around the bridge to meet it.

**Arda:** "Under the bridge!"

He reaches the other side just as the boat emerges.

### Shot 13 — The Rhyme (3:00–3:15)

Arda stands beside the stream, looking at the boat and then at the camera. He recites the
rhyme with delight, using his hands to demonstrate each line. His body sways gently with
the rhythm.

**Arda:** "Push hard? Tip! Push soft? Float! Blow gentle — there it goes!"

He points at the boat with a proud smile.

### Shot 14 — Blow Under (3:15–3:30)

Arda blows on the boat one more time — a long, gentle breath. The boat glides forward under
the bridge arch again, rocking slightly in the shadow. Arda watches it pass beneath the
stone, his face full of wonder.

**Arda:** "Under!"

The boat travels smoothly under the bridge.

### Shot 15 — Emerges (3:30–3:45)

The boat emerges from under the bridge on the other side, still floating perfectly. The
sunlight catches its colourful sides as it comes back into the light. Arda runs around the
bridge, laughing, reaching the boat as it drifts gently toward the meadow path.

**Arda:** "There it is!"

He kneels beside the boat, proud and happy.

### Shot 16 — Toward the Meadow (3:45–4:00)

Arda follows the stream as it winds toward Friendship Meadow. He walks along the bank,
occasionally blowing on the boat to keep it moving. The meadow opens up ahead — soft green
grass, rounded shrubs, bright blue sky. Arda points ahead, excited to explore.

**Arda:** "To the meadow!"

The stream leads into the wider meadow water.

## Module C — Boat Parade (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca's Boat (4:00–4:15)

Luca appears on the path beside the stream, holding his own soft toy boat — a different
colour, but equally rounded and safe. He crouches down and places it in the water. The boat
rocks gently. Luca looks up at Arda and waves.

**Luca:** "Arda! I have a boat too!"

Arda runs toward Luca, excited to share the game.

### Shot 18 — Noah's Boat (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, carrying a third soft toy boat. He crouches beside the
stream and places his boat in the water. It rocks gently beside the other two. Noah grins
with delight.

**Noah:** "Three boats!"

All three friends are now together, three boats floating on the stream.

### Shot 19 — Three Boats (4:30–4:45)

The three soft toy boats float together on the stream — each a different colour, all
rocking gently side by side. The three friends crouch beside the water, watching their boats
bob in the current. The boats drift close together, almost touching.

**Arda:** "Look! Three boats!"

**Luca:** "They rock together!"

### Shot 20 — Gentle Race (4:45–5:00)

All three friends gently push their boats with one finger at the same time. The boats glide
forward, side by side, moving smoothly through the water. The friends walk alongside the
stream bank, watching their boats race gently. No one pushes hard — they all learned.

**All:** "Go!"

The three boats glide forward together.

### Shot 21 — Blow Parade (5:00–5:15)

All three friends lean down and blow on their boats at the same time. Three gentle puffs of
air send three boats gliding forward in a line — a boat parade. The boats move together,
rocking slightly, their colourful sides catching the sunlight. The friends laugh with shared
joy.

**All:** "Blow!"

Three boats glide forward in perfect formation.

### Shot 22 — Meadow Boats (5:15–5:30)

The three boats reach the wider stream in Friendship Meadow. They float gently in a row,
rocking on the calm water. The meadow stretches around them — soft green grass, rounded
shrubs, bright blue sky. The three friends stand at the stream edge, watching their boats
with pride.

**Arda:** "They all float!"

**Luca:** "Gentle push!"

**Noah:** "Gentle blow!"

### Shot 23 — Sitting Together (5:30–5:45)

The three friends sit together beside the stream in the warm afternoon light. Their three
boats float peacefully in the water in front of them, rocking gently. The moment is calm
and warm. A gentle breeze stirs the tree branches above.

**Arda:** "I love boats."

**Luca:** "Me too."

**Noah:** "Me three."

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

All three friends face the camera, sitting together beside the stream. Their three boats
float in the water before them, rocking gently. They sway gently side to side. They say the
rhyme one last time together, using their hands to demonstrate each line.

**All:** "Push hard? Tip! Push soft? Float! Blow gentle — there it goes!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda blows gently on his boat — one last glide. The camera holds on the warm moment as the
scene fades softly.
