# Full Screenplay — Let's Cross!

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — A New Idea (0:00–0:15)

**Time:** 0:00–0:15

Noah points toward the Friendship Meadow in the distance.

**NOAH**
Can we make it go all the way there?

Arda and Luca look at the meadow, then at each other, and nod.

**ARDA**
Let's build it longer!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 02 — Building Together (0:15–0:30)

**Time:** 0:15–0:30

All three friends work together. Arda places blocks. Luca steadies them. Noah brings more blocks from the pile. They work in a gentle rhythm.

**ANLATICI**
Together, they made the bridge longer and longer.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 03 — Reaching the Meadow (0:30–0:45)

**Time:** 0:30–0:45

The extended bridge now reaches the entrance of the Friendship Meadow. The friends step back and admire their work.

**ARDA**
It reaches!

**LUCA**
It's so long!

**NOAH**
Let's try it!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 04 — The Full Crossing (0:45–1:00)

**Time:** 0:45–1:00

Arda places the car at the start of the long bridge. He pushes it gently. The car rolls up the ramp, across the wide bridge, and all the way to the meadow. The friends follow alongside, watching it go.

**ARDA** (running beside the car)
Go, go, go!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 05 — Soft Arrival (1:00–1:15)

**Time:** 1:00–1:15

The car gently stops in the soft grass of the Friendship Meadow. Birds chirp softly in the background. The three friends arrive and kneel beside the car.

**ARDA** (softly)
We did it.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 06 — Warm Beat (1:15–1:30)

**Time:** 1:15–1:30

The three friends sit beside the bridge, looking at their creation. Warm sunlight falls on them. They smile quietly.

**ANLATICI**
Sometimes the best games are the ones you build together.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 07 — The Return (1:30–1:45)

**Time:** 1:30–1:45

Arda pushes the car back. It rolls down the bridge, over the ramp, and into his hands with a soft "poof."

**ARDA**
One more time?

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 08 — Loop Ending (1:45–2:00)

**Time:** 1:45–2:00

All three friends stand together. They say the rhyme one more time, swaying gently.

**ALL THREE**
Short? Make it long! Narrow? Make it wide! Up and down—the ramp!

Arda bounces the car gently in his hands. The camera holds on their warm smiles as the scene fades softly.

**Duration:** 15 seconds
