# Full Screenplay — Let's Build Together

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Luca Arrives (0:00–0:15)

Arda walks through the garden and into Friendship Meadow, passing his tower visible through the
window. Luca steps into view from behind a rounded shrub, eyes wide with excitement at seeing
the tower in the distance.

**Luca:** "Wow, Arda! You built a tower!"

**Arda:** "I did! It fell first, then I made the base wider."

### Shot 02 — Noah Joins (0:15–0:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, waving cheerfully. He looks at the tower through the garden
window, then back at Arda and Luca.

**Noah:** "Can we build together?"

**Arda:** "Yes! Let's build together."

All three children smile at each other.

### Shot 03 — Building Together (0:30–0:45)

The three friends sit in a wide, safe circle in Friendship Meadow. Soft blocks are spread
between them. Arda places a wide base while Luca and Noah hand him blocks one at a time. They
work in rhythm.

**SFX:** `thump thump thump`

**Arda:** "Base it wider!"

### Shot 04 — Two Towers (0:45–1:00)

Two towers stand side by side in the meadow — one tall built by Arda, one slightly shorter built
by all three friends together. The friends stand back and look at both towers.

**Luca:** "Two towers!"

**Noah:** "They're both strong!"

**Arda:** "Because the base is wide."

### Shot 05 — A Bridge Idea (1:00–1:15)

Arda picks up a long, soft block. He looks at the gap between the two towers, then at his
friends.

**Arda:** "What if we connect them?"

Luca and Noah look at each other and nod.

**Luca:** "Let's try!"

### Shot 06 — The Bridge Holds (1:15–1:30)

Very carefully, Arda places the long block across the tops of the two towers. It rests gently
and holds. All three children watch with bated breath, then cheer softly.

**SFX:** `thump`

**All:** "It holds!"

**Noah:** "The wide bases keep them strong!"

### Shot 07 — Standing Together (1:30–1:45)

The three friends stand together behind their construction — two towers connected by a bridge.
They sway gently, admiring what they built. The warm afternoon light falls on the pastel blocks.

**Arda:** "We built it together."

**Luca:** "Together is best."

### Shot 08 — One More Time? (1:45–2:00)

All three friends face the camera. They sway gently side to side and say the rhyme one last
time together. Arda holds a soft block in his hands.

**All:** "Taller? Wider first! Stronger? Base it wider!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda places the block gently on the ground, and the camera holds on their warm smiles as the
scene fades softly.
