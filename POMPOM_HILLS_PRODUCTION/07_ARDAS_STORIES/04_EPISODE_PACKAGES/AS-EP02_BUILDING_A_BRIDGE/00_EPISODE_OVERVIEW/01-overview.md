## Logline

When Arda's toy car cannot cross a gap, he builds a bridge with blocks—first too short, then too narrow, then needing a ramp—and discovers that slowing down to think leads to a solution he can share with friends.

---

## Story Promise

This episode extends *Arda's Stories* into construction play. Arda begins with a simple goal (get the car across), meets three escalating physical problems (short, narrow, no ramp), tries again with a calmer method each time, and carries what he learned into a shared building game with Luca and Noah.

## Repeated Rhyme

```text

Narrow? Make it wide!

```

---

## Learning Outcome

Children see that construction has understandable physical rules, that a first failed attempt is safe, and that slowing down to observe can reveal a better solution. The final game models turn-taking, sharing materials, and enjoying a shared building activity without competition.
