# Screenplay — Building a Bridge

---

## MODULE A — THE GAP (0:00–2:00)

---

### Shot 01 — The Car Rolls

**Time:** 0:00–0:15

A small, rounded toy car rolls across the soft floor. Arda's hands guide it gently. The car moves from left to right, approaching a small gap between two colourful cushions.

**ARDA** (whispering, excited)
Vroom!

The car rolls happily, then stops at the edge of the gap.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 02 — The Gap Problem

**Time:** 0:15–0:30

Arda pushes the car forward. It tips over the edge and lands softly in the gap with a gentle "plop." Arda's eyes go wide.

**ARDA**
Oh no!

He looks down at the car, then looks up at the camera with a curious expression.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 03 — Looking for a Solution

**Time:** 0:30–0:45

Arda looks around the room. His eyes land on a pile of colourful wooden blocks near the wall. His face lights up.

**ARDA**
Blocks!

He toddles over to the blocks and picks one up, examining it with delight.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 04 — Too Short

**Time:** 0:45–1:00

Arda places three blocks across the gap, one by one. Each makes a soft "tık" sound as it lands. He steps back proudly.

**ARDA**
Ready!

He pushes the car toward the bridge. The car rolls onto the first block, then stops—the bridge doesn't reach the other side. The car hangs over the edge.

**ANLATICI**
Did the bridge reach?

**ARDA, LUCA & NOAH** (together, hands on hips)
Noooo! Too short!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 05 — Adding More Blocks

**Time:** 1:00–1:15

Arda adds two more blocks, making the bridge longer. He counts as he places them.

**ARDA**
One… two!

He pushes the car again. This time it reaches the other side—but the wheels slip off the edges. The car wobbles.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 06 — Too Narrow

**Time:** 1:15–1:30

The car tilts to one side and falls off the narrow bridge. Arda catches it just in time.

**ARDA** (holding the car)
It's too narrow!

He looks at the single line of blocks and scratches his head.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 07 — The Rhyme

**Time:** 1:30–1:45

Arda sits back on the grass and says the repeated rhyme, looking at the blocks thoughtfully.

**ARDA**
Short? Make it long! Narrow? Make it wide! Up and down—the ramp!

He stands up with renewed determination.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 08 — Widening the Bridge

**Time:** 1:45–2:00

Arda places blocks side by side, making the bridge wider. Two blocks wide, then three. He pats the bridge gently.

**ARDA**
Wide enough!

He pushes the car across. This time the car rolls smoothly from one side to the other. Arda claps.

**ARDA**
I did it!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

## MODULE B — TOO NARROW (2:00–4:00)

---

### Shot 09 — The Ramp Problem

**Time:** 2:00–2:15

Arda tries to push the car up a small incline made of stacked blocks. The car rolls backward each time.

**ARDA**
Up… up… no!

The car slides back down with a soft "whoooosh."

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 10 — Luca Arrives

**Time:** 2:15–2:30

Luca appears from behind a tree, watching Arda try again. He tilts his head curiously.

**LUCA**
What are you doing?

**ARDA**
My car can't go up!

Luca walks over and looks at the incline.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 11 — Trying Harder

**Time:** 2:30–2:45

Arda pushes the car with more force. It bounces off the incline and rolls back into his lap.

**ARDA** (laughing)
Oops!

He looks at the car, then at the blocks, thinking.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 12 — The Ramp Piece

**Time:** 2:45–3:00

Luca spots a curved wooden ramp piece in the toy box. He picks it up and brings it to Arda.

**LUCA**
Maybe this?

Arda's eyes light up. He takes the ramp piece and examines it.

**ARDA**
A ramp!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 13 — Ramp Success

**Time:** 3:00–3:15

They place the ramp at the start of the bridge. Arda pushes the car up the ramp—it goes up smoothly, crosses the bridge, and comes down the other side.

**ARDA & LUCA** (together)
It worked!

The car rolls gently to a stop.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 14 — Noah Arrives

**Time:** 3:15–3:30

Noah runs in from the right, curious about the excitement.

**NOAH**
What's happening?

**ARDA**
We built a bridge! Watch!

Arda pushes the car across again. Noah watches with wide eyes.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 15 — Turn-Taking

**Time:** 3:30–3:45

Arda pushes the car across. Then Luca. Then Noah. Each one says "My turn!" and pushes the car gently over the bridge.

**ANLATICI**
One push each. Taking turns.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 16 — Cheering

**Time:** 3:45–4:00

Each time the car crosses successfully, all three friends cheer softly. Arda pumps his fist. Luca claps. Noah hops.

**ARDA**
Again, again!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

## MODULE C — LET'S CROSS! (4:00–6:00)

---

### Shot 17 — A New Idea

**Time:** 4:00–4:15

Noah points toward the Friendship Meadow in the distance.

**NOAH**
Can we make it go all the way there?

Arda and Luca look at the meadow, then at each other, and nod.

**ARDA**
Let's build it longer!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 18 — Building Together

**Time:** 4:15–4:30

All three friends work together. Arda places blocks. Luca steadies them. Noah brings more blocks from the pile. They work in a gentle rhythm.

**ANLATICI**
Together, they made the bridge longer and longer.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 19 — Reaching the Meadow

**Time:** 4:30–4:45

The extended bridge now reaches the entrance of the Friendship Meadow. The friends step back and admire their work.

**ARDA**
It reaches!

**LUCA**
It's so long!

**NOAH**
Let's try it!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 20 — The Full Crossing

**Time:** 4:45–5:00

Arda places the car at the start of the long bridge. He pushes it gently. The car rolls up the ramp, across the wide bridge, and all the way to the meadow. The friends follow alongside, watching it go.

**ARDA** (running beside the car)
Go, go, go!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 21 — Soft Arrival

**Time:** 5:00–5:15

The car gently stops in the soft grass of the Friendship Meadow. Birds chirp softly in the background. The three friends arrive and kneel beside the car.

**ARDA** (softly)
We did it.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 22 — Warm Beat

**Time:** 5:15–5:30

The three friends sit beside the bridge, looking at their creation. Warm sunlight falls on them. They smile quietly.

**ANLATICI**
Sometimes the best games are the ones you build together.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 23 — The Return

**Time:** 5:30–5:45

Arda pushes the car back. It rolls down the bridge, over the ramp, and into his hands with a soft "poof."

**ARDA**
One more time?

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 24 — Loop Ending

**Time:** 5:45–6:00

All three friends stand together. They say the rhyme one more time, swaying gently.

**ALL THREE**
Short? Make it long! Narrow? Make it wide! Up and down—the ramp!

Arda bounces the car gently in his hands. The camera holds on their warm smiles as the scene fades softly.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

*End of screenplay.*
