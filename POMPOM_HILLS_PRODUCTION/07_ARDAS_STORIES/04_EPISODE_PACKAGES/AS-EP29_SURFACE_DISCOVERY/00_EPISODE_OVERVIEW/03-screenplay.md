# Full Screenplay — Surface Discovery

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Moss Patch (0:00–0:15)

Arda notices a soft green moss patch growing at the base of the tree. He kneels beside it and
touches the moss gently with one finger, feeling its soft, bumpy texture. His expression is
curious.

**Arda:** "Moss! So soft and bumpy."

### Shot 02 — Slow on Moss (0:15–0:30)

Arda places the acorn on the moss and gives it a gentle push. The acorn barely moves — it rolls
very slowly, almost rocking in place. Arda's eyes widen with surprise and delight.

**SFX:** `frrr………`

**Arda:** "So slow on moss! Even slower than grass!"

### Shot 03 — Sandy Patch (0:30–0:45)

Arda walks a few steps and finds a small sandy patch beside the path — fine, soft sand. He kneels
and runs his fingers through it, feeling the texture. He looks at the acorn in his other hand.

**Arda:** "Sand! Let's try sand."

### Shot 04 — Medium on Sand (0:45–1:00)

Arda places the acorn on the sand and pushes it. The acorn rolls — faster than on moss, but
slower than on the smooth path. Arda watches it roll and nods slowly, comparing.

**SFX:** `frrr-frrr`

**Arda:** "Not so fast. Not so slow. Sand is in the middle!"

### Shot 05 — Leaf Patch (1:00–1:15)

Arda walks to where dry fallen leaves gather in a small soft pile near the tree. He crouches and
touches the leaves — they crackle softly under his fingers. He looks at the acorn with a
speculative smile.

**Arda:** "Leaves! What happens on leaves?"

### Shot 06 — Bumpy on Leaves (1:15–1:30)

Arda places the acorn on the leaf pile and pushes gently. The acorn bounces softly from leaf to
leaf, rolling unevenly — sometimes fast, sometimes slow, wobbling. Arda giggles at the funny
movement.

**SFX:** `tink-tink-tink`

**Arda:** "It bounces! The leaves make it wobble!"

### Shot 07 — Three Surfaces (1:30–1:45)

Arda arranges the moss, a small pile of sand, and a leaf patch in a row on the ground. He
places the acorn at the start of each one and pushes it gently along each surface, watching
carefully. His expression is focused and scientific.

**Arda:** "Moss — slow. Sand — middle. Leaves — wobbly."

### Shot 08 — Rhyme Again (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands up, holds the acorn proudly in both hands, and repeats the rhyme with a bigger
smile. He turns toward the meadow path in the background, his eyes shining with excitement to share.

**Arda:** "Smooth? Fast! Bumpy? Slow! Different surface, different roll!"

He calls out toward the meadow, "Come see!" even though no one is there yet, then begins walking toward Friendship Meadow, the acorn cupped safely in his hands, building anticipation for his friends.
