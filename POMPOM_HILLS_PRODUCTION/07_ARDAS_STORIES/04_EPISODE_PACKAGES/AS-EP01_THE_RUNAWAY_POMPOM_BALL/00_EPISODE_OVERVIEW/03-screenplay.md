# Full Screenplay — The Runaway Pompom Ball

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — Three Bounces (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — A Ball-Sized Surprise (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on the canonical Pompom Ball in extreme close-up. It rises with a soft `poof`,
revealing Arda's delighted face behind it. He catches it securely in both hands and smiles.

**Arda:** "Got it!"

### Shot 02 — Three Times (0:15–0:30)

The camera settles into a child-eye-level medium-wide view of Arda's garden. Arda holds the ball,
shows three with a simple hand gesture, then returns both hands to the ball.

**Arda:** "Three bounces. Count with me! Ready?"

A short listening pause invites the viewer to answer.

**Arda:** "I'm ready!"

### Shot 03 — One (0:30–0:45)

Arda gently pushes the ball down. It compresses very slightly, rises slowly, and returns to his
hands.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "One!"

He hugs the ball for a tiny celebratory beat.

### Shot 04 — Two, to the Right (0:45–1:00)

Arda bounces again. The ball drifts slightly to his right. He takes one safe side-step and catches
it with both hands.

**SFX:** `poof`

**Arda:** "Two! It went right."

He does a little victory wiggle, proud of his catch.

### Shot 05 — A Stronger Push (1:00–1:15)

Arda lifts the ball with excited but controlled body language.

**Arda:** "Now three!"

He pushes a little more strongly. The ball compresses softly and rises higher than before.

**SFX:** `boing`

**Arda:** "Three!"

### Shot 06 — Over the Hands (1:15–1:30)

Arda reaches up, but the floaty ball passes just over his hands. On its way down, it gently
`boops` his nose with a soft bounce. Arda crosses his eyes trying to look at it, then giggles.

**SFX:** `boop`

**Arda:** "Hey! You booped me!"

The ball lands on the grass, returns to shape, and begins rolling toward the open garden play gate.

**Arda:** "Oh!"

A brief visual replay is not used; the cause and effect remains clear in one continuous action.

### Shot 07 — Let's Go (1:30–1:45)

Arda watches the ball roll, looks at his empty hands, then smiles with determined curiosity.

**Arda:** "The ball got away. Let's go!"

He follows at a safe walking pace.

### Shot 08 — Through the Play Gate (1:45–2:00)

The ball rolls through the rounded garden play gate onto a connected, vehicle-free path. Arda
reaches the gate and points after it.

**Arda:** "Wait for me, ball!"

The ball continues with a quiet `frrr-frrr`.

## Module B — Roll, Roll, Roll (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Slow on the Flat Path (2:00–2:15)

The ball turns slowly across the flat, soft-edged path. Arda follows with exaggerated small steps,
matching its pace.

**Arda:** "Slow... slow... slow."

**SFX:** `frrr... frrr...`

### Shot 10 — Faster Down the Gentle Slope (2:15–2:30)

The path becomes a very gentle grassy slope. The ball speeds up slightly; Arda's eyes widen and his
walking rhythm becomes quicker without becoming a chase.

**Arda:** "Now it's faster!"

**SFX:** `vrr-vrr-vrr`

### Shot 11 — Around to the Right (2:30–2:45)

The ball curves around a rounded patch of flowers. It disappears behind a tall bloom for a moment.
Arda's eyes go wide — he thinks it's gone!

**Arda:** "Where did it—"

But then the ball bounces back out from the other side of the flowers, rolling right toward him.

**Arda:** "There it is!"

He leans his body gently in the same direction and points.

**Arda:** "Right!"

He stays behind the ball on the clear path.

### Shot 12 — Tink, Then Left (2:45–3:00)

The ball lightly touches a small, rounded Pompom stone.

**SFX:** `tink`

It changes direction and rolls left. Arda changes his pointing arm.

**Arda:** "Now left!"

### Shot 13 — Too Soon (3:00–3:15)

The ball begins to slow. Arda steps forward and reaches too early. A soft grassy bump lifts the ball
just beyond his hands.

**SFX:** `boing`

**Arda:** "Almost!"

### Shot 14 — Puff and Sit (3:15–3:30)

The ball floats back and softly touches Arda's tummy.

**SFX:** `puff`

Arda takes one surprised step and settles safely onto the thick grass. He looks at the ball, then
giggles.

**Arda:** "The ball bounced. I sat down!"

### Shot 15 — Try Again (3:30–3:45)

Arda stands, brushes one soft blade of grass from his trousers, and watches the moving ball instead
of rushing toward it. He claps a slow rhythm.

**Arda:** "Try, try, try again. Slow down, watch, and try again."

### Shot 16 — Watch, Wait, Catch (3:45–4:00)

Arda tracks the ball with his eyes. As it slows, he walks ahead on the clear path, kneels, and lets
the ball roll gently into both hands.

**Arda:** "I found it!"

He holds it up proudly as Friendship Meadow becomes visible beyond him.

## Module C — Your Turn (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — A Sound in the Meadow (4:00–4:15)

Arda enters Friendship Meadow's Group Game Space holding the ball. He hears a playful, gentle
`poof` from behind a rounded shrub and turns. No one is there. He hears a quiet giggle from the
other side and turns again.

**Arda:** "Who's there?"

### Shot 18 — Adventure Friends (4:15–4:30)

Luca and Noah step into view from the clear side of the shrub. Luca points toward the open game
space; Noah gives a cheerful wave.

**Luca:** "Adventure friends!"

**Noah:** "Let's make it fun!"

### Shot 19 — Shall We Play? (4:30–4:45)

Arda places the ball on the grass between them. All three children can see one another clearly.

**Arda:** "Shall we play together?"

Luca and Noah nod.

**Luca and Noah:** "Yes!"

### Shot 20 — Arda, Luca, Noah (4:45–5:00)

Arda rolls the ball softly to Luca. Luca waits until it reaches him, stops it with both hands, then
rolls it to Noah.

**Arda:** "Luca's turn."

**Luca:** "Noah's turn."

### Shot 21 — To Arda's Foot (5:00–5:15)

Noah studies the ball with a playful smile.

**Noah:** "I have an idea!"

He sends a gentle curved roll toward Arda. The ball arrives beside Arda's shoe and softly taps it.

**Arda:** "It found my foot!"

All three laugh gently.

### Shot 22 — One, Two, Three (5:15–5:30)

The friends form a wide, safe triangle. Each child completes one slow roll.

**Arda:** "One!"

**Luca:** "Two!"

**Noah:** "Three!"

The ball remains within the central safe region throughout.

### Shot 23 — Right, Left, Centre (5:30–5:45)

They repeat the passing game. Arda rolls right to Luca; Luca rolls left to Noah; Noah rolls through
the open centre back to Arda.

**Arda:** "Right!"

**Luca:** "Left!"

**Noah:** "Centre!"

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

Arda holds the ball with both hands. He takes a deep breath, then does a special trick bounce — he
bounces the ball once, claps his hands, and catches it on the way back down. Luca and Noah's eyes
go wide, then they clap excitedly.

**SFX:** `poof` `clap-clap`

**Luca:** "Wow!"

**Noah:** "Do it again!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda rolls the ball softly toward the camera until its fluff fills the frame, matching the opening.
