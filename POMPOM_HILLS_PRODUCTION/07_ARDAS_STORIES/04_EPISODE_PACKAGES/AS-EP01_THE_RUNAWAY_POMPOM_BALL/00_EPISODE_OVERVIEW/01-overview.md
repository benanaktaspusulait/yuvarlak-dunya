## Logline

When Arda's soft Pompom Ball bounces out of his garden, he follows it through a playful trail,
learns that watching and waiting works better than rushing, and turns the chase into a friendly
turn-taking game with Luca and Noah.

---

## Story Promise

This episode introduces the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful movement,
meets a small physical problem, tries again with a calmer method, and carries what he learned into
play with friends.

## Repeated Rhyme

```text
Try, try, try again.
Slow down, watch, and try again.
```

---

## Learning Outcome

Children see that movement has understandable causes, that an unsuccessful first try is safe, and
that slowing down to observe can reveal a better solution. The final game models waiting, passing,
and enjoying a shared activity without competition.
