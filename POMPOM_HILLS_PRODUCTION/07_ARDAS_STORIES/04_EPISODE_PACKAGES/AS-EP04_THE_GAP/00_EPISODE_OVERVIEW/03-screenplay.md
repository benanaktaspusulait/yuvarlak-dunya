# Full Screenplay — The Gap

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — The Car Rolls (0:00–0:15)

**Time:** 0:00–0:15

A small, rounded toy car rolls across the soft floor. Arda's hands guide it gently. The car moves from left to right, approaching a small gap between two colourful cushions.

**ARDA** (whispering, excited)
Vroom!

The car rolls happily, then stops at the edge of the gap.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 02 — The Gap Problem (0:15–0:30)

**Time:** 0:15–0:30

Arda pushes the car forward. It tips over the edge and lands softly in the gap with a gentle "plop." Arda's eyes go wide.

**ARDA**
Oh no!

He looks down at the car, then looks up at the camera with a curious expression.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 03 — Looking for a Solution (0:30–0:45)

**Time:** 0:30–0:45

Arda looks around the room. His eyes land on a pile of colourful wooden blocks near the wall. His face lights up.

**ARDA**
Blocks!

He toddles over to the blocks and picks one up, examining it with delight.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 04 — Too Short (0:45–1:00)

**Time:** 0:45–1:00

Arda places three blocks across the gap, one by one. Each makes a soft "tık" sound as it lands. He steps back proudly.

**ARDA**
Ready!

He pushes the car toward the bridge. The car rolls onto the first block, then stops—the bridge doesn't reach the other side. The car hangs over the edge.

**ANLATICI**
Did the bridge reach?

**ARDA, LUCA & NOAH** (together, hands on hips)
Noooo! Too short!

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 05 — Adding More Blocks (1:00–1:15)

**Time:** 1:00–1:15

Arda adds two more blocks, making the bridge longer. He counts as he places them.

**ARDA**
One… two!

He pushes the car again. This time it reaches the other side—but the wheels slip off the edges. The car wobbles.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 06 — Too Narrow (1:15–1:30)

**Time:** 1:15–1:30

The car tilts to one side and falls off the narrow bridge. Arda catches it just in time.

**ARDA** (holding the car)
It's too narrow!

He looks at the single line of blocks and scratches his head.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 07 — The Rhyme (1:30–1:45)

**Time:** 1:30–1:45

Arda sits back on the grass and says the repeated rhyme, looking at the blocks thoughtfully.

**ARDA**
Short? Make it long! Narrow? Make it wide! Up and down—the ramp!

He stands up with renewed determination.

**Duration:** 15 seconds

---

### Shot 08 — Widening the Bridge (1:45–2:00)

**Time:** 1:45–2:00

Arda places blocks side by side, making the bridge wider. Two blocks wide, then three. He pats the bridge gently.

**ARDA**
Wide enough!

He pushes the car across. This time the car rolls smoothly from one side to the other. Arda claps.

**ARDA**
I did it!

**Duration:** 15 seconds
