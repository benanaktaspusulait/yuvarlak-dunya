# Full Screenplay — Rolling Race

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Meadow Path (0:00–0:15)

Arda enters the Friendship Meadow path — a wide, inviting path with grass on both sides. He
holds the acorn and looks around happily. In the distance, he spots Luca and Noah walking toward
him.

**Arda:** "Luca! Noah! Come see what I found!"

### Shot 02 — Show and Tell (0:15–0:30)

Luca and Noah arrive. Arda holds out the acorn for them to see. Luca leans in close; Noah's
eyes go wide.

**Luca:** "What is it?"

**Arda:** "A round acorn! Watch — it rolls fast on smooth ground."

He places it on the path and pushes it. The acorn rolls quickly.

**Noah:** "Whoa! Fast!"

### Shot 03 — Choose a Surface (0:30–0:45)

Arda points to different surfaces nearby — the smooth path, a grass patch, a mossy area, a
sandy spot. Luca finds a smooth flat stone. Noah picks up a dry leaf.

**Arda:** "Pick a surface! Let's roll together!"

**Luca:** "I'll try the path with my stone!"

**Noah:** "I'll try the leaves with my leaf!"

### Shot 04 — Ready, Set, Roll! (0:45–1:00)

The three friends line up at the same starting point. Arda has his acorn on the smooth path.
Luca places his stone on the path beside it. Noah puts his leaf on a leaf patch nearby. They
look at each other excitedly.

**Arda:** "Ready?"

**Luca and Noah:** "Ready!"

**Arda:** "Roll!"

All three push their objects at the same time.

### Shot 05 — Speed Comparison (1:00–1:15)

Arda's acorn rolls fastest on the smooth path. Luca's stone rolls a bit slower beside it.
Noah's leaf floats and tumbles slowly on the leaf patch. Arda's eyes track all three, comparing.

**Arda:** "My acorn is the fastest! The stone is slower! The leaf is the slowest!"

**Noah:** "The surface makes it different!"

### Shot 06 — Swap and Try (1:15–1:30)

The friends laugh and decide to swap surfaces. Arda rolls his acorn on the grass. Luca rolls
his stone on the sand. Noah places his leaf on the smooth path.

**Arda:** "Let's swap! Try a new surface!"

**Luca:** "Good idea!"

They each push their objects on the new surfaces.

### Shot 07 — Same Pattern (1:30–1:45)

Arda's acorn slows on the grass. Luca's stone rolls medium on the sand. Noah's leaf glides
fast on the smooth path. All three friends look at each other with recognition.

**Arda:** "Smooth? Fast! Bumpy? Slow!"

**Luca and Noah:** "Different surface, different roll!"

They clap together, delighted by the shared discovery.

### Shot 08 — Warm Ending (1:45–2:00)

After the rolling race, Arda, Luca, and Noah sit together on the soft grass, each holding their object — Arda with his acorn, Luca with his stone, Noah with his leaf. They smile at each other, feeling happy and connected.

**Arda:** "Same acorn, different roll!"

They all smile warmly, and the camera zooms in on their happy faces, ending the episode on a note of friendship and shared discovery.
