### Shot 01 — A Block-Sized Plan (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's hands lifting a large, soft, pastel-coloured block above his head with
delighted energy. The block is rounded, colourful, and clearly child-safe. Arda's face beams
behind it.

**Arda:** "I'm going to build a tower!"

He lowers the block and looks at the soft floor where he will build.

### Shot 02 — One (0:15–0:30)

The camera settles into a child-eye-level medium view of Arda's indoor play area. Arda kneels
and places the first block gently on the soft floor. It lands with a soft `thump`.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "One!"

He pats the top of the block and smiles.

### Shot 03 — Two (0:30–0:45)

Arda picks up a second block and carefully places it on top of the first. The tower is now two
blocks tall. It stands still.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Two! It's getting taller!"

He looks up at the tower with growing excitement.

### Shot 04 — Three (0:45–1:00)

Arda lifts a third block with both hands. He places it on top of the second block. The tower

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Three!"

He takes a small step back to look at his tower. It sways gently.

### Shot 05 — Four (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches for a fourth block. He lifts it high and places it on top. The tower wobbles more
noticeably this time. Arda's eyes widen.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Four! It's... wobbly."

He steadies the tower with his hands for a moment, then pulls them away carefully.

### Shot 06 — Tumble (1:15–1:30)

The tower wobbles once more, then gently tumbles. The soft blocks fall with a series of soft
`thump thump thump` sounds and scatter across the floor. No block hits Arda.

**SFX:** `thump thump thump`

**Arda:** "Oh!"

He watches the blocks settle around him.

### Shot 07 — Sitting Back (1:30–1:45)

Arda sits back on the soft floor, legs crossed, looking at the scattered blocks. His expression
is thoughtful, not frustrated. He tilts his head.

**Arda:** "It fell down."

A brief, calm pause lets the moment breathe.

### Shot 08 — Try Again (1:45–2:00)

Arda looks at the blocks, then looks at the camera with a small determined smile.

**Arda:** "Let's try again!"

He stands up and moves toward the blocks with renewed energy.
