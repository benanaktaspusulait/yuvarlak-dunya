# Full Screenplay — The Kite That Would Not Fly

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — No Wind (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Holding the Kite (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, still afternoon. The sky is pale blue, the
sun is warm, and everything is perfectly calm — not a leaf moves. Arda stands in the middle of
the garden holding a soft, colourful triangle kite with pastel rainbow panels (pink, yellow,
blue, mint green) and a fluffy yarn tail. His eyes are bright with excitement.

**Arda:** "My kite!"

He holds it up, the tail dangling straight down — no wind at all.

### Shot 02 — First Run! (0:15–0:30)

Arda grips the kite string and runs across the garden, holding the kite up with his other hand.
He runs fast, his bare feet on the soft grass. The kite lifts for a split second — then flops
gently to the ground behind him. Arda stops, turns around, and looks at the kite lying on the
grass. He blinks.

**Arda:** "Flop!"

He walks back to the kite, puzzled.

### Shot 03 — Running Faster! (0:30–0:45)

Arda picks up the kite and tries again. This time he runs even faster across the garden, holding
the kite high. The kite lifts a little more — it rises to his shoulder height — then it wobbles
and flops down again. Arda stops and watches it fall. He scrunches his nose.

**Arda:** "Again!"

He picks up the kite with determination.

### Shot 04 — The Small Hill (0:45–1:00)

Arda climbs onto a small grassy mound in the garden. He stands on top, holds the kite up, and
runs down the slope. The kite lifts — it rises above his head for a moment — then the air goes
still and it drops softly to the ground. Arda stops at the bottom of the mound, looking at the
kite.

**Arda:** "Almost…"

He picks up the kite and looks at it thoughtfully.

### Shot 05 — Sitting with the Kite (1:00–1:15)

Arda sits on the soft grass beside his kite, which lies flat on the ground. The fluffy tail is
still. Arda looks at the kite, then looks up at the sky. The sky is clear and calm. Not a cloud
drifts. Not a leaf moves. Arda's expression is quiet — not angry, not sad, just thinking.

**Arda:** "Why won't you fly?"

He gently touches the kite's pastel surface.

### Shot 06 — Still Garden (1:15–1:30)

Arda looks around the garden. Everything is perfectly motionless — the leaves on the shrubs hang
limp, the flowers stand straight, the grass does not sway. The world is holding its breath. Arda
holds his hand up, feeling for wind. Nothing. The garden is completely still.

**Arda:** "No wind…"

He looks up at the pale blue sky with understanding.

### Shot 07 — Waving the Kite (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands up and holds the kite out in front of him. He waves it up and down, trying to make
it fly by moving it himself. The kite swings like a pendulum — it does not lift. The tail dangles
and sways with each wave, but there is no upward force. Arda waves harder. Nothing.

**Arda:** "Fly!"

The kite just hangs from his hands, limp and patient.

### Shot 08 — Looking Up (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands in the garden, holding the kite at his side, looking up at the still sky. His face is
thoughtful. He looks at the sky, then at the kite, then at the sky again. A small, determined
nod. He knows what he needs to do — find the wind. He starts walking toward the path that leads
out of the garden.

**Arda:** "Let's go find wind."

---

## Module B — Waiting for Wind (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Walking Up (2:00–2:15)

Arda walks along a gentle path that leads up to a higher hilltop. He carries the kite in one hand,
the string trailing behind him. The path winds through soft grass and small wildflowers. The sky
above is open and wide. Arda walks with purpose, looking up at the breeze-less sky as he climbs.

**Arda:** "Up, up, up."

### Shot 10 — The Twitching Leaf (2:15–2:30)

Arda reaches the hilltop. He stands still, looking around. On the ground near his feet, a dry
leaf lies on the grass. Arda crouches down to look at it. The leaf twitches — just barely — a
tiny nudge, as if something invisible touched it. Arda's eyes widen. He watches the leaf closely.

**Arda:** "A leaf…"

### Shot 11 — Feeling the Breeze (2:30–2:45)

Arda stands up on the hilltop. A gentle breeze reaches him — he feels it on his face. His hair
moves softly. His t-shirt ripples slightly. Arda closes his eyes and smiles, feeling the air
brush against his cheeks. He holds his hand out, palm up, feeling the invisible touch of the wind.

**Arda:** "I feel it…"

### Shot 12 — The Leaf Flies (2:45–3:00)

The breeze strengthens. The leaf on the ground lifts off — it floats upward, drifting gently in
the air, turning slowly as it rises. Arda watches it go, his mouth open in wonder. The leaf
dances across the hilltop, carried by the wind. More leaves join — two, three — all floating
gently upward. Arda's eyes follow them into the sky.

**Arda:** "It's flying!"

### Shot 13 — Wind Arrives (3:00–3:15)

The wind picks up across the hilltop. The grass sways in soft waves. The wildflowers nod their
heads. Arda's kite tail flutters — the first real movement. Arda looks at his kite with growing
excitement. The wind rustles the shrubs, and the whole hilltop comes alive with gentle motion.

**Arda:** "Wind!"

### Shot 14 — Kite Held High (3:15–3:30)

Arda stands on the hilltop, holds the kite up high with both hands. The wind catches the kite's
pastel panels — the triangle shape fills with air, the tail streams behind it. Arda's face is
full of anticipation. He feels the kite pulling upward in his hands, tugging gently against his
fingers.

**Arda:** "Ready…"

### Shot 15 — It Flies! (3:30–3:45)

Arda lets go. The kite lifts from his hands — it rises, rising higher and higher into the bright
sky. The pastel colours glow in the sunlight, the fluffy tail dances behind it. Arda's face
lights up with pure joy — his eyes wide, his mouth open in a delighted laugh. The kite soars
above him, steady and free.

**Arda:** "It flies! It flies!"

### Shot 16 — Running with the Kite (3:45–4:00)

Arda runs down the hill toward the meadow, holding the soft string. The kite soars above him,
dancing in the wind, its pastel panels catching the sunlight. Arda laughs as he runs, looking up
at his kite. The string is taut in his hands. He runs through the grass, joyful and free, the
kite trailing above like a colourful flag.

**Arda:** "Wheee!"

---

## Module C — Flying Together (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

In Friendship Meadow, Luca appears from the path, holding a soft green kite with a white yarn
tail. He sees Arda running with his kite and waves excitedly. Luca holds his kite up and runs a
few steps — his kite lifts immediately in the steady wind. Luca laughs with delight.

**Luca:** "Arda! Look! My kite!"

Arda runs toward Luca, both kites dancing in the sky above them.

### Shot 18 — Noah Runs In (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side of the meadow, holding a soft red kite with a yellow tail. He
runs in with a big grin, holding his kite up. The wind catches it instantly — it lifts and soars.
Noah laughs, his face full of joy. All three friends are now in the meadow, each with a kite.

**Noah:** "My kite flies too!"

**Arda:** "Three kites!"

### Shot 19 — Three Kites Ready (4:30–4:45)

The three friends stand together in a line in Friendship Meadow. Arda holds his pastel rainbow
kite, Luca holds his green kite, and Noah holds his red kite. The wind blows gently around them.
They look at each other and smile, ready to launch together. The meadow is wide and open, perfect
for kite flying.

**Arda:** "On three?"

### Shot 20 — Triple Launch! (4:45–5:00)

Arda counts down. On three, all three friends run forward and release their kites at the same
time. Three colourful kites — pastel rainbow, green, and red — rise into the sky together. The
fluffy tails dance in the wind. The friends look up, laughing with delight as their kites soar
side by side.

**All:** "Up, up, up!"

### Shot 21 — Running Together (5:00–5:15)

The three friends take turns running across the meadow, each pulling their kite higher. They
laugh and chase each other, the three kites dancing and dipping above them. Sometimes two kites
cross paths and the friends giggle. The string hums gently in the wind. Pure, shared joy.

**Luca:** "Mine is higher!"

**Noah:** "No, mine!"

**Arda:** "Look at all three!"

### Shot 22 — Sitting and Watching (5:15–5:30)

The three friends sit together in the soft grass of Friendship Meadow, holding their kite strings
loosely. They look up at their three kites dancing in the bright sky. The kites sway gently in the
wind — the pastel rainbow, the green, and the red — three colourful shapes against the blue sky.
The moment is peaceful and warm.

**Arda:** "They all fly."

**Luca:** "Together."

### Shot 23 — Three Kites in the Sky (5:30–5:45)

The camera looks up at the sky. Three kites dance high above the meadow — Arda's pastel rainbow
kite in the centre, Luca's green kite on the left, Noah's red kite on the right. Their fluffy
tails stream behind them. The sky is bright blue with a few soft white clouds. The kites dip and
rise together, carried by the gentle wind. A perfect, peaceful moment.

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

The three friends face the camera, sitting together in the meadow, their kite strings resting in
their laps. The three kites are visible high above them in the sky. They sway gently side to side.
They say the rhyme one last time together, pointing up at the kites with each line.

**All:** "Run fast? Flop! Wait soft? Whoosh! Look up — there it goes!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

The camera holds on the warm moment — three friends, three kites, one bright sky. The scene fades
softly.
