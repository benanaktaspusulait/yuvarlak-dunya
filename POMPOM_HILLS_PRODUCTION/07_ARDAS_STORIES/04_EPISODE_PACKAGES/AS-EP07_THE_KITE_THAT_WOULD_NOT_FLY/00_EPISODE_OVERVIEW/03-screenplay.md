# Full Screenplay — The Kite That Would Not Fly

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — No Wind (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Holding the Kite (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, still afternoon. The sky is pale blue, the
sun is warm, and everything is perfectly calm — not a leaf moves. Arda stands in the middle of
the garden holding a soft, colourful triangle kite with pastel rainbow panels (pink, yellow,
blue, mint green) and a fluffy yarn tail. His eyes are bright with excitement.

**Arda:** "My kite!"

He holds it up, the tail dangling straight down — no wind at all.

### Shot 02 — First Run! (0:15–0:30)

Arda grips the kite string and runs across the garden, holding the kite up with his other hand.
He runs fast, his bare feet on the soft grass. The kite lifts for a split second — then flops
gently to the ground behind him. Arda stops, turns around, and looks at the kite lying on the
grass. He blinks.

**Arda:** "Flop!"

He walks back to the kite, puzzled.

### Shot 03 — The Head Wrap (0:30–0:45)

Arda picks up the kite and tries again. This time he runs even faster across the garden, holding
the kite high. The kite lifts — it rises above his head — then a sudden gust (from his own
running!) wraps the kite string around his head like a hat! Arda stops, tangled in the string,
the kite resting on top of his head like a floppy hat. He giggles and untangles himself.

**Arda:** "Silly kite!"

He pulls the kite off his head, laughing.

### Shot 04 — The Flower Landing (0:45–1:00)

Arda tries a new strategy — he stands on a small grassy mound, holds the kite high above his
head, and THROWS it up with all his might! The kite flies upward for a moment — then nosedives
straight into a big, soft daisy flower! The kite lands perfectly balanced on the flower, its pastel
panels draped over the petals like a blanket. Arda runs over, impressed.

**Arda:** "Oh! The flower caught it!"

He gently lifts the kite off the daisy, patting the flower apologizing.

### Shot 05 — The Ground Drag (1:00–1:15)

Arda tries one more time — he runs along the garden path, dragging the kite behind him on the
ground. The kite skids along the grass, making a soft swishing sound, picking up leaves and grass
clippings. Arda runs faster — the kite bounces and tumbles behind him, collecting a small trail
of debris. He stops, out of breath, and looks back at his messy kite.

**Arda:** "Oops!"

He shakes the leaves off the kite, brushing it clean with his hands.

### Shot 06 — The Bounce Try (1:15–1:30)

Arda sits on a small garden step, holding the kite in his lap. He thinks, then gets an idea! He
bounces the kite up and down on his knee like a ball — boing, boing, boing! The kite bounces a
little each time but never actually flies. Arda bounces faster — the kite wobbles and flops off
his knee onto the grass. He picks it up, still bouncing it gently in his hands.

**Arda:** "Bounce, bounce... no fly!"

He looks at the kite thoughtfully.

### Shot 07 — The Spin Try (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands up and holds the kite out to his side. He starts spinning in a circle, holding the
kite out like a dancer! The kite swings around him, its tail streaming out. Arda spins faster
and faster — the kite lifts a little from the centrifugal force! But when he stops spinning,
the kite wobbles and drops. Arda gets dizzy and stumbles, laughing at himself.

**Arda:** "Wheee... whoa!"

He sits down on the grass, dizzy but smiling.

### Shot 08 — Looking Up (1:45–2:00)

Arda sits in the garden, holding the kite at his side, looking up at the still sky. His face is
thoughtful. He looks at the sky, then at the kite, then at the sky again. A small, determined
nod. He knows what he needs to do — find the wind. He starts walking toward the path that leads
out of the garden.

**Arda:** "Let's go find wind."

## Module B — Observing and Experimenting (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Walking Up (2:00–2:15)

Arda walks along a gentle path that leads up to a higher hilltop. He carries the kite in one hand,
the string trailing behind him. The path winds through soft grass and small wildflowers. The sky
above is open and wide. Arda walks with purpose, looking up at the breeze-less sky as he climbs.

**Arda:** "Up, up, up."

### Shot 10 — The Twitching Leaf (2:15–2:30)

Arda reaches the hilltop. He stands still, looking around. On the ground near his feet, a dry
leaf lies on the grass. Arda crouches down to look at it. The leaf twitches — just barely — a
tiny nudge, as if something invisible touched it. Arda's eyes widen. He watches the leaf closely,
leaning in close, his nose almost touching it.

**Arda:** "A leaf..."

### Shot 11 — Leaf Watching (2:30–2:45)

Arda lies flat on his belly on the grass, watching the leaf like a scientist! The leaf twitches
again — then lifts slightly off the ground! Arda's eyes follow it, his head turning as the leaf
drifts a few inches. He reaches out to touch it, but the leaf skitters away. He army-crawls
after it, giggling.

**Arda:** "Come back, leaf!"

The leaf dances just out of his reach.

### Shot 12 — Kite as a Bird (2:45–3:00)

Arda stands up, holding the kite in front of him. He flaps the kite up and down like a bird's
wings — flap, flap, flap! The kite's pastel panels catch the air and flutter. Arda flaps faster,
running in a circle, making the kite "fly" in his hands. He chirps like a bird — "Cheep cheep!"
The kite flutters but doesn't lift on its own.

**Arda:** "Fly, bird kite! Fly!"

He flaps harder, laughing at his own game.

### Shot 13 — The Spin Experiment (3:00–3:15)

Arda holds the kite above his head and starts spinning in a circle, like a carousel! The kite
lifts slightly as he spins — the tail streams out behind it. Arda spins faster and faster, his
feet barely keeping up. The kite starts to rise! Just as it's about to lift away — Arda gets
dizzy and plops down on the grass, the kite landing softly in his lap.

**Arda:** "Almost... whoa!"

He lies on the grass, dizzy but grinning.

### Shot 14 — Feeling the Breeze (3:15–3:30)

Arda sits up on the hilltop. A gentle breeze reaches him — he feels it on his face. His hair
moves softly. His t-shirt ripples slightly. Arda closes his eyes and smiles, feeling the air
brush against his cheeks. He holds his hand out, palm up, feeling the invisible touch of the wind.
The breeze strengthens — it ruffles the kite's tail in his hands!

**Arda:** "I feel it..."

### Shot 15 — The Wind Arrives! (3:30–3:45)

The wind picks up across the hilltop. The grass sways in soft waves. The wildflowers nod their
heads. Arda's kite tail flutters — the first real movement! Arda looks at his kite with growing
excitement. The wind rustles the shrubs, and the whole hilltop comes alive with gentle motion.
Arda stands up, holding the kite high — it fills with air, the panels puffing out!

**Arda:** "Wind! Wind!"

### Shot 16 — It Flies! (3:45–4:00)

Arda lets go. The kite lifts from his hands — it rises, rising higher and higher into the bright
sky. The pastel colours glow in the sunlight, the fluffy tail dances behind it. Arda's face
lights up with pure joy — his eyes wide, his mouth open in a delighted laugh. The kite soars
above him, steady and free. Arda runs down the hill toward the meadow, the kite trailing above
like a colourful flag.

**Arda:** "It flies! It flies!"

## Module C — Flying Together (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

In Friendship Meadow, Luca appears from the path, holding a soft green kite with a white yarn
tail. He sees Arda running with his kite and waves excitedly. Luca holds his kite up and runs a
few steps — his kite lifts immediately in the steady wind. Luca laughs with delight.

**Luca:** "Arda! Look! My kite!"

Arda runs toward Luca, both kites dancing in the sky above them.

### Shot 18 — Noah Runs In (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side of the meadow, holding a soft red kite with a yellow tail. He
runs in with a big grin, holding his kite up. The wind catches it instantly — it lifts and soars.
Noah laughs, his face full of joy. All three friends are now in the meadow, each with a kite.

**Noah:** "My kite flies too!"

**Arda:** "Three kites!"

### Shot 19 — The Kite Race Challenge (4:30–4:45)

The three friends stand together in Friendship Meadow. Arda points to the sky, then points to
his kite, then to the others. He makes a "who's higher?" gesture, looking between Luca and Noah.
Luca grins and nods. Noah bounces on his feet, excited. The challenge is on — which kite can
fly the highest?

**Arda:** "Highest kite wins!"

**Luca:** "Ready!"

**Noah:** "Go!"

### Shot 20 — Running to Launch (4:45–5:00)

All three friends run forward together across the meadow, each holding their kite high. Their
feet pound the soft grass, their kites bounce and flutter behind them. The wind is strong and
steady — all three kites catch the air at the same moment! They let go simultaneously, and three
colourful kites — pastel rainbow, green, and red — rise into the sky together!

**All:** "Up, up, up!"

### Shot 21 — The Race Is On! (5:00–5:15)

The three kites climb higher into the sky. Arda's pastel rainbow kite takes the lead — it rises
above the others! Luca's green kite follows close behind. Noah's red kite wobbles, then catches
a thermal and zooms upward! The friends run across the meadow, pulling their strings, cheering.
Luca points — his kite passes Noah's! Noah jumps — his kite passes Luca's! It's neck and neck!

**Luca:** "Mine is higher!"

**Noah:** "No, mine!"

**Arda:** "Look at all three!"

### Shot 22 — Dancing Together (5:15–5:30)

The three kites reach the same height — they dance together in the sky, their tails streaming
in the wind. The friends stop running and look up, amazed. The kites sway gently side to side,
as if they're friends too, dancing together in the breeze. The pastel rainbow, the green, and
the red — three colourful shapes against the blue sky. The race is over — they're all winners.

**Arda:** "They all fly together!"

**Luca:** "Same high!"

### Shot 23 — Three Kites in the Sky (5:30–5:45)

The camera looks up at the sky. Three kites dance high above the meadow — Arda's pastel rainbow
kite in the centre, Luca's green kite on the left, Noah's red kite on the right. Their fluffy
tails stream behind them. The sky is bright blue with a few soft white clouds. The kites dip and
rise together, carried by the gentle wind. A perfect, peaceful moment.

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

The three friends face the camera, sitting together in the meadow, their kite strings resting in
their laps. The three kites are visible high above them in the sky. They sway gently side to side.
They say the rhyme one last time together, pointing up at the kites with each line.

**All:** "Run fast? Flop! Wait soft? Whoosh! Look up — there it goes!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

The camera holds on the warm moment — three friends, three kites, one bright sky. The scene fades
softly.
