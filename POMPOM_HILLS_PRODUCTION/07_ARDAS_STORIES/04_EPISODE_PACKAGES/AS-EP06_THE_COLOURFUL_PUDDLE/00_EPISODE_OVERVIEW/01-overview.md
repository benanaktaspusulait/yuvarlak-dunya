## Logline

After rain, Arda discovers a colourful puddle in his garden. He jumps in — splash! — and notices
the puddle reflects the sky and trees above. He tries to touch the reflection, but ripples distort
the image. Arda slows down, observes, and learns that puddles show what's above them. Luca and
Noah arrive, and together they splash and discover the puddle reflects three happy faces.

---

## Story Promise

This episode extends the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful physical play
in a puddle, meets a small puzzle — his reflection disappears when he touches the water — tries again
with a calmer method, discovers the simple truth that puddles mirror the world above, and carries
that discovery into shared splash play with Luca and Noah.

## Learning Outcome

Children see that puddles reflect the world above them — sky, trees, light. When Arda tries to touch
his reflection, ripples distort the image. An unsuccessful first try is okay. Observing before trying
again reveals a simple, wonderful truth: puddles show what's above them. The final splash game models
cooperation, shared discovery, and joyful group play after rain.
