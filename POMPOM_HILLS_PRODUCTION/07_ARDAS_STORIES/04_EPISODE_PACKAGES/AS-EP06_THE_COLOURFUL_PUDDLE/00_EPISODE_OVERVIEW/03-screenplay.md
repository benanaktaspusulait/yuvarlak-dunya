# Full Screenplay — The Colourful Puddle

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — First Splash (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — After the Rain (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden after rain. The grass is wet and glistening, small
puddles dot the ground, and the sky is clearing — soft blue with fluffy white clouds. Arda
stands at the garden door in his orange t-shirt and blue shorts, looking out at the shiny,
fresh garden. Droplets hang from the rounded shrubs.

**Arda:** "Oooh… rain!"

He steps forward onto the wet grass, eyes wide with wonder.

### Shot 02 — Wet Garden (0:15–0:30)

Arda walks through the garden, his bare feet making soft sounds on the wet grass. He looks
down at the ground — small puddles reflect the sky like little mirrors. He hops from one
small puddle to another, testing the water with his toes. Each puddle shimmers with light.

**Arda:** "So many puddles!"

He looks around the garden with bright, curious eyes.

### Shot 03 — The Colourful Puddle (0:30–0:45)

Arda's eyes land on one large puddle near the garden path. It is the biggest puddle he has
ever seen — perfectly still, its surface reflecting the blue sky, white clouds, green trees,
and warm golden sunlight. The puddle shimmers with all the colours above it. Arda stands
very still, mesmerised.

**Arda:** "A colourful puddle!"

He takes a step closer, his reflection appearing in the water.

### Shot 04 — Splash! (0:45–1:00)

Arda jumps into the puddle with both feet — SPLASH! Water sprays up around his legs in a
joyful burst. Arda laughs with delight, his face lighting up with pure happiness. He jumps
again — SPLASH! The water ripples outward from his feet.

**SFX:** `splash! splash!`

**Arda:** "Splash!"

He giggles, wobbling in the shallow water.

### Shot 05 — Look Down! (1:00–1:15)

Arda stops jumping and looks down into the puddle. The water has calmed, and the reflection
is clear again — he sees the blue sky, white clouds, and green tree branches reflected
perfectly in the water. His own face appears among the clouds. Arda gasps softly.

**Arda:** "Look down… sky!"

He points at the puddle with wonder.

### Shot 06 — Hello, Me! (1:15–1:30)

Arda waves at his reflection in the puddle. The reflected Arda waves back. Arda laughs
delighted — he waves again, bigger this time. The reflection waves in perfect sync. He
tilts his head; the reflection tilts too. He grins; the reflection grins.

**Arda:** "Hi!"

He waves one more time, fascinated by his watery twin.

### Shot 07 — Can I Touch It? (1:30–1:45)

Arda kneels beside the puddle, leaning forward. He reaches one finger toward the surface,
hesitating just above the water. His reflection reaches back from below. Arda's eyes are
wide with curiosity. He moves his finger closer — closer —

**Arda:** "Touch it?"

His fingertip hovers just above the water surface.

### Shot 08 — Ripples! (1:45–2:00)

Arda's finger touches the water — a gentle ripple spreads outward from the point of contact.
The reflection wobbles, distorts, breaks apart into shimmering fragments. The sky and trees
in the puddle scramble and swirl. Arda pulls his hand back quickly, watching the ripples
spread and the reflection scatter.

**SFX:** `bloop`

**Arda:** "Ripples!"

He watches, fascinated and a little surprised.

---

## Module B — Reflection Game (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Coming Back (2:00–2:15)

The ripples from Arda's touch slowly fade. The water calms, bit by bit. The distorted
reflection begins to reform — first the blue sky returns, then the green tree branches,
then the white clouds. Arda kneels beside the puddle, watching the image repair itself.
His own face gradually reappears in the water.

**Arda:** "It's coming back…"

He watches with patient, fascinated eyes.

### Shot 10 — Still Water (2:15–2:30)

The puddle is perfectly still again. The reflection is crystal clear — sky, trees, clouds,
Arda's smiling face. Arda waves at the reflection one more time. The reflected Arda waves
back, crisp and undistorted. Arda nods, understanding something.

**Arda:** "Still water. Clear picture."

He waves slowly, watching the perfect mirror image.

### Shot 11 — Gentle Touch (2:30–2:45)

Arda reaches down again, this time more carefully. He touches the water surface with just
one fingertip — a small, single ripple appears, spreading outward in a perfect circle.
Arda watches the ripple travel across the puddle, distorting the reflection in its path.

**SFX:** `bloop`

**Arda:** "One finger…"

He observes the single ripple's effect.

### Shot 12 — Small Ripple (2:45–3:00)

The small ripple from Arda's gentle touch reaches the far edge of the puddle and fades.
The reflection settles back — the image is slightly wobbly, then still. Arda nods to
himself. A small touch makes a small ripple. A small ripple makes a small distortion.

**Arda:** "Small ripple…"

He watches the last wobble fade away.

### Shot 13 — Watching (3:00–3:15)

Arda sits beside the puddle in the warm sunlight, perfectly still. The puddle is calm and
the reflection is clear — the whole sky above is mirrored in the water. Arda watches the
reflection for a long, peaceful moment. A leaf drifts down from a tree and lands gently on
the far edge of the puddle, creating a tiny ripple.

**Arda:** "The sky is in the puddle."

He smiles quietly, at peace with the discovery.

### Shot 14 — The Rhyme (3:15–3:30)

Arda stands up and looks at the puddle, then at the camera. He recites the rhyme with
delight, pointing at the puddle for each line. His body sways gently with the rhythm.

**Arda:** "Jump in? Splash! Look down? Sky! Touch it? Ripples!"

He claps his hands once with satisfaction.

### Shot 15 — The Whole Sky (3:30–3:45)

Arda looks down into the puddle one more time. The reflection shows the entire sky — blue
above, clouds drifting slowly, tree branches swaying. Arda's face is reflected among it
all. He gasps softly with amazement, his eyes wide and sparkling.

**Arda:** "The whole sky!"

He crouches down, nose almost touching the water, gazing at the infinite sky in the puddle.

### Shot 16 — Puddle Walk (3:45–4:00)

Arda walks along the garden path toward the meadow, splashing gently through small puddles
along the way. Each puddle he passes reflects a piece of the sky. He looks down at each one
as he walks, pointing and smiling.

**Arda:** "Puddle… puddle… puddle!"

The path opens toward Friendship Meadow ahead.

## Module C — Splash Friends (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Splashes! (4:00–4:15)

Luca appears on the path ahead, jumping into a puddle with both feet — SPLASH! He is wearing
his green t-shirt and beige shorts, and he laughs with delight as water sprays up around him.
He sees Arda approaching and waves excitedly.

**Luca:** "Arda! Puddles! Come splash!"

Arda runs toward Luca, excited to share the game.

### Shot 18 — Noah Jumps In! (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, wearing his red t-shirt and dark shorts. He spots a puddle
and jumps in with a big, enthusiastic splash — SPLASH! Water sprays everywhere. Noah laughs,
his face pure joy. All three friends are now on the path, surrounded by puddles.

**Noah:** "Big splash!"

**Arda:** "You found the puddles too!"

### Shot 19 — Three Faces (4:30–4:45)

The three friends gather around one large, still puddle near the meadow edge. They all look
down together. The puddle reflects the blue sky, and three small faces appear in the water —
Arda, Luca, and Noah, reflected among the clouds. All three gasp softly.

**Arda:** "Look! Three faces!"

**Luca:** "That's me!"

**Noah:** "And me!"

### Shot 20 — Triple Wave (4:45–5:00)

All three friends wave at the puddle at the same time. Three reflections wave back in perfect
sync. They laugh with delight, waving faster, watching their watery twins mimic every move.
The puddle shimmers with the movement of six waving hands.

**All:** "Hi!"

They wave in different directions — up, down, side to side — watching the reflections follow.

### Shot 21 — Big Splash! (5:00–5:15)

Arda counts down with his fingers. On three, all three friends jump into the puddle at the
same time — BIG SPLASH! Water sprays up in a joyful burst around all of them. Ripples
overlap, the reflection shatters completely, and all three friends laugh with pure joy.

**SFX:** `BIG SPLASH!`

**All:** "Splash!"

They stand in the puddle, giggling, water dripping from their shorts.

### Shot 22 — Back Again (5:15–5:30)

The ripples from the triple splash slowly settle. The water calms, and the reflection
returns — first the sky, then the trees, then three smiling faces looking down. The three
friends watch their reflections reform, their grins reflected back at them.

**Arda:** "It comes back!"

**Luca:** "Three of us!"

They look at each other and then back at the puddle, amazed.

### Shot 23 — Sitting Together (5:30–5:45)

The three friends sit together beside the large puddle in the warm afternoon light. They
look down at the sky reflected in the water, their three faces among the clouds and trees.
The moment is peaceful and warm. A gentle breeze stirs the tree branches reflected above.

**Arda:** "I love puddles."

**Luca:** "Me too."

**Noah:** "Me three."

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

All three friends face the camera, sitting together beside the puddle. The reflected sky
glitters behind them in the water. They sway gently side to side. They say the rhyme one
last time together, pointing at the puddle with each line.

**All:** "Jump in? Splash! Look down? Sky! Touch it? Ripples!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda reaches one finger toward the puddle — one last tiny ripple. The camera holds on the
warm moment as the scene fades softly.
