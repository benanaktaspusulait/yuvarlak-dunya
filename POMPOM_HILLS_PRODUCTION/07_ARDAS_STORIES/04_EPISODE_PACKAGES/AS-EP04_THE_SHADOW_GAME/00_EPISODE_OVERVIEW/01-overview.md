## Logline

When Arda discovers his shadow on the sunny ground, he tries to step on it, chase it, and catch
it — only to learn that shadows move when you move, and that they are always with you. Friends
arrive, and three shadows dance together.

---

## Story Promise

This episode extends the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful play, meets
a physical puzzle — a shadow that moves when he does — tries again with a calmer method, discovers
the simple truth that shadows mirror your movement, and carries that discovery into shared play
with Luca and Noah.

## Repeated Rhyme

```text
Step on it? It moves!

```

---

## Learning Outcome

Children see that shadows are natural and safe, that cause and effect connect their body
movement to the shadow's movement, and that an unsuccessful first try is okay. Observing before
trying again reveals a simple, wonderful truth: shadows always come with you. The final game
models cooperation, shared wonder, and joyful group play.
