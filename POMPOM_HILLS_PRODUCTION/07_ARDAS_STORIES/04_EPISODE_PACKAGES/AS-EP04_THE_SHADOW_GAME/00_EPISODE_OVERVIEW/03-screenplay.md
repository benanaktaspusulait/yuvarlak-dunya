# Full Screenplay — The Shadow Game

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — First Shadow (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Sunny Start (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, sunny day. Warm golden sunlight fills the
space. Arda runs into the garden with joyful energy, his feet soft on the grass. He stops in
the middle of the sunny patch, breathing happily.

**Arda:** "What a sunny day!"

He looks around the garden with delight, arms slightly open, feeling the warmth.

### Shot 02 — There It Is (0:15–0:30)

Arda stops running and looks down. A clear, soft-edged shadow stretches on the ground beneath
him, mirroring his standing shape. His eyes go wide with curiosity. He tilts his head to one
side, studying the dark shape.

**Arda:** "What's that?"

He leans forward, looking closely at his own shadow on the grass.

### Shot 03 — Step On It (0:30–0:45)

Arda lifts his right foot and carefully tries to step on the shadow. The moment his foot moves,
the shadow shifts too — it stays just ahead of his foot. Arda's foot lands on empty grass.

**Arda:** "Hey!"

He looks at his foot, then back at the shadow, which now sits a tiny bit further away.

### Shot 04 — The Chase (0:45–1:00)

Arda takes quick, playful steps toward the shadow, trying to trap it under his feet. The
shadow moves with every step he takes, always staying just out of reach. Arda's energy is
playful and determined, never frustrated.

**Arda:** "Come back!"

He laughs as the shadow slides away from each step.

### Shot 05 — Running (1:00–1:15)

Arda runs toward the shadow at full speed. The shadow runs too — always the same distance
ahead. Arda runs faster; the shadow keeps pace. He stops, panting happily, hands on his knees.

**Arda:** "It runs with me!"

He looks at the shadow, which has stopped too, matching his position.

### Shot 06 — Plop (1:15–1:30)

Arda plops down onto the grass with a soft thump, legs spread out. The shadow sits right
underneath him now, perfectly matching his seated shape. Arda looks down at the shadow between
his legs and giggles.

**SFX:** `puff`

**Arda:** "Ha! It's under me!"

### Shot 07 — Still There (1:30–1:45)

Arda looks at his hands, turning them over. The shadow on the ground shows two dark hands
moving exactly the same way. He wiggles his fingers; the shadow wiggles too. His expression
shifts from playful to thoughtful.

**Arda:** "It moves when I move."

A calm beat lets the observation land.

### Shot 08 — Growing Tall (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands up very slowly from the ground. As he rises, his shadow stretches and grows on the
grass beneath him — from a small seated shape to a full standing shape. His eyes follow the
shadow's growth upward. A look of quiet wonder crosses his face.

**Arda:** "It grows with me!"

He stands still, looking at the shadow at his feet, then at the sunny sky above.

## Module B — Shadow Dance (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — A Wave (2:00–2:15)

Arda raises his right hand slowly and waves. The shadow on the ground raises its arm and waves
too, perfectly synchronized. Arda's face lights up with delight. He waves again, watching the
shadow copy every movement.

**Arda:** "It waves back!"

### Shot 10 — Two Hands (2:15–2:30)

Arda waves with both hands, swaying his body slightly. The shadow mimics every gesture — two
arms waving, body swaying. Arda giggles and claps his hands. The shadow claps too.

**SFX:** `puff puff`

**Arda:** "It's dancing with me!"

### Shot 11 — Running Together (2:30–2:45)

Arda runs forward along the garden path. His shadow stretches behind him on the ground, running
with him in perfect sync. The camera follows Arda at child eye level as he moves with joyful
energy.

**Arda:** "Let's go!"

### Shot 12 — Stopping (2:45–3:00)

Arda stops suddenly on the path. His shadow stops too, lying still on the ground beside him.
Arda looks down at the motionless shadow, then takes one step. The shadow moves one step. He
takes another; the shadow moves again.

**Arda:** "Stop... go... stop... go!"

He giggles at the pattern.

### Shot 13 — Spin! (3:00–3:15)

Arda spins in a circle on the grass. His shadow spins with him, a dark shape rotating on the
ground. Arda spins faster, laughing. The shadow keeps up, always matching his movement.

**Arda:** "Round and round!"

### Shot 14 — Jump! (3:15–3:30)

Arda jumps up from the grass with both feet. As he leaves the ground, his shadow briefly
compresses and then stretches long as he lands. Arda jumps again, watching the shadow change
shape with each jump.

**Arda:** "Up! And down!"

### Shot 15 — The Rhyme (3:30–3:45)

Arda stands on the garden path, catching his breath happily. He sways gently and recites the
rhyme with delight, looking at his shadow and then at the camera.

**Arda:** "Step on it? It moves! Catch it? It waits! Stop and look — there it is!"

He points down at his shadow with a grin.

### Shot 16 — Walking Together (3:45–4:00)

Arda walks along the garden path toward Friendship Meadow. His shadow stretches beside him on
the ground, walking in perfect step. Arda glances down at it warmly, as if walking with a
companion. The path opens toward the meadow ahead.

**Arda:** "You're always with me."

## Module C — Our Shadows (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

Arda walks along the path toward Friendship Meadow. Luca steps into view from behind a rounded
shrub, eyes wide. Luca points down at Arda's shadow on the ground.

**Luca:** "Arda, look! Your shadow!"

**Arda:** "I know! It dances with me!"

### Shot 18 — Noah Joins (4:15–4:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, waving. He looks down and sees his own shadow on the sunny
ground. His eyes go wide with wonder. All three children now look at the ground, seeing three
distinct shadows.

**Noah:** "I have one too!"

**Arda:** "We all have shadows!"

### Shot 19 — Three Waves (4:30–4:45)

The three friends stand in a row on the sunny path. They all raise their hands and wave. Three
shadows on the ground wave in perfect unison. The friends look at each other and laugh with
delight.

**SFX:** `puff puff puff`

**Luca:** "They all wave!"

### Shot 20 — Walking in a Line (4:45–5:00)

The three friends walk in a line along the path — Arda leading, Luca in the middle, Noah at the
back. Three shadows walk on the ground beside them in the same formation. They walk in rhythm,
feet moving together.

**Noah:** "Left, right, left, right!"

### Shot 21 — Into the Meadow (5:00–5:15)

The path opens into Friendship Meadow. The friends walk onto the soft green grass. Their
shadows stretch long and wide on the meadow ground in the warm afternoon sun. The friends stop
in the middle of the meadow, looking around at the wide open space.

**Arda:** "Our shadows came to the meadow too!"

### Shot 22 — Silly Walk (5:15–5:30)

The three friends do an exaggerated silly walk across the meadow — high knees, wobbly arms,
silly steps. Their three shadows copy every silly movement on the ground. The friends laugh
together as their shadows mirror the silliness.

**SFX:** `puff puff puff`

**All:** "Ha ha ha!"

### Shot 23 — Standing Together (5:30–5:45)

The three friends stand in a row in Friendship Meadow, catching their breath. Their three
shadows lie side by side on the grass in front of them. The warm afternoon light is golden and
soft. The friends look at each other and smile.

**Arda:** "Our shadows are always with us."

**Luca:** "Always."

### Shot 24 — One More Time? (5:45–6:00)

All three friends face the camera, standing together in Friendship Meadow. They sway gently
side to side. Their shadows stretch behind them on the grass. They say the rhyme one last time
together.

**All:** "Step on it? It moves! Catch it? It waits! Stop and look — there it is!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda waves at his shadow on the ground. The shadow waves back. The camera holds on the warm
moment as the scene fades softly.
