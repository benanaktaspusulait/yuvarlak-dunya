## Logline

Arda finds a beautiful golden leaf in his garden. He plays with it, but a gust of wind lifts it away!
He chases the leaf through the garden — it hides behind a flower, under a mushroom, beside a stone.
Arda learns to slow down and look carefully. When Luca and Noah arrive to help, they find the leaf
together — and the hiding game becomes a shared adventure.

---

## Story Promise

This episode extends the central promise of *Arda's Stories*: Arda begins with joyful physical play
— playing with a golden leaf — meets a small, safe problem — the wind takes it — tries to chase it
with energy but it hides. He slows down, observes carefully, and discovers that looking closely is
the key. When friends help, the private game becomes a shared game, and the joy multiplies.

## Learning Outcome

Children see that sometimes things hide and you need to look carefully to find them. Arda's golden
leaf blows away and hides behind flowers, under mushrooms, and beside stones. Instead of getting
frustrated, he slows down and observes. When friends arrive to help, the search becomes a fun
game. The episode models careful observation, patience, and the joy of shared discovery — finding
things together is more fun than finding them alone.
