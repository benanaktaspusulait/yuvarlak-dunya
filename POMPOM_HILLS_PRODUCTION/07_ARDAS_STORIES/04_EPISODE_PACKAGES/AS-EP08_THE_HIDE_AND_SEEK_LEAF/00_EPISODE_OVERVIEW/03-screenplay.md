# Full Screenplay — The Hide and Seek Leaf

> Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. Actions stay gentle and readable. Dialogue is intentionally
> sparse so the episode can travel well across platforms and languages.

---

## Module A — The Leaf Appears (0:00–2:00)

### Shot 01 — Spotted! (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a warm autumn afternoon. The sky is pale blue, the sun
is golden, and a few leaves drift lazily in the soft breeze. Arda walks along the garden path,
barefoot on the soft grass. He stops. His eyes catch something on the ground — a single golden
autumn leaf, glowing in the sunlight. Arda crouches down, his face full of curiosity.

**Arda:** "A leaf!"

### Shot 02 — A Golden Treasure (0:15–0:30)

Arda picks up the golden leaf carefully. He holds it up to the sunlight — the golden colour glows
warm and bright. The leaf is large and soft, with gentle curves. Arda turns it slowly, admiring the
way the light shines through it. His eyes are wide with wonder. He holds it close to his face, then
holds it out again.

**Arda:** "So pretty!"

### Shot 03 — Leaf Play (0:30–0:45)

Arda stands in the garden and plays with the golden leaf. He tosses it gently into the air — it
floats down slowly, spinning softly. Arda catches it with both hands, giggling. He tosses it again,
higher this time. The leaf dances in the air, catching the light. Arda reaches up and catches it
once more. Pure, simple joy.

**Arda:** "Catch!"

### Shot 04 — The Wind Takes It! (0:45–1:00)

Arda holds the leaf up high, about to toss it again. A gust of wind sweeps through the garden —
the leaf lifts from his open hand and floats away! Arda's hand closes on empty air. His eyes go
wide. The golden leaf drifts upward, carried by the breeze, spinning gently. Arda watches it go,
surprised.

**Arda:** "Hey!"

### Shot 05 — Just Out of Reach (1:00–1:15)

Arda reaches up toward the floating leaf — his fingertips stretch but the leaf drifts just beyond
his grasp. It floats to the left, Arda moves left. It floats right, Arda moves right. The leaf
seems to play with him, always one step ahead. Arda jumps gently, trying to catch it. The leaf
floats higher.

**Arda:** "Come back!"

### Shot 06 — The Leaf Dance (1:15–1:30)

The golden leaf dances across the garden — it swoops low over the grass, then rises high, then
dips behind a shrub, then floats up again. The wind carries it in gentle loops. Arda watches from
the centre of the garden, turning to follow its path. The leaf is beautiful in flight — a golden
spark against the green garden and blue sky.

**Arda:** "Where are you going?"

### Shot 07 — The Chase (1:30–1:45)

Arda runs after the leaf. It weaves between the rounded shrubs, floats over the small garden
stones, and dances past the wooden fence. Arda chases with delight — his bare feet on the soft
grass, his arms outstretched. The leaf is fast but playful, always staying just ahead. Arda
laughs as he runs.

**Arda:** "I'm coming!"

### Shot 08 — Where Did It Go? (1:45–2:00)

The leaf settles somewhere in the garden — it drops behind a cluster of shrubs and disappears.
Arda stops running, breathing softly, looking around. The garden is quiet again. He looks left,
then right. The leaf is gone. Where did it hide? Arda stands in the middle of the garden, hands
on his hips, thinking.

**Arda:** "Where did it go?"

---

## Module B — Where Did It Go? (2:00–4:00)

### Shot 09 — Searching (2:00–2:15)

Arda scans the garden slowly. He looks at the shrubs, the flowers, the stones. His eyes move
carefully from place to place. The garden is full of hiding spots — big leaves, colourful flowers,
smooth stones. The golden leaf could be anywhere. Arda takes a deep breath and begins to look
carefully.

**Arda:** "Let me look…"

### Shot 10 — Behind the Flower (2:15–2:30)

Arda walks toward a big, colourful flower in the garden corner. The flower is tall with wide
petals — bright pink and yellow. Arda crouches down and peeks behind the flower stem. He looks
left and right behind it. The golden leaf is not there. Arda stands up, looking thoughtful.

**Arda:** "Behind…"

### Shot 11 — Behind? Look! (2:30–2:45)

Arda says the rhyme and looks again — this time he looks more carefully. He leans around the
flower from the other side. He checks under the lowest petals. He looks at the ground around the
stem. The leaf is definitely not behind the flower. Arda stands up, shakes his head gently.

**Arda:** "Behind? Look! Not here…"

### Shot 12 — Under the Mushroom (2:45–3:00)

Arda walks to a cluster of round, soft mushrooms growing beside the path. The mushrooms are
beige with white spots — large and rounded. Arda crouches down and peeks underneath the biggest
mushroom cap. He looks under it, around it, beside it. The space beneath is empty.

**Arda:** "Under…"

### Shot 13 — Under? Peek! (3:00–3:15)

Arda says the rhyme again. He lies on his belly on the soft grass and looks under the mushroom
from a low angle. He sees the ground clearly — no golden leaf. He lifts the mushroom cap gently
and peeks underneath. Nothing. Arda sits up, brushing grass from his t-shirt.

**Arda:** "Under? Peek! Not here either…"

### Shot 14 — Beside the Stone (3:15–3:30)

Arda walks to a smooth, round garden stone near the edge of the garden. The stone is grey with
soft edges, warm from the sunlight. Arda kneels beside it and looks on all sides — left, right,
behind, in front. The golden leaf is not beside the stone.

**Arda:** "Beside…"

### Shot 15 — Found It! Here It Is! (3:30–3:45)

Arda says the rhyme one more time, hopeful. He looks behind the stone, under it, on top of it.
He pats the ground around it. The leaf is not there. Arda sits back on his heels, a small smile
on his face — not frustrated, just thinking. The rhyme didn't work this time.

**Arda:** "Found it! Here it is! …Oh. Not here."

### Shot 16 — Slow Down (3:45–4:00)

Arda sits down on the soft grass in the middle of the garden. He looks around slowly, breathing.
He remembers — the leaf was floating toward the meadow edge when he last saw it. He closes his
eyes for a moment, thinking. Then he opens them, stands up with a calm expression, and starts
walking carefully toward the Friendship Meadow edge.

**Arda:** "Slow down. Watch. Try again."

---

## Module C — Found Together (4:00–6:00)

### Shot 17 — Luca Arrives (4:00–4:15)

In Friendship Meadow, Luca appears from the path. He sees Arda walking slowly and carefully near
the meadow edge, looking at the ground. Luca tilts his head, curious. He walks over to Arda.

**Luca:** "Arda! What are you looking for?"

**Arda:** "My golden leaf! The wind took it!"

Luca's face lights up — a game!

### Shot 18 — Noah Joins (4:15–4:30)

Noah runs in from the other side of the meadow, excited. He sees Arda and Luca searching near the
bushes. He runs over with a big grin.

**Noah:** "A leaf hunt? I'll help!"

**Arda:** "Let's look together!"

The three friends stand together, ready to search.

### Shot 19 — Team Search (4:30–4:45)

The three friends spread out and search together. Luca looks behind the tall flowers, carefully
peeking around each petal. Noah checks under the low bushes, lying on his belly to look. Arda
walks slowly, scanning the ground near the meadow edge. They search with care and focus, each in
their own spot.

### Shot 20 — A Glint of Gold (4:45–5:00)

Arda stops. Near the edge of Friendship Meadow, beside a small bush, he sees it — a tiny glint
of gold. The sunlight catches the leaf's surface. Arda's eyes widen. He holds his hand up to
signal the others. He points carefully.

**Arda:** "I see something…"

### Shot 21 — Careful Approach (5:00–5:15)

Arda walks slowly toward the bush. He does not run. He moves carefully, quietly, like the leaf
might fly away again. Luca and Noah watch from behind, holding their breath. Arda reaches the
small bush and looks behind it gently.

### Shot 22 — There It Is! (5:15–5:30)

There it is! The golden leaf, nestled beside a small smooth stone at the base of the bush. It
sits perfectly, as if it has been waiting for Arda to find it. The golden colour glows in the
sunlight. Arda's face breaks into a huge smile. He reaches down slowly and picks up the leaf.

**Arda:** "There you are!"

### Shot 23 — I Found It! (5:30–5:45)

Arda stands up, holding the golden leaf high. Luca and Noah run over, cheering. Arda's face is
full of pride and joy. He found it — not by running fast, but by looking carefully. The three
friends gather around the leaf, admiring its golden glow.

**Arda:** "I found it!"

**Luca and Noah:** "Yay!"

### Shot 24 — The Leaf Game (5:45–6:00)

The three friends sit together in Friendship Meadow. Arda holds the golden leaf. They look at
each other and smile. Then Arda places the leaf on the ground, and Luca hides his eyes. Arda
moves the leaf to a new spot. Noah giggles. They say the rhyme together — the hiding game has
become a shared game.

**All:** "Behind? Look! Under? Peek! Found it! Here it is!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

The camera holds on the warm moment — three friends, one golden leaf, a garden full of hiding
spots. The scene fades softly.
