## Extraction Rules

- A Short may reorder micro-beats within its listed source shots, but it must not change causality.
- Do not add burned-in educational text to the master footage.
- Platform-native captions may be authored separately during publishing.
- Keep Arda's face and the golden leaf visible in the central vertical-safe region.
- Each export needs its own opening hook and complete payoff; do not publish a contextless fragment.
- The golden leaf and its hiding/searching must be clearly visible in every Short.
- The rhyme ('Behind? Look! Under? Peek! Found it! Here it is!') should be implied or present
  in every Short where it appears in the source.
