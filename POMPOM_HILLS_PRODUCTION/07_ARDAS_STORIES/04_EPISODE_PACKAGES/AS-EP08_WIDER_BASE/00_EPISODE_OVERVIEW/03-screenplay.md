# Full Screenplay — Wider Base

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Gathering (0:00–0:15)

Arda gathers the scattered blocks into a neat pile. He sits back and looks at them, chin
resting on his hand. His expression is curious and thoughtful.

**Arda:** "Hmm..."

He picks up one block and turns it over in his hands.

### Shot 02 — Looking Around (0:15–0:30)

Arda's eyes wander around the room. He notices a small rounded decorative stone sitting on two
tiny flat stones near the wall. The wider base underneath keeps it steady. His eyes light up.

**Arda:** "Oh! That one is standing strong."

He points at the stone on its two-stone base.

### Shot 03 — Two Side by Side (0:30–0:45)

Arda places two blocks side by side on the soft floor. They sit next to each other, forming a
wider base. He looks at the base, then looks at the remaining blocks.

**Arda:** "Wider first!"

**SFX:** `thump thump`

He picks up a third block.

### Shot 04 — Three on Top (0:45–1:00)

Arda places the third block on top of the two-block base. The tower stands. It wobbles very
gently but holds.

**SFX:** `thump`

**Arda:** "Three! It's standing!"

He claps once, softly.

### Shot 05 — Even Wider (1:00–1:15)

Arda looks at the tower. He decides to make the base even wider. He adds a third block to the
bottom row, making a three-block-wide base. He pats it firmly.

**Arda:** "Base it wider!"

**SFX:** `thump thump`

The base now sits solid and wide.

### Shot 06 — Growing Tall (1:15–1:30)

Arda stacks the fourth block, then the fifth block on top. The tower rises — four, five blocks
tall — and stands strong on its wide base. It does not wobble.

**SFX:** `thump thump`

**Arda:** "Four... five! It's tall and strong!"

He steps back with wide eyes and a huge smile.

### Shot 07 — The Rhyme (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands beside his tall, stable tower. He sways gently and recites the rhyme with pride,
looking at the tower and then at the camera.

**Arda:** "Taller? Wider first! Stronger? Base it wider!"

He claps his hands together once.

### Shot 08 — Proud Builder (1:45–2:00)

Arda stands with his hands on his hips, looking at the tower. It stands tall and steady. The
warm indoor light falls on the pastel blocks. He takes one satisfied breath.

**Arda:** "My tower stands!"

He smiles, then turns toward the garden door, hearing a sound outside.
