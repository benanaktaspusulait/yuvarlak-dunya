# Full Screenplay — No Wind

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Holding the Kite (0:00–0:15)

The frame opens on Arda's Home garden on a bright, still afternoon. The sky is pale blue, the
sun is warm, and everything is perfectly calm — not a leaf moves. Arda stands in the middle of
the garden holding a soft, colourful triangle kite with pastel rainbow panels (pink, yellow,
blue, mint green) and a fluffy yarn tail. His eyes are bright with excitement.

**Arda:** "My kite!"

He holds it up, the tail dangling straight down — no wind at all.

### Shot 02 — First Run! (0:15–0:30)

Arda grips the kite string and runs across the garden, holding the kite up with his other hand.
He runs fast, his bare feet on the soft grass. The kite lifts for a split second — then flops
gently to the ground behind him. Arda stops, turns around, and looks at the kite lying on the
grass. He blinks.

**Arda:** "Flop!"

He walks back to the kite, puzzled.

### Shot 03 — The Head Wrap (0:30–0:45)

Arda picks up the kite and tries again. This time he runs even faster across the garden, holding
the kite high. The kite lifts — it rises above his head — then a sudden gust (from his own
running!) wraps the kite string around his head like a hat! Arda stops, tangled in the string,
the kite resting on top of his head like a floppy hat. He giggles and untangles himself.

**Arda:** "Silly kite!"

He pulls the kite off his head, laughing.

### Shot 04 — The Flower Landing (0:45–1:00)

Arda tries a new strategy — he stands on a small grassy mound, holds the kite high above his
head, and THROWS it up with all his might! The kite flies upward for a moment — then nosedives
straight into a big, soft daisy flower! The kite lands perfectly balanced on the flower, its pastel
panels draped over the petals like a blanket. Arda runs over, impressed.

**Arda:** "Oh! The flower caught it!"

He gently lifts the kite off the daisy, patting the flower apologizing.

### Shot 05 — The Ground Drag (1:00–1:15)

Arda tries one more time — he runs along the garden path, dragging the kite behind him on the
ground. The kite skids along the grass, making a soft swishing sound, picking up leaves and grass
clippings. Arda runs faster — the kite bounces and tumbles behind him, collecting a small trail
of debris. He stops, out of breath, and looks back at his messy kite.

**Arda:** "Oops!"

He shakes the leaves off the kite, brushing it clean with his hands.

### Shot 06 — The Bounce Try (1:15–1:30)

Arda sits on a small garden step, holding the kite in his lap. He thinks, then gets an idea! He
bounces the kite up and down on his knee like a ball — boing, boing, boing! The kite bounces a
little each time but never actually flies. Arda bounces faster — the kite wobbles and flops off
his knee onto the grass. He picks it up, still bouncing it gently in his hands.

**Arda:** "Bounce, bounce... no fly!"

He looks at the kite thoughtfully.

### Shot 07 — The Spin Try (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands up and holds the kite out to his side. He starts spinning in a circle, holding the
kite out like a dancer! The kite swings around him, its tail streaming out. Arda spins faster
and faster — the kite lifts a little from the centrifugal force! But when he stops spinning,
the kite wobbles and drops. Arda gets dizzy and stumbles, laughing at himself.

**Arda:** "Wheee... whoa!"

He sits down on the grass, dizzy but smiling.

### Shot 08 — Looking Up (1:45–2:00)

Arda sits in the garden, holding the kite at his side, looking up at the still sky. His face is
thoughtful. He looks at the sky, then at the kite, then at the sky again. A small, determined
nod. He knows what he needs to do — find the wind. He starts walking toward the path that leads
out of the garden.

**Arda:** "Let's go find wind."
