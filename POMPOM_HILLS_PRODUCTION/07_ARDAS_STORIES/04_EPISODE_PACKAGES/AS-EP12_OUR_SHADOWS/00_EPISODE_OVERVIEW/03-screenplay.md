# Full Screenplay — Our Shadows

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Luca Arrives (0:00–0:15)

Arda walks along the path toward Friendship Meadow. Luca steps into view from behind a rounded
shrub, eyes wide. Luca points down at Arda's shadow on the ground.

**Luca:** "Arda, look! Your shadow!"

**Arda:** "I know! It dances with me!"

### Shot 02 — Noah Joins (0:15–0:30)

Noah arrives from the other side, waving. He looks down and sees his own shadow on the sunny
ground. His eyes go wide with wonder. All three children now look at the ground, seeing three
distinct shadows.

**Noah:** "I have one too!"

**Arda:** "We all have shadows!"

### Shot 03 — Three Waves (0:30–0:45)

The three friends stand in a row on the sunny path. They all raise their hands and wave. Three
shadows on the ground wave in perfect unison. The friends look at each other and laugh with
delight.

**SFX:** `puff puff puff`

**Luca:** "They all wave!"

### Shot 04 — Walking in a Line (0:45–1:00)

The three friends walk in a line along the path — Arda leading, Luca in the middle, Noah at the
back. Three shadows walk on the ground beside them in the same formation. They walk in rhythm,
feet moving together.

**Noah:** "Left, right, left, right!"

### Shot 05 — Into the Meadow (1:00–1:15)

The path opens into Friendship Meadow. The friends walk onto the soft green grass. Their
shadows stretch long and wide on the meadow ground in the warm afternoon sun. The friends stop
in the middle of the meadow, looking around at the wide open space.

**Arda:** "Our shadows came to the meadow too!"

### Shot 06 — Silly Walk (1:15–1:30)

The three friends do an exaggerated silly walk across the meadow — high knees, wobbly arms,
silly steps. Their three shadows copy every silly movement on the ground. The friends laugh
together as their shadows mirror the silliness.

**SFX:** `puff puff puff`

**All:** "Ha ha ha!"

### Shot 07 — Standing Together (1:30–1:45)

The three friends stand in a row in Friendship Meadow, catching their breath. Their three
shadows lie side by side on the grass in front of them. The warm afternoon light is golden and
soft. The friends look at each other and smile.

**Arda:** "Our shadows are always with us."

**Luca:** "Always."

### Shot 08 — One More Time? (1:45–2:00)

All three friends face the camera, standing together in Friendship Meadow. They sway gently
side to side. Their shadows stretch behind them on the grass. They say the rhyme one last time
together.

**All:** "Step on it? It moves! Catch it? It waits! Stop and look — there it is!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

Arda waves at his shadow on the ground. The shadow waves back. The camera holds on the warm
moment as the scene fades softly.
