# Full Screenplay — Found Together

> Standalone duration: 120 seconds. Every shot is exactly 15 seconds. No footage or context from another episode is required.

---

### Shot 01 — Luca Arrives (0:00–0:15)

In Friendship Meadow, Luca appears from the path. He sees Arda walking slowly and carefully near
the meadow edge, looking at the ground. Luca tilts his head, curious. He walks over to Arda.

**Luca:** "Arda! What are you looking for?"

**Arda:** "My golden leaf! The wind took it!"

Luca's face lights up — a game!

### Shot 02 — Noah Joins (0:15–0:30)

Noah runs in from the other side of the meadow, excited. He sees Arda and Luca searching near the
bushes. He runs over with a big grin.

**Noah:** "A leaf hunt? I'll help!"

**Arda:** "Let's look together!"

The three friends stand together, ready to search.

### Shot 03 — Team Search (0:30–0:45)

The three friends spread out and search together. Luca looks behind the tall flowers, carefully
peeking around each petal. Noah checks under the low bushes, lying on his belly to look. Arda
walks slowly, scanning the ground near the meadow edge. They search with care and focus, each in
their own spot.

### Shot 04 — A Glint of Gold (0:45–1:00)

Arda stops. Near the edge of Friendship Meadow, beside a small bush, he sees it — a tiny glint
of gold. The sunlight catches the leaf's surface. Arda's eyes widen. He holds his hand up to
signal the others. He points carefully.

**Arda:** "I see something…"

### Shot 05 — Careful Approach (1:00–1:15)

Arda walks slowly toward the bush. He does not run. He moves carefully, quietly, like the leaf
might fly away again. Luca and Noah watch from behind, holding their breath. Arda reaches the
small bush and looks behind it gently.

### Shot 06 — There It Is! (1:15–1:30)

There it is! The golden leaf, nestled beside a small smooth stone at the base of the bush. It
sits perfectly, as if it has been waiting for Arda to find it. The golden colour glows in the
sunlight. Arda's face breaks into a huge smile. He reaches down slowly and picks up the leaf.

**Arda:** "There you are!"

### Shot 07 — I Found It! (1:30–1:45)

Arda stands up, holding the golden leaf high. Luca and Noah run over, cheering. Arda's face is
full of pride and joy. He found it — not by running fast, but by looking carefully. The three
friends gather around the leaf, admiring its golden glow.

**Arda:** "I found it!"

**Luca and Noah:** "Yay!"

### Shot 08 — The Leaf Game (1:45–2:00)

The three friends sit together in Friendship Meadow. Arda holds the golden leaf. They look at
each other and smile. Then Arda places the leaf on the ground, and Luca hides his eyes. Arda
moves the leaf to a new spot. Noah giggles. They say the rhyme together — the hiding game has
become a shared game.

**All:** "Behind? Look! Under? Peek! Found it! Here it is!"

**Arda:** "One more time?"

**Luca and Noah:** "One more time!"

The camera holds on the warm moment — three friends, one golden leaf, a garden full of hiding
spots. The scene fades softly.
