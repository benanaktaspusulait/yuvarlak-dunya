# Shot 01 — Little Forest Establishing

## Scene Context
EP03 opening. Wide establishing shot of Little Forest. Tall gentle trees, soft dappled light filtering through leaves, a few golden leaves floating down. Peaceful but slightly melancholic mood.

## Purpose
Establish the quiet, reflective atmosphere. The floating leaves foreshadow the loss that's coming.

A few golden leaves gently floating down. Small mushrooms at tree bases. Mossy ground. 2D cartoon style, Pompom Hills aesthetic. Warm but slightly muted golden-green palette. Clean lines. Peaceful atmosphere with a hint of melancholy. Children's animation style.
