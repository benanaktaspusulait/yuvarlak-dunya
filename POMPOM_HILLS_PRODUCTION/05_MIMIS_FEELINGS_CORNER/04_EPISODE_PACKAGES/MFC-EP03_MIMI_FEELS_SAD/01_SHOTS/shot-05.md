# Shot 05 — Kiko Sits With Mimi

## Scene Context
EP03 Shot 05. Kiko walks in quietly and sits down next to Mimi. She doesn't say anything at first — just sits close, shoulder to shoulder. Then she puts a gentle arm around Mimi.

## Purpose
Model compassionate presence. Kiko doesn't fix the sadness — she shares it. This teaches children that sometimes just being there is enough.

## Visual Prompt
Two cute round cat characters sitting together on a forest floor. Pink cat (#F8BBD0) sitting close to blue cat (#90CAF9), arm gently around blue cat's shoulder. Blue cat still has drooped ears and teary eyes but is leaning slightly into the pink cat. Quiet companionship. 2D cartoon style, Pompom Hills aesthetic. Soft warm light breaking through trees. Forest background. Pastel tones, clean lines. Moment of comfort and connection. Children's animation style. Tender and safe.

## Dialogue
Kiko: (quietly) "It's okay to feel sad."

## Negative Prompt
No talking too much. No loud expressions. No realistic textures. No muted colors. No photorealistic style. No harsh shadows. No 3D rendering. No dismissive body language. No fixing behavior.
