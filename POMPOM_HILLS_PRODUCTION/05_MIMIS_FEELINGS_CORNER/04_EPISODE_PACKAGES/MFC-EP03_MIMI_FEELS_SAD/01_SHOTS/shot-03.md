# Shot 03 — Wind Blows the Leaf Away

## Scene Context
EP03 Shot 03. A gust of wind comes from the left. The golden leaf is pulled from Mimi's paws and flies away to the right. Mimi's paws are left empty, reaching out.

## Purpose
The loss moment. Simple, clear, devastating in its simplicity. Children understand losing something you love.

## Visual Prompt
A cute round blue cat character (#90CAF9) in a forest, paws reaching out as a golden leaf is blown away by wind. Motion lines showing wind from left to right. Leaf mid-air, flying away. Character's expression shifting from surprise to worry. Ears starting to flatten. 2D cartoon style, Pompom Hills aesthetic. Dynamic wind effect with leaves swirling. Golden-green forest palette. Clean outlines. Dramatic moment of loss. Children's animation style.

## Dialogue
Mimi: "Wait!" (reaching out)

## Negative Prompt
No violent wind. No destruction. No dark atmosphere. No realistic textures. No muted colors. No photorealistic style. No harsh shadows. No 3D rendering. No scary elements.
