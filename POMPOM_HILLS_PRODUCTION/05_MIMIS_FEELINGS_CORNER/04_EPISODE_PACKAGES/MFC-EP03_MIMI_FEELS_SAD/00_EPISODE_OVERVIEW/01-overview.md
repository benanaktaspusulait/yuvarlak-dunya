# MFC-EP03: Mimi Feels Sad

## Genel Bilgi
- **Seri:** Mimi's Feelings Corner
- **Bölüm:** EP03 — Mimi Feels Sad
- **Süre:** 90 saniye (6 shot × 15 saniye)
- **Karakterler:** Mimi, Kiko
- **Mekan:** Little Forest
- **Ana Duygu:** Üzüntü (Sad)

## Hook
A golden leaf blows away from Mimi's paw — simple, powerful visual metaphor for loss.

## Özet
Mimi is holding a beautiful golden leaf in Little Forest. A gust of wind blows it away. Mimi feels sad — her ears droop, her eyes well up. Kiko sits beside her quietly. Together they find a new leaf, and Mimi smiles again.
