## Characters
- Mimi (rabbit) — #90CAF9

- Opa (grandpa owl) — #A5D6A7

## Setting
Little Forest — evening, soft purple-blue sky, fireflies beginning to glow.

## Hook
Rustle in bushes. Mimi's ears shoot straight up.

## Story Beat
1. Mimi, Kiko, and Opa walk through Little Forest as evening falls. Fireflies blink softly.
2. A sudden rustle in the bushes. Mimi freezes. Her ears shoot straight up. She feels SCARED.
3. A branch cracks nearby. Mimi jumps behind Kiko, trembling. Her eyes are wide.
4. Kiko holds Mimi's paw gently: "I'm right here, Mimi. You're safe."
5. Opa looks up calmly: "It's just the wind playing with the branches, dear. Nothing to fear."
6. The wind passes. Mimi peeks out. She sees fireflies dancing peacefully. She holds Kiko's paw and smiles: "I feel brave now."

## Emotional Arc
Curious → Scared → Comforted → Brave

## Learning Objective
Scary feelings are normal. Holding someone's hand helps. Understanding what causes the fear brings courage.
