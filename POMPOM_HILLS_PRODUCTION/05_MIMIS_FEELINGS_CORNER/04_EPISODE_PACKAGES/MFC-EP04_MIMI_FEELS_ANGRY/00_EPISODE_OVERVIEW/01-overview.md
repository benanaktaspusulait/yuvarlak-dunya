## Characters
- Mimi (rabbit) — #90CAF9

## Setting
Kiko's Home — cozy indoor space with blocks, cushions, warm afternoon light.

## Hook
Block tower falls. Mimi's ears perk up in frustration.

## Story Beat
1. Mimi carefully stacks blocks into a tall tower at Kiko's home.
2. The tower topples — blocks scatter everywhere. Mimi's face flushes, ears shoot up. She feels ANGRY.
3. Mimi stomps her foot. She crosses her arms. A small angry hum escapes her.
4. Kiko sits beside her calmly: "It's okay to feel angry, Mimi. Let's take a deep breath together."
5. They breathe in slowly… breathe out together. Mimi's shoulders relax. Her ears lower gently.
6. Mimi and Kiko rebuild the tower together — even taller this time. Mimi smiles: "I feel better now."

## Emotional Arc
Angry → Frustrated → Calmed → Proud

## Learning Objective
Angry feelings are normal. Breathing helps. You can calm down and try again.
