# MFC-EP01: Mimi Feels Excited

## Genel Bilgi
- **Seri:** Mimi's Feelings Corner
- **Bölüm:** EP01 — Mimi Feels Excited
- **Süre:** 90 saniye (6 shot × 15 saniye)
- **Karakterler:** Mimi, Kiko
- **Mekan:** Flower Hill
- **Ana Duygu:** Heyecan (Excited)

## Hook
Mimi hops 3 times, ears bouncing — immediate visual energy to grab young viewers.

## Özet
Mimi arrives at Flower Hill and starts hopping excitedly. Her ears bounce with each hop. Kiko notices and names the feeling: "You feel excited!" Together they celebrate with a joyful dance.
