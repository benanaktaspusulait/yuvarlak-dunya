# MFC-EP02: Mimi Feels Shy

- **Karakterler:** Mimi, Kiko

## Hook
Mimi peeks from behind Kiko — instant visual communication of shyness and seeking safety.

## Özet
Mimi and Kiko walk into Central Square. New friends appear. Mimi hides behind Kiko, peeking out shyly. Kiko gently names the feeling: "You feel shy. That's okay." Mimi relaxes and slowly steps out.
