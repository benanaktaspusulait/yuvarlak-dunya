# Shot 02 — Mimi and Kiko Walk In

## Scene Context
EP02 Shot 02. Mimi and Kiko walk into Central Square together. Mimi looks around nervously — eyes darting, ears slightly back. Kiko walks confidently beside her.

## Purpose
Establish the contrast between Kiko's comfort and Mimi's unease. Plant the seed of shyness visually.

## Visual Prompt
Two cute round cat characters walking into a town square. Blue cat (#90CAF9) on the left, looking around nervously with wide eyes and ears slightly flattened back. Pink cat (#F8BBD0) on the right, walking confidently with a relaxed smile. 2D cartoon style, Pompom Hills aesthetic. Town square background with benches and trees. Pastel tones, clean outlines. Body language contrast between the two characters. Children's animation style.

## Dialogue
Kiko: "Here we are!"

## Negative Prompt
No dark expressions. No realistic proportions. No complex backgrounds. No muted colors. No photorealistic style. No harsh lighting. No 3D rendering. No scary elements.
