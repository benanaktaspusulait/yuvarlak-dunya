# Shot 01 — Central Square Establishing

## Scene Context
EP02 opening. Wide establishing shot of Central Square. Bright benches, leafy trees, a small fountain. Peaceful atmosphere before new friends arrive.

## Purpose
Establish the social setting — a public place where meeting new people happens. Calm before the shyness triggers.

## Visual Prompt
Wide shot of a charming cartoon town square. Round benches under leafy green trees. A small cute fountain in the center. Colorful flower pots. Bright daylight. Soft shadows. 2D cartoon style, Pompom Hills aesthetic. Pastel color palette — soft greens, yellows, and pinks. Clean lines. Empty square, waiting for characters. Children's animation style. Warm and inviting atmosphere.

## Dialogue
(None — ambient birds chirping)

## Negative Prompt
No dark or moody lighting. No realistic textures. No complex architecture. No muted colors. No photorealistic style. No scary elements. No 3D rendering. No harsh shadows.
