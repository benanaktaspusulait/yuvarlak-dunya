# Shot 07: The Cycle Continues

| Context | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Scene** | Cloud Hill |
| **Characters** | Kiko (#F8BBD0), Opa (#A5D6A7) |
| **Camera** | Panoramic View |

## Purpose
To visually link the rain back to the earth and water cycle.

## Visual Prompt
A wide view of Cloud Hill. The rain is stopping, and a bright, cheerful rainbow is beginning to arc over the hill. Kiko and Opa are looking at the rainbow in awe. Whimsical 3D animation style.

## Dialogue
**Opa:** "See? It always comes back to the earth."

## Negative Prompt
No dark clouds, no realistic textures, no sharp edges.
