| Context | Details |

## Purpose
To visualize the concept of clouds holding water.

## Visual Prompt
A soft, stylized close-up of the bottom of a fluffy white cloud. Tiny, sparkling blue droplets are visible suspended inside the cloud structure, like hidden gems. Whimsical 3D animation style.

## Dialogue
**Opa (Off-screen):** "The clouds carry the water for us."

## Negative Prompt
No realistic water physics, no grey clouds, no heavy rain.
