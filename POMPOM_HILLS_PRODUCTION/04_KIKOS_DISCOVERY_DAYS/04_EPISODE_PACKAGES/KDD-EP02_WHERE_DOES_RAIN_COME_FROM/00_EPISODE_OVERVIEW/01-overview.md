# KDD-EP02: Where Does Rain Come From?

## Episode Overview
- **Episode Title:** Where Does Rain Come From?
- **Episode Code:** KDD-EP02
- **Characters:** Kiko (#F8BBD0), Opa (#A5D6A7)
- **Location:** Cloud Hill
- **Hook:** A single raindrop falls toward Kiko's face.
- **Story Summary:** Kiko feels rain, wonders where it comes from. Opa shows clouds carry water. They watch clouds.

## Production Notes
- **Style:** Whimsical, educational, soft textures.
- **Educational Goal:** Basic water cycle concept (evaporation, clouds, rain).
- **Emotional Arc:** Curiosity -> Wonder -> Understanding.
