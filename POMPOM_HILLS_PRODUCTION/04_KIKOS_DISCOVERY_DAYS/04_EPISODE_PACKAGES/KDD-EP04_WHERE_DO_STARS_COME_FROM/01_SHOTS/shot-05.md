# Shot 05: The Constellation

| Context | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Scene** | Opa's Tree, Night |
| **Characters** | (None) |
| **Camera** | Looking up at Sky |

## Purpose
To introduce the idea of shapes in the stars.

## Visual Prompt
A view of the night sky where five bright stars are connected by a faint, glowing line to form a simple "Big Dipper" shape. Whimsical 3D animation style.

## Dialogue
**Opa (Off-screen):** "Look, they make a shape."

## Negative Prompt
No realistic star maps, no complex constellations, no dark sky.
