## New Set-Piece — Tooth Helper Corner

A cozy, rounded nook at the edge of Central Square (near the low pastel houses), not a clinic:

- Soft mint & cream rounded awning / small tent
- One soft cushioned round "smile chair"
- Small wooden shelf with little round tooth mirrors and a friendly water cup
- Potted pastel flowers, dappled tree-shadow, warm morning light

**Forbidden in this set:** clinical white surfaces, bright surgical lamp, metal trays,
needles, drills, sharp tools. See `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md` §4.
