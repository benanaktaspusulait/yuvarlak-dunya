# My First Day — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — At the Learning Room Door

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Wide establishing shot. The warm cottage doorway is open, revealing LR-TABLE-01 (large round
wooden table) and LR-BOARD-01 (soft chalkboard) inside. Amara sits at the table and waves
softly. Kiko and Opa stand at the doorway; Kiko looks inside with curiosity and hesitation.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Stay With Me?

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Close-up on Kiko's small hand holding Opa's wing-tip, then pull-out to medium two-shot.
Kiko's face shows mild hesitation — not fear, just a quiet "stay with me" feeling. She asks:
"Stay?" Opa looks down with a warm, steady expression.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — I'll Be Back Soon

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Medium shot following Opa's descent to Kiko's eye level. Opa kneels; their eyes meet at the
same level. Opa says gently: "I'll be back soon." Kiko's face softens from hesitation to
quiet trust.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — A Small Step In

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Medium shot from inside the room, looking toward the doorway. Kiko glances back at Opa one
last time, then crosses the threshold. Amara shifts to make space at the table. Opa remains
visible at the doorway.

## Shot 05 (51-66 sn) — First Activity

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Close-up on large round crayons rolling across the wooden table, then tilt up to medium
two-shot of Kiko and Amara. Amara rolls crayons toward Kiko: "These are fun!" Kiko:
"What colors?" Curiosity replaces hesitation.

## Shot 06 (66-81 sn) — Friendship Begins

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Close-up on paper as Kiko draws a circle with a coral crayon, Amara adds a square with a
yellow crayon. Pull-back to medium two-shot; both look at their shapes and smile. Friendship
formed.

## Shot 07 (81-93 sn) — Opa Returns

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Medium shot of doorway as Opa appears, calm and smiling. Pan to Kiko's face — joy lights up.
"You came back!" Opa: "I came back." Drawing and crayons still on the table.

## Shot 08 (93-105 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Learning Room — Entrance / Activity Area

Medium three-shot at child eye level. Kiko stands between Opa (doorway) and Amara (table).
Opa: "I came back." Kiko echoes: "I'll be back soon." All three smile; warm fade.
