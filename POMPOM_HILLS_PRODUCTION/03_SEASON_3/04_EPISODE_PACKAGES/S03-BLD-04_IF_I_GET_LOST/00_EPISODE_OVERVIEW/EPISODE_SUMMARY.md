# Brave Little Days EP14: If I Get Lost — Episode Summary

---

## Story

On a warm morning in Central Square, Kiko and Opa walk together near the Big Pompom Tree.
When Kiko can't see Opa for a moment, she doesn't panic — she places her hands on her chest,
breathes deeply, and whispers: "Stay calm." She spots Remi, a familiar face at the Care Corner,
and walks toward her calmly: "Excuse me." Remi kneels to her eye level: "I can help." They
wait together until Opa reappears from behind the tree: "There you are." Kiko's face relaxes
with relief. The three share a warm moment of gratitude. Kiko delivers the lesson: "Stay calm,
find a helper."

## Learning

Calmness • Problem-solving • Community trust

## Characters

Kiko, Opa, Remi

## World

Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge
