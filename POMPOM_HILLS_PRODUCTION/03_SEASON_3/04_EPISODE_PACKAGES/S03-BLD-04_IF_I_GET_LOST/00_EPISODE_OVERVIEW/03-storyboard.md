# If I Get Lost — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — A Busy-But-Gentle Square

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Wide establishing shot. Big Pompom Tree centred. Two silent background figures wave at the far
edge. Kiko and Opa walk together on soft grass. Warm morning light.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — Where's Opa?

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Medium shot. Kiko stops walking. She looks around — Opa was just beside her but is now out of
frame. "Opa?" — a calm question. Her expression shows mild concern, not panic.

## Shot 03 (27-39 sn) — Stay Calm

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Close-up on Kiko. She places her hands on her chest, takes a deep breath, whispers: "Stay calm."
Eyes open with renewed composure. Self-calming moment.

## Shot 04 (39-51 sn) — Finding a Helper

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Medium shot following Kiko. She spots Remi near the Care Corner edge and walks toward her calmly.
"Excuse me." Remi notices and smiles warmly. No running, no urgency.

## Shot 05 (51-63 sn) — Waiting Together

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Close two-shot. Remi kneels to Kiko's eye level: "I can help." Kiko's face softens. They stand
together quietly. Companionship, not rescue.

## Shot 06 (63-75 sn) — Opa Returns

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Wide then medium. Opa steps into view from behind the Big Pompom Tree, walking calmly. Kiko sees
him and her face relaxes: "There you are." Relief without drama.

## Shot 07 (75-87 sn) — Safe Reunion

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Medium three-shot. Opa thanks Remi: "Thank you, Remi." Remi: "Anytime." The three share a warm,
quiet moment. Community and gratitude.

## Shot 08 (87-108 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Central Square — Big Pompom Tree / Care Corner edge

Medium shot. The three stand together, facing camera. Kiko: "Stay calm, find a helper." They give
a small gentle wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
