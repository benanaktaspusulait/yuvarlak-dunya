# Share and Play — Storyboard v1.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — A Soft Ball

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Wide establishing shot. The round blue slide and ball pit are visible. One soft purple ball
rolls gently out toward Kiko. Kiko notices with a bright smile. Amara stands nearby.

## Shot 02 (15-27 sn) — It's Mine

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Medium shot. Kiko picks up the purple ball and holds it against her chest. Amara's smile
fades slightly. A gentle, natural possessiveness moment.

## Shot 03 (27-42 sn) — Can I Play Too?

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Two-shot. Amara steps closer and asks softly. Kiko pauses — looks down at the ball, then
up at Amara's face. A quiet moment of recognition.

## Shot 04 (42-54 sn) — You Can Play Too

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Medium shot. Kiko lowers the ball gently to the grass between them. Her expression shifts
from possessive to open. Amara's smile returns.

## Shot 05 (54-69 sn) — Rolling Together

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Low medium shot. Both characters kneel and guide the ball in a gentle circle on the grass.
Cooperative play — neither dominates.

## Shot 06 (69-84 sn) — Sharing Is More Fun

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Medium two-shot. They continue rolling, laughing softly. Kiko delivers the repeated phrase.
Amara nods with bright joy.

## Shot 07 (84-96 sn) — Kiko's Pride

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Medium shot. Kiko pushes the ball toward Amara, then they bring it back together. Kiko's
face shows quiet pride — sharing feels right.

## Shot 08 (96-108 sn) — Warm Closing

**📍 Location:** Benny's Playground — Ball Pit Area

Medium shot. Both hold the ball together, facing the camera. They say the repeated phrase
and give a small wave. Warm smiles, soft fade.
