# Pompom Hills - Detailed World Guide

---

## 1. WORLD OVERVIEW

### What Is Pompom Hills?
Pompom Hills is a small, cosy valley where everything is soft and rounded. It is a safe, warm, and gentle place where a little girl named Kiko lives with her rabbit friend Mimi and is watched over by a wise owl named Opa. The world feels like a living toy set — everything looks like it could be picked up and cuddled.

### World Feelings
- **Safe**: No danger, no fear, no harm
- **Warm**: Always sunny or softly lit
- **Gentle**: Everything moves slowly and softly
- **Cosy**: Small spaces, comfortable homes
- **Playful**: Fun things to discover everywhere
- **Friendly**: Everyone is kind to everyone

### World Rules
- No sharp corners anywhere
- No dark or scary places
- No dangerous objects
- No fast movements
- No loud sounds
- No mean characters
- No punishment
- No hurry

---

## 2. GEOGRAPHY

### The Valley Shape
- The valley is bowl-shaped, like a soft hug
- Gently curved edges, no steep walls
- Green grass covers everything
- The ground is soft, like a carpet

### Hill Positions (Clockwise from Top)

| Hill | Name | Height | Colour | Feature |
|------|------|--------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Sun Hill | Tallest | Light Green | Big pompom tree on top |
| 2 | Flower Hill | Medium | Soft Green | Round flowers everywhere |
| 3 | Cloud Hill | Short | Pale Green | Closest to clouds |
| 4 | Stone Hill | Medium | Grey-Green | Round stepping stones |
| 5 | Tree Hill | Tall | Dark Green | Many pompom trees |
| 6 | House Hill | Short | Light Green | Kiko's house |
| 7 | Rest Hill | Very Short | Pale Green | Opa's favourite perch |

### Central Square
- Round shape, slightly elevated
- Large pompom tree in exact centre
- Smooth, soft ground (like a carpet)
- Round stepping stones in a circle around the tree
- This is where all paths meet

### Paths
- All paths are curved, never straight
- Made of round stepping stones
- Different soft colours for each path
- Paths connect all hills to the square
- Paths are easy to follow (no getting lost)

## 3. LOCATIONS IN DETAIL

### Kiko's House (House Hill)

**Exterior:**
- Round, one-storey house
- Soft coral pink walls
- Round arched door (white with coral pink frame)
- Two circular windows (white frames)
- Small round chimney (no smoke, just decoration)
- Low, soft fence (coral pink)
- Small garden with round flowers

**Interior:**
- One round room
- Soft white walls
- Round window with soft curtains (coral pink)
- Small round bed (coral pink blanket)
- Tiny round table (white)
- Two tiny round chairs (white)
- Small round rug (white with coral pink dots)
- No clutter, no complex furniture

**Garden:**
- Small, round garden bed
- Three round flowers (red, yellow, pink)
- Soft green grass
- One small pompom bush

### Mimi's Home (Near Kiko's House)

**Exterior:**
- Small, round burrow in a gentle hill
- Round entrance (no door)
- Soft blue frame around entrance
- Small pompom bush above entrance
- Round stepping stone at entrance

**Interior:**
- Small, cosy space
- Soft blue walls
- Round cushion (soft blue)
- Small round window (looks out to valley)
- No furniture, just cushion

### Opa's Tree (Rest Hill)

**The Tree:**
- Large pompom tree
- Round, fluffy green top
- Thick, soft trunk
- Branch at perfect height for Opa
- Round leaves

**The Perch:**
- Opa sits on the branch
- Can see the whole valley
- Soft, comfortable spot
- Sometimes has a small book

### The Big Pompom Tree (Central Square)

**Description:**
- Largest tree in the world
- Round, fluffy top (warm green)
- Very thick, soft trunk
- Round leaves
- Round flowers in spring
- Changes with seasons

**Around the Tree:**
- Round stepping stones in circle
- Soft grass
- Characters gather here
- Meeting point for all adventures

### Flower Hill

**Description:**
- Gentle slope covered in round flowers
- Flowers: red, yellow, pink, purple, orange
- Large round petals
- Soft green stems
- Butterflies (round, soft, pastel)

### Cloud Hill

**Description:**
- Shortest hill, closest to sky
- Round clouds sometimes rest here
- Very soft grass
- Peaceful, quiet spot
- Good for watching clouds

### Stone Hill

**Description:**
- Medium hill with round stepping stones
- Stones are different pastel colours
- Path winds up and around
- Good for walking and exploring

### Tree Hill

**Description:**
- Many pompom trees of different sizes
- Trees are different soft colours
- Creates a small forest feel
- Safe, not scary
- Good for hide and seek

## 4. SIGNATURE VISUAL DETAILS

### Pompom Trees

**Appearance:**
- Round, fluffy tops (like pompoms)
- Thick, soft trunks (no bark texture)
- Different sizes (small, medium, large)
- Different colours (green, pink, yellow, blue)
- Bounce gently in wind

**Animation:**
- Sway slowly side to side
- Fluffy tops bounce
- Leaves fall slowly (when windy)
- Never shake violently

### Spiral Paths

**Appearance:**
- Paths curve around hills in spirals
- Made of round stepping stones
- Different soft colours per path
- Smooth, no rough edges

**Animation:**
- Characters hop from stone to stone
- Stones glow slightly when stepped on
- "Boop" sound when stepped on

### Round Stepping Stones

**Appearance:**
- Perfectly round
- Smooth surface
- Different pastel colours
- Slightly raised from ground

**Animation:**
- Soft glow when stepped on
- "Boop" sound effect
- Characters love stepping on them

### Round Doors

**Appearance:**
- Perfectly round or arched
- Bright, inviting colours
- No handles (push to open)
- Look like sticker cutouts

**Animation:**
- Swing open gently
- Soft "squeak" sound
- Characters push with hands/paws

### Round Windows

**Appearance:**
- Perfectly circular or oval
- White frames
- Soft curtains inside
- Look out to valley

**Animation:**
- Curtains sway gently
- Light shines through
- Characters wave through windows

### Round Flowers

**Appearance:**
- Large, round petals
- Bright, happy colours
- Soft green stems
- No thorns, no sharp parts

**Animation:**
- Sway gently in wind
- Bloom with "boop" sound
- Petals are soft, not realistic

### Round Clouds

**Appearance:**
- Perfectly round, puffy
- White or soft pastel
- Float slowly
- Never dark or grey

**Animation:**
- Drift slowly across sky
- Bump into each other gently
- Change shape slowly

### Boop Sounds

**What They Are:**
- Soft, magical "boop" sound
- Like a gentle bubble pop
- Used for small magical moments

**When They Happen:**
- When a flower blooms
- When a star twinkles
- When a character has an idea
- When stepping on a stone
- When something good happens

### Sleepy Moon

**Appearance:**
- Round, soft moon
- Gentle, yawning face
- Soft glow (not bright)
- Sometimes wears a nightcap

**When It Appears:**
- Only in bedtime episodes
- Waves goodnight
- Yawns gently
- Glows softly
