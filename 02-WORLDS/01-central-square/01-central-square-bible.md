## Visual Richness & World Charm

> See `02-WORLDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Central Square must NOT be reduced to "a plaza." Reject Central Square if
> it is only an empty plaza, the Big Pompom Tree is missing, or there is no
> community/play feeling.

Central Square should feel like: **"the place where everyone meets and something is always happening."**

### Iconic Landmark

CS-TREE-01 — the Big Pompom Tree, always centred, always the tallest and
most colourful element in frame. This is the one object that makes any wide
shot instantly readable as Central Square.

### Allowed Charm Details

**Landmark Charm**
- CS-TREE-01, Big Pompom Tree (canopy #81C784, trunk #A1887F) — always present, always centred
- ring of coloured stepping stones circling the tree (CS-STONE-PNK/YEL/BLU/GRN)

**Surface Charm**
- soft carpet-like green ground (CS-GRASS-01)
- dappled tree-shadow patterns on the grass around the trunk

**Small Prop Charm**
- low pastel houses visible at the edge of the square (background only, not redesigned)
- small wooden bench near the tree, if canonised for a shot

**Motion Charm**
- gentle leaf rustle on the Pompom Tree canopy
- soft ambient movement of distant figures/characters greeting each other (story shots only)

### Forbidden Clutter

```
✗ empty plaza with no tree
✗ Big Pompom Tree missing or undersized
✗ market stalls, signage, or modern street furniture
✗ crowd of more than a few background characters at once
✗ sharp-edged paving or geometric plaza design
✗ dark or dramatic lighting
```

### Thumbnail Hook

The Big Pompom Tree in full bloom at the centre of a warm, colourful stone
circle, with soft pastel houses in the background.

### Child Recognition Test

**"The square where friends meet and play, with the big tree in the middle."**
