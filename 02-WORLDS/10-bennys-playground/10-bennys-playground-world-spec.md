# Benny's Playground — World Creation Specification

> Source of truth: `10-bennys-playground-bible.md` (canonical, creative). This document
> is a production-oriented extraction for AI-assisted world generation. It does

---

## Purpose

Benny's Playground is a bright, rounded outdoor playground that functions as
the energy heart of Pompom Hills. It is where movement, friendship, and
discovery come alive through the simple joy of swinging, sliding, and climbing.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Benny's Playground and must never change:

- Open-air playground with rounded equipment
- Two round swings (red and yellow)
- One round blue slide
- One low rounded climbing hill (orange)
- One round ball pit with colorful balls
- Soft green grass ground surface
- Colorful entrance gate with welcome sign
- No sharp corners anywhere

---

## Visual Identity

A fully open-air playground with no rectangles, no straight edges, no sharp
corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every piece of equipment a
handcrafted, toy-like quality. The palette is bright and cheerful, centered
on primary colors with green grass. The overall feeling is a "living toy
playground" — approachable, safe, and visually simple enough for a preschool
audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous play space rather than a set of separate
areas. The entrance leads into the playground, which flows naturally from
swings to slide to climbing hill to ball pit to rest corner. All zones are
open and connected without fences or barriers between them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The entrance gate is visible from every zone.
- The swing area connects naturally to the slide zone.
- The slide is visible from both the swings and the climbing hill.
- The climbing hill provides a view of the entire playground.
- The ball pit is visible from the climbing hill.
- The rest corner is visible from all areas but feels slightly separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Playground Entrance** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the playground. Visual identity: colorful gate, welcome sign, round shapes. Important props: entrance gate, sign. Typical use: arrival, first impression.

**Swing Area** — Purpose: rhythmic movement zone. Visual identity: two round swings (red and yellow), soft ground. Important props: swings. Typical use: swinging, rhythmic motion.

**Slide Zone** — Purpose: excitement and sliding zone. Visual identity: one round blue slide, soft landing area. Important props: slide. Typical use: sliding, climbing up.

**Climbing Hill** — Purpose: climbing and discovery zone. Visual identity: low rounded orange hill, soft steps. Important props: hill, steps. Typical use: climbing, viewing from top.

**Ball Pit Area** — Purpose: soft ball play zone. Visual identity: round pit with colorful balls (red, yellow, blue, green, purple). Important props: ball pit, balls. Typical use: ball play, color discovery.

**Rest Corner** — Purpose: rest and calm zone. Visual identity: soft bench under shade tree. Important props: bench, tree. Typical use: resting, drinking water.

---

## Materials

All surfaces: soft matte, rounded, toy-like. Equipment: soft matte plastic in
bright colors. Ground: soft plush grass texture. Balls: soft, lightweight,
colorful. Benches: smooth matte wood. No metal, no glass, no hard surfaces
anywhere. Everything reads as safe and child-friendly.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is bright daylight. Morning brings soft golden light; midday is
bright and even; evening turns golden-orange with longer soft shadows; night
the playground is closed. Outdoor lighting is always natural daylight. Shadows
are always soft — never hard, never dark. Shade areas provide relief from
bright sun.

---

## Colour Palette

Ground: soft green grass. Swings: red and yellow. Slide: blue. Climbing hill:
orange. Balls: red, yellow, blue, green, purple. Entrance gate: colorful.
Benches: natural wood. The palette stays within this bright, cheerful set
across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. Equipment is low and safe —
swings are close to the ground, the slide is modest, the climbing hill is
gentle. No structure exceeds 1.5 meters in height. The playground feels
spacious enough to run but intimate enough to feel safe.

---

## Atmosphere

Bright. Soft. Safe. Energetic. Every surface reads as touchable and gentle.
The mood is always playful and inviting — full of movement and laughter. The
playground never feels empty or quiet during operating hours.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The playground is always open-air with rounded equipment — never enclosed, never sharp-edged.
- Equipment colors stay fixed: red/yellow swings, blue slide, orange hill.
- The ground is always soft green grass.
- No metal, no glass, no hard surfaces anywhere.
- No equipment exceeds 1.5 meters in height.
- No dark or threatening lighting.
- The playground's core shape, equipment, and colors are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The playground must be generated as a single open play space with soft
equipment, not as separate fenced areas — this is the defining trait of this
world and the most common failure point to watch for. Keep all shapes rounded
with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour palette limited to the bright
cheerful set defined above. Favor a soft, matte, handcrafted material language
over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering. Treat the playground's
equipment, colors, and proportions as a fixed identity to preserve exactly,
not to reinterpret, across every generation.

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `02-WORLDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Benny's Playground must never be reduced to "swings and a slide."

This layer protects Benny's Playground from becoming too plain. Keep the
open, rounded silhouette clear and add only the canon-approved charm details
below. Do not add random objects. Do not remove the bright, energetic,
friendship-driven emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** the BP-SLIDE-01 round blue slide stays the iconic
landmark at the centre of the play space, framed by the colourful entrance
gate and welcome sign. Surface charm comes from the soft plush green grass
covering the whole area and the rounded steps on the orange climbing hill.
Small prop charm comes from the BP-BALLPIT-01 ball pit with its
red/yellow/blue/green/purple balls and the wooden bench in the shaded rest
corner. Motion charm comes from the red-and-yellow swings swaying rhythmically
and balls gently rolling in the ball pit.

**Forbidden clutter:** metal equipment, tall or dangerous structures, sharp
corners anywhere, fast or dangerous movement, broken equipment, cold colours,
hard ground surface, dark or threatening lighting, large crowds, and
competitive scoring elements.
