---

## Purpose

Wish Pond is a small, round, magical pond that functions as the spiritual
heart of Pompom Hills. It is where wishes, hope, and imagination come alive
through the simple beauty of star reflections on still water.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Wish Pond and must never change:

- Small, round, clear pond
- Deep blue-purple water at night
- Star reflections floating on surface
- Smooth round stones around shore
- Calm, meditative atmosphere
- Magical night sky full of stars
- Warm, soft moonlight
- No sharp corners anywhere

---

## Visual Identity

A small, fully round pond with no rectangles, no straight edges, no sharp
corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every element a natural,
handcrafted quality. The palette is deep and magical, centred on blue-purple
water with golden star reflections and warm stones. The overall feeling is a
"living toy pond" — approachable, magical, and visually simple enough for a
preschool audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous magical space rather than a set of separate
areas. The entrance leads to the pond, which flows naturally from star
reflection point to stone circle to wish corner to shore lookout to quiet
rest area. All zones are open and connected without barriers between them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The pond entrance is visible from the path.
- The star reflection point can be seen from the shore.
- The stone circle is visible from the entrance.
- The wish corner is visible from the stone circle.
- The shore lookout provides a view of the entire pond.
- The quiet rest area is visible from the shore but feels separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Pond Entrance** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the pond. Visual identity: smooth round stones, clear water edge. Important props: stones, water. Typical use: arrival, first star sighting.

**Star Reflection Point** — Purpose: where stars reflect on water. Visual identity: deep blue-purple water with golden star reflections. Important props: water, stars. Typical use: wonder, counting.

**Stone Circle** — Purpose: natural stones around the pond. Visual identity: smooth round stones in a circle. Important props: stones. Typical use: grounding, touching.

**Wish Corner** — Purpose: wishing and hoping zone. Visual identity: quiet corner with best star view. Important props: sky, water. Typical use: wishing, closing eyes.

**Shore Lookout** — Purpose: viewing the entire pond. Visual identity: wide view of pond and sky. Important props: view. Typical use: perspective, calm.

**Quiet Rest Area** — Purpose: rest and meditation. Visual identity: soft stones under tree shade. Important props: stones, tree. Typical use: resting, meditating.

---

## Materials

Water: clear, deep, reflective. Stars: soft, golden, floating. Stones: smooth,
round, grey. Trees: soft, round, providing shade. Sky: deep blue-purple.
No hard surfaces, no sharp edges, no artificial materials anywhere. Everything
reads as natural and magical.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is starlight and moonlight. Evening brings the first stars;
night is full of golden starlight reflected on water; late night is deepest
and most magical; dawn brings soft golden light. Pond lighting is always
natural starlight. Shadows are always soft — never hard, never dark.

---

## Colour Palette

Water: deep blue-purple. Stars: golden yellow. Stones: warm grey. Sky: deep
blue. Moon: soft white. The palette stays within this deep, magical set
across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. The pond is intimate, not
vast — small enough to see across, large enough to feel like a real pond.
Water is shallow and safe. Stones are smooth and child-sized. The pond
never feels overwhelming.

---

## Atmosphere

Magical. Soft. Safe. Meditative. Every surface reads as natural and gentle.
The mood is always peaceful and inviting — full of quiet wonder and gentle
light. The pond never feels dark or threatening.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The pond is always small, round, and magical — never dark, never vast.
- Water stays deep blue-purple across every generation.
- Star reflections stay golden and floating.
- Stones stay smooth and round.
- No sharp corners, no dark shadows, no threatening elements.
- No dark or high-contrast lighting.
- The pond's core shape, colours, and proportions are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The pond must be generated as a single magical water space with star
reflections, not as a dark or threatening body of water — this is the
defining trait of this world and the most common failure point to watch for.
Keep all shapes rounded with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour
palette limited to the deep magical set defined above. Favor a soft, matte,
natural material language over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering.
Treat the pond's shape, colours, and proportions as a fixed identity to
preserve exactly, not to reinterpret, across every generation.
