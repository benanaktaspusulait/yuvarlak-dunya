# Camping Grove — World Creation Specification

> Source of truth: `21-camping-grove-bible.md` (canonical, creative). This document
> is a production-oriented extraction for AI-assisted world generation. It does
> not replace the Bible and does not duplicate its storytelling, educational,
> character, or workflow content.

---

## Purpose

Camping Grove is a small, round camping area that functions as the nighttime
heart of Pompom Hills. It is where camping, storytelling, and stargazing
come alive through the simple joy of warm firelight and starry skies.

---

## Immutable Identity

The following elements define the canonical identity of Camping Grove and must never change:

- Small, round camping area with safe campfire
- Round tents in warm colours (red, yellow, green)
- Round stone fire pit with contained safe fire
- Warm orange firelight glow
- Starry night sky
- Soft, natural surroundings
- Warm, safe atmosphere
- No sharp corners anywhere

---

## Visual Identity

A small, fully round camping area with no rectangles, no straight edges,
no sharp corners anywhere. Soft matte surfaces give every element a
handcrafted, toy-like quality. The palette is warm and cozy, centred on
orange firelight with warm tent colours and starry blue sky. The overall
feeling is a "living toy camp" — approachable, warm, and visually simple
enough for a preschool audience to read instantly.

---

## World Layout

The world reads as one continuous camping space rather than a set of separate
areas. The entrance leads into the grove, which flows naturally from fire
circle to tent area to star gazing point to story circle to quiet corner.
All zones are open and connected without barriers between them.

---

## Spatial Relationships

- The camp entrance is visible from the path.
- The fire circle can be seen from the tent area.
- The tent area is visible from the fire circle.
- The star gazing point provides a view of the sky.
- The story circle is visible from the fire circle.
- The quiet corner is visible from the story circle but feels separate.

---

## Playable Areas

**Camp Entrance** — Purpose: welcoming approach to the camp. Visual identity: round welcome stones, tents in background. Important props: stones, tents. Typical use: arrival, first fire sighting.

**Fire Circle** — Purpose: central campfire area. Visual identity: round stone fire pit, warm orange glow, safe contained fire. Important props: fire pit, stones, fire. Typical use: warmth, gathering.

**Tent Area** — Purpose: sleeping and rest zone. Visual identity: round tents in warm colours, soft interior light. Important props: tents. Typical use: rest, comfort.

**Star Gazing Point** — Purpose: sky watching zone. Visual identity: open sky, bright stars, tree canopy. Important props: sky, stars. Typical use: wonder, imagination.

**Story Circle** — Purpose: storytelling zone. Visual identity: fire circle with seating, warm glow. Important props: fire, stones. Typical use: storytelling, bonding.

**Quiet Corner** — Purpose: rest and calm zone. Visual identity: soft stones under tree shade, moonlight. Important props: stones, tree. Typical use: resting, reflecting.

---

## Materials

Tents: soft, round, warm fabric. Stones: smooth, round, warm grey. Fire:
contained, safe, warm orange glow. Grass: soft, natural green. Trees: round,
wide, providing shade. No hard surfaces, no sharp edges, no artificial
materials anywhere. Everything reads as natural and safe.

---

## Lighting

Primary light is warm firelight. Evening brings the first fire glow; night
is full of warm orange firelight with starlight above; late night is quiet
with embers; dawn brings soft golden light. Camp lighting is always warm
firelight mixed with starlight. Shadows are always soft — never hard, never
dark.

---

## Colour Palette

Fire: warm orange. Tents: warm red, yellow, green. Stones: warm grey. Sky:
deep blue with golden stars. Grass: soft green. The palette stays within
this warm, cozy set across every zone.

---

## Scale

Everything is sized for a small preschool child. The camp is intimate, not
vast — small enough to feel safe, large enough to feel like a real camp.
Tents are child-sized. The fire pit is low and safe. The camp never feels
overwhelming.

---

## Atmosphere

Warm. Soft. Safe. Cozy. Every surface reads as natural and gentle.
The mood is always inviting and comforting — full of warmth and gentle
light. The camp never feels dark or threatening.

---

## Consistency Rules

- The camp is always small, round, and warm — never dark, never vast.
- Fire stays contained and safe across every generation.
- Tents stay round and warm-coloured.
- Stones stay smooth and round.
- No open flames, no dangerous fire, no sharp rocks.
- No dark or high-contrast lighting.
- The camp's core shape, colours, and proportions are never redesigned or reinterpreted.

---

## Generation Considerations

The camp must be generated as a single warm camping space with safe fire,
not as a dark or threatening woodland — this is the defining trait of this
world and the most common failure point to watch for. Keep all shapes
rounded with no sharp corners anywhere. Keep the colour palette limited to
the warm cozy set defined above. Favor a soft, matte, natural material
language over any glossy, metallic, or realistic rendering. Treat the
camp's shape, colours, and proportions as a fixed identity to preserve
exactly, not to reinterpret, across every generation.

## Visual Richness Layer

> See `02-WORLDS/GLOBAL_ENVIRONMENT_STANDARD.md` § Visual Richness Principle.
> Camping Grove must never be reduced to "tents in a forest."

This layer protects Camping Grove from becoming too plain. Keep the round
stone fire circle readable and the cosy starlit-clearing silhouette clear —
add only the canon-approved charm details below. Do not add random objects.
Do not remove the warm, safe, gathering-place emotional identity.

**Allowed charm details:** CG-FIRE-01, the round stone-ringed campfire with
a warm contained flame, is the primary landmark and must be visible in
every scene here. CG-TENT-01, the 3-4 round colourful domed tents circling
the fire, adds landmark charm. The deep starlit night sky with many soft
glowing stars, plus warm firelight patterns dancing on nearby grass and
tent walls, add surface charm. Smooth round sitting stones near the fire
and a soft round moon above the treeline are the central reusable small
props. Gentle motion — the flicker and warm glow of CG-FIRE-01 and the soft
twinkling of the stars overhead — keeps the world feeling alive without
adding clutter.

**Forbidden clutter:** an open or uncontained flame, a missing fire circle,
dark or dense scary forest surroundings, an ordinary daytime sky instead of
stars, sharp corners anywhere, modern camping gear (metal, plastic, gas
equipment), and cold or desaturated night colours.

### Hero View Quality Tests

Before accepting a Hero View, it must pass:

1. **Silhouette Test** — recognisable as Camping Grove from the round stone fire circle and surrounding domed tents alone?
2. **Colour Test** — recognisable from the warm orange firelight, deep starlit sky, and colourful round tents alone?
3. **Charm Test** — does it make a child want to sit by the fire and listen to a story under the stars?

If any test fails, reject.
