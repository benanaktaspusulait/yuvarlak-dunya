Macera ve birliktelik hissi.

---

## Why This World Exists ⭐

Camping Grove introduces night scenes and campfire storytelling, expanding the series' emotional range beyond daytime adventures. The starry sky and warm firelight create intimate moments for character bonding and gentle narrative delivery. It teaches children about nighttime wonder while maintaining the series' signature safety and warmth.

## Emotional Purpose

Camping Grove, çocukların geceyi güvenli ve sıcak hissettiği bir mekan. Kamp ateşi etrafında toplanmak, yıldızları izlemek, masal dinlemek — burada gece korku değil, macera hissi verir.

---

## Play Philosophy

Kurallar yok, birlikte yaratmak var. Çocuklar ateş başında hikaye anlatır, yıldızlara bakar ve hayal eder. Yarışma yok, işbirliği ve hayal gücü var. Her an birlikte yaşanır.

---

## Material Language

Çadırlar yuvarlak, taşlar yumuşak, ateş sıcak ama güvenli. Her şey doğal ve el yapımı hissi veriyor — sert köşe yok, sıcaklık her yerde. Oyuncak sıcaklığında bir kamp.

## Canonical OpenArt Reference Pack

### Reference 01 — Hero View (100% environment, no characters)

Full camping grove at twilight with round tents and safe campfire. Starry sky above, warm orange glow from fire. Round stones surrounding fire pit. Peaceful atmosphere with gentle night sounds implied. No characters present.

### Reference 02 — Entrance View

Camping grove entrance path with round welcome stones. Tents visible in background with warm interior lights. Campfire glow drawing visitors in. Safe, inviting nighttime atmosphere. Child-friendly perspective.

### Reference 03 — Trail View (mid-environment)

View from inside campfire circle looking outward. Tents arranged in protective circle. Stars visible through tree canopy above. Warm firelight on nearby surfaces creating safe perimeter. Depth through firelight to darkness beyond.

### Reference 04 — Detail View

Close-up of round campfire stones with warm embers. Safe flame contained within stone circle. Warm orange glow on nearby grass. Macro perspective showing safe fire design. Soft nighttime lighting.

---

## OpenArt Production Rules

- Maximum 4 reference images per environment
- Hero View is mandatory for all OpenArt prompts
- Consistent warm firelight and starlight colour palette across all references
- No characters in any reference images — environment only
- Fire must appear contained and safe (round stones)
- Night scenes must maintain warmth, not fear
- Maintain visual consistency with existing ENV-021 asset

## Production Notes

### Best Characters

- All characters together (group scenes)
- Tillo (storyteller by fire)
- Kiko (stargazer)

### Avoid

- Open flames or dangerous fire
- Dark or scary forest backgrounds
- Cold or threatening atmospheres
- Sharp rocks near fire

### Typical Duration

30-60 seconds per scene

### Best Emotional Themes

- Togetherness and bonding
- Wonder and stargazing
- Storytelling and imagination
- Warmth and comfort
