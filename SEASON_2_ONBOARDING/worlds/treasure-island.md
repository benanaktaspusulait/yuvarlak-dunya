## World Description

A small, hidden island in the middle of a gentle pond.

The island has soft sand, round stones, and a single palm-like tree. Under the tree, there's a small treasure chest filled with smooth, colourful stones.

This is a place of adventure and discovery.

## World Introduction Video Concept

### Title: "Welcome to Treasure Island"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Gentle water, ripples | Soft water |
| 5–10 sec | Camera reveals the island | Quiet, mysterious |
| 10–15 sec | Single tree, treasure chest | Gentle breeze |
| 15–20 sec | Luca points excitedly | Happy |
| 20–25 sec | They cross the shallow water | Splash |
| 25–30 sec | They reach the island | Soft sand |
| 30–35 sec | They open the treasure chest | Soft creak |
| 35–40 sec | Colourful stones inside | Shiny |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Treasure Island" | Adventure jingle |

**Key Moment:** Opening the treasure chest — this becomes the island's signature.
