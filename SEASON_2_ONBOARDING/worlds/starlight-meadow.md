## World Description

A quiet meadow that glows softly under the stars.

The grass is soft and silver-blue. Tiny lights float above the flowers like fallen stars. The air is cool and peaceful.

This is a place for bedtime stories and quiet moments.

---

| Element | Detail |

## Hero View Concept

Wide shot of the meadow at night. Stars above, silver-blue grass below, tiny lights floating. Kiko and Opa sit together.

## World Introduction Video Concept

### Title: "Welcome to Starlight Meadow"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Night sky, stars twinkle | Crickets |
| 5–10 sec | Camera moves down to the meadow | Quiet, peaceful |
| 10–15 sec | Silver-blue grass glows softly | Soft wind |
| 15–20 sec | Tiny lights float above flowers | Gentle shimmer |
| 20–25 sec | Opa sits under a tree | Quiet |
| 25–30 sec | Kiko walks in, sleepy | Soft footsteps |
| 30–35 sec | They sit together, looking at stars | Warm, safe |
| 35–40 sec | Opa points at a star | Soft chime |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Starlight Meadow" | Gentle lullaby |

**Key Moment:** The tiny floating lights — this becomes the meadow's signature.
