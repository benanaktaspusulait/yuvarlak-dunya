## World Description

A green hill where the trees make music.

When you tap the trees, they play different notes. When the wind blows, the leaves create soft melodies.

The hill is bright and cheerful. Flowers bloom in time with the music.

## World Introduction Video Concept

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Green hill, round trees | Soft wind |
| 5–10 sec | Camera moves up the hill | Gentle melody |

| 15–20 sec | Arda taps another tree — different note | Different chime |
| 20–25 sec | They look at each other, surprised | Quiet wonder |
| 25–30 sec | They tap more trees — melody builds | Gentle music |
| 30–35 sec | Flowers sway to the rhythm | Soft rustle |
| 35–40 sec | They dance together | Happy |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Music Hill" | Musical jingle |

**Key Moment:** The trees making music when tapped — this becomes the hill's signature.
