## World Description

A soft, quiet valley where everything is gently sleepy.

The trees have soft, droopy leaves. The flowers close their petals. The stream flows slowly. The air feels warm and drowsy.

This is the ultimate bedtime world.

Wide shot of the valley at sunset. Trees with droopy leaves. Slow stream. Warm golden light. Everything looks drowsy and peaceful.

## World Introduction Video Concept

### Title: "Welcome to Sleepy Hollow"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Sunset, warm golden light | Crickets |
| 5–10 sec | Camera moves into the valley | Quiet, sleepy |
| 10–15 sec | Trees with droopy leaves | Soft wind |
| 15–20 sec | Flowers close their petals | Gentle rustle |
| 20–25 sec | Stream flows slowly | Soft water |
| 25–30 sec | A character yawns | Soft yawn |
| 30–35 sec | They sit under a tree | Quiet |
| 35–40 sec | They close their eyes | Peaceful |
| 40–45 sec | Title card: "Sleepy Hollow" | Lullaby |

**Key Moment:** The flowers closing their petals — this becomes the hollow's signature.
