## Character Description

Fifi is a small, bright bird who loves to fly high and see the world from above.

She is cheerful, energetic, and loves sharing what she sees.

She does not speak yet — she communicates through chirps, wing flutters, and dive-bombs.

She is adventurous but kind. She always comes back to her friends.

## Intro Video Concept

### Title: "Meet Fifi!"

**Duration:** 30–45 seconds

**Script:**

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–5 sec | Cloud Hill, open sky | Soft wind |
| 5–10 sec | A small bird circles high above | Distant chirp |
| 10–15 sec | Fifi dives down, loops, pulls up | Chirp, wing flutter |
| 15–20 sec | Fifi lands on a branch, tilts head | Quiet pause |
| 20–25 sec | Fifi chirps at Kiko, who looks up | Cheerful chirp |
| 25–30 sec | Fifi takes off again, circles once | Wing flutter |
| 30–35 sec | Title card: "Fifi" | Soft jingle |

**Key Moment:** Fifi's loop-de-loop — this becomes her signature gesture.

## First Story Use

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Episode | Season 2, Episode 5 |
| Title | "The Stream Singer" |
| Characters | Kiko, Mimo |
| World | Rainbow Creek |
| Fifi's Role | Visual cameo — flies across the sky |
| Speaking | None |

**Story beat:** Kiko and Mimo sit by the creek. A small bird flies across the sky, chirping. Kiko waves. The bird does a loop and flies away.

**Purpose:** Build recognition. Children see Fifi, learn she is fast and cheerful, associate her with sky and freedom.

---

## Regular Episode Use (After Onboarding)

After Episode 5, Fifi can appear in:
- Cloud Hill episodes (background or supporting)
- Kite Field episodes (main or supporting)
- Any episode needing a "sky observer" character

Do NOT place Fifi in complex multi-character episodes until Season 3.

---

## Voice Notes (Future — Season 3)

When Fifi speaks:
- Quick, bright voice
- Short sentences
- Chirps between words
- "Look!" or "Up there!" as common phrases
- Never slow, never heavy
