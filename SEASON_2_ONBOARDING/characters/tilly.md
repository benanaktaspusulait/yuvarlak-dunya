## Character Description

Tilly is a small, gentle turtle who moves slowly and thinks carefully.

She loves gardens, flowers, and quiet paths.

She does not speak yet — she communicates through slow nods, gentle hums, and patient waiting.

She is wise beyond her size. She never rushes.

## First Story Use

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Episode | Season 2, Episode 3 |
| Title | "The Garden Helper" |
| Characters | Kiko, Tillo |
| World | Tillo's Garden |
| Tilly's Role | Visual cameo — walks slowly through garden |
| Speaking | None |

**Story beat:** Kiko helps Tillo in the garden. A small turtle walks slowly along the path. Tillo says "That's Tilly. She comes every spring." Tilly nods at the flowers.

**Purpose:** Build recognition. Children see Tilly, learn she is slow and wise, associate her with gardens.

---

## Regular Episode Use (After Onboarding)

After Episode 3, Tilly can appear in:
- Tillo's Garden episodes (background or supporting)
- Flower Hill episodes (background or supporting)
- Any episode needing a "patient observer" character

Do NOT place Tilly in complex multi-character episodes until Season 3.

---

## Voice Notes (Future — Season 3)

When Tilly speaks:
- Slow, deliberate voice
- Short sentences
- Pauses between words
- "Hmm..." or "Yes..." before speaking
- Never rushed, never loud
