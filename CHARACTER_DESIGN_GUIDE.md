# Pompom Hills - Character Design Guide v2.1

---

## 1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Core Principle
Every character should look like a soft toy that a toddler would want to hug. No sharp edges, no complex details, no realistic features. Everything is round, soft, and simple.

### Toy-Like Quality
- Characters look like they could be picked up
- Soft, plush appearance
- Simple shapes that are easy to remember
- Clear, readable expressions from far away

### Emotional Clarity
- Expressions are instantly readable
- No subtle or complex emotions
- Happy, sad, sleepy, curious, surprised, proud
- Always clear, never ambiguous

## 2. PROPORTIONS

### Kiko (Human Child)

**Head to Body Ratio:**
- Head: 40% of total height
- Body: 60% of total height
- Very large head for cuteness

**Head:**
- Perfectly round
- Width: 1.2x body width
- No neck visible

**Eyes:**
- Very large (30% of face width)
- Round, bright
- White highlights (two per eye)
- Centred on face

**Mouth:**
- Small (15% of face width)
- Simple curve
- Never wide open (except yawning)

**Nose:**
- Tiny, round
- Almost invisible
- Just a small circle

**Cheeks:**
- Rosy pink circles
- On each side of face
- Always visible

**Arms:**
- Short, chubby
- No elbows visible
- Simple圆 shape
- Hands are round, no fingers defined

**Legs:**
- Short, chubby
- No knees visible
- Simple圆 shape
- Feet are round, no toes defined

**Overall Shape:**
- Like a snowman with arms
- Very soft, very round
- No angular features

### Mimi (Rabbit)

**Head to Body Ratio:**
- Head: 35% of total height
- Body: 65% of total height
- Ears add extra height

**Head:**
- Round, soft
- Width: 1.0x body width
- Merges with body (no neck)

**Ears:**
- Long (50% of body height)
- Floppy, soft
- Round tips
- Pink inside

**Eyes:**
- Large (25% of face width)
- Round, bright green
- White highlights

**Nose:**
- Small, pink
- Round
- Always visible

**Whiskers:**
- Thin, white
- 3 on each side
- Very subtle

**Body:**
- Round, soft
- White belly (round patch)
- Fluffy tail (small, round)

**Arms:**
- Short, round
- Blue fur
- White paws (round)

**Legs:**
- Short, round
- Blue fur
- White paws (round)

**Overall Shape:**
- Like a round bunny plush
- Ears are most distinctive feature
- Very soft, very cuddly

### Opa (Owl)

**Head to Body Ratio:**
- Head: 40% of total height
- Body: 60% of total height
- Wings add width

**Head:**
- Round, soft
- Width: 1.1x body width
- Merges with body

**Eyes:**
- Large (35% of face width)
- Round, warm brown
- Behind round golden glasses
- White highlights

**Beak:**
- Small, orange
- Round, not sharp
- Friendly appearance

**Feather Tufts:**
- Small tufts on head
- Like hair
- Green, soft

**Body:**
- Round, soft
- Light green belly (round patch)
- Green feathers everywhere

**Wings:**
- Short, round
- Like arms
- Can gesture
- Green feathers

**Feet:**
- Small, round
- Orange
- Three toes (round)

**Tail:**
- Short, fluffy
- Green
- Round tip

**Overall Shape:**
- Like a round owl plush
- Glasses are most distinctive feature
- Wise but soft appearance

## 5. EXPRESSION GUIDE

### Happy Expression

**Kiko:**
- Wide smile (mouth curved up)
- Eyes wide and bright
- Cheeks rosy
- Arms slightly raised
- Body bounces

**Mimi:**
- Wide smile
- Eyes wide and bright
- Ears perked up
- Tail wags
- Body bounces

**Opa:**
- Gentle smile
- Eyes crinkle at corners
- Head tilts slightly
- Wings slightly open
- Body puffs up slightly

### Sad Expression

**Kiko:**
- Downturned mouth
- Eyes droopy
- Cheeks less rosy
- Arms down
- Body slumps

**Mimi:**
- Downturned mouth
- Eyes droopy
- Ears down
- Tail still
- Body slumps

**Opa:**
- Slight frown
- Eyes soft
- Head tilted down
- Wings close to body
- Body lowers

### Sleepy Expression

**Kiko:**
- Half-closed eyes
- Small yawn
- Mouth open slightly
- Arms limp
- Body sways

**Mimi:**
- Half-closed eyes
- Ears drooping
- Mouth closed
- Body droops
- May lie down

**Opa:**
- Half-closed eyes
- Glasses slide down slightly
- Beak slightly open
- Wings droop
- Body puffs up to rest

### Curious Expression

**Kiko:**
- Eyes wide
- One eyebrow raised
- Head tilted
- One hand on chin
- Leaning forward

**Mimi:**
- Eyes wide
- Ears perked forward
- Head tilted
- Nose twitching
- Leaning forward

**Opa:**
- Eyes wide
- Head tilted
- Glasses adjusted
- One wing up
- Leaning forward

### Surprised Expression

**Kiko:**
- Eyes very wide

**Mimi:**
- Eyes very wide
- Ears straight up
- Mouth small
- Body jumps
- Tail puffs

**Opa:**
- Eyes wide
- Beak opens slightly
- Feathers ruffle
- Wings spread slightly
- Body lifts

### Proud Expression

**Kiko:**
- Big smile
- Chest out

- Head up

**Mimi:**
- Big smile
- Chest out
- Ears up
- Tail up
- Sparkle in eyes

**Opa:**

## 6. MOVEMENT GUIDE

### Kiko Movement

**Walking:**
- Gentle bounce with each step
- Arms swing softly
- Pigtails bounce
- Steps are soft and light

**Running (short distances):**
- Slightly faster bounce
- Arms swing more
- Hair bounces more
- Still soft, never fast

**Jumping:**
- Small, soft jumps
- Both feet together
- Arms up for balance
- Lands softly

**Sitting:**
- Slowly lowers
- Legs fold under
- Hands in lap
- Settles gently

**Standing Up:**
- Pushes up slowly
- Balances
- Straightens clothes
- Ready to go

### Mimi Movement

**Hopping:**
- Main movement style
- Soft, bouncy hops
- Ears flop with each hop
- Front paws together

**Walking:**
- Small steps
- Body sways
- Ears sway
- Tail bounces

**Sitting:**
- Lowers on hind legs
- Front paws together
- Ears relax
- Settles comfortably

**Lying Down:**
- Rolls to side
- Ears spread out
- Eyes close
- Relaxes completely

**Ear Movement:**
- Perked up: curious, happy
- Floppy: relaxed, sleepy
- Straight up: surprised, alert
- Down: sad, scared

### Opa Movement

**Flying:**
- Slow, gentle wing flaps
- Glides more than flaps
- Soft, graceful
- Never fast, never急

**Landing:**
- Slows down
- Extends feet
- Soft touchdown
- Settles wings

**Perching:**
- Sits comfortably
- Adjusts glasses
- Opens wings slightly
- Settles in

**Gesturing:**
- Uses wings like hands
- Points with wing tip
- Opens wings for emphasis
- Adjusts glasses

## 7. INTERACTION PATTERNS

### Kiko and Mimi

**When Happy Together:**
- Walk side by side
- Look at each other
- Smile at same time
- Bounce together

**When Exploring Together:**
- Kiko leads (curious)
- Mimi follows (loyal)
- They stop together
- They discover together

**When Comforting:**
- Kiko hugs Mimi
- Mimi nuzzles Kiko
- They sit together
- They hold hands/paws

**When Playing:**
- Chase each other (gently)
- Hide and seek (simple)
- Share toys
- Laugh together

### Kiko and Opa

**When Learning:**
- Kiko sits near Opa
- Opa tells story
- Kiko listens carefully
- Kiko nods

**When Seeking Help:**
- Kiko approaches Opa
- Kiko asks question
- Opa responds warmly
- Kiko feels safe

**When Saying Goodbye:**
- Kiko waves
- Opa waves wing
- Kiko blows kiss
- Opa smiles

### Mimi and Opa

**When Listening:**
- Mimi sits near Opa
- Mimi looks up at Opa
- Ears perked
- Very still

**When Comforted:**
- Opa pats Mimi's head
- Mimi leans in
- Soft moment
- Safe feeling

### All Three Together

**When Gathered:**
- Kiko and Mimi sit together
- Opa perches above
- Everyone smiles
- Safe, warm feeling

**When Discovering:**
- Kiko points
- Mimi looks
- Opa nods
- Together they learn

**When Saying Goodnight:**
- Kiko waves
- Mimi yawns
- Opa smiles
- Stars twinkle

## 8. DESIGN CONSISTENCY RULES

### Always Include

**Kiko:**
- Round face
- Big brown eyes
- Brown pigtails with coral pink bands
- Coral pink shirt
- White shorts
- Rosy cheeks
- Gentle smile

**Mimi:**
- Round blue body
- White belly
- Long floppy blue ears (pink inside)
- Green eyes
- Pink nose
- Yellow t-shirt
- White tail

**Opa:**
- Round green body
- Light green belly
- Brown eyes with golden glasses
- Orange beak
- Brown shawl
- Feather tufts on head

### Never Change

**Kiko:**
- Eye colour (brown)
- Hair colour (brown)
- Outfit colour (coral pink + white)
- Body shape (round, soft)
- Expression style (gentle)

**Mimi:**
- Fur colour (soft blue)
- Eye colour (green)
- Belly colour (white)
- Ear shape (long, floppy)
- Body shape (round, soft)

**Opa:**
- Feather colour (warm green)
- Eye colour (brown)
- Glasses (round, golden)
- Beak colour (orange)
- Body shape (round, soft)

### Size Relationships
- Kiko is tallest (child height)
- Mimi is slightly shorter (rabbit height)
- Opa is medium (owl height when perched)
- When flying, Opa can be any height

### Colour Balance
- Each character has one main colour
- Main colours are distinct (pink, blue, green)
- Outfits are simple (1-2 colours max)
- No patterns, no complex designs

**Always Include:**
1. Character name
2. Species/type
3. Main colour
4. Key features (eyes, hair, outfit)
5. Body shape (round, soft)
6. Style (toy-like, 3D cartoon)
7. Lighting (warm, soft)
8. Background (clean or specified location)
