- Freckles across nose and cheeks

### Body
- Preschool child proportions
- Rounded arms and legs
- Small soft hands
- Friendly open posture

### Outfit
- Blue striped short-sleeve shirt
- Small denim-blue chest pocket

## Personality

### Core Traits
- Funny: Tries to make friends laugh
- Talkative: Shares ideas quickly
- Imaginative: Turns toys into stories
- Playful: Loves games and pretend play
- Cheerful: Keeps group energy bright

### Likes
- Building things
- Jokes
- Board games
- Dinosaurs
- Small adventures with friends

### Dislikes
- Being bored
- Waiting in line
- Losing games
- Play that becomes too serious
