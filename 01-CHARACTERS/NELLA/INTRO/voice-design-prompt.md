# Nella - Canonical Voice Design

## Voice Design Prompt

Warm adult female voice, around 35 to 45 years old. Calm, patient, nurturing and reassuring, with a soft cheerful warmth. Clear neutral English, medium-low pitch, smooth natural tone and slow preschool-friendly pacing. Low-to-medium energy with a gentle smile in the voice. She should sound safe, kind and encouraging to young children.

## Test Text

Hello, Kiko. I am Nella.
I will be very gentle.
Let us count your smile.
One, two, sparkle.
You are doing very well.
All happy and healthy.

## Negative Voice Traits

Avoid elderly, teenage, babyish, high-pitched, loud, strict, clinical, robotic, breathy, dramatic, sleepy, flat, regional, sarcastic, nervous, cold, shouting, and sing-song delivery.

## Canonical Save Rule

After approval, save this voice as Nella Canonical Voice. Do not redesign or regenerate it in future episodes. Always reuse the same saved voice.
