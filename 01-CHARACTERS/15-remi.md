# Remi v1.0

## Overview
- **Role**: Care Helper of Pompom Hills (gentle health check-up helper)
- **Age**: Kind grown-up (helper age)
- **Type**: Bear
- **Main colour**: Honey Tan (#D7B98E) + Soft Blue care scarf (#90CAF9)
- **Catchphrase**: "All strong and healthy!"
- **First appearance**: Brave Little Days EP03 — "Kiko's Check-Up Day"

> Remi, Brave Little Days life-skills alt-serisi için tanıtılan ikinci yumuşak "helper"
> karakteridir (ilki: Nella, tooth helper). Çekirdek 13 karakterin anlatı çıpasını değiştirmez;
> doktor/check-up temalı bölümlerde tekrar kullanılabilir güvenli bir yetişkin-yardımcıdır.
> Referans: `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md`.

---

## Physical Appearance

### Face
- Round, soft, friendly face
- Big kind brown eyes
- Small round black nose
- Warm, calm, reassuring smile

### Body
- Big, round, soft, plush body — like a cuddly soft toy
- Warm honey-tan fur, cream belly
- Short round arms and legs, soft round paws
- Feels like a warm, safe hug

### Outfit
- Soft blue care scarf / light care coat (round pockets)
- No mask, no gloves, no clinical white uniform
- Soft blue = calm/care cue, never sterile white

---

## Personality

### Core Traits
- Gentle: slow, soft movements; never sudden
- Calm: a big, reassuring, unhurried presence
- Warm: makes check-ups feel like a friendly visit
- Patient: waits as long as a child needs
- Encouraging: celebrates with a warm smile and a star sticker

### Emotional Function
Remi models that **check-ups are safe and helpers are kind**. A big, soft, calm grown-up
who makes health visits feel warm instead of scary.

---

## Speech Style

### Repeated Phrases
- "All strong and healthy!"
- "I'll be very gentle."
- "Let's listen to your heart."
- "You did so well!"

Voice: warm, slow, soft, deep-but-gentle, reassuring. Never loud. Never clinical.

---

## Props (Safe / Non-Scary Only)

| Prop | Notes |
|---|---|
| Soft "listening heart" | A friendly rounded stethoscope with soft, padded ends |
| Gentle ear light | Soft, dim, warm glow — NOT a bright surgical lamp |
| Soft height chart | Friendly wall chart to measure how big you've grown |
| Star stickers | Warm reward at the end |

**Forbidden:** needles, syringe, vaccination, sharp instruments, bright surgical lamp, mask,
gloves, cold metal tools, drilling. See `00-CORE/32_BRAVE_LITTLE_DAYS_LIFE_SKILLS_FORMAT.md` §4.
