## Physical Appearance

### Face
- Round, soft, friendly face
- Big kind brown eyes
- Small round black nose
- Two small, rounded, friendly front teeth (never large or scary)
- Warm, calm, reassuring smile
- Soft round glasses (caring-helper look)

### Body
- Round, soft, plush body — like a soft toy
- Warm chestnut-brown fur, cream belly
- Short round arms and legs, small round paws
- Flat rounded beaver tail (soft, cartoon, not detailed)

### Outfit
- Soft mint-green helper apron (round pocket)
- No mask, no gloves, no clinical uniform
- Mint = gentle "care/clean" cue, never sterile white

---

## Personality

### Core Traits
- Gentle: moves and speaks slowly, never sudden
- Calm: never rushes a nervous child
- Warm: greets with a smile, makes visiting feel safe
- Patient: waits as long as a child needs
- Encouraging: celebrates small brave steps

### Emotional Function
Nella models that **helpers are kind and visiting them is safe**. She is the reassuring
grown-up in health/care situations — the "it's okay, I'll be gentle" presence.

---

## Speech Style

### Repeated Phrases
- "Let's count your smile!"
- "One, two, sparkle!"
- "All happy and healthy!"
- "I'll be very gentle."

Voice: warm, slow, soft, reassuring. Never loud. Never clinical.
