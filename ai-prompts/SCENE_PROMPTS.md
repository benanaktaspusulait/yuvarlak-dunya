# Pompom Hills - Scene And Poster Prompts

Production prompts for moodboards, lineup images, interaction scenes, loopable backgrounds, and README hero/poster assets.

Use these after `ai-prompts/VISUAL_REFERENCES.md`, `ai-prompts/ENVIRONMENT_PROMPTS.md`, `ai-prompts/TECHNICAL_SHEETS.md`, and `ai-prompts/ADVANCED_PRODUCTION_PROMPTS.md` are approved.

## 1. Shared Scene Style

Append this style foundation to scene prompts unless the prompt defines a stricter visual mode.

```text
Pompom Hills world, rounded shapes, pastel colours, toy-like 3D animation look, soft matte clay surfaces, warm gentle lighting, toddler-safe emotional tone, clean readable composition, no sharp edges, no realistic textures, no scary shadows, no extra characters, no changed outfits, no watermark
```

## 7. Usage Order

1. Generate the moodboard first to align visual identity.
2. Generate the character lineup after front views and scale chart are approved.
3. Generate interaction scenes after character and prop references are locked.
4. Generate minimal backgrounds for animation tests and motion loops.
5. Generate the hero poster last, after world, character, scale, and globe references are approved.

## 8. Scene QA Checklist

- Kiko remains 100 units with coral shirt and white shorts.
- Mimi remains 80 units, soft blue, and in yellow t-shirt.
- Opa remains 120 units but low, rounded, and non-authoritarian.
- Scene prompts contain no extra characters.
- Text appears only in moodboard or poster outputs.
- Backgrounds are simple enough for 3-4 year olds to parse in under 2 seconds.
- No hard shadows, photoreal textures, sharp edges, cliffs, weapons, or city details.
