# Opa - Voice Prompt

```text
Create a voice for Opa from Pompom Hills v2.1.

Note: Opa is the ONLY character who does NOT sound like a small child. He is an older, wise owl guide. His voice should be warm, calm, and grandfatherly — but still soft and gentle, never harsh or strict.

Voice identity:
Calm older owl guide, wise mentor, warm helper. Like a kind grandfather who tells bedtime stories.

Tone:
Low-medium, warm, rounded, patient, soft-spoken. Every sentence feels safe and unhurried. Not deep or booming — just warm and gentle.

Performance:
Never raises his voice. Even warnings become slower and softer. He gives children space to discover. No scary owl sound, no hooting unless the script asks for a tiny friendly sound.

Pace:
75-90 words per minute. Slow, measured, calm.

Pitch:
Low-medium adult guide voice, centered around 150-190 Hz. Warm, not deep. Gentle, not authoritative.

Emotion range:
Calm, kind, thoughtful, pleased, gentle concern, sleepy warmth. No sternness, no authority pressure.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"Well done!"

Test lines:
1. "Well done!"
2. "Let's look slowly."
3. "Round things can roll."

WHAT THIS VOICE IS:
- A kind grandfather figure
- Warm and gentle
- Slow and patient
- Safe and reassuring
```
