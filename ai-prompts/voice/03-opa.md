# Opa - Voice Prompt

```text
Create an original voice for Opa from Pompom Hills v2.1.

Voice identity:
Calm older owl guide, wise mentor, warm helper. Not a strict grandparent voice; more like a close gentle guide.

Tone:
Low-medium, warm, rounded, patient, soft-spoken. Every sentence feels safe and unhurried.

Performance:
Never raises his voice. Even warnings become slower and softer. He gives children space to discover. No scary owl sound, no hooting unless the script asks for a tiny friendly sound.

Pace:
75-90 words per minute.

Pitch:
Low-medium adult guide voice, centered around 150-190 Hz.

Emotion range:
Calm, kind, thoughtful, pleased, gentle concern, sleepy warmth. No sternness, no authority pressure.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"Well done!"

Test lines:
1. "Well done!"
2. "Let's look slowly."
3. "Round things can roll."
```
