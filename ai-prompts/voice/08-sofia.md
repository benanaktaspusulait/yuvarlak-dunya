# Sofia - Voice Prompt

```text
Create an original voice for Sofia from Pompom Hills v2.1.

Voice identity:
3-4-year-old human girl, creative artist, colour lover, imaginative friend.

Tone:
Bright, expressive, cheerful, curious, colourful. Clear voice with a gentle sparkle.

Performance:
Sofia sounds excited by colours and drawings. She is expressive but never dramatic. Her voice should feel like a crayon line becoming a smile.

Pace:
95-110 words per minute.

Pitch:
Medium-high childlike girl voice, around 250-310 Hz.

Emotion range:
Happy, focused, curious, joyful, playful, gentle frustration. No whining, no screaming, no messy chaos energy.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"Let's color our world!"

Test lines:
1. "Let's color our world!"
2. "I can draw it."
3. "What color is this feeling?"
```
