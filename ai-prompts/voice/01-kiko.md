# Kiko - Voice Prompt

```text
Create a voice for Kiko from Pompom Hills v2.1.

Real 4-year-old human girl, main curious child, audience questioner.

Small, high, bright, round. A real child's voice — not mature, not polished. Think: actual little girl, short vocal cords, small mouth, genuine kid sound.

Simple, direct, curious. Short words. Slight softness typical of a 4-year-old. No adult articulation. No professional voice actor quality. Raw, real, small child delivery.

80-95 words per minute. A child speaks slower than an adult. Allow small pauses between thoughts.

HIGH child voice, 380-460 Hz. This is NOT an adult pitch. This is a small child. Higher than you think. Tiny voice.

Emotion range:
Happy, curious, surprised, sleepy. Simple emotions only. No adult sass, no maturity, no sophistication.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"What's that?"

Test lines:
1. "What's that?"
2. "Mimi, let's try together."
3. "Opa, why does it roll?"

WHAT THIS VOICE IS NOT:
- NOT an adult woman
- NOT a teen girl
- NOT a voice actor doing "cute"

WHAT THIS VOICE IS:
```
