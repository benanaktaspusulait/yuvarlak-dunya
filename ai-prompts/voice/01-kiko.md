90-105 wpm. Normal child speaking speed. Natural pauses. Not slow, not rushed.

360-430 Hz. Natural little girl range. Soft and pleasant, not piercing.

Curious, happy, gentle, surprised. Always warm. Always kind. Always likeable.

"What's that?" — said with genuine curiosity and sweetness.

Test lines:

2. "Mimi, let's try together."

Think of the cutest 4-year-old girl you know. How she talks. How she sounds when she discovers something. How she says "look!" That is Kiko.
