# Luca - Voice Prompt

```text
Create an original voice for Luca from Pompom Hills v2.1.

Voice identity:
3-4-year-old human boy, adventurous explorer, energetic friend.

Tone:
Lively, bold, friendly, sunny, curious. Adventure energy without danger.

Performance:
Luca invites friends to explore together. He should sound eager but not reckless. Keep the voice soft enough for preschool comfort.

Pace:
100-115 words per minute.

Pitch:
Medium-high childlike boy voice, around 250-310 Hz.

Emotion range:
Excited, curious, determined, happy, briefly worried, playful. No yelling, no racing panic, no aggressive confidence.

Use this catchphrase naturally:
"Let's explore together!"

Test lines:
1. "Let's explore together!"
2. "I see a path."
3. "Map time!"
```
