# Pompom Hills - Technical Sheet Prompts v2.1

Turnaround, scale, prop ve storyboard sheet promptları. Her prompt `{style}`, `{camera}`, `{lighting}` içerir.

## Variables

```text
{style} = Pompom Hills v2.1, rounded preschool toy world, pastel colors, matte clay-plush surfaces, toddler-safe emotional clarity
{camera} = orthographic or stable 70mm reference camera, centered studio sheet, no perspective distortion
{lighting} = even studio lighting, soft shadow opacity below 15 percent, no dramatic contrast
```
