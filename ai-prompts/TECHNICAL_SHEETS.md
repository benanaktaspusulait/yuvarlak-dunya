# Pompom Hills - Technical Sheet Prompts v2.1

Master character sheet, turnaround, scale, prop ve storyboard sheet promptları. Her prompt `{style}`, `{camera}`, `{lighting}` içerir.

## Variables

```text
{style} = Pompom Hills v2.1, rounded preschool toy world, pastel colors, matte clay-plush surfaces, toddler-safe emotional clarity
{camera} = orthographic or stable 70mm reference camera, centered studio sheet, no perspective distortion
{lighting} = even studio lighting, soft shadow opacity below 15 percent, no dramatic contrast
```

## Production Character Bible Sheet Standard

Bu bolum, tek satirlik karakter promptlari yerine resmi karakter incili sayfasi uretmek icindir. Her karakter promptu 16:9 landscape, ultra temiz beyaz arka plan, minimal infographic layout, bol bosluk, soft shadow ve okunabilir typography ister. Promptlar `NEGATIVE_PROMPTS.md` ile birlikte kullanilmalidir.

Tum master character sheet promptlari su panelleri ister:

- Large title with character name, `POMPOM HILLS v2.1`, and `Character Sheet`.
- Left-side large hero render looking toward camera with friendly preschool-safe expression.
- Center-top turnaround: front, 3/4 front, side, 3/4 back, back.
- Top-right size and proportions infographic with 100-unit scale logic.
- About panel with age, role, personality, catchphrase, likes, dislikes, and simple matching icons.
- Expression sheet with 10 gentle expressions: happy, curious, excited, surprised, thinking, laughing, sad, sleepy, shy, playful.
- Pose sheet with 6 full-body personality poses.
- Details panel with close-ups for face, eyes, hair/fur/feathers, nose/beak, mouth, hands/paws/wings, feet, tail if any, clothes texture, and accessories.
- Color palette with labeled swatches and HEX values.
- Outfit and accessories separated neatly.
- Green consistency checklist: always rounded shapes, large expressive eyes, no sharp edges, soft materials, rosy cheeks where applicable, consistent proportions, family-friendly design, preschool safe.
- Short character summary paragraph.
- Bottom scale comparison with other Pompom Hills characters; silhouettes may be used if necessary.
