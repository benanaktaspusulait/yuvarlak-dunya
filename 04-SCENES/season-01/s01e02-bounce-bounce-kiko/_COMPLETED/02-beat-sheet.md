# Bounce, Bounce, Kiko! — Beat Sheet v2.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1: Kiko finds a yellow ball on Flower Hill, starts bouncing it.
Beat 2: Ball rolls to Mimi's burrow. Mimi joins the game.
Beat 3: They play in Butterfly Meadow. Ball rolls to Central Square.
Beat 4: Opa says 'Well done!' They smile together.
```
