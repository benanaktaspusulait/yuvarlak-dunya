# Opa's Storytime: The Sleepy Leaf — Episode Summary

---

## Story

At sunset, Kiko and Noah settle onto a story blanket near the Big Pompom Tree in
Central Square. Opa tells a gentle story about a busy little leaf who never wanted
to stop dancing in the wind, until it learns that resting is part of play too.
Kiko and Noah ask questions and react throughout the story.

## Learning

Rest • Calm • Bedtime routine

## Characters

Opa, Kiko, Noah

## World
