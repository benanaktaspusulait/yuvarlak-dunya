# The Little Cloud That Waited — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Mimi arrive at the Reading Corner, sit near Opa. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa opens the book. "Once there was a little cloud." Luca asks if it was tiny.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): The cloud sees flowers waiting below. Mimi asks if they were thirsty.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Luca asks why the cloud didn't rain right away. Opa: "The wind whispered, wait."
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The flowers wake up but are not ready. Luca reacts with wonder.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): One gentle drop finally falls. Mimi asks if the flowers opened.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa gives the lesson through the story. Luca connects it to himself: "I can wait too."
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Quiet warm hold. Book closes gently. No new dialogue, just warmth.
```
