# The Little Seed That Listened — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Kiko welcomes Opa and Mimi into her home reading corner. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa begins: "Once, deep in the soil, there was a little seed." Kiko asks if it was scared in the dark.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): The seed hears many sounds — rain, worms, wind. Mimi asks what sound it liked best.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Kiko wonders which sound the seed should follow. Opa: "It listened to all of them, gently."
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The seed waits patiently, still listening. Kiko reacts with quiet patience.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): A small green shoot appears — the seed grew when it was ready. Mimi gasps softly with joy.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa gives the lesson. Kiko connects it to herself: "I listen with my ears and my heart."
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Quiet cosy hold in the home reading corner. No new dialogue, just warmth.
```
