# The Quiet Bird — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Mimi sit with Opa in the Reading Corner as rain falls softly outside. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa begins: "Once, in a forest full of chatting birds, there was one quiet bird." Luca asks why she never sang loudly.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): All the other birds talked and sang, but the quiet bird just listened. Mimi reacts, recognising herself a little.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Luca and Mimi both ask why the bird stayed so quiet. Opa explains gently.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): Because she was quiet, the bird noticed things the others missed — a soft rustle, a tiny movement. Luca leans in, fascinated.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): The quiet bird finds a tiny hidden nest that no one else had seen. Mimi gasps softly with delight.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa gives the lesson. Mimi connects it to herself: "Being quiet helps me notice more."
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Quiet hold by the rainy window. No new dialogue, just warmth.
```
