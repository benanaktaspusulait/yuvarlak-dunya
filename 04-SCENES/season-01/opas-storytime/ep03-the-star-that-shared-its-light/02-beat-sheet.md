# The Star That Shared Its Light — Beat Sheet v1.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1 (Shot 01): Luca and Noah arrive under Opa's Tree at night. Series opening beat: "Come close, little friends."
Beat 2 (Shot 02): Opa begins: "Once, high in the sky, there was a little star." Luca asks if it was the brightest.
Beat 3 (Shot 03): The star wanted to keep all its light for itself. Noah reacts with surprise.
Beat 4 (Shot 04): Luca and Noah ask why the star wanted to keep its light alone. Opa explains gently.
Beat 5 (Shot 05): The star tries sharing one small beam with a neighbor star. Noah reacts hopefully.
Beat 6 (Shot 06): The whole sky glows brighter when all the stars share. Luca gasps with wonder.
Beat 7 (Shot 07): Opa gives the lesson. Noah connects it to himself: "Sharing makes more light, not less."
Beat 8 (Shot 08): Quiet starlit hold under the tree. No new dialogue, just warmth.
```
