# Where Does Rain Come From — Storyboard v4.0

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sec) — Curiosity

Wide Cloud Hill shot, gently settling toward medium.

Luca and Mimi climb gently, not rushed. Wind moves the grass around their feet. The existing Cloud Hill summit stone, CH-STONE-01, is visible from a distance. Luca stops first and looks up. Mimi follows his gaze. They share a quiet moment under the huge clouds.

---

## Shot 02 (15-30 sec) — Wonder

Medium shot with a soft sky tilt.

Mimi points to one drifting cloud. Luca watches it move. The grass bends softly in a small gust. They laugh a little, then Luca asks if rain comes from up there.

---

## Shot 03 (30-45 sec) — Thinking

Calm medium static shot near CH-STONE-01.

Both children sit or crouch close to the grass. The clouds darken slightly. Mimi observes rather than explains. Luca and Mimi look from the cloud to each other, then back up. Silence carries the thinking.

---

## Shot 04 (45-60 sec) — Waiting

Slightly closer medium shot, with hands and facial expressions readable.

The hill becomes still for a moment. Luca slowly holds one hand out, not because he knows what will happen, but because he is listening to the sky. Mimi watches the cloud and the grass. The wind moves softly. No answer is spoken yet.

---

## Shot 05 (60-75 sec) — First Drop

Medium discovery shot.

One small raindrop lands on Luca's hand. He notices it before speaking. Mimi leans in softly, then looks back to the cloud. The connection becomes clear without a lesson.

---

## Shot 06 (75-90 sec) — Gentle Rain

Warm wide final shot.

Gentle rain begins beside the same CH-STONE-01 anchor. Luca and Mimi smile, look back up at the clouds, and stay together in the soft rain. The ending feels like they discovered the answer together while Cloud Hill remains clearly recognizable.
