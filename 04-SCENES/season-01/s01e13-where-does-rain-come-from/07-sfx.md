# S01E13 — Sound Effects

---

## Sound Principle

The episode should sound calm, airy and observant.

Use silence as part of the storytelling.
Avoid heavy thunder, dramatic music stings or sudden weather sounds.

---

## Ambiance

| Sound | Used In | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Soft wind | All shots | Gentle, consistent Cloud Hill atmosphere |
| Grass rustle | 01, 02, 03, 04 | Follows small wind changes |
| Open-air quiet | All shots | Leaves room for wonder, thinking and waiting |
| Very soft distant cloud murmur | 02, 04 only, optional | Low and friendly, not scary |
| First raindrop | 05 | A small clear drop on Luca's hand |
| Gentle rain | 06 | Soft pitter-patter after the discovery |

---

## Scene-Specific SFX

| Shot | Sound | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Footsteps on grass | Luca and Mimi climb gently uphill |
| 01 | Wind through grass | Establishes Cloud Hill as alive and calm |
| 02 | Slight wind lift | Grass moves as the cloud drifts |
| 02 | Optional soft cloud murmur | A quiet sign the cloud is changing |
| 03 | Quiet ambient pause | Supports the thinking beat |
| 03 | Grass brushing | Tiny environmental movement during silence |
| 04 | Soft wind dip | The hill feels quiet and expectant |
| 04 | Grass brushing | Waiting moment without explanation |
| 05 | Single raindrop | Lands on Luca's hand before dialogue |
| 05 | Tiny clothing/hand movement | Luca notices the drop softly |
| 06 | Gentle rain begins | Builds softly, never loud or dramatic |
| 06 | Warm open-air ambience | Keeps the ending calm and safe |

---

## Music

If music is used, keep it minimal and warm.

No magical reveal cue.
No dramatic thunder cue.
No energetic ending sting.

The ending should feel like a quiet discovery shared with a friend.
