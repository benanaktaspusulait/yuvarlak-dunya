# S01E13 — Camera Plan

---

## Camera Principle

The camera should feel like a calm preschool observer.

Use stable child-eye-level framing.
Do not turn the episode into a dramatic weather scene.
Do not use tight framing, fast push-ins, handheld movement or cinematic tension.

The answer should be discovered through quiet looking, not forced by camera emphasis.

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sec)

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Lens | 35mm wide to gentle medium |
| Height | 0.85m |
| Angle | Child eye level |
| Movement | Static start, then very slow settle toward medium |

Purpose:
Show Luca and Mimi arriving at Cloud Hill, with CH-STONE-01 visible from a distance, then noticing the clouds.

---

## Shot 02 (15-30 sec)

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Lens | Medium |
| Height | 0.85m |
| Angle | Child eye level, with a soft upward tilt only when looking at clouds |
| Movement | Slow tilt up to clouds, then settle back to the children |

Purpose:
Let the children and audience notice the moving cloud together.

---

## Shot 03 (30-45 sec)

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

| Height | 0.80m |
| Angle | Child eye level |

Purpose:

No tight framing.

No cutaway explanation.
