# What Stars Say v2.0

---

## Duration

1:00 (4 shots × 15 seconds)

---

## Theme

Night, wonder, imagination

---

## Learning

The night sky is beautiful and safe

---

## Characters

| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| Kiko | Dreamer |
| Opa | Guide |

---

## World

Central Square (night)

---

## Story Summary

Kiko sees stars for the first time at night. She is amazed. She asks Opa what stars say. Opa says they say goodnight. Kiko waves at stars. They twinkle back.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Kiko learns that the night sky is beautiful and safe.
