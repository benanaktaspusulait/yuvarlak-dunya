# What Stars Say — Storyboard v2.1

> Mekan her shot'ta **Opa's Tree (Night)** — generic "tree" kullanılmaz.
> Shot 01 yeni Take Shot; Shot 02-04 önceki shot'tan devam eder (tek sürekli çekim hissi).

---

## Shot 01 (0-15 sn) — New Take Shot
Established Opa's Tree at night. Luca and Opa are already sitting quietly at the base, naturally discovered by the camera as it gently tilts up to the starry sky. Opa is revealed softly, never appearing suddenly.
**Visible action:** Both look up at the stars.

## Shot 02 (15-30 sn) — Continuation
Continue inside the same scene. The focus stays on Luca and Opa. Luca looks up toward Opa with gentle curiosity and asks his question.
**Quiet moment:** After "What do stars say?", hold a brief, wonder-filled pause before Opa responds — the world stays still, stars twinkle, then Opa begins to think.

## Shot 03 (30-45 sn) — Continuation
Continue inside the same scene. Opa gestures softly and warmly. The focus naturally shifts toward Luca, whose eyes grow heavy with sleepy wonder.

## Shot 04 (45-60 sn) — Continuation (intimate ending)
Continue inside the same scene. Luca leans against Opa and drifts to sleep. The camera performs a gentle pull-back **within the existing scene** — same world, same composition, same scale. Intimate, not a new wide establishing shot.
