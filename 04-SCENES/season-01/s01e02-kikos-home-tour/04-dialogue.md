# Dialogue — Kiko's Home Tour

> **15 saniye. 7 replik.**

---

## Complete Dialogue

```
Shot 01 (0–4 sn):
Kiko: Oh! I almost forgot!
Kiko: Come on! Let me show you inside!

Shot 02 (4–8 sn):
(Kiko walks toward door, opens it, enters. Camera follows. No dialogue.)

Shot 03 (8–12 sn):
Kiko: This is where I read stories.
Kiko: And these are my favourite toys.
Kiko: I love looking outside from here.

Shot 04 (12–15 sn):
Kiko: Welcome inside!
```

---

## Voice Direction

| Shot | Tone | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| 01 | Excited, inviting | "Come on!" rises with enthusiasm |
| 02 | — | Movement only, no speech |
| 03 | Proud, sharing | Points while speaking, gentle pauses |
| 04 | Warm, welcoming | Final greeting, smile in voice |
