# S01E07 — Camera Plan

> Preschool-safe movement only: gentle push-in, soft pan, subtle focus shift, gentle
> pull-back at the ending, stable child-eye-level framing. No fast zooms, whip pans,
> handheld shake, dramatic movement, or camera reset between continuity shots.
> 35mm-equivalent soft preschool framing throughout.
