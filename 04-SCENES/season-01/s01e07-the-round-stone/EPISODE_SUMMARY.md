## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — Discovery (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Luca walks among the rounded rocks, spots a round flat stone on the ground, bends down, picks it up and examines it with a curious smile.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Oh! What's this?"
**Camera:** 35mm wide → 50mm medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 02 — Showing Kiko (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Kiko approaches from a distance. Luca runs over and shows the stone.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Look what I found!" / Kiko: "Wow!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 03 — Like the Moon (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Kiko takes the stone, examines it, glances at the sky, then smiles.
**Dialogue:** Kiko: "It looks like the moon!" / Luca: "You're right!"
**Camera:** Medium → close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 04 — Together (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Stone Hill — Kiko lifts the stone up; both look at it as the sunlight turns golden. A warm, shared moment.
**Dialogue:** "Let's keep it." / "Together!"
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

---

*Learning: A small discovery, shared with a friend, becomes something special.*
