## Purpose

A very short, calm "Stone Hill" atmosphere opener that plays BEFORE the story.

This micro-opening is story-external. It does not add any narrative, problem, or
character action. The actual episode is still Shot 01-06 = 90 seconds. With this opener
the total video runs about 93-94 seconds.

Do NOT count this opener as part of the 90-second story. Do NOT extend or repeat it.

## Continuity

Its final frame must connect smoothly into the Shot 01 opening frame (same Stone Hill
set, same warm morning daylight, same Episode Colour Master). Think of it as a soft lead

---

## Stone Hill Visual Identity

Stone Hill is the gentle stone-discovery hill of Pompom Hills: smooth rounded toy-like
stones, a soft pebble path, warm moss patches, small grass tufts, a gentle slope and
child-safe rounded rock clusters. Calm, tactile and safe.

Required anchors: smooth rounded stone clusters, soft pebble path, gentle slope,
warm moss between stones, child-safe rounded rock shapes, warm morning daylight.

Never: sharp rocks, cliffs, realistic quarry, dark cave, dangerous boulders,
generic rocky field.

## Background Object Lock

The frame shows the same physical Stone Hill set used in Shot 01. All visible stone
clusters, the pebble path, moss patches and slope remain stable throughout the opener.

Do not:
- introduce characters
- add cliffs, houses or modern objects
- add text, logos or a title card
- repaint or morph the environment
- change the Stone Hill layout

Allowed: very slow camera movement, gentle grass/moss motion, soft light shimmer.
