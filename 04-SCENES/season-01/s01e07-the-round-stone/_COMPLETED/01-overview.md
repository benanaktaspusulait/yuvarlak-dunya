# The Round Stone v2.0

---

## Duration

1:00 (4 shots × 15 seconds)

---

## Theme

Discovery, shapes, sharing

---

## Learning

New discoveries are exciting when shared

---

## Characters

| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| Luca | Discoverer |
| Kiko | Friend |

---

## World

Stone Hill

---

## Story Summary

Luca finds a perfectly round stone on Stone Hill. He is excited. He shows it to Kiko. They find more round stones. They stack them. The stack falls. They laugh and try again. They build a small stone tower together.

---

## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Luca learns that new discoveries are exciting when shared.
