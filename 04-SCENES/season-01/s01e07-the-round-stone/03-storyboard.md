# S01E07 — The Round Stone — Storyboard

> All shots: Time of Day = Morning, Lighting = Warm morning daylight (Shot 01 is the
> Episode Colour Master). No golden afternoon, no moonlight.

---

## Shot 01 — Luca Discovers the Stone (0-15 sn)

**Kamera:** 35mm wide → gentle push-in to medium
**Mekan:** Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area
**Karakter:** Luca

**Aksiyon:**
- 0-5 sn: Wide establishing. Luca walks the pebble path among rounded stones, spots a smooth round flat stone.
- 5-10 sn: Bends down, gently picks it up.
- 10-15 sn: Examines it with a curious smile.

**Dialogue:**
```
Luca: Oh! What's this?
```

**Işık:** Warm morning daylight (Episode Colour Master)
**Ses:** Birds, footsteps on pebble path, gentle "boop"

---

## Shot 02 — Luca Shows Kiko (15-30 sn)

**Kamera:** Continue from Shot 01 → medium-wide
**Mekan:** Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area
**Karakter:** Luca, Kiko

**Aksiyon:**
- 0-5 sn: Kiko enters naturally from the same Stone Hill path, sees Luca.
- 5-10 sn: Luca turns and shows the stone.
- 10-15 sn: Kiko looks at the stone with wonder.

**Dialogue:**
```
Kiko: Hi Luca!
Luca: Look what I found!
Kiko: Wow!
```

**Işık:** Warm morning daylight
**Ses:** Soft footsteps, happy greeting

---

## Shot 03 — They Examine the Stone (30-45 sn)

**Kamera:** Medium, gentle focus shift
**Mekan:** Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area
**Karakter:** Kiko, Luca

**Aksiyon:**
- 0-5 sn: Kiko holds the stone, Luca points to its round shape.
- 5-10 sn: They turn it slowly in their hands.
- 10-15 sn: Both smile with wonder.

**Dialogue:**
```
Kiko: It's so round.
Luca: Like a tiny moon!
```

**Işık:** Warm morning daylight
**Ses:** Quiet ambient wind, tiny stone-handling sound
**Not:** "Tiny moon" imaginative only — no literal moon needed.

**Kamera:** Gentle pan across already-visible stones → back to characters


**Işık:** Warm morning daylight

## Shot 05 — A Place for the Stone (60-75 sn)

**Kamera:** Medium-low, stone placement visible
**Mekan:** Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area
**Karakter:** Luca, Kiko

**Aksiyon:**
- 0-5 sn: They find a small safe mossy spot.
- 5-10 sn: Kiko gently places the stone flat on the moss.
- 10-15 sn: Both look at the placed stone warmly.

**Dialogue:**
```
Luca: Should we keep it?
Kiko: Maybe we can share it.
```

**Işık:** Warm morning daylight
**Ses:** Ambient wind, soft stone-set-down sound
**Güvenlik:** Stone placed flat — no stacking, no balancing.

---

## Shot 06 — Together (75-90 sn)

**Kamera:** Gentle pull-back → wide quiet hold
**Mekan:** Stone Hill — Rounded Stone Discovery Area
**Karakter:** Luca, Kiko

**Aksiyon:**
- 0-5 sn: Both step back and look at the placed stone.
- 5-10 sn: They smile at each other, then at the stone.
- 10-15 sn: Warm shared hold; ~2s quiet final frame.

**Dialogue:**
```
Luca: Now everyone can see it.
Kiko: Together.
Luca: Together.
```

**Işık:** Warm morning daylight (no golden shift)
**Ses:** Ambient wind, birds, soft warm ending tone
