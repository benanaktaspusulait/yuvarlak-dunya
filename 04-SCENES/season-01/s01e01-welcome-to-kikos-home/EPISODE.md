## Story Summary

Kiko warmly welcomes viewers to her home in Pompom Hills. She proudly shows her favourite places in the front garden, introducing the flowers and the big tree that make her home feel special. She invites everyone to visit again soon.

---

## Micro-Moment

Kiko's small joyful hop before saying goodbye — the moment children will remember.

---
