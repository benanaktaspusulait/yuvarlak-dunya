# Goodnight Tree — Beat Sheet v2.0

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1: Arda arrives at Opa's Tree at sunset, feels tired.
Beat 2: Opa tells Arda a story, but Arda asks about the tree.
Beat 3: Arda asks "Is the tree sleepy too?" Opa says yes.
Beat 4: They say goodnight to the tree. The tree rustles back. Arda falls asleep.
```
