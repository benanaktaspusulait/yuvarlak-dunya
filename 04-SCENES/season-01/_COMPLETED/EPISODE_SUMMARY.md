## Micro-Moment (Key Discovery)

Arda asks: "Is the tree sleepy too?" — a small, memorable idea children will remember and repeat.

---

## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — Sleepy Evening (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — Evening. The sky turns orange and pink. Opa's tree stands quietly, leaves swaying gently. A tired, sleepy-eyed Arda arrives and looks up at the tree.
**Dialogue:** Arda: "Opa? I'm sleepy."
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 02 — Tell Me a Story (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — Opa sits on his branch above, looks down, and smiles. Arda asks for a story and Opa begins.
**Dialogue:** Opa: "Hello, little one." / Arda: "Tell me a story." / Opa: "Once upon a time..."
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 03 — Is the Tree Sleepy Too? (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — Arda pauses, watches the leaves sway, and wonders aloud. Opa looks at the leaves, then at Arda. The leaves rustle softly, as if agreeing.
**Dialogue:** Arda: "Is the tree sleepy too?" / Opa: "Even trees need sleep."
**Camera:** Medium → close-up, slightly low angle, 0.80m.

### Shot 04 — Goodnight, Tree (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Opa's Tree — Arda sits at the base of the tree, looks up, and says goodnight. The leaves rustle back. Opa smiles. Arda closes his eyes and falls asleep as the camera pulls back to reveal the starry sky, the tree, sleeping Arda, and Opa.
**Dialogue:** Arda: "Goodnight, tree." / Opa: "The tree says goodnight too."
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

---

*Learning: Even the tallest trees need to rest.*
