## Shot-by-Shot

### Shot 01 — A Ball (0-15 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — A round yellow ball sits quietly. Luca arrives and sees it, then Noah arrives and sees the same ball.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "A ball!" / Noah: "I see it!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.
**Asset:** `03-PROPS/Toys/1-yellow-ball.png`

### Shot 02 — My Turn! (15-30 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Both reach for the ball. Luca holds one side, Noah the other. They look at each other.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "My turn!" / Noah: "No, my turn!"
**Camera:** Medium → medium close-up, eye level, 0.85m.

### Shot 03 — Your Turn (30-45 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — A quiet moment, then Luca gives the ball to Noah. Noah bounces it (boop!), smiles, and gives it back.
**Dialogue:** Luca: "Your turn." / Noah: "Your turn!"
**Camera:** Medium → close-up, eye level, 0.80m.

### Shot 04 — That Was Fun (45-60 sn)
**Location:** Central Square — Together they bounce the ball (boop! boop!). It flies up, both watch it land, and they smile.
**Dialogue:** "That was fun!" / "Again!"
**Camera:** Wide → medium, eye level, 0.85m.

*Learning: Sometimes sharing is even more fun than playing.*
