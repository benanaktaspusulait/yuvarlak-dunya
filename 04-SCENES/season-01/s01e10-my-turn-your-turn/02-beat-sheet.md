# S01E10 — Beat Sheet

---

## Story Rhythm

```
Beat 1: Luca and Noah discover the ball.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 2: Both want to play with it.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 3: Luca lets Noah try first.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 4: They play together happily.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 5: The ball rolls away.
────────────────────────────────
Beat 6: They find it together.
```

---

## Emotional Progression

Curiosity → Mild desire → Pause and think → Taking turns → Ball rolls away → Finding it together

The lesson is shown through action, not explained.
